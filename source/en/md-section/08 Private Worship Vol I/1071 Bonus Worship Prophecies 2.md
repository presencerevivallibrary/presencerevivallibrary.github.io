# Private Worship Bonus Prophecies 2

## Compassion Looks for a Place to Manifest Through Love

A vision, a picture appeared, and it took me a little by surprise. I saw a countryside full of people, and God was there.

What I said before, I will say again: not many have been able to receive much of My compassion and My love. Most of it is manifested to them in My works as I save them, heal them, and comfort them. But not too many have been able to receive My love in the form of fellowship, and to receive it in fellowship with Me, that I might bring them into the place of the deep-seatedness of My love that I have for those who are bound, for those who are destroyed, and for those who have become habitations of devils.

Oh, how I search for a place where I can love those people through the manifestations of My compassion. But not too many will fellowship with Me there, even though I have opened the inroads to know Me.

You ask for My keys. The more fullness of My love is the abortion of greed. You ask for keys that will let you into My storehouse. You ask for keys that will cause you to receive and divide My inheritance to those places that have been devastated, and to those places that have not had the moves and compassion of My miracles and deliverances.

You ask to step behind with Me and receive My inheritance. What would set you more free from greed than My heart beating in yours?

Have you not embraced the teaching from this camp? Have you not tried to balance yourself in your ever-ending search for truth, as you went here and there? You took all keys in the fullness of those who preached them in their understanding. You worked this key and that key. You operated many times, only to be disappointed, fall short, and wonder, "Why am I not able to work these things according to what Your Word says?"

Here again is where most fall short: they do not take My life and My love and apply it in every room where what is in that room is stronger than they are.

Is there anything stronger than the manifestation of My love, when I provided in every aspect for your freedom? What makes you think that not understanding My love would not be a problem to receiving something I provided for you because of My love? When you begin to receive it and know Me, you will know I have not withheld any of those things.

Speaking this to me, He said, "I keep coming on you like this, for many of these will dare to believe. They will take the grace I am offering them, and they will leave years behind and come into My presence and find out that these things are so."

## Love in the Reservoir Shortens the Fight Over Emotions

Joined together with My Word, this is the part many do not understand. This is why I said, "Minister to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, making melody in your heart to the Lord, giving thanks for all things to God and the Father."

Many of you do not understand that as you are ministering to Me, worshiping, and handling My Word on the lips of worship, spending those times in My presence, I am able to pour My love into the reservoir, the unlimited capacities of your born-again spirit. It is the residency of that love that shortens the fight.

What many do not understand is that the added ingredient to win over emotions is My love magnified in you. That sustains you and causes you to win. The missing ingredient is My love moving within you to sustain.

Many try without preparing their heart, baptizing themselves in My love, and spending time giving thanks for these things to God and the Father. Those who do not spend that time take the long road and are not fully equipped for the battle. Therefore many abort before their time.

I find pleasure that I have found enough to cause the people. In times past, some slipped in and did not know what they found. I was unable to teach them, only let them experience. Some found it quite by accident but did not know how to repeat the process.

But it pleases Me when I can lay this path out and give you instructions to win as certainly as the victory I provide.

We have been preaching lately and prophesying on this question: how much He loves you. The fullness of that was manifested at the cross. You could feed the world and do every miracle ever done, and you could not get Him to love you any more than was already manifested at the cross.

When I found out He cannot love me more, but I can receive more of His love, then I understood what I was prophesying when He said, "I cannot love Myself in you for you, but in this I exalt you."

He said, "As you hold Me there and exalt Me, I am exalting Myself in you. My love is changing you, and each time you will love Me more in the change I have changed you in."

## The Well of Fellowship Quenches the Thirst That Nothing Else Can

For I speak of times to come, of mysteries hidden from the foundations of the world, walked in by some and barely touched by others. Yet the outpourings, these places in Me to be uncovered in this generation, many are still waiting to be discovered.

So hold fast to what is good, pure, decent, and applicable. Hold fast to those things that have to do with My love, compassion, and forgiveness, and you will be strong. You will always be fruitful, and you will never fall.

Therefore receive My love, that you may love Me in that place. For in this place I have with you, nothing can be withheld. No good thing will be withheld. All I have called you for and saved you into is yours.

Do you not know that each place you make with Me, and in this limb I have given you, the last and most recent is in the fellowship with Me? You will reach more places there. I will be able to give you more of Myself because you have given Me more of yourself. Indeed, you are in store for a very interesting and powerful time.

For you and those who hear, continue in these things I have said and the things I have taught you concerning My Word. Many do not know that the thirst they have keeps resurfacing. They run here to try to quench it, and it is only temporary. They run there, and it is only temporary. They run over here, and it is only temporary.

Yet when your thirst is quenched by coming into that fellowship with Me and knowing Me in that kind of fellowship, which I ordained from the foundations of the world when I reclaimed you through a new nature and entered into fellowship, then the Spirit says, "Come. Come."

You who are thirsty, come and drink from the well that never runs dry, that springs up into everlasting life. When you get thirsty, I will open a new vein of the well and quench your thirst time after time after time.

You who have ears to hear, much awaits you. I am pulling you through. I have pulled many of you through to drink of this water who were not even close enough to take this step. Yet I reached through with My grace and gave you enough of My standing and strength because I want you to taste this fellowship with Me, that you might know how free you are and become bold in My compassion and in My love.

I have the impossible laid out for you. I have the impossible for you to attain and achieve. Therefore for many of you, this is a great day in the Spirit and in your walk. It will show up immediately in your strength. It will cause great joy in your life. It will cause the strength you need to finish getting past the evil one, who swore you would never come behind the door of this anointing, says the Lord.

That is for the ears that will hear.

## Line Upon Line Has Prepared a People to Know Him

Line upon line and precept upon precept, I have laid these foundations and truths, says the Lord. I did so in your maturity and in establishing truth, that you might walk in it. The foundations being laid in this generation are being laid because those before you prepared the way and made their contribution. Much of it you were able to build upon, which brings us closer and closer to My return.

For this I say to you: yes, I have spoken much about your fellowship with Me. I waited a long time that I might reveal certain truths that would stop everything that was stopping you, so you might come into this place of fellowship with Me.

Yes, I said much about the time when you would enter into fellowship with Me and get to know Me by the nature that has made you a partaker of My divine nature. I have waited line upon line, precept upon precept, here a little and there a little, for those to first appear in this generation who knew and know what it means to be in fellowship with Me.

They know what it means to the power that is in your life, what it means to answered prayer, what it means to My direction in your life, and what it means to the strifes and divisions that are so prevalent and are the cause of much sickness, indecision, and lack of direction currently in the body of Christ today.

So hear what the Spirit would say to you this day. Did I not lay truth out so that, upon comprehending and understanding this truth I give to you, you could again take steps on the foundation I have given you: Jesus Christ crucified, the understandings on righteousness, the understandings on the peace available to you? Now this line upon line I desire to lay, that you might come to know Me as individuals in that kind of fellowship with Me.

Know you not that what most of you are looking for, some search for by trying to find something they can do in the natural world. Others keep trying to pay a price of suffering. Still others believe they can work their way up into all I have for them.

Know this: it is by My grace and not by your works, lest any man should boast. Is any of what I have redeemed you out of and into unavailable to you? All sickness must give way and answer to what I have done. All strongholds that keep you from peace and truth must give way to what I have done.

When you get into this kind of fellowship with Me and find out about being a partaker of My divine nature, fellowshipping with Me in that holy, holy place where you were born again, then in getting to know Me like never before, your senses and the place they hold in this world will start giving way and coming under subjection to My Spirit and My Word.

If I can minister to your emotions and your senses by bringing them under subjection not only to My Spirit but to My love, then I can build My compassion in you. All you are looking for, freedom from all things, you will find fulfilled in My Word as you come to know Me through that love and then minister that love to those around you.

Therefore be strong. Anyone who has walked this walk knows that strength is to leave the things of the flesh behind and enter into the things that are of the Spirit.

## Conquer This Love and the Anointing, Direction, and Discernment Will Follow

Yes, I come time after time and lay line upon line, and give you precept upon precept. I exhort you continually, for I have desired to say these things concerning My nature, My love for you, and the love resident in My very being for you. I have desired to say many of these things for a long period of time, and now that they are being understood, they will be said.

Know this: conquer this, and you will put yourself five years ahead of most of the running being run in this world. Conquer this in love, and you will operate in the power, the anointing, the direction, and the discerning that you have known was possible, set your sights on, and did not know how to enter into the fullness of.

Tell those who fellowship with Me in the mantle I have separated them to, and those whom I have called to abundance that they might be yielded vessels I would use in My abundance: as in every call, whether you are called to helps, governments, prayer ministries, pastor, teacher, or evangelist, many do I equip with My utterances. They say what is in My mind. Still others carry all and accomplish mighty moves of My Spirit and set everything into order.

Hear what the Spirit would say: any time fame, glory, and power are at hand, there are forces. Any time uncommon forces are loose concerning the flesh, concerning fame, glory, and exaltation, these things come with a power and a pull that feeds pride and ego. Very few have been able to turn that down when they offered themselves in the areas of pleasure. They almost sufficed in the counterfeit of what I am offering to you.

So hear the missing ingredient. Where most fell short was that, for Me to fully accomplish what I needed, they had to be free from all things so they might administer all things. They need what they can only receive through intimacy and fellowship with Me.

The fellowship with them, the wisdom, and the times together are what I desire. Your wealth is the byproduct of that.

Footnote: the wealth that belongs to Him is the byproduct of that. I think He is talking about the change from loving you, then you loving Him, and then He takes that increase in love and loves the world through you. It looks like the fellowship you find in there is more than all the temptation wealth can offer you. You will finish your course.

He edifies and continues to edify. He loves the interaction where He gets to flow spontaneously, interact with you, speak His mind, flow, bless, and heal. This is all part of the edification process. This is all part of church. He loves being able, through the gifts and the Word, to interact with you, where He can step in any time and do what His Word says.

He particularly loves it when you spontaneously fellowship with Him because, in your estimation, He is so good you just have trouble not doing it.

## Compassion Can Be Increased Until Joy Overcomes Fear

Know you not that when a person operates in My compassion, you are within range of all things being possible to him that believes? But hear this mystery and understand: what most do not know is that the capacity for compassion can be ministered to by yourself and continually increased and increased and increased.

Did I not say all things are possible to him that believes? Put your feet on the path that goes somewhere. Put your feet on the path that goes where I am. When My servant finds himself where I am, then these things you know, understand, and long for will become part of you.

He is talking about, "He that loses his life shall find it." For this kind of servant, where I am, there will My servant be.

What most have experienced by way of fellowship with Me through the common teachings being done this day, most do not attain to the place I have for you: a place of fellowship, a place of presence, a place of joy.

Most are looking for complete victory, and not in the things this world can give. That is only temporary pleasure. What most are looking for is deep-seated joy. It is operated by My kind of love that takes away all torment and fear. Those who have the capacity to look to the future and say, "I will be all right," those who look at the present and say, "I am winning here," and those who can look every direction at once and know the answer even before the journey begins, have no torment and no fear.

Come, come, come to the manifestation of My love, which is compassion. Come and walk in My joy, which has its roots in the depths of your spirit where My peace comes from, that is not of this world.

Come finish your journey. My intention is to use the same love I love you with to gently nurture and herd you down the paths I have established for you. All that I said I am is who I am, and the blessings I have in store for you are what I prepared for you from the foundations of the world.

Therefore rejoice. Every person who has My joy knows that I can turn every circumstance and correct every circumstance. As I said, I care for you. He that casts his care on Me will see that I can bestow My care upon him. I can take any impossible situation and turn it. I can turn opposition.

I even care about the things you care about most, which many of you do not know because you do not know what makes you happy. He cares about your relationships. He cares about your marriages. He cares about you being happy in those areas, and those are not circumstances beyond Him to work out.

It is on this path of the things that are really of the heart, and the things I am able to do, that I herd gently and nurture along the way.

For the dispensation of this kind of fellowship, your nature has been made a partaker of Mine. I have waited out the seasons, times, and teachings, and moved according as I could in each dispensation and each move, waiting for this time when you could enter into this level of fellowship with Me, and I could enter into this level of fellowship with you.

From the foundations of the world, in the recovery, great things were scheduled for this time: things that called for My presence, things that called for the miraculous. The days are here that many of the prophets spoke of, the days when mighty harvesters will be taken from the fruit of the earth.

Hear what the Spirit would say: those who know Me will move forward, those who know Me from the very depth of their nature, who know what I am capable of, and not only what I am capable of, but what I will do. You will see exploits. You will see My hand.

So band together and strengthen each other in fellowship, for the evil one is throwing a fit in the land. Band together, stay strong, and build your house on My rock. If you walk in this place in Me, know that all the angels are there for you, and not a hair on your head needs to perish.

You who have ears to hear, hear what the Spirit would say. I am talking to you who have had this level of experience and have decided to walk this way. There are not many places, and none known to you, where you can go and find out how to receive what you need for the next generation of My anointings, and for that material which I shall lay upon the foundation that hastens My return.

For those who are experienced in change, know this: where can you inquire? Is there anyone you can talk to about what to expect when I am raising you up and establishing uncommon anointings? Where are you going to go? What counsel are you going to receive? Who do you know who can tell you what to expect in these realms I am bringing you to, where it is necessary that My love flow on a whole new level to this generation with My compassion?

I am speaking to you who have yielded yourselves to Me and decided you were going to go where I am concerning the things of the anointing and the power I have to face the things of this generation and prevail through signs and wonders, and through equipping in many diversities of My power and glory.

Will you go over here to inquire? Will you go over there? Will you go back here? Or can you go forward? Where will you go to learn those things of My compassion that speak of those places, but to Me?

Many are looking for what they seek in many places, but what you are looking for you will find with Me. I gave those who experienced Me, heard My voice, and could understand mysteries as much of My understanding as I could give, and they passed that understanding on to you. Yet when they speak of the place of My glory, they still fall short of telling you what is waiting for you.

What He said to you, He said to me. But I do not even know what to expect in the places of glory He is calling us to.

Therefore take the instruction you have been taught, even though he who is teaching you has not experienced the fullness, and come where I am. Blaze the trail of My glory so many may follow.

Know this, you who have ears to hear: I am waiting for you. Concerning My love, nothing is going to bring you there but the time you spend in My presence visiting Me with your worship and Me pouring Myself back out on you. You are the next scheduled move on the foundation.

## Isaiah's Prophecy Points to Glory That Undoes Every Deformity

Do not mind all you have known to be true and all you have seen in the Spirit. The prophets have prophesied about it, and many people have declared the possibility of the impossible as they were moved by My Spirit, looked into the mirror of My Word, saw My face, and knew these things were possible. But the bridge that bridged the gap, not many made it.

Hear what the Spirit would say. Did I not say to you in Isaiah 52, in My prophecies, what price would be paid? The chastisement of My peace was made. The bearing of sickness and pain was made. Did I not say My visage would be marred more than any man, and My form more than the sons of men?

There is no one who will be born, no deformities, no body parts missing, nothing that can be done to the human form at birth that would be deformed more than I was in the day of My visitation. "More than the sons of men" means all who are born to you. There will be nobody who went beyond the price I paid.

In the day of My visitation and the day of My glory, know this: there will be nothing the curse has done that will not be undone before Me. In My manifested glory, diseases and deformities will run. It will be a great day of revival, and you will see amazing things take place in your presence.

Many have seen these things in the Spirit but have not seen them in reality. But those who will come where I am will finish their course and know Me in the fullness of My love and the power of My compassion.

Am I getting this straight? We found a path in. We found the key. All of us have even been hanging around the door, admiring what is on the other side. But we found it. We have at least got the door open and are looking in.

What I am finding out is that everything that was ever promised seems to be in the compassion, going into the love, and the fullness of all those things that have been waiting for every child of God since Jesus said, "It is finished."

Jesus, am I getting this straight? I know that I am.

What did I see? I saw foundation and revelation compounded on revelation, line upon line and precept upon precept. I watched it come up like this, and then it would crumble almost all the way. It would start back up and crumble back. Then it would start back up and crumble.

This time it made it here, everything changed color, and He started talking about a place that few had visited. It was so bright, and it seemed to consist of that part of the substance of faith that made all the difference between the astounding and the absolute overflowing of God. Once again, it had to do with everything I thought it would have to do with: compassion and love.

What do you know about going in? Not much, just what I have been yelling about all week: getting in behind the door far enough to find out who He is, prophesy about it, yell about it, and have more Word open up than every dream possible.

Just enough to look behind it, marvel, and come back and see results and things happening. It is amazing me, but He keeps telling me I am just at entry level.

Many of these people have caught up. They have caught up because finally I made it somewhere where I was not that much further ahead of them, and I did not know how to go on very well. They caught up, and now it looks like a whole bunch of us get to lock arms and go into this thing shoulder to shoulder.

But you who come into My compassion and My love will understand many things you had not understood before. You will say, "I have never been this way before."

Why should you be surprised? The person who walks closer to the fullness of the attributes of love, forgiveness, and compassion is a person who has more of Me. To have more of Me is to have more of the answers you are looking for.

If they would learn to love their neighbors as they love Me and as I love them, a multitude of sins would be prevented. Most of what is happening to My people could have been prevented had they not walked down the alternative path, even the acceptable path, and not the perfect.

The devil has taught you, "Sacrifice, sacrifice, sacrifice," and has hidden the true meaning of what it means to walk in My fullness. You think you would lack concerning your flesh and the appetites of your flesh.

Does not every answered prayer really begin with you believing it? Who is it that loves Me and in whom I manifest My love that I will not do for them what I promised I would do? Mostly, you will be able to receive it.

Praying in tongues cannot give you what private worship does, but neither can private worship give you what praying in tongues can. So when I release another key into your understanding, it is My desire that you use that key to unlock the door at hand.

For many of you want to take another step. Do you think I do not know and understand the desires of your heart? Do you think I do not know the kind of water you thirst for? I gave things in the order I gave them so you might take the key and unlock the door. This is the next step into Me and out of your problem.

Many of you have prayed yourself to this place. So go on now. Go on, for a great outpouring awaits you.

In all the times of your walk with Me, milk and meat, I loved you then, I love you now, and I will love you tomorrow. You give Me your best; I will give you Mine. In this year to come, as you begin to walk with Me, because of what I have been released to work in you, I will be able to do things this year that I have been able to do for you in no other year leading up to this one.

But in maturity, is it not line upon line, precept upon precept, here a little and there a little? As gifting comes into place and as I equip with power, many of you will receive walks with Me in the Spirit that not many have had up to this hour.

As all things close in for a great harvest that I will orchestrate in these end times, know this: you have prayed out and made yourself part of a viable plan that I have set into motion, and I looked for those who would discover and walk it out.

When I say what I will say in this year to come, I will be able to lay a foundation that many will join themselves to very quickly. You are about to taste of the grapes of the land I have called you to conquer, and many will look and say, "This is good." They will release Me to do a quick work, then a quicker work, then a quicker work.

Know that what I ask you to do, you are more than able to do, because you are stronger and greater in might than he who is against you. Therefore rejoice and worship much, that I may deal with emotions that are lagging behind. My joy indeed is your strength. So conquer and know that you have the weaponry for it.

All He needs is somebody He can take to that place, and you will see those things in their fullness again.

## A Shift Begins Toward Endurance, Readiness, and Increasing Love

I am pleased. I am pleased with the progress you have been making, and My presence is welcome here. Many things have come against you, many that you could not see but could feel the consequences of. But do you not know there is a time coming when the change will be noticed by everyone, and I will be involved in that?

Be strong, because a definite shift is beginning to take place. You will know it as a new place in the Spirit. It will be for many people, and you will have reason to be excited, because it will be an exciting time on this road to our victory, says the Lord.

Take these things to heart, for times are changing and seasons are changing. Know that many things are going to come. These things are going to come whether many people do what they are supposed to or not. So hear what the Lord would say and move forward. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready.

Keep in mind, Paul could take great pleasure in the presence of God and worship, whether he was in the ocean, getting a beating, imprisoned in a sewer, or having his feet broken. It must be a powerful key that switches you over to grace. It activates what He has done about it.

The road the enemy finds most interesting into you is to stop you from doing the daily things that free Me to move in My plan for you day after day. Anyone who stays in My presence for lengths of time and abides in Me is freeing Me to get closer to them, closer to the fullness of their call and heart, and do things I cannot do on a normal basis.

Walking in more of what I have for you does not necessarily depend on your title. It depends on where you want to walk and what you want to receive that I have for you. Did I not say to all that these signs would follow them that believe? I did not name certain persons. I said, "Them that believe."

My fellowship with you is in great delight when you come to Me walking in My truth and I fellowship with you around that truth. I am able to do many things I desire to do, and you will receive many things you desired to receive. When I fellowship with you and it fills you with joy, I rejoice with you, says the Lord.

These places you are moving into, I have prepared. I prepared them by the power of My Spirit. To say long waits were on purpose--long waits were because it was necessary. If I have My way, says the Lord, you will move into a place that has been guaranteed. You will see all that I told you. Just stay in. Stay in the Spirit. Pray in the Holy Spirit and worship Me. Worship Me.

Something is getting closer. I did not give you that word endurance for nothing. So be strong. You will see much in these last days, says the Lord.

Do not be alarmed, but rather rejoice when change seems to be coming, because this change that is coming is good. You will see that not only will much good come out of it, but things that have not been understood before will be understood.

Rejoice and know that in My joy and in My presence everything will be increasing. As this love continues to increase, you will understand things you have not been able to understand in the past. You will know why this could have happened, and this could have happened, because of the love of God that was attained to by these different seasons.

So be strong. Hold Me up and know that love never fails.

## The More Excellent Way Is Pursuing Love With Tenacity

The reason it has been so hard to bring you to this place is because so few have been there to paint any kind of picture you can see. Therefore trust Me when I say that beyond anything you have believed yet, I am waiting for you with all I have promised for the days to come.

How many of you will understand what your worship does to your soul? When I know you are coming before Me to draw closer to Me, it pleases Me, says the Lord. For I seek such to worship Me.

As you begin to flow in all that I have, My Spirit, even wisdom, you will notice there is a closer point between the natural and the spiritual than most walk in. From My fullness, you can bring My power over to that side and bring that side into My power.

Every time My church during their dispensation would get serious with Me, serious about prayer and fasting, My love and that walk in it would spring up time after time, because this is who and what I am, says the Lord. They will not travel down the path I have laid for them supernaturally without running into My love and My desire to bring them forward into the fullness of it.

In that there is no fear, and in that you will never fail.

I said, "Covet earnestly the best gifts, yet I show unto you a more excellent way." Though I speak with tongues of men and angels and have not love--what He was saying is this: the more excellent way is to go after the love of God with the same tenacity that you had to go after the workings of miracles that would have exalted you in the body of Christ.

If you go after the love of God with the same tenacity that we would go after that kind of power, that is the more excellent way. When you go after the love of God with tenacity and refuse to back down, then everything you need to minister to and to help, eventually you will be equipped with.

I have much to teach you about the ways of entering into My rest. You will be amazed how it will strengthen you, strengthen you, strengthen you. This rest is based on My fellowship with you and the Word of God alive in you.

Not only can I give you a job; I can see to it that you receive one you love, not just for the money. You are wondering how I would do that. If it took Me a season, I can do it because I can change this and that and let it head in your direction.

The thing is, do you spend time with Me? What do you want? All things are possible to him that believes that I am what I said, and that you are what I said you are.

In fellowship with Me, you will find it grows richer and richer as you approach Me walking in My love and My manifestations, because this is what I am. When you see the revivals come into manifestation in these last days, you will understand why they were not accessed before, and that the one ingredient that is a power gift more than anybody knows is the manifestation of My love when it is flowing in its fullness, says the Lord.

I can tell you over and over how much I love you, but when you begin to practice returning that love, you will get the power that is in it. By returning that love, I mean your heart in fellowship with Mine. He loves such that worship Him in spirit and in truth.

He waited four thousand years and waited the dispensations out for the first Man to appear after Adam who had a nature like His and could give Him the fellowship He wanted. That was Jesus, to worship Him in spirit and in truth. This is what He loves.

When you worship and pray in the Spirit, you are setting yourself a direction. You cannot worship and pray in the Spirit without setting yourself a direction. When you do these things, you welcome Me to come in My strength and set your direction.

When you worship Me, says the Lord, as My sons and My daughters, you put yourself in a place with Me. You recognize Me as your Father, and I recognize you as My sons and daughters.

Do you think I do not take great pleasure in answering your prayers and giving you the things I have to give you? It is the enemy who is accusing you. I have always been here, and I have always enjoyed giving you the things you love in the relationship and fellowship I have with you.

You are My sons and daughters. I do not hold back from you, nor do I turn My back on any need you have. I am here. So do not be programmed by the enemy. Be programmed by Me, by My Word, and by what I have promised you.
