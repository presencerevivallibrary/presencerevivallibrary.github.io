# Bonus Worship Prophecies 2

## New Worshipers Will Taste His Presence Without the Weight of Religion

For those who are new, when they come forward, new to worship and new to My presence, it will be as though they tasted of a good thing and said, "It is good, and it is well with my soul."

There will be many, many in this generation who have not tasted, but shall taste. The delight will be so great that they will run with it. Those who come in and are not worn down by religion, by carrying around brass that has been traded for gold, will delight in My presence and run. They have not been worn out by that which is old.

All who have walked before you have accomplished realms of the Spirit to walk in My glory, My power, and the standing that I have made available to you. But all who walked in these things before you faced the same things you face. So come aside with Me, that I might bring you up higher, and you will understand these things just as those who went before you.

Great things are ahead. Only the sufficiency of My grace can bring you to those places of My standing and the places of the victories that many of you are seeing in your spirit, even as you pray and worship.

So be strong, for you are closer to personal victory than you think. You have been thinking too much on the war, so you were not able to adjust your thinking to the victory. So He has to tell you: you are closer than you think. Do not let up on the war.

## Joy and Peace Change the Journey From Drudgery to Strength

The joy of the Lord indeed is your strength, and he who lacks these things cannot see very far. For My joy is your strength. For you who do not have strength, even in the beginnings when you are doing little more than practicing My presence, there will be a day of change. My joy indeed will become your strength, and your outlook and your journey will change.

Who said you could not have great joy and peace along the way? My peace I give unto you, whose origin is not of this world. Take it and walk in it. You can have heaven to go to heaven with.

Who has preached the gospel of breakdown, the gospel of drudgery? I saw a whole broken-down gospel that spoke very loudly that God was not the answer, carried by many, many, many. Leave those rags behind. Pick up My robes of righteousness and walk in what I have provided for you.

Come taste of My joy and peace. Stay in My presence until My joy becomes your strength, and then walk in it, for it will change your whole life and outlook, says the Lord.

In the days to come, many of you will understand the value of time spent with Me in worship. You will understand the value because you will begin to understand the benefit, and the benefit is the ongoing fellowship you will find with Me on a day-by-day basis.

You will find My joy, for My joy will be your strength. What can defeat you or pull you down when My strength is your strength because My joy is yours? Therefore do that which profits. Make your journey into fellowship with Me, and you will find that I am not the One standing back from you; you stood back from Me.

## Fasting, Worship, and Prayer Will Individualize the Callings

The time has come, and many are coming to the place where they can hear Me. Many this year have spent more time with Me than in any previous year.

Know this: fasting will come online again and be very prevalent among you. Many will give themselves to Me in seasons of fasting, worship, and prayer. You will do these things, and as this year goes by, I will begin to individualize your call. I will begin to empower you and equip you for what I have separated you for.

As all of you begin to minister the place I have given you by My grace, and the faith I measured out to you to operate in that faith, all these working together will see to all things concerning revival.

Hear what the Spirit would say, for I have many things yet to say and many moves of My Spirit. One of the moves will be that suddenly there will be knowledge, wisdom, and understanding. In fact, it will be revelation in the field you walk in.

There will be entrepreneurs who hear new and more excellent ways to do things. Still, there will be those who run My governments, and they will see, know, and arrange. There will be no little ones pushed to the side and lost in the shuffle.

Be patient, for in it you will possess not only your souls, but many souls that shall come into the kingdom that day. Be strong and know that the time of that vision is drawing closer and closer as you receive what I have for you.

So be not as some who have little and do not enter in, and what little they have is taken away from them. Be as those who have much and will receive more, so that more may be given to them. Gird yourself up and prepare yourself, for it is at hand.

## Tender Hearts Will Lead Others Into the Presence They Practice

There will be the most unusual people stepping forward to lead worship, because they never esteemed this of themselves. They will be those who always occupied a place in the company of the people, but they themselves will become so excited about spending time in My presence that My presence will come and spend time with them in all the excitement. They will qualify themselves for leading large groups into what they are walking in.

Hear what the Spirit would say: it is those who yield their heart and not their garment. Rend your heart, not your garment. That tender, meek, receiving spirit is precious gold before Me in what is to come, saith the Lord.

For prayer centers, you must know this: even as Paul was singled out by a messenger, not because he made of himself anything, but because of an age that was young, I found what I needed in him. Even as I told him from the beginning, his life would testify under much persecution.

Because of the abundance of the revelation and all that I had given him, he laid a foundation that the church is even resting on today. Was his life easy? No. Well, you can know this: you have been accounted worthy of part of his attention.

Not one, but many tests have been passed. Many of you have been found faithful, even at times when you did not understand what was going on, nor your own feelings, but waged a war without giving in to hopelessness. You won. You won in a realm where My grace is becoming your strength.

You too can sing the praises of God in any kind of prison Satan would ever try to lock you up in, for there is no prisoner who stays a prisoner long who knows how to sing My praises of victory.

This is something He has been waiting for. Hear these words: you have the grace for it. So move on. Move on. Move on.

## Grace Strengthens the Small Places Where the Fight Is Won

Move on, for the strength you feel and the strength you stand in will be the kind that will not relent. In the most powerful standings, it is in the most insignificant things, because the little foxes spoil the vine.

Not many believers knew they were being overtaken until enough of them had been had that the fight was too great to turn back. But the noble ones waged the war on the line and would not surrender even the smallest rights they had in My kingdom.

For this day I give grace, for you have survived enough to receive it. Be strong, for the greatest days of your harvest are ahead, saith the Lord.

Do you think that your heart and the healing of it are anything to Me? Do you think there is anything I cannot approach and do for those who believe? In the same measure that I cannot move for those who do not believe, I can move for those who do.

For in this I say that all things are possible to him that believeth. Not only am I able to move in the heart of your spirit, but I am able to move in your physical heart. So why be afraid of anything the enemy would send your way? He cannot overpower you emotionally, nor can he cancel out your physical man.

Put yourself into My hands, for it is in My presence that you rise, stand, and strive to do what I have separated and called you to do as individuals. When you strive to walk the walk I have laid out for you, then My will comes into play. When you are walking in My perfect will, there is nothing I will not do for you.

## Unlimited Resources Require Breakthrough in a Difficult Realm

These areas you are hearing of, and the grace I am speaking of, are the least attained to. The love of money indeed is the root of all evil. Therefore hear what the Spirit would say: if you will receive instruction, I am about to teach you through many sources. You will understand My grace, and you will see how to operate in an area where very few have operated because of the difficulty.

It is not that these things were not revealed. It is not that in times past these things were not taught. Most of the time, they were not received.

Therefore be strong, for I am looking for those who break through the front line and become standards that others will be taught from and follow. Of all the moves that have come and gone, this has been the least frequent.

Be strong and know that your enemy is at your gate, and he is there with a monstrous-sized lock. It is a big lock. You can hear the rust as the key turns. Nobody has been in there for some time. I did not say that people did not run off with it. Many have run off with it. But nobody has been in that room for some time.

So why would you think it would be an easy thing? Why would you think it would be easy to open this gospel up to unlimited resources?

This is what I take advantage of. This is what I use to come and bless you. There is no atmosphere such as this where I am not present, and when I am present in a tangible way, everything I am is present. Therefore I am available with My grace and My power this very hour to give you what you need.

Had I not said that he who believes does not have to walk away with empty hands? Therefore yield yourself over to Me. You who make a practice of yielding yourself to Me when things are bad, know particularly how to yield yourself to Me when things are good. I am Him who not only pulls you out, but lifts you up and exceedingly blesses.

For a better word and interpretation, you should learn how to practice His presence whether anybody else does it or not. Those who will be diligent are those who will receive.

## Fasting, Assimilation, Tongues, and Worship Release the Standing of Christ

Indeed your time shall be shortened, because the sower will overtake the reaper. Consistently, all these things I give you to realize grace do nothing but substantiate and release His standing within your life.

Indeed, the sower shall overtake the reaper because of adding the key of fasting, the key of assimilation, the key of praying in tongues, and the key of private worship. When these things are in place, all of them will operate equally with each other and cause you to rise up in the grace and standing that is in My Son.

When you open the door and let Me come, then I come and can do those things that are generally left undone. When I can move by My grace, marvelous and wonderful things take place. I reach in where My standing abides, and you will come out of it with My life.

Many of you should learn how to pause, practice My presence, and invite Me to come. In your lives you will leave with less and less that has not been done.

State your position. What does My Word say about your problem? Even more, why do you not enter into celebration with Me, a celebration that speaks of how thorough your victory is? That would be worship practice.

Did I not say, "All you who are heavy laden, come to Me and I will give you rest"? Therefore I have revealed the Father unto you. Did I not say, "To whom I would reveal"? Therefore take My rest and know that I am your comfort and I am your peace in times of trouble.

He who dares leave his place of natural security and go to My place of supernatural peace is the one who frees Me to move on his behalf by worshiping and glorifying Me, yes, even in the desert, especially in the desert.

When you yield yourself to Me in worship and establish yourself while in the desert, what will I do through you in the times of full inheritance?

Think on these things that are pure and of good report. Set your mind on things above, for I am the Lord God of your peace and your joy. You will find strength in My presence and in your joy. This kind of strength prevails because the enemy is unable to wear down a person who will not wear down.

## Worship in Weak Places Lets Him Become Strength

Establish yourself on My rock and find out where My presence comes in on that establishment. As you go further and further into this year, you will notice the deep-seated changes you have hoped for for a long time coming into manifestation.

Therefore, for what you cannot see, magnify and glorify Me, and I will give you what is Mine. When you glorify and praise and take your place in this thankful part of this generation, whatever place you glorify Me in is what I will become to you. You who fellowship with Me in the places where you are weak, those are the places I will become your strength.

Many of these things I have said, and I have laid out the path that you might follow it many times in the past. Now many of you are receiving ears that hear, and indeed you are about to enter another part of all this I have called you to do.

You who have these ears are living in anticipation. You can discern the change in the atmosphere of the things that are spirit. You can feel them on the way as you can feel the change in the weather.

So rejoice and be exceedingly glad. Be glad in Me and rejoice, for I am not only your answer but your strength along the way to your answer.

I heard reasoning going on: "How could I ever do anything?" And this term seems to mean something to you: "My pockets are empty." I heard it in the background, and His response was, "You tell them what I can do with My grace."

What I will do for you is run ahead with My grace, because I can do that through prayer. But this is what I expect from you: when My blessing begins to show up for you, I expect you to give yourself more to Me. I will operate in you and toward you with My grace. But with you operating in fellowship with Me, you will receive the change necessary before the enemy reclaims the ground, so that you can hold on to it.

Heed what I say to you. Do not talk that way anymore, but receive My grace, for I am faithful. This is a day of Me imparting and giving. This is a day that I am measuring your love and utilizing all that I can to give you things. I cannot love Myself in you for you. Therefore you give yourself to Me, and I will give Myself to you.

## Worship Positions You Before the Fight Moves Closer to Home

What the enemy uses against you who are strong is long battles that cause weariness, especially in the process of separation to emancipation, separation to the grace and strength of those places you are going to. Most will pull back and begin to do more of the things that avail not than the things that do.

Did I not give you keys in the spirit when you could not attain to levels of grace that spelled out your victory for the manifestation of that very moment? Did I not give you keys that would get you in? Why would you beat the air and do things that avail not because of long battles of weariness?

If you will fellowship with Me, I become the refreshment and your joy along the way. It is imperative that you take this key I have given you, this key wherein you worship and exalt Me high in that place I provided for you.

The enemy is planning this next season to take the war closer to home and to those things that mean much to you. Therefore the things you need are the things I have provided for you from this place called done. Exalt Me and lift Me up with this key of worship, that I might position you where I am against what is coming. I will insulate you.

The definite impression of "closer to home" would be you, people you really love, the family unit, and those kinds of things, the things that mean something to you.

So come on. When He tells us ahead of time, we get to know what the devil is planning before he gets here. He wants you to take that key. We know there is not a thing you can do to earn grace, but when you have done all you can trying to step into the circle where it is, He loves you so much that He came and provided keys that unlock the doors along the way that are trying to keep you out.

One of those keys is the private kind of worship that exalts Him high above everything. In that place He has given to you, no matter how bad it gets or how bad it looks, if you can continue to exalt Him high in that place, He can continue to give you His strength.

No matter how bad it gets or what it looks like, remember this: it is not over until it is over. I used to think, "Well, it is not over until God says it is." No, it is not over until the devil says it is. No, no, it is not over until you say it is.

## Entering Rest Is the Wise Way to Stand in What Is Finished

To enter into My rest and My peace is the way of a wise man, for he who seeks peace is he who can walk in My standing. Many weapons have I, but some of the greatest are those weapons that cause you to stand fast in what I have already finished and already done, and not be moved to the left or the right, but single-mindedly and with purpose stand in My presence and magnify and glorify Me for these things I have given you.

The joy of the Lord is your strength. In the mouths of these newborn babes is My praise even able to be perfected.

Therefore come unto Me, you who are heavy laden, and I will use all that I have given you to give you rest. When you worship, you leave the limitations of where you are walking and come up and stand with Me as the God of the possession.

Surrender to Me, and I will give Myself to you. You will see that My peace and My strength have always been yours.

He took me back to another prophecy where a group of people were standing and looking into a place in God that nobody had walked in for generations, probably centuries. He was so delighted that the people were close enough to look in. They were not there, but it was a people standing there looking in.

I had the distinct feeling that nobody had even looked into that place for some time, and God was having a celebration just like you were a few minutes ago. His pleasure was rolling over me in waves because people were looking in there once again.

I felt His pleasure in a way I had not felt since, not to that degree. I am accurate, because I am describing the same things as when I finished getting the blame off God, when I was talking about the blind baby. If I prayed for him, is it God's will? Could He heal him and did not, or did He not because He could not?

When I realized the only reason He does not heal a blind baby is because He could not, in other words, He did not find enough present to do it with, I went through that war. I can remember a long time before my heart quit accusing Him. I made my mouth quit, but that did not help how my heart felt, thinking somewhere reserved inside, "He can do it if He wanted. I mean, He is God."

When I finished and my heart did not accuse Him, I felt His pleasure so much that He started talking to me. He said, "Like Job's comforters, many have spoken things of Me that are not true."

That is the same kind of waves of pleasure I felt when these people were looking into this place that obviously nobody had walked in for several generations.

## The Fellowship Ahead Exceeds Former Hopes and Past Experiences

The second half goes something like this: your fellowship with Me and your union is going to exceed anything you have seen in your past or hoped for in your future. It is at this point that the enemy diverts off on the side trails, but you have camped on the vision and have decided that you will not go the way of the flesh; you will go My way.

For most, the deciding factor was what they did with money. For the times of fellowship and the times of joy that lie ahead, My joy, which becomes your strength, you will see it is in the inner court. It is in the fellowship you will find with Me and the discoveries you will make concerning My love.

I am moved by compassion, and I do look for a people who can move with Me in this compassion. I will do things you have not seen before. So remain strong and continue to pray the prayers that change you.

There is a place of mortification waiting for you that releases the kind of love I have been exhorting.

When those people were looking into that place, it seemed to please Him so much. This time, in the second half of that prophecy being added, He was speaking differently. He was prophesying about us enjoying the fellowship with Him in what is over there.

He was expressing His joy over the maturing, line upon line, of a generation coming to a place where they will be able to receive the love He is pouring out, and He will be able to receive the love they become capable of. He is really looking forward to a generation of that kind of fellowship.

## The Great Divide Closes Through Mysteries, Understanding, and Love

For the great divide that the Holy Spirit is closing, as you give yourself over to My mysteries, you are also giving yourself over to My understandings. When you understand the operation of the kingdom, that is a safe place.

As many of you journey into Me, the things you will find out more about are My strength. There awaits for you a fellowship with Me that will translate out into powerful abiding faith. Faith for My love is a force within itself.

Many have looked for answers in many places. Many have walked in seasons and different levels of torments and fears. Men have searched Me and searched My Word diligently for answers.

Know this, little children: he who enters into fellowship with Me, he who receives My fellowship through the nature I have given to you, and you who spend time with Me becoming what I am, I am not only in fellowship but in strength.

Many of you desired to take the shorter route. You said, "I want everything You have now," but you have not equipped yourself for the shorter route. Know you that you must understand My Word, and you must enter into a relationship to know Me. My Word has always pointed toward a relationship with Me.

Therefore, if you will take heed and listen to what I am about to say, you can shorten your time because your testings and trials will be shortened by My intervention with victories.

Hear what the Spirit would say: he who partakes of My joy finds out that My joy is truly his strength. It will sustain you through anything the enemy wants to throw at you. Have I not given My strength to you? Is not My Word yours? Is not My standing yours? Then My victory is yours also.

## Practicing Joy in Low Places Is a Strong Manifestation of Faith

He who will practice My joy on purpose is he who will separate himself and worship Me through the lowest of the low parts, that he might celebrate Me through the highest of the high parts. I was there in both seasons. I am your strength, and I love you the same. Your relationship with Me is the same when you could not be any lower as it is when you could not be any higher.

Therefore why do you rejoice more when you are higher than when you are lower? Your relationship to Me is the same, so you might as well celebrate all the time, for you will shorten the manifestation of your victory.

Where is the fight of faith in this? Does not faith come by hearing, and hearing by the Word of God? Yes, and I would say to you that faith comes by hearing who you are and what is yours in Me.

Hear what the Spirit would say: are you not exercising faith when your senses, circumstances, and body say you cannot go on, yet you lift your hands and worship Me and say, "Because of Him I can go on"? That is the strongest manifestation of faith. When you worship Me, your whole spirit, soul, and body come into agreement with what I have done. My joy is your strength, and there is only one thing left: your victory.

For it has been some time, saith the Lord, that I have been preparing this foundation. You are nearing the time when I will be able to pour My Spirit out upon this city.

## A City Outpouring Will Require Prepared Ministers and Teachers

Hear this: have I not prepared you for that outpouring? Many of you will be called on by Me to move forward, for there will be more ministry than a large crowd can handle when I pour My Spirit out.

So be strong. Be prepared. Walk softly in My presence as one who wears the armor of My light. I am preparing you for a time that is short, a time when I will unveil My hand in a mighty way.

Therefore be prepared to step forward, be prepared to be used, be prepared to nurture, and be prepared to teach. Oh, there will be much teaching required. I will need those who expound on the mysteries of My milk, those who expound correction for those who walk wrong paths, those who teach who they are in Christ, and those I will enlist to transfer what they have received.

I just saw Him transfer anointings, for those who will not only have received but will transfer what they have received. Many, many will leave this city armed in a short time with what they received from My outpouring.

I desire to do a great thing in this city. I have desired it for many years. I have had My servants come here, and they would bring revival to a certain point. But hear this: all this has worked together for the good. I have had servants in this city whose heart was after Me. All this has worked together for the good, for I am getting ready for this great outpouring, and it will take all that you know collectively to sustain what I am doing, saith the Lord.

For you who embrace what you have heard, this next year coming will be a time of fellowship with Me like you have never experienced. Many of you have given yourselves enough to Me to get close enough that I can pull you into what is about to come.

Know this: there will be times of worship. There will be times when we are not able to stop what is going on, and it will go night after night after night.

Therefore get strong. Come to know Me in the Spirit as your strength, for the joy that I have for you is your strength. You will acclaim that time as supernatural times that extend themselves into the supernatural, and I will be there with you in the harvest, saith the Lord. I have waited some time for this to come to pass.

## Worship Shortens Trials by Choosing the Ground of Battle

You will shorten the duration of testings and trials. The enemy will measure them out according to your strength, and he will come after you with many persecutions, afflictions, and cares. But when you exalt Me in the place I hold in the Father and in your life, you are choosing the terms and the grounds you will fight the enemy on. You are choosing the time spans.

There is no one who has glorified Me in the midst of impossibility who did not shorten his stay in the place he was in. You can take the long route or the short route, but I would say to you: step aside and get to know Me. You will be blessed.

I will remind you that in the days to come there will be waves of My glory sweeping through and spontaneous worship that will last for hours. Many of you pioneered, struggling past the flesh, inviting Me in and giving Me something to work with. But when My glory is resident here, then My power will be here. When My power is here, I will visit you with waves of My glory and presence.

Many of you will find yourselves caught up for hours and hours. In doing so, I will be able to escalate the next steps you are taking toward personal victory, the victory of your lost loved ones, and the purpose I have placed you in Tulsa for.

You who are pioneering, I have been looking for an avenue to come. It is My desire to pour out the fullness of My glory, but in many places where they worshiped, one of the things that was lacked was the foundation I need to sustain it after it has been poured out.

Hear and listen. You believe you have waited long. I have waited longer, saith the Lord. Both of our times are coming near. Be faithful to that which opposes the flesh. Place My keys in the doors and open them, for it is necessary that My grace operate in these things, pull them down, and replace them with My standing and My grace.

## Giftings and Freedom Will Spread Over the City

It is My desire to disperse a variety of giftings. It is My desire for a disbursement of the mighty workings of miracles, gifts of healings, and especially the discerning of spirits and casting out devils. It is My plan to overspread this town with My power.

So open yourselves up, for indeed a wave will wash over you that you have not experienced before. What is Mine is yours. It is time for My hand to be opened and spread over this city.

Rejoice and be exceedingly glad, for this is what you have called upon Me for. Many who came late will not be late in receiving My giftings. Many who came early will have such a foundation under them that they will be able to guide those who suddenly come into My power by sheer grace.

All the people who will be saved in this day will be a joy for you to count. Follow Me closely, and your path will lead you to fullness of days, power, and great reward at the end of your days.

So strengthen yourself inside. Pick yourself up and be strong, for what I have for you and what I will give to you is stronger than you are. It is stronger than your enemy. You can lean on Me in times of testing and lean on Me in the good times. You will see in this day that you will have great joy as you bask in My presence, saith the Lord.

The way this is going to show up, saith the Lord, is in your freedom. Some of you did not have freedom. You came in and battled strongholds, and your emotions were down. You fought and fought, and it seemed to be to no avail. You will see. You will have a new freedom when you present yourself to Me, saith the Lord.

Many of you thirst, and sometimes you do not know what that thirst is for. That thirst is quenched in My presence. What your heart is looking for is what no man can give you. No matter how much time you spend with men, they cannot replace the time you spend with Me, saith the Lord.

Many of you came with an anticipation that I put in you. In this anticipation you knew that this week you would find answers, and that is correct. Many of you desiring to take another step have come hungry, and indeed you will take this other step. You who are thirsty will drink from My well.

## Surrender Opens the Deep Places Where Permanent Change Comes

This is My presence. This is that manifestation of My power and My grace. Many times I use this to prepare the ground for what I am about to do. In those times when you are surrendered to Me and your heart has been opened, I can go to those deepest places where all permanent change comes from. My victory is yours.

Those who know how to yield themselves go from where they are, out of their works, into where I am, in a flow of My grace.

Many of you desire the kind of works that are worked by My power as a manifestation of My grace. Oh, My intention before this week is over is to feed many of you in the place that you thirst for.

You who desire to know My love in ever-increasing proportions, know that as far as My grace is concerned, no one can stand up and say, "Well, I have fellowship with Him; I do not really have to have it. It is all by grace."

You must spend time in fellowship with Me, saith the Lord, to get to know Me. You who spend that time, I will not lead you astray. I will move upon you in those times that you need to establish yourself in tongues and establish yourself in worship. In the tongues and in the worship, every time you come to a dangerous intersection, you who have ears to hear, hear: I will move upon you to strengthen yourself in My foundation.

If you will turn the Holy Spirit loose in you, He will build you up above it until you have the benefit of both worlds: a solid, solid foundation with My glory raining upon it, saith the Lord.

Rejoice always. Make My joy a permanent part of your life. When the joy leaves, rejoice and it will come back. These are the things that cause Me to come suddenly. When you think it is past due and nothing works, if you will not stop, suddenly, suddenly, all that I do by My Spirit, every place you occupy with Me, saith the Lord, will come to a higher place.

## The Spoken Word, Written Word, and Worship Will Come Alive Together

In these generations, you have only heard the beginnings of what I have in worship. You have only heard the beginnings of what I have in revelation to be added to the platform toward the completion of My return, saith the Lord. You have only seen the beginnings of the omnipotence and magnificence of My power.

I am all-powerful. Give Me an avenue, and I will manifest Myself, saith the Lord. I am the path. I am the way, the truth, and the life. No man comes to the Father but by Me.

I have set the way, and it is engraved with My truth, and it leads you to My life. I am the first to attain. Know that I have all things with Me pertaining to life. I will put you on the way to miracles, truth, and life. It shall be a great time.

For you who throw yourself into all that I am, know that I have the joy that surpasses all understanding waiting for all of you who will go into the dark places. My joy will be your strength, and you will have no torment and no fear because I am with you.

Sometimes I have waited centuries to speak things that ears had not heard. Know this: as this generation comes to an end and the new one begins, I am able to say things I have not been able to say in this generation. Therefore what I say will precede what I will do, for I will do things that have not been done in many generations.

Surely many of you who sit in this place will see My hand uncovered as no one alive has seen before. Therefore rejoice and know that you are part of a plan I have been working on for a long time.

You who have ears to hear, continue to hear, for I am working My plan in this place, and all things are indeed becoming possible to them who believe. My spoken Word and My written Word will come alive. My worship, for those who communicate in Me and to Me and worship, will come to a place that not many have seen.

Be strong, for the things you hear and understand will cause the slow to go slower and the fast to go faster. The incredible things I will say and do will cause traditions and religion to come to a far division, where they will also see the truth and hear and know that a great harvest of the earth is at hand.

I need those who will come to a place to speak at My command the truth I will give you, that I might precede you and work in this earth according to My will, saith the Lord.

It pleases Me that you are hearing truth, and truth is coming to such a place in your life that you will speak the truth. It is by that that I will do the mighty things I will do in this earth, says the Lord.

For there is the place of sustaining joy, power, and peace.

## Eternity Gives Him Time for the Small Moments of Fellowship

Hear what the Spirit of the Lord would say: have I not told you from the beginning that My desire is for fellowship with you, that I am the Lord your God, your Father?

I was wondering what that middle part was about because it did not get very clear. I was riding along with Tim, and he was talking about something that was orchestrated. It was just little insignificant details, but God orchestrated them in such a way that you knew His hand was in them.

So I said, "Tim, I will tell you what I found out in this joy. I found that He is just the opposite of how religion has made Him."

We think because He is eternity, all-powerful, omnipotent, mighty, sovereign, off making worlds somewhere, He does not have time for you. Let me tell you why He does: because eternity is His servant.

Do you know what that means? It means He savors those little incidental moments, those insignificant moments with you, because eternity is His servant. He loves those minutes you thought He did not have time for. What He did may seem so insignificant to you, but because eternity is His servant, He has all the time, not in this world, but all the time in time eternal, to enjoy that moment you thought He did not have time for.

You think, "Well, He would not take time to come here and do something small like that." But eternity is His servant. He would really like to spend that insignificant moment with you, if you do not mind.

That is the middle of the prophecy. It was about this fellowship with you, and I was wondering why it trailed off. He wanted me to use an example that He already spoke.

## Gratitude Turns Into Worship, Faith, and Answered Prayer

Your fellowship with Me will become rich when you are not affected by the things of this world, by the circumstances and trials that come your way. Not as though they do not exist; they do. But your standing and victory with Me speak much louder than the circumstances with them.

Therefore come over to My side and make your emotions servants of Me, and you and I will spend many joyful times together, saith the Lord.

Yield yourself to Me. Yield yourself to Me, and you will see. You cannot speak My Word and yield yourself to Me without gratitude, and gratitude will turn into flurries of worship. Worship will turn into statements of faith, and in this fellowship with Me, you have what you ask.

Therefore receive now, for what you do and what I do many times are two different things. But when a person learns to follow Me, then you will always do what you see Me do, saith the Lord.

There are many, many things I would have been able to do for many of you had you not made the wrong turn because the enticement of the natural path looked more real than the one I was laying out before you by My Spirit.

What I say to one, I say to all. He who will stay in close fellowship with Me, who will take that time to set himself aside, work My path by the power of the Holy Spirit, and celebrate and rejoice with Me along the way like he has already received it, saith the Lord, will not only be glad and full of joy, but it pleases Me to see My children obey Me and follow after Me, saith the Lord.

In an atmosphere such as this, when My Spirit is here in the freedom you feel, how can I not move in the diversities of what I am? How can I not move in My power, My healing, and My grace? How can I not?

Therefore continually remain in an atmosphere of gratitude, grace, and worship. Stay in unbroken communion with Me as you go about your day, and you will realize an immediate increase, saith the Lord.

## Faith Maps the Path to Victory With the Word and Thanksgiving

Your faith is the substance of the thing you are looking for. It is all the evidence you will ever need of the thing you cannot see.

He who will not quit knows this: the path I direct is always a path to victory. In order to be on a path of victory, he must always set his sight on the faith that is the substance of the thing hoped for.

Therefore map your path well, laced with My Word, rejoicing, worshiping, and giving thanks for all things to God and the Father in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Be bold and speak My Word, for at the end of the path you will have found that its truth and victory will be the results once again.

To make My joy your joy is to make My victory your victory. My victory has already been won for you. So make what is yours yours by making My victory yours, by making My joy yours.

Do you think I have any down days? I have no down days. You might as well walk with Me, saith the Lord, for I am the Lord your God who strengthens you.

Know you not that this thing has to fall, and before My presence it has to move out of the way? Walk with Me. Walk with Me, and you will see.

Take no detours. Let no testings and trials dictate to you how your relationship will turn out with Me. Put yourself aside and seek Me, and I will give you your joy for this year to come, saith the Lord.

## Strength Comes by Separating Yourself to the Answer

I nurture a people to become a separate people. In generations and centuries past, not many were nurtured to become a separate people unto Me. But that does not mean this place of grace does not exist. It only means that those who fell to weaknesses and temptations, I was there for them with what I am here for you with.

So be strong. Come past. Receive My strength. Go beyond what those who have fallen at this place have gone beyond, and come where I am and receive My strength.

The more you receive, the more you know I am your strength. The weaker you remain, the more you know I am not your answer. The more strength you receive, the more you will know I am your answer.

So come to the answer. Come where I am. Separate yourself to Me and glorify Me for the answer to your problem. Do not glorify the problem with your mouth that stands against Me. Glorify the answer with your mouth.

Take the two-edged sword and make it your weapon, saith the Lord, and drive and cut to pieces that which opposes. I am the Lord your God who strengthens you.

Lift Me up and begin to worship, glorify, and thank Me, for I have done this for you. Receive what is Mine and make it yours.

## Money Becomes a Servant When Fellowship Is Rich

What do you think of those whom I have made wealthy? For those whom I have made wealthy, what is theirs is Mine. I have need but to ask.

Know you not that those who discovered these things, these principles, are those whom circumstances nor conditions could ever stop? It was not about the money, but about their fellowship with Me along the way.

Those who break free from the bonds and control of money are those who fellowshiped with Me and changed along the way. The money is nothing to Me. Nothing. But the fellowship is everything, saith the Lord.

I said that to say this: do not try to get the results ahead of the change you are receiving in the spirit. If you will hold with My fellowship and hold with My edification, the change is inevitable. There is no one who has ever prayed out of My mind who did not receive the benefits of it.

People determine their success by statistics. I determine your success by your willingness to change. Looking at sums of money that come in does not change you, but I do. You will serve the sums if you walk without Me, but if you walk with Me, I will make every sum your servant as you serve Me.

Know you not that when you give something to Me, I make it My own? Even if you are not qualifying yourself to receive the answer you have asked Me for, know you not that I do not cast your request aside immediately? I go into operation to change those things for you, that you might receive what you are asking for.

What was the purpose for which I sent My Son? What is the purpose that you stand in My presence, saith the Lord, born again? Is it not that I so loved you that I gave My only begotten? Why would I not, with the greatest interest and the greatest love of My heart, take that which you are giving Me in the Name? Why would I not answer your prayer?

When you see Me, saith the Lord, look at Me through the eyes of faith and know that I desire to do these things more than you desire for them to be done.

Therefore stand up bold. Acquaint yourself with Me and acquaint yourself with My ways, and your life will change measurably before the next quarter of this next year.

When you dismiss yourself from this part of what I am doing, walk away rejoicing and celebrating within your spirit. Maintain this celebration. Never be far from Me. Rejoice and celebrate with Me, and you and I will walk in victory together.

## The Closing Desire Is Fellowship With Those Who Go On to Know Him

I have said it once, and I will say it again; later I will say it again, and after that I will say it again: it is My fellowship with you that I am after.

Oh, I will provide the wisdom to make you wealthy along the way, as long as your fellowship with Me is rich. I will say it again: it is My fellowship with you I desire.

I am He who sits on the perimeters and the circumferences of everything that is. I am victory, and I have no limits. My desire with you is an all-inclusive fellowship. I can fellowship with you at any moment of any time throughout all eternity, as I can fellowship the same moment with all My creation.

Therefore fellowship with Me, and I will give those things you need to make your life complete.

Come unto Me, you who are heavy laden, and take My yoke upon you to learn of Me, and you will find rest for your souls. What I do for one, I will do for all.

The whole time you receive those things that make you know I love you, for I know what a child is and I know what makes a child happy. I know how to nourish a baby. I know how to discipline a teenager, saith the Lord. I know all these things and what will motivate.

It is My pleasure to give you those things that make you happy. But know this: I am brought much pleasure when you hand the Gentiles to Me for an inheritance. Therefore I delight in shedding abundantly good things upon you.

My desire is for you to present yourself to Me against the days that redemption will be done, and we will all gather together in the eternities of times with great reward, and rejoice in your lives that you surrendered to Me in these days, saith the Lord.

For I have a schedule in this place, and mighty things shall be done by and through those who go on to know Me, saith the Lord.
