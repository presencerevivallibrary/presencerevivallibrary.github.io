# Private Worship Vol IV - 10

## A Prophetic Word Announces a Definite Shift in the Spirit

Yes. I am pleased. I am pleased with the progress that you have been making in My presence, which is welcome here.

Many things have come against you, many that you could not see, though you could feel the consequences of them. But do you not know that there is a time coming when the change will be noticed by everyone, and I will be involved in that?

So be strong, because a definite, different shift is beginning to take place that you will know as a new place in the Spirit. It will be for many people, and you will have reason to be excited, because it will be an exciting time on this road to our victory, saith the Lord.

## The New Birth Carries God's Own Love Into the Human Spirit

When He revealed a couple of things to me, like, "Where is our glorified body? Where is it now?" and, "If we have the love of God, how did we get it when we were born again?" I had to look at that birth.

Somebody lays hands on you, prays, and you are born again. Where did His love come from in that birth?

Nicodemus wanted to know, "What must I do to be born again?" He was a teacher of the Jews. I like the way Jesus ended up answering him. He said:

> The wind bloweth where it listeth, and thou hearest the sound thereof, but canst not tell whence it cometh, and whither it goeth: so is every one that is born of the Spirit.

I can just see Nicodemus, the teacher in Israel: "The wind bloweth where it listeth. So is everyone that is born again." Now we know everything we need to know about being born again.

But Jesus did not teach without parables. That goes back to Adam. When Adam was just lying there, his body formed and ready to be brought to life, do you think God leaned over and breathed ordinary air into him? No. God is not dependent on one of His own creations, which is air. Believe me, He does not breathe. He created the stuff.

What He did was impart spiritual life into Adam. Then Adam began to breathe the breath of life.

That is what Jesus was referring to. The wind blows where it wants. You can hear the sound of it, but you do not know where it comes from or where it goes. So is everyone born of the Spirit. That simply means you can be at the North Pole, and someone else can be at the South Pole, and both of you can bow your knees and pray, and both of you will have that life breathed into you from those two different places, because there is nowhere that wind is not.

So I was wondering about that birth, the DNA of the spirit man. Where did the love come from?

God wanted to give me a kind of love in me that He had, but still, it is not only His; it is mine. I am affected by it. I am controlled by it in certain actions. I can still do some things wrong and then do some things right, but it is still His love.

You would think if I received His love, just an ounce of it would slay me on the ground and I would do everything right from then on. But all I had to know was that it was mine, though it came from Him, because I am His son. I can say my son has my love. It came from me, but it is still not mine; it is his.

The important part of fellowship is this: when you love Him, you love Him with that. That means something to Him, because it is coming from a love you can walk in if you want to.

But I know some pretty mean believers. It looks like their love is sealed up in a keeping box somewhere.

## The Costliest Thing Heaven Sustained Was Our Right to Choose

How many times have we taught that the most costly thing heaven ever sustained was our right to make choices?

When I would argue religion with other religions, they would say, "Why does God not just forgive them? Why did He not just forgive them?"

Because He cannot lie. The reason you are guaranteed answered prayer is that He cannot lie. It is not that He does not want to lie; He cannot. He said, "I am not a man, that I should lie."

So He had to go through everything. He had to prophesy four thousand years into the future, to Jesus' death, when He would bruise Satan's head. He went that far just to die in our place, so He could go on with His plan and charge everything to the cross, to be paid in full at Jesus' resurrection, because He cannot lie.

We go before Him, and He loved us that much to go through all that trouble. That must have been monotonous with the ifs and the buts:

"No, you cannot eat that food. Yes, you can eat that. No, that is wrong. Do not go behind there. Do not touch the ark. No, you cannot do that today. Not today."

All those laws had to be followed so your death could be laid on Him in the end, and you could become His family, a son and a daughter through all of that.

Why? So you do not have to think, after He paid all that price for you through all that time, that when you walk out and ask Him something in Jesus' name, you have to doubt whether He will answer your prayer. After all the hell the devil tried to put Him through getting up to here, He is not going to hear you? Give me two breaks.

He gives us the most precious, powerful thing He has to use: His Word. He cannot lie.

I had to know this because heaven sustained the most costly thing when we had a right to make a choice. Look what He had to pay in return. Heaven sustained all of that just so we could have fellowship with Him.

So I had to know about the love I have. Is it as if He turned a faucet on Himself and filled me up a little at the gas station? "Of course I love God. I just took it out of His pump."

I had to know whether I am giving back to Him something that was His and that He gave to me. He did give me His love, but it came through the spiritual creation I received. If I want to, I can hold it down where it hardly ever manifests. I can even be mean to you if I want to.

Or it can be developed. It can be developed to the nth degree. Or it can be left dormant, where you snap back at somebody so fast, get mad, and get irritated.

So it is ours, but it is made by Him. If I give that back to Him, then He really knows I love Him.

That was important to me because He has so many Scriptures in the Word that deal with this subject. In the beginning of ministry, I did not think much about it. I knew there were some people who talked about love too much. Now I am one of them.

## The More Excellent Way Is Pursuing Love With the Tenacity Used for Power

In all the places in the Word where He counted the highest and greatest kind of success, it was always those who took heed to what He said about love. No matter what it took, they began to walk in it, mature in it, and grow up.

I have been so happy around here that Allen and some of them took the hard verses on love. Love never fails. Perfect love casts out all fear. All fear. It never fails.

Then he took on the master of them all:

> Though I speak with tongues of men and of angels, and have not charity, I am become as sounding brass, or a tinkling cymbal.
> Though I have all faith, so that I could remove mountains, and have not charity, I am nothing.

Well, the mountain still moved. Yes, it did. Obviously there are giftings. When Paul said, "Though I speak with tongues of men and angels and have not love," he said speak. You can be full of the Holy Ghost and still say, "I can speak in tongues, but if you cross that line again, I will dock you."

So yes, that can be carried quite a ways in ministry. If He did not make room for imperfection, He would have had a very small harvest. There are people not filled with the Holy Ghost who do not know three pages in the Bible, but they get people born again.

When I first studied those two chapters, I saw it. "Desire earnestly the best gifts." Then he says, "Yet I show unto you a more excellent way." A more excellent way to do what? To desire earnestly the best gifts.

Then the chapter changes and says, "Though I speak with tongues of men and of angels..." What is the better way? The better way is to go after the love of God with the same tenacity you would use to go after power, money, or anything else. That is the better way. It will not only give you a life you never dreamed of; it will give all that other stuff to you too.

Hallelujah. Hallelujah, Jesus.

## Paul Learned to Take Pleasure in God's Presence During Suffering

Would you not know that if there is a way to develop that love, and it carries a wallop like that, the devil would fight it?

What do you think happened to Paul that made him able to survive five times thirty-nine lashes? Four times, all the bones in his feet were broken because they put him in stocks where they could hit the bottoms of his feet.

"I will teach you to spread this stuff, Paul."

He was stoned. The purpose of stoning was to kill him. He was not left alive.

I like it when he was chained up in Rome's sewer. Can you imagine being chained in the sewer? But what God said was true. He said, "My grace is sufficient."

The thing Paul found out to do was this. He asked, "Will You get rid of this thing?" God said, "The grace I gave you is sufficient. It will get rid of it. It will do anything. You just have to believe."

Paul finally resigned himself to what to do. He said, "I learned how to take pleasure in necessities, perplexities, and distresses."

Pleasure? Not in the necessities and distresses. You would have to be a sadist to like people sticking rods in you, hitting you, and tying you up in manure and things like that. You would have to be a sadist.

What he could do was take pleasure in the presence of God. All that other stuff is temporal. They are lying. They are liars. They can torment. They can do all they want. God is still who He is, and He is my answer. He said, "My grace is sufficient."

That grace had to switch Paul out of the natural and onto the Spirit. Paul was not martyred before his time. He said, "Are you taking me today? Yes. I have fought a good fight. I have kept the faith. I have finished my race. I am ready to go, but not before I was ready."

Somebody says, "I am kind of afraid to go over to a foreign land."

Then get unafraid.

What did Paul do in that? He learned that no matter the distress, how bad it was, or how bad it was getting, he could take pleasure in fellowship, in the presence of God, undoubtedly in worship, praise, magnifying, and glory.

I can see him riding along, worshiping God and praising Him. Too bad he did not have a donkey like that prophet, who ran him into a wall and made him so mad that he started beating the donkey. This was a very sane prophet. He had it together. Suddenly the donkey turned around and said, "Excuse me. May I ask you a question? Why are you beating me?"

Did he not know he was crazy by then? "Excuse me. Why are you beating me?"

You will have to hear the rest of the story.

## A Children's Hospital Exposes the Difference Between Ordinary Church and Revival

I was questioning today about St. Jude Children's Hospital. He said, "Do you not think that if something like that had happened in the last hundred years or so, you possibly would have heard something about the effect it would have on the town?"

I said, "Yes."

Those kinds of revivals do not basically exist too much. I am not talking about God suddenly saying, "Excuse me. I want to tell you something nobody knows." We are talking about times of praying.

Any key He ever told me, you can ask Gary, I used. I wore him out using the fasting key that time. He said, "Oh no, it is Thanksgiving, eating day, and he is preaching on fasting." You have to get tickled sometimes.

Do not be surprised if I can hardly get off this love, because I know that taking a revival into account, especially like the one we are believing for, you just do one St. Jude and every parent in the entire city will know. The mayor will know. The governor will know. In fact, the governor of the state would probably know before that night.

Now we are talking about disturbing something the size of a little state-sized nation.

I asked Him, "How come we do not see and do not hear of movements like that?"

The answer was pretty plain. He shook me a little bit, and I could feel it. He said, "Would you not know that perfect love casts out all fear? And not only does it do that, it never fails."

I said, "You mean what I am looking for is in this?"

"Yes. That is why the devil took such a stance."

You want a revival like that? He always would. But I can understand why they have not been attained, except at different spotted times you can dig up in history. Then again, the devil beats everybody's brains out until he puts them back into a dark age.

Well, I think we are going to bring Him back. I think our generation is going to bring Him back. I do not know. That is okay if He does. If He does not, I will just stick my tongue out at the devil for another fifty years. Well, maybe thirty. Okay, twenty-five.

## Private Worship First Opened the Anointing That Heals the Heart

This is what changed with this meeting: a tangible presence came and ministered to my heart, which most definitely informed me a little bit down the road, and also gave a little instruction and direction.

Right now, I feel His grace beginning to come real strong. Because it is, I know I can give you a little bit more of the rest, but I will have to pull up. That is okay. I have already taught you into the ground two or three times over love.

This is a step I have been taking that I basically did not know anything about either.

In these meetings, I kept explaining different things to them, and they wanted to know more and more about worship. In teaching them about worship, I somehow lined myself up, and He dropped a revelation on me that I had never seen. I said, "Oh my God, this is true, is it not?"

When I taught you about private worship, it all began with me because of that little trip to Oklahoma City to the Passion Play. I said, "How do You get presence in here?" It was a beautiful play, but there was no presence of God.

Then He told me what to do. Me being Pentecostal Holiness, ten years removed, we could send a woman to hell over an earring. It was the devil's stirrup. We were severe in those days. You could not wear Levi's or britches. Are you kidding? Not in our church.

But He said, "This has to do with presence. That is what you asked, is it not?"

I said, "Yes."

He said, "This has to do with presence. Go home. Yes, you can start this way: worship Me all night."

Did I? I was busy. It was too much.

But a year later I was standing right there and fell out under the power. Nobody got there. Nobody did anything. I was just there. Some people were in the line, and I think somebody was there praying, but I just fell. When I opened my eyes, He said, "Now are you going to begin that time in private worship?"

I said, "Yes, sir."

He gave me a kind to do. He said, "Go and do that." So I went and did it.

It was not long, maybe three months, before an anointing started that I had not seen anywhere. I think anybody could have it.

At the camp meeting that year, our yearly one, people were leaving and saying to others, "You had better go down there. Something has happened that has not happened before."

Those of you who went through that with me remember: I could not get up and just start talking without people being out of control, bawling and coming up to the front. Things were turning loose, and they had no choice when everything in them was giving in.

I saw that anointing and thought, "That is really something." Then I went to another meeting out of town, and it was the same thing. Then another meeting where they did not speak our language, but after the interpreter talked, the same atmosphere and presence was there. They wept and bawled, lost people got saved, and anybody who wanted the Holy Ghost could not resist it.

That lasted for a period of time. I am not quite sure how it started slowing down. Yes, I do remember. The devil launched a big war that almost killed me. I mean my heart. I do not think I can ever remember being hurt worse than in that era. But I am not now.

## Corporate Worship and Private Worship Both Lead Into the Same Grace

When He linked that worship with St. Jude and with the history of Tulsa, we could not remember too much ever happening like that. Yet it is possible. It is possible.

Then He brought me back and started teaching me again. This is what happened in the next meeting: all of a sudden, He came and opened a whole other light on worship, corporate worship and you-by-yourself worship. He opened a whole brand-new light to me and started telling me why this is this and this is this.

What it boiled down to, among more that I will have to stop with, is that He arranged it. Preacher, teacher, apostle, prophet, helps, governments; I do not care what it is. He fixed it for what that kind of seeking God will accomplish. He fixed it so you can have as much of Him as you want.

And it does not depend on anybody else.

Do you know how hard the devil fought me making it this far, getting to see this and getting to touch that love on that level?

We were at Gatlinburg at the camp meeting. A woman had been watching that anointing in operation, with people weeping all over the place and losing it. She had a friend they prayed for, and they had been really hurt and hurt toward each other. He brought her over when the love of God was there like that for that season. He said, "I think you need to help them."

I stepped up and just raised my hand. I said, "Father God," and they both broke down and wept. I hardly said anything. They hugged each other.

I found out there are two roads in worship. Two. Next time I will get to the one you can have. It is your choice. You can have it.

I found out it is something you can do on purpose. When this revelation dropped down on me there, I had never felt something quite like that. It made me know we are a stone closer.

## The Principality Over the Region Reacted to the Threat of Revival

Finally, I went to the last Benny Hinn meeting in the round building. I called it the round one because that is how I saw it from a vision.

When he got done, people were fellowshipping, and I was heading for the door doing good. I looked up. I do not have visions like that. I have only had maybe two like that.

I saw a big figure standing over there, six or seven feet tall: the principality of the air that guards this whole area. We were threatening revival. It is his job, when we have a hot spot, to call the troops in and put a stop to it.

When I saw it, the ceiling fanned out with a bunch of devils, and they were all chanting the same thing: "Something is wrong here. Something is wrong here."

And a whole lot of people left saying, "Something is wrong here."

So I saw a vision like that. This was the same kind of vision. The crowd was there, and all of a sudden it made a platform for them. That was all I could see, just them. They were standing around watching everything.

Then He said, "This anointing will heal. This anointing will heal the guy who stays home."

It took me a minute to understand. Here I am at home:

"Sweet husband of mine, would you bring me a piece of pizza?"

"Sure."

"Thank you. Would you bring me another?"

"Yes, sure."

"Does it have to be so small?"

"I will teach you a lesson. You will think about small."

Then I go to church.

The person who comes to church is usually not the same person who is at home. At church: "Hi. Yeah, great church."

This is what He said: the healing gift that will develop from that love and that art will be very powerful. It will heal the front person, the phony person that the person who stays home sends to church. He will get healed.

A lot of people are scarred by this and scarred by that. Sometimes your soul establishes a root system from the way you were treated. You get born again and do not know enough to get past it. But gifts of healings are plural. That means more than your body. It means He can reach in and trade off your weakness for His grace. He can heal that as quickly as He can heal a hurt arm.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

## Grace Can Trade Soul Wounds for God's Standing

When I saw those two different things and what He told me about revival at that point, I wanted to tell you so badly, but I knew I could not get it all into one service.

A lot of people are really hurt. They are saved, but they are still hurt. I know what is coming.

It was just after that time that I used to have those grace lines, where He would trade something that weakened you and had given you trouble for His standing, His unmerited, unearnable grace, and heal you in that area.

How many remember those lines? How many received something from one of those lines?

Take these things to heart. Times are changing. Seasons are changing. Know that many things are going to come. These things are going to come whether many people do what they are supposed to do or not. So hear what the Lord would say and move forward, and you will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready.

Keep in mind, Paul could take great pleasure in the presence of God and worship, whether he was sunk out in the ocean, getting a beating, imprisoned in a sewer, or having his feet broken. It must be a powerful key. It switches you over to grace. It activates what He has done about it.

I like it when Peter and them got thrown in jail, and God came and opened it back up and said, "Come out of there. Get out of there. You have things to do."

The jailer was going to kill himself. "You do not have to do that. Go ahead." It started a little revival. The devil made a mistake, did he not?

Hallelujah, Jesus.

If you desire prayer, and you have something that causes you to get depressed, or if you carry things from your childhood that are rooted in the soul as a stronghold, come.

A stronghold is a system of thought empowered by emotion. Praying in tongues will eventually get you past them, because He will open your eyes and let you see them, how ugly they are, and it causes your faith to let them go and turn them loose.

My background was not real good. Not the same.

So if you do have something, you do not even have to spell it out or say what it is if you do not want to. I am not the one who brought this up to myself in the beginning, that He was ready with His grace to trade off for something you have this week.

Hallelujah.

If you desire, come up and put your toes on the green line.

I know one thing about myself and that grace. I told you something about the future. I am not staying the same. It broke my heart when He ran over St. Jude with me.

"Do you remember in history?"

"No, sir. No, sir, I do not."

Can He have His revival before He comes back? I think so. That "think so" is another word for "I know so." It is supposed to be used like, "I do not think so."

We will see it all. Oh yes. Oh yes.

Did I not give you a language to tell and retell? Did I not go further beyond those who exercised what I said in every people who obeyed and followed My direction? Yes, I did. Why would it be different now?

It is your choice. You make the decision. You can see all that I have for people, or you can see defeat. Choose all that I have, for you are on that road. Defeat will not be here.

Many choose defeat by choosing the comfortable and convenient things that are ahead or in front of them. But the trouble with that is that most of those did not originate with Me, saith the Lord.

So be strong and stay on this path, and you will see. Especially in eternities to come, you will be exceedingly glad.

I got really happy because I get to stay and continue. What He taught me about the next part of that love, He narrowed down in the teaching to what you can do about it as an individual. In other words, you do not have to depend on anybody else. It will just be you who brings you to that.

So next time, that is what He showed me when I was up there. I found out I do not have to go the rest of my life staring into the same face year after year and wondering. I would do my best if I had to, but not if I do not have to.

I am not going to stare into the same face year after year and wonder. I am not going to do it.

You will find out too: it is a decision. It is a decision.
