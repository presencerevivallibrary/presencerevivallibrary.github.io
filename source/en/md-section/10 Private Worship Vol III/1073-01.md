# The Father's Love for Us

## Each Key Adds a Dimension No Other Key Can Replace

This is prophesying, but He is doing it in an unusual way. He is forming the understanding in it now, the interpretation.

He said to me, "Do you remember when you were lying on the floor and I said to you, 'You do not know anything about worship'? You said to Me, 'I am on a fast.' And I said to you, 'Fasting cannot give you what worship can. But neither can private worship give you what fasting can.'"

He said, "I gave all the keys for a reason. When it comes time for them, they are the only things that can add that dimension to your life if you are lacking in that area."

A lot of people put all their major emphasis on one key. Unfortunately, there are many things He cannot do for them because they do not let Him finish.

I felt a little bad for the moment because my major key was praying in tongues. He knew what I was thinking, so He answered before I could finish the thought.

He said, "Fortunately, you gave yourself to a key that was a revelation gift. With a revelation gift, when it came time for the other keys, fasting, private worship, inheritance, and the love of God, I was able to add them to your walk."

I thought worship was a limb, but they were all part of the revelation of the love of the Father.

The interpretation in tongues was this: you are going to cost yourself time if you dismiss the worship limb lightly. It is going to cost you time.

I say that in the light of your hearts laying hold of God. Most of what you are laying hold of Him for comes through these keys. If you are just biding your time, waiting for worship to pass, and you do not take any of it home with you, then you are missing the route.

He said there is really only one route into what you are looking for, and it is to take that key and add it to the rest of your walk. If you pass it by now, even in the services, eventually He is going to have to line you up, and you will have to take the route anyway.

Not the law of private worship, but what private worship will do for you for the eternities of time to come.

You are His family. He is your Father.

## The Fellowship He Waited For Is With You, Not Your Title

This is the one He waited for. He waited for your spirit to be in fellowship with Him. Then He waited for the gifting. Then He waited for you to be able to crawl up into His presence and begin fellowship with Him while you are still wearing a body given to you by Adam.

In reality, He wants to enter into fellowship with you, a fellowship He is going to spend with you for the eternities of time.

This is the weakness in my understanding of Him: I cannot understand a self-sufficient Being wanting something from me. Then, after being illuminated by the Spirit, I found out that the something He wants from me is me.

Of all the keys that trump flesh, pull down strongholds, and allow the standings of grace Jesus gave you to come forward, this one is the one. All those standings of grace are Jesus' standing in you, with all the benefit that goes with it. But the greatest benefit is to be back in fellowship with the Father.

That is the part He waited for.

One billion years from now, as we know them, you will sit and talk with Him, and the fellowship will be rich. You will not exhaust the fellowship and knowledge of a Being who came from forever.

There is something about Him that encompasses you. He wants something from you.

He wants you.

That is why He has wanted to get this revelation across for some time about God seeking those who worship Him in spirit and in truth. He seeketh such. He came looking, wanting something from you.

You.

The part of you that is going there awaits glorification, but your spirit has been put back into fellowship across the barrier of flesh. The world is spiraling headlong into a dark hole called death and eternity, and He thought it was all right to ask you whether you minded getting in the way of those people.

Your fellowship with Him will be rich, and His fellowship with you will be rich. He wants to enter into a fellowship with you that will last for eternity. He wants to become all He is here to you while you are in the way of a whole lot of people trying to jump off into hell.

His promise to you is this: if you become all you can be to Him and let Him have you in fellowship, He will become all He is to you. Then you will be something to reckon with when you jump in the way of that dark hole.

## Grace Makes Fellowship Possible but Does Not Replace It

If you think there is some shortcut to fellowship with Him, His grace is what put you in fellowship with Him. It did not replace fellowship. It made fellowship possible.

It was not so you could stand on the outer perimeter as a distant relative and watch Him perform. He wanted you.

He wanted you.

Not your titles.

He is grateful that you gave the sacrifice of your call to Him, but He is not after your title. He is not even after making a great person out of you so that some great reward will follow you. All those things are available because you are trying to get people redeemed whom He loves.

If there were no resistance factor, what would be the point of a reward for a job well done?

When all is said and done, no matter what I do for Him, I cannot get Him to love me any more. It was demonstrated in Jesus, and I got the full package handed to me. I cannot get Him to love me more.

I can go into more of His love. I can receive more of it. But I cannot make Him give more. It is already there.

He is looking past your titles, the greatness of your person, the bloodline you were born from, and what DNA you came from. He wants you in your journey.

He hands out many things along the way, but not one of those things you do for Him will make Him want you any more.

Could His love have room to manifest itself more than when the fullness of your judgment was laid on Jesus? When and how are you going to make Him love you more?

His love was manifested when Jesus stood there and took all the judgment God had to pour out.

"There is just one tiny thing yet in the way of the fullness of My love. I am going to have to lay it on You, Jesus. I am going to have to judge You for it."

"Go on. Do it, so they can go free."

You cannot get Him to love you more, but you can go where He loves you. You can receive more.

## The Know-Me Limb Takes the Brass Out of Works

What is the reward? Some kind of merit badge for your level of response to His love? "I responded here, I responded there," and then He rewarded you for it? You are thinking He loved you because He rewarded you. He could not have loved you any more if you came in dry and parched by the skin of your teeth.

When you love Him, you let Him love you.

We are always trying to build ourselves into something, and we designate that we have accomplished it when we start receiving things. But if you want to build yourself into something that will always stand, deliver, and never break down or fail you, this is the limb I am talking about: the know-Me limb.

"Here I am. There is something about Me. I know I am going to spend forever with you, but I do not want to wait until you change bodies. I have a lot of people I could minister to if you would receive Me for who I am and what I have for you."

Are you trying to please Him with your works? He says, "Look, I cannot love you any more."

When you come to Him and start receiving that love, your works will lose all the brass. You cannot manipulate Someone into doing something for you when He already loved you to the ends of the earth and manifested it in the judgment of His own Son.

Take the brass out of your works. Approach Him because you love Him.

Do not sidestep this key. There is more in it than you know you are looking for.

## Many Are Within Reach of the Miracle They Desire

Hear what the Spirit would say:

For this I say to many of you: you are within reach. Many of you, as the desires of your heart hold on, refer to it as your miracle. Many of you are within reach of your miracle.

With strong words, revelation, and teaching, I desire to bring these things within range. I withhold no good thing from those who love Me. Delight yourself in Me, and I will grant you the desires of your heart.

If you have desires based in the flesh, those will give way to Mine. When you come through the fullness of spirit and fellowship with Me out of the nature that has been made a partaker with Me, you will find that much of what you thought you wanted, you do not want.

No one comes to Me in the magnification of their new nature and the revelation of Me by the Spirit while still wanting the things of the world or trying to hold on to them.

Yet many of you are within reach of what you refer to as your miracle. Many of you are undergoing mortification. The hindrances in the path and wrong directions are being dealt with now. Obstacles and obstructions are being dealt with.

This year they will not deter you, for you will not take a road around the obstruction. You will remove it, and it will be there no more.

Many of you are within range. Concerning My love and the desires that come from Me, many of the things that bring you great peace, satisfaction, and gratification do not consist of natural things. They belong to the realm of the fruit of the Spirit: My love, My kindness, My fellowship.

For many of you, the desires I will grant have to do with your joy, which will manifest itself in your happiness. Things you could not believe you could have before, because of multitudes of disappointments, you will find yourself receiving this year and next.

So do not sidestep the trade-off of brass for gold. Get to know Me this year.

## Wisdom Will Build What God Ordered and Leave Nothing Undone

God bless you for a new program, a program of wisdom. For the carnal man, it is out of sight. Yet you will see it, because it will consist of the building framed and made by Me.

People will come into it and immediately cry with a loud voice, "I have been set free!"

The tools of My freedom are not according to the flesh. They come from the throne room. They are always My best. They are needed for people who come at the lowest place. They are needed for people who have been destroyed. They are needed for the people from whom the devil has taken what they needed to resist him with.

But I am the Restorer of all things.

This wisdom that will come will not leave anything undone. It will build the building that I ordered. It will build the building that I ordained from the day I said, "It is finished."

So shall it be in your walk. Therefore be strong, for the plantings of these are of the Lord, saith the Lord.

## Romans 5 Shows Worship Turning Tribulation Into Hope

Are you praying for me? I need it.

He is getting ready to finish this prophecy in the Scripture. Are you in Romans 5? Let us begin with the first verse.

I could stop right now and say I have already been filled up in this camp. Really, I could. I could go home and say there is a dimension added to me. Maybe not a dimension. How about this: He went into my heart and did things further than He has ever done before.

One little prophecy. But if I were you, I would get that little prophecy and listen to it.

Romans 5:1 says:

> "Therefore being justified by faith,
>
> we have peace with God
>
> through our Lord Jesus Christ:
>
> By whom also we have access by faith
>
> into this grace wherein we stand,
>
> and rejoice in hope of the glory of God."

Then:

> "And not only so,
>
> but we glory in tribulations also:
>
> knowing that tribulation worketh patience;
>
> And patience, experience;
>
> and experience, hope:
>
> And hope maketh not ashamed;
>
> because the love of God is shed abroad in our hearts
>
> by the Holy Ghost which is given unto us."

When he says, "not only so," he means not only in the good times, when the glory is so strong you can hardly stand yourself and there is so much victory you are wallowing in it. He says we do the same thing in tribulation. We glory. We worship God. We magnify, extol, and lift Him up.

Why? Because we know something.

Tribulation with worship is different from tribulation without worship. Tribulation without worship is not working the right kind of patience. It may work a kind of endurance that becomes a lifestyle, and you may die with the same test. But tribulation with worship causes tribulation to employ patience, and patience goes out and employs experience, and experience goes out and gets hope.

Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen. If faith comes by hearing and hearing by the Word, then the hope I have had better be filled with His Word.

When the devil sinks your ship, people beat you, people get ugly, and everything is taken from you, you throw your arms up and begin to worship Him. That starts working the right kind of patience. Patience hires experience, and experience says, "I need something to fill up," so it lays hold of hope.

Then the Holy Ghost comes with the love of God and sheds it abroad in your heart, making you not ashamed when you come out on the other end of the situation.

## It Is Better to Be a Worshiping Mess Than a Silent One

It is better to be a person in trouble who prays than a person in trouble who does not. It is better to be a worshiper in tribulation than a non-worshiper in tribulation.

The devil will try to tell you, "Until you get that bright, shiny armor on, God will not hear you. Look what you did yesterday. Get yourself straightened up, and then He will hear you."

Mistake.

You need to get your hands up and worship God because you are free. This is the time God is waiting to get in. This is when you really need Him. He is so kind. The devil is lying to you. God is not the One who will not come. God is not the One who will not hear you. Quite the contrary.

He may not be giving you apple pies and Mercedes-Benzes. He is waiting for you to say the right things:

> Help me, Lord God.
>
> I worship You.
>
> I glorify You.
>
> I call myself strong.

But do not refuse to pray. Do not refuse to worship.

Worship in tribulation is confidence that He is the answer. God enjoys it, and you minister to yourself until you end up believing it.

Anybody can shout over a Mercedes. But what about when you have lost everything? If you are secure in God, the circumstance is temporal.

I remember saying, "If your house burns down and you are sitting on the curb in your underwear, and you cannot worship God, you do not have much." A week later, it happened.

## The Holy Ghost Uses the New Nature to Make You More Free

Watch what the Holy Ghost does with someone whom tribulation cannot get out of worship. He keeps singing praises, glorifying and magnifying the One who has the answer.

Tribulation starts working patience, but it is patience with an end to it, because worship caused patience to hire experience. Experience says, "I am being worked by the Word from the other end," so it goes and gets hope and says, "I need something to fill up." Then the Holy Ghost sheds the love of God abroad in your heart and makes you not ashamed.

The Holy Ghost only has one thing to use in you: your new nature.

The Holy Ghost did not set you free in place of Jesus' work, though He helped in the work of regeneration Jesus paid for. The new nature is what sets you free, and that belongs to you forever. If the Holy Ghost alone were what set you free, then if He left, you would not be free anymore. But it is your new nature that sets you free.

So He uses what has already set you free to set you more free with. He does not bypass it. He rises up in your new nature and amplifies it until who you are starts taking over every dark area of your life.

When the love of God is shed abroad in your heart by the Holy Ghost, He is not magnifying two things. He is magnifying one: the nature He gave you.

Your spiritual mind is standard equipment for the new creation. When I was born again, I received a mind that can discern spiritual things. It is my own spiritual mind, but it is like Christ's in its capacity to discern. I also received His nature.

With my spiritual mind I discern and understand revelation knowledge, but with my nature I love.

When I am getting full of revelation knowledge, my spiritual mind is receiving it. But when I start receiving the fruit of the Spirit, such as the unlimited capacity of God's love, that capacity exists in the nature He gave me as a new creation.

Your born-again spirit, in its nature, has an unlimited, unfathomable capacity to reservoir and pool the compassion of God. He was addressing that fathomless well when He said the Holy Spirit sheds His love abroad in your heart.

## God's Love Was Proven Before You Had Anything to Offer

Paul begins to explain God's willingness by comparing opposites. He shows how much God loved you when you were lost, unredeemed, and did not care, yet He paid the same price for you in that state.

Romans 5:6 says:

> "For when we were yet without strength,
>
> in due time Christ died for the ungodly."

Then:

> "For scarcely for a righteous man will one die:
>
> yet peradventure for a good man
>
> some would even dare to die.
>
> But God commendeth his love toward us,
>
> in that, while we were yet sinners,
>
> Christ died for us."

Much more, being justified by His blood, we shall be saved from wrath through Him. When we were enemies and did not care, He reconciled us to God by the death of His Son.

If He did all that for me when I was His enemy, what will He do for me now that I have accepted Him and been saved by His life?

That is why worship in tribulation matters. It starts filling you with the love of God that says, "You do not have a problem, and you are not going to be ashamed when this is over."

"I do not feel like worshiping Him."

That is the time you will get through more powerfully, because whatever was stopping you is not able to stop you at that point. You went on anyway.

The obstruction took its best shot: compounded emotions, hopelessness piled on circumstances, a mob with machetes outside the door. Are you going to let a trivial thing like that dampen your worship?

## Christ Nourishes and Cherishes the Church From a Place of Grace

There is a place in Him where Paul says, "Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church." Christ nourishes and cherishes the church.

Do you know there is a place in grace where Jesus became absolute? He cried out, "I am a worm, and no man." He was saved to the lowest degree of depravity and stripped of everything so the devil would never again have the power to do that.

When Paul compared the relationship of Christ and the church to marriage, he used words like nourish and cherish.

There is a place of grace in Him where we are nourished. That is what you do to someone who is sick: you nourish him, overshadow him, and see to every need and pain. But we are talking about being nourished by Christ, by His things and His love.

Cherish is powerful. A man marries someone for better or worse, and when the worst comes out, he is still able to cherish her. Those are powerful actions toward that person.

Christ not only nourishes; He cherishes. He builds me up and nourishes me to the point that He wants to give me everything.

How am I going to get there if I hang below the ceiling of that grace and let the devil accuse God in every tribulation, telling me how unfair He is and how He will not do anything?

If that place exists, I am going to leave where I am and go there. He is going to pour His love abroad in my heart, and I am going to be highly unashamed.

You will not have to defend God to me. Everybody I know can die, and I will still say He does not have to apologize. He did not do it.

I want that grace where He loves, nourishes, and cherishes. I am on a journey, and I will end up giving from that place where I know I am nourished and cherished. That is the highest kind of giving: not trying to invoke Him into something, not trying to get Him to love me, but going into the price He paid and letting Him love me.

Nobody will turn my river off.

## Betty Baxter's Healing Shows Worship Holding the Promise

Let me give you a glimpse from one of the generals, as I classify them.

Betty Baxter was born that way. By ten, twelve, or thirteen years old, she was all bent over in a wheelchair, twisted up, with her mouth over to one side. She was deformed and ate through a straw.

The preacher said, "Do not get this girl's hopes up. You will destroy her." Her daddy did not believe it either.

But Betty Baxter's mother believed it. Day after day, though the condition was visually impossible to get past, her mother worshiped God. This was back when faith teaching was not everywhere, but no preacher could convince that mother differently. She kept worshiping God and thanking Him, day after day after day, calling it done.

Tribulation began to work patience. Patience hired experience.

One day her mother was upstairs with her knitting or something, and Betty Baxter was over in her chair. A silver-dollar-sized light appeared in the room and kept getting bigger and bigger until Jesus stepped out of that realm into hers and stood in front of her.

That was when Betty Baxter's mother paid attention and saw Jesus over there. Betty was trying to reach her hand out to touch Him, but she could not quite reach.

Her mother said, "Oh, Jesus, would You step over so she can touch You?"

He stopped, turned around, and said, "That is the reason I have come."

Jesus put His hand on her, and she started cracking and popping out loud. Her hands popped and cracked loose. Her spine cracked, popped, and unfolded. Her mouth came back to the front. She stood up.

Her mother had already bought her a dress. The first thing she did was march downstairs and say, "Daddy, you were wrong." Then she went down the street and told the preacher what she thought too: "You were wrong."

They put her on a box, and the whole town paraded through, because they knew her and wanted to see the miracle.

If He could manifest more love than He did at the cross, then I would say beg Him. But He already carried the fullness out on the Son and gave you the result. You have to receive it.
