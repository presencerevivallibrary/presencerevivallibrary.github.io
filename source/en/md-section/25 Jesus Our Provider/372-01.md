It does not sound like that has much to do with Ephesians 5, where He said:

> Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave Himself for it.

Being the head of the church means that Jesus Christ, at the right hand of the Father, is the covering for the church. Savior is the same as provider. He is not only the covering of the church; He is the provider, the Savior of the body.
