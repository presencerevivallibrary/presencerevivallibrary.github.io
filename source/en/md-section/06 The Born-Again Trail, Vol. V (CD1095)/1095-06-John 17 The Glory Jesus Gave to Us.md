# John 17: The Glory Jesus Gave to Us

## Prophecy Grows With the Revelation and Faith in the Speaker

So turn to John 14, and then put a marker in Romans 5. Hallelujah.

You can hardly walk out before the Spirit of God comes, and the Head of the Church wants to prophesy. We misunderstood prophecy in the past. We thought it was just the person who jumped up and said, "Yea, thus saith the Lord," or personal prophecies being handed out in lines.

But when He says, "The man that prophesies, let him do it in proportion to the faith that is given to him," we always thought He just knocked us in the head and took us over. But if you can only prophesy in proportion to faith, then a lot of that prophecy has to do with your knowledge of God, the Word you have in you, the mysteries, and the revelation.

When the Head of the Church comes, the more offices you fulfill that you are called to, and the more gifts you operate in, the better you know God. Then the more needs He can meet in a single hour, in a single geographical location. If you are full of mysteries and answers, then the Head of the Church can come and just about solve any problem with it.

But when He does, you begin to prophesy and cut away all excuses. To the lost person, he falls down and says, "Christ is in your midst of a truth." When the secrets of your heart get matched with revelation knowledge, you get to see yourself in the light that God sees you.

## The Born-Again Trail Was the Next Step After the Walk of the Spirit

You know that the born-again trail was the next step in the evolution from the walk of the Spirit. It was beginning to really understand who we were when we were born again. I am convinced that lasting revival is impossible without that. Longevity would be impossible to attain without finding out who He is and who you are.

For years we believed for revival. Come to find out, the day the born-again trail dropped into my spirit was the day He took us the next step closer.

He put limb after limb on and corrected some things from the past. They were not foundationally off, but the limbs could have been a little better. On the born-again trail, He revealed who we were in Christ, and Scripture began to open up. Then fasting as in the born-again trail, worship as in the born-again trail, giving as in the born-again trail, our inheritance, and on down the line.

Finally, God is attempting to move on me and show me His glory, which is by far the most difficult thing I have ever attempted to understand. Outside of His Spirit, it would be absolutely impossible.

But to see His glory for just a moment as in the born-again trail is to see Jesus in His glorified humanity, and to see Him in everything He has made available to humanity.

## Tongues Is a Revelation Gift That Lets the Holy Spirit Reveal Jesus

I love the ministry of the Holy Spirit. As we discovered very early, praying in tongues has to do with understanding mysteries, who Christ is, and the plan of God. Finally He condensed it down into a single statement that I have been making ever since: praying in tongues is a revelation gift.

Can you imagine possessing a revelation gift that you can operate at your own will? Can you imagine that?

You wonder why the devil fought the baptism of the Holy Spirit like a crazy man. Then once you received Him, he equally fought your prayer life. Can you understand, with emerging understanding of the glory of God, what a devastating war machine you had become?

God wants cities. He wants cities. So, of course, the devil would fight you.

But oh, I love what Jesus said in John 16 about the Holy Spirit:

> He shall not speak of Himself; but He shall glorify Me.

Now we want to understand His glory. Jesus said the Holy Spirit would not speak of Himself, but He would glorify Me. How would He do that?

> He shall receive of Mine, and shall show it unto you.

This is how He glorifies Jesus. He receives what is His and shows it to you so that you can glorify Him.

I just cannot get over it. After all these years, twenty-seven years of ministry, being saved some thirty-five or thirty-six years, you would think after thirty-five years a man would have some kind of retirement from a principle. But after thirty-five years, I still cannot get over how simple He has made it.

You can be dumb and going somewhere to prove it, and it works the same. If you do not know how to read, it works the same.

He gave us a revelation gift to understand Him when we are powerless and do not know whether we are going forward or backward. He says, "It is all right. I will take your prayer life over for you. If you can just have somebody tell you, or read from the Word where this will end up, you will end up understanding My mysteries, and I will be working My plan in you. I can even give you the gift of reading the way I gave you the gift of tongues if you are really hungry."

I do not know. My problems, the fights, the wars, they just pale. They become so puny, putrefying, clabbered, milky, and powerless in His presence.

## The Church Must Not Let Redeemed Needs Go Unanswered

To see His glory, even the short little glimpse that I had, I knew this: that child I was standing in front of, twelve or thirteen years old, every year for five or six years the father and mother had been bringing the child to Palm Springs. Every year I got to stand in front of that child, twelve or thirteen years old, who never spoke. I think the most the child could do was gesture: needing something, wanting something, pain.

The father was here in October. It was not so long ago. He came because he wanted to hear more. After I got that first glimpse of glory, I pulled him aside, just bawling. He said, "He is in the process of making me accountable." Anyway, he made a trip up here to hear more about it.

But we have to figure something out. It is the church failing.

We are sure big at yelling about having a covenant in certain areas. Usually it is what Jesus was talking about when He said, "It is Corban," meaning that which I might profit by. Those are the areas we like to hang around in. We sure yell and scream covenant long, hard hours about that which we would profit by.

But we do not seem too bothered by entering church and leaving church, entering church and leaving church, year after year after year, and letting that part of our so-called covenant just slip by.

You have to settle it. Either He wants to do it, or He does not. Either He has some reason outside of totally redeeming all of those children and giving them the same start out of the gate that you got, some reason outside of redemption that He is not doing it, or else we have to find out why He redeemed them and then will not heal them.

Why are you making so much noise? He does not expect that much out of us. One thing He does not expect is for us to camp somewhere and let those things and people's needs go unanswered in any area where He has been glorified.

And He said, "My glory I give unto you."

## His Glory Is the Finished Redemption Work Given to Us

To help understand the word glory, think of a computer genius. He may be a fellow with one sock higher than the other and a big pencil pack, but he is a genius because he can do anything with a computer. His glory, the glory he gives to you, is that absolute genius when it comes to computers.

Arnold Schwarzenegger's glory is another thing. It is his body, all those bulging muscles and what he made with his life centered around what he did with his body. That is his glory.

Jesus' glory is this: He is the Son of God, and He came and totally redeemed you. His glory given to you is a finished redemption work. That was the glory.

> My glory I give unto you.

Jesus said the Holy Spirit has come to glorify Him by taking what is His and showing it unto you.

He does not expect a whole lot out of us, but He does expect us not to let questions go unanswered.

"Give Me My day in court. Do not let Me fail because I sent someone to somebody who did not have answers. Give Me answers, and let Me decide if I want to fail or not. If I want to throw My life away, let Me decide that. If I want to do those things that will cause me to grow old and nothing passes over, if you are going to take that away from me, let me go into it with my eyes open."

Just a moment with Him can change your direction, your destiny, and your whole life forever and ever and ever.

I do not mind telling you, making it to this limb on the born-again trail, just about the time you think it cannot get any more exciting, it proves you wrong.

Hallelujah.

You remember what He said to me when He first pulled me in and showed me the born-again trail. He said, "All I have ever promised you from My Word, you will find it on this trail. All the personal promises I ever gave you, you will find the answers on this trail."

That was almost three years ago. I thought it was a three-to-six-month adventure. No.

So let us pass in review a little bit, in addition to what we have already said.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

## John 14 Shows the Place Jesus Prepared in His Father's House

John 14:1 says:

> Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God, believe also in Me.
>
> In My Father's house are many mansions.

Not big houses with gold two-by-fours and things like that. They may be in our future somewhere, but that is not what He is talking about here.

"You believe in God; believe also in Me, because in My Father's house are many rooms. If it were not so, I would have told you."

Then He said:

> I go to prepare a place for you.
>
> And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto Myself; that where I am, there ye may be also.
>
> And whither I go ye know, and the way ye know.

Thomas said unto Him:

> Lord, we know not whither Thou goest; and how can we know the way?

Jesus made it very plain:

> I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by Me.

He said, "In My Father's house are many rooms. If it were not so, I would have told you."

In His glorified humanity, He ought to know, because He occupied one of those rooms. He was one of those rooms. In His humanity, He is saying, "In My Father's house are many rooms. I ought to know, because as His Son, I occupy one of them. I am going to go away and prepare a place for you, that where I am as His Son, you may be also. I go by way of the cross. I am the way, the truth, and the life, and no man can come to the Father but by Me. I go by way of the cross, and I am going to prepare a place for you. The room I occupy in His house as a glorified Son of God, I will receive you unto Myself, that where I am in the Father, you may be also."

That is hard to understand on the level of deity. But Jesus was both humanity and deity. You will never occupy the place He holds as deity. But you will occupy that room in His Father's house that He holds in His humanity, because He said, "My glory I give unto you."

Again, that is all the glory He held in His humanity with the Father, the glory of redemption and the finished work. He has given it all to us in the form of an inheritance. He has given it all to us.

He said, "Where I am, I want you to come and occupy."

There are some sons and daughters of God in town. They know who they are. We are first learning to exercise our authority by loosing heaven on this city. Now we are going to find out the part we are going to play in it.

Hallelujah.

## Jesus Entered a Condemned World to Open the Door Out

What do you think Jesus would do if He came into this town?

It is the Christmas season. He came not into this world to condemn the world, but that the world by Him might be saved. He that believeth not on Him is condemned already.

It is hard for me to conceive of this Christ child, this Man who came into the world and could not even get an inn. He was born in straw and a manger. What kind of triumphant entry into the world is this? He came into a world already condemned to open the door and let you out, if you wanted out.

> He that believeth not is condemned already.

In dealing with a friend of mine, he loves me because all I have ever done is demonstrate Jesus. He flew in specially to spend about six hours with me. So I went through the Word with him. He tried to get me to say that he was okay. He spent hours trying to get me to say that.

I said, "Friend, you know that I cannot say that."

"But we believe in Jesus."

I said, "I know, but He is the way, the truth, and the life. You know I cannot say that."

I said, "This is the Great Commission He gave us: to go into the world and preach the gospel. Them that believe shall be saved. Them that believe not will be damned. He came into this world not to condemn it, but that the world through Him, and only Him, might be saved. He that believeth not is condemned already. He is the only way to the Father."

He spent hours trying to get me to say otherwise. I said, "You know that I cannot."

What kind of God do we serve that He prizes our individuality above everything else? At the cost of millions in hell, He still cannot invade your will if you do not want Him.

He, the Creator of everything that is, had to leave this whole gospel in the hands of 120 men and women. It seems ridiculous, being God, to be limited to such a start. But it did not start there. Here is the Savior of the whole world lying in a manger. His triumphant entry into Jerusalem, when they waved the palm branches, was on a donkey. This gospel had to be left in the hands of 120 people.

Any planner, any government person, any engineer, if they were going to engineer the salvation of the world, being God, would certainly think He should be able to come and put Himself on display somehow. Pass through heaven, do something.

Yet He limited this thing.

I said, "Why?"

I am becoming so amazed at finding out who He is.

"Why did You do that? Everything You do, and the way You do it, looks like You are doing it in a way that retards the whole gospel. Why do You do it that way?"

Believe me, when Jesus cried out, "If there be any other way," and His sweat became as great drops of blood as He prayed, He was asking, "If there is any other way to save mankind, let this pass from Me."

Do you think a billion equations did not go down? There was no other way to save you. He asked if there was, if it were possible. There was not.

It started with a single man's spirit being alive. Just one. There was no other chance for humanity. He made all that He is available, but He has to go through you.

If God were going to do anything against man's will, it would have been stopping Adam. God did not create the devil as some great universal purging process, as if we had to go through this to prove our loyalty. If He were like that, He might pull something weird again in the future and create some race to fall. If He is like that, what is the point?

But He made all that He is and has available to us. Thank God.

Since you love Him here, He offers you eternity with Him. He does not think it is such a high price for you to sell out. If He were going to stop anyone, it would have been Adam. Yet He could not.

Way out in the eons of the future, anyone who serves Him will serve Him because they love Him. He is not going to get weird on you a few millenniums out there and decide that a new purging needs to come through. He is not going to create some new kind of devil to take His creation away again. He is the same yesterday, today, and forever. His Word remains the same. When you believe it, it is impossible for Him to do anything else but what He said.

Do you not think He would like one of you to be able to see His glory so that He can put His tender lovingkindness and mercies on display in case some hardened sinner wants to get born again?

No, there was no other way. He left it with 120 men and women, including the apostles. Mary, the mother of His humanity, was in the upper room. He left them with a new nature and the earnest of their inheritance, the down payment. Heaven says, "You have all this mighty inheritance, and it is all yours. To make sure you inherit all of it, heaven has given you an earnest of its sincerity to walk in power here and to inherit all that heaven is there."

He had to leave 120 people, a new nature, an old body, and the precious Holy Spirit.

He said, "Now I have to leave the world in your hands. Are you up to it?"

The devil tracked down apostles and slaughtered them, sported them, marched on nails, dissected them, made fun of them, and used their deaths for entertainment. But he could not put us out.

He wants me. He has got me.

Hallelujah.

## Scripture Is the Map to the Room Jesus Opened for Us

Now we have a collection of the canon of Scripture. God saw to it that this came together. This has been posted for all eternity concerning us being lost and saved. This is the book. This is the map. This points the road.

He said, "In My Father's house are many rooms. I occupy one. I am going by way of the cross so I can come again and receive you unto Myself, that where I am in God as a Son, you may be also."

You will never touch His deity, but you will be like Him in His glorified humanity.

How many of you are glad, as ambassadors, that God saved you, filled you, and left you behind for a purpose? You get to stay here and point people to the cross. You have the privilege of giving your whole life. How many feel good about this?

I have let some things slip, and that happens when the devil wears you down and the cares of this world, eking out your living, especially keeping up with the Joneses, wear you down. This care and that care wear you down until you start letting things slip.

I know, because I have fallen into that pattern enough. It is easy to lose sight and vision of why we are here, what is going down, and why He has given to us.

But in my Father's house there are many, many places to occupy. "I occupy one of them. Where I am, you can be, because I am going to pay the price, and I am going to come and receive you unto Myself."

Now let me clear up a foundation mystery so you can get on with your meditation.

Let us go over to John 17.

Hallelujah.

## John 17 Prays for All Who Believe Through the Apostles' Word

Let us begin in John 17:20 this time:

> Neither pray I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe on Me through their word.

That means through the apostles and those who followed their word. Right there in verse 20, a couple thousand years ago, Jesus prayed a prayer for you. Find out what it was, so it can come to pass.

He said:

> Neither pray I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe on Me through their word.

That is me.

When we believed in Jesus through their word, which we did, then verse 21 took place:

> That they all may be one; as Thou, Father, art in Me, and I in Thee, that they also may be one in Us: that the world may believe that Thou hast sent Me.

Verse 22:

> And the glory which Thou gavest Me I have given them; that they may be one, even as We are one.

When He gave it to us, it made it possible that we may be one even as They are one.

How is that?

Verse 23:

> I in them, and Thou in Me, that they may be made perfect in one; and that the world may know that Thou hast sent Me, and hast loved them, as Thou hast loved Me.

Jesus said, "I in them." Then to the Father, "Thou in Me." I in them, Thou in Me, that they, all of us, may be made perfect in one, and that the world may know that Thou hast sent Me and loved us with the same love He loved Jesus.

Boy, that means a lot to a waterhead baby, do you not think?

## The Glory He Gave Us Is His Born-Again Humanity and Finished Redemption

Backing up to verse 22 for just a moment, you are going to have to hang in close:

> And the glory which Thou gavest Me I have given them.

When the Holy Spirit glorifies Jesus, He takes what is His and shows it unto us. Jesus said, "The glory Thou hast given Me, I have given unto them."

Again, what is Arnold Schwarzenegger's glory? His glory is the body he developed. What is the computer genius's glory? His knowledge of computers.

What was the glory Jesus gave us?

He said, "The glory You have given Me, I have given unto them." What He gave us was the finished redemption work. He was born alive and died in our place. Then we got to partake of that glory the Father gave Him, which was in the form of the first Man to be born spiritually alive in four thousand years, the Son of God.

What God gave Him in His humanity, that glory He gave unto us: a born-again human spirit, a finished redemption work, and all the glory Jesus operated in with the Father. He has given it to us.

There is a whole world going to hell. How about we start operating in it?

We have not understood this prayer very well, because we have always looked at it through deity. He is one with the Father, and He wants us to be one with Him as He is one with the Father. We said, "Yes, but Jesus is God, and He is one with the Father. How can we be one with Them?"

No. He was not talking about Jesus as deity. He was talking about the oneness He has with His Father in His humanity, which is that spiritually alive spirit that was made alive after He died in my place. He is one with the Father as His Son, and I am one with Him in that new nature. That makes me one with the Father, with Jesus, in that born-again humanity, in the new nature He gave me that put me back into fellowship with my Father.

I do not want to be God. Neither do I. I just want to operate in the glory He gave me.

I have never heard such a thing.

He said that they may be one. He prayed for us, all of humanity who would listen to what the apostles had to say, and the other 102 or 108 people. He prayed for all those who would believe on Him through the preaching and ministry of the apostles, and through the others who carried that word. He prayed for us, that we might also be one as He is one with the Father.

## Romans 5 Shows How Oneness in Adam Is Reversed in Christ

To understand the oneness He is talking about, take the pendulum to the opposite swing and find out how we became one in the fall of the first man, Adam. We became one with him when we inherited his nature.

So how are all of us going to become one with Jesus and the Father except by the reversal of that process, by being baptized into the second Adam as one with the new nature, as He in His humanity is one with the Father in that new nature?

Romans 5.

Hallelujah.

Is anybody having a good time?

Let us go to verse 17:

> For if by one man's offence death reigned by one; much more they which receive abundance of grace and of the gift of righteousness shall reign in life by One, Jesus Christ.

One with the Father. All of us become one in Him. And that is by how many men?

We became one in God, all of us, through how many men? Just one.

How did we inherit Adam's nature? Was it through many offenses? No. Through one man's offense, the whole world was baptized into his death nature.

Well, let us reverse that.

> For if by one man's offence death reigned by one; much more they which receive abundance of grace and of the gift of righteousness shall reign in life by One, Jesus Christ.
>
> Therefore as by the offence of one judgment came upon all men to condemnation; even so by the righteousness of One the free gift came upon all men unto justification of life.

Oh, Father, He said, "I pray not for these twelve and the other hundred and eight who believe on Me now. I pray not just for these, but for all, all down through the history of the ages to the present time, who are going to believe on Me through their testimony. I pray that they will all be one in You, Father, as I am one in You through their baptism into My new nature that You gave Me from the life that was in You."

For as the Father had life within Himself, He gave to the Son to have life within Himself, and the Son gave to you to have life within yourself. We all became one in the Father through that new life He gave us that made us one in Jesus.

"My glory I give unto them," He says.

Yes, He did. And He gave me the earnest of heaven's sincerity, the power of the Holy Spirit, to reveal that glory so that I can walk through this life in that prayer Jesus believed for me: that I would believe in Him and receive His glory.

## The Devil Resists Revelation, but He Has No Power Over This Life

I think a lot of little squealing devils are running every direction right now to report to Satan that there is a group breaking through. They are breaking through. They are starting to understand.

I have understood, and I know it to be true also, that several prophecies have gone forward that a new wave of resistance is going to come. Well, that is just like clockwork, is it not?

Why did Jesus say the devil came? Satan comes immediately to remove the Word of God. Mark 4.

Why did Paul say he came? Because of the abundance of revelation that was given to me.

Of course another wave is going to come. Of course the devil is going to have to stop us. Of course.

But I have news for him. You can look him right in those fallen, squinty eyes that the light has gone out of, just like Jesus did, and tell him, "You have no power over me whatsoever. You have no power over me."

It was just like when Pilate said to Jesus, "I have power to crucify You." Jesus said, "You would have no power at all against Me, except it were given thee from above."

I have news for the devil. He may cause me to suffer some, but I can guarantee you it will only be for righteousness' sake. It will only be for carrying this gospel. He has no power over me except in the avenue where God wants me to go into troubled situations so people can hear the gospel, and I am persecuted for it.

Now the devil can have some power, not over you in the sense of being able to reign over you, but he will send people against you. You really do not have power over people, but you have power over him. People may cause me some persecution, and I cannot rule over their lives. But as far as one-on-one goes with the devil, sickness, disease, his personal control, and all those things, he has none.

So you can look him right in the eye and say, "You cannot cause any kind of suffering on my part. When it is persecution for righteousness' sake, get this straight, devil: I am full of joy and I am dancing on the inside. Get this straight. You are not my God. If there is anybody He wants to see saved, I do not care what it costs me. I suggest you back off."

I want that prayer to be answered in my life:

> That they may be one.

Baptized into His life, that life He received from the Father:

> I in Thee, Thou in Me, and them in Us.
