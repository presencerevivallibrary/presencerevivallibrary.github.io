# Worship in Spirit and in Truth

## John Introduces the Logos as Creator, Redeemer, and Second Person of the Godhead

John 1:1. We will work our way through this in passing review and highlight it so you can understand the depth we are going to enter into. Passing in review will be about forty minutes.

How do you know you are not limiting God? I know a pacing in my spirit when God sets one. I know it.

Hallelujah.

John 1:1 says:

> "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God."

In the beginning, before anything you and I understand to exist had a beginning, was the Word, the Logos. Before the natural universe and all substance had a beginning, there was the mighty Logos, the second Person in the Godhead. The Logos was with God, and the Logos was God.

Verse 2 says:

> "The same was in the beginning with God."

Genesis 1:1 says:

> "In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth."

Here John is talking about the perspective roles the Godhead took in creation. John is able to divide them by understanding who took what role. In the beginning, before anything had a beginning, back before the eternities of time and anything existed, was the mighty Logos, the second Person in the Godhead. Yet He was with God.

When you say, "I love You, God," you are talking to the three members of the Godhead who make up the One.

When you say, "I love You, Father," you are talking to Him whom the Jews referred to as mighty Jehovah.

When you say, "I love You, Jesus," you are talking to the glorified humanity of Him who is both deity and humanity, the second Person in the Godhead, the mighty Logos.

It is all right to say, "I love You, Holy Spirit." It is all right if you try to worship Him. His ministry is to divert that worship to Jesus. He will just glorify Him. He does not talk about Himself. He does not speak of Himself. All He teaches, speaks of, and glorifies is Jesus.

When He first taught me to worship, He is the One I went to. I went to the Holy Spirit to be taught how to worship. I did not know if anybody else knew how.

Hallelujah.

## Creation Displays the Distinct Roles of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit

When Genesis 1:1 says, "In the beginning God created," the three members of the Godhead took their respective roles and created everything that was.

Now John isolates the roles they took. The Father was the Planner. He planned everything. Hebrews says concerning Jesus, "by whom also he made the worlds." John 1:3 says:

> "All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made."

How do you know John is talking about Jesus? Because verse 10 says:

> "He was in the world, and the world was made by him, and the world knew him not."

As Colossians says, by Him, for Him, and through Him all things consist: thrones, dominions, principalities, powers, things present, things to come, angels, men. In the beginning, all things consisted by Him.

That means the substance they consist of came from the one Person of the One we have come to know and love so much as our Redeemer, Jesus. The atoms your chair is made of came from the consistency of Him. By Him, for Him, and through Him all things consist.

Glory be to God forever.

He is the Creator. Mighty Jehovah was the Planner. The Holy Spirit is the Regenerator. In Genesis, after God created the heavens and the earth, the Holy Spirit moved upon it by His power to make it habitable for man.

The Holy Ghost has always been the Regenerator. Even when it came time for your salvation, He regenerated you. He has always been known as the mighty Regenerator.

## God's Attributes Reveal Why He Can Be Trusted Without Shadow of Turning

Anthropomorphism is when God assigns characteristics to Himself that you can understand Him by. You are the creature. He is the Creator. The capacity of your mind was created by Him. Do you think you can understand Him unless He reveals Himself?

The Father is an anthropomorphism. We can understand fatherhood. The Son is an anthropomorphism. The Son of God had a beginning in the humanity of Jesus, not in His deity.

You can be equal to the glorified humanity of Jesus as His brother and sister, but you will never touch His deity. Never.

The Godhead is one in essence. There are three Persons, but They are one in essence. This essence of God that each Person of the Godhead possesses consists of nine attributes. The Father has this one essence. The mighty Logos has these nine attributes of one essence. So does the mighty Holy Spirit. They are all one in essence and three in Persons.

Those nine attributes are:

> absolute sovereignty, righteousness, justice, immutability, eternal life, love, omnipotence, omniscience, and omnipresence.

When I understood the essence of God, I knew what made Him God was that He could not vary from that essence. He was sovereignly love, meaning He could not operate outside of love. He was sovereignly justice, derived from the words just and justification, meaning He is just and justified in every decision or judgment He is forced to render or hand down.

At the great white throne judgment, there will be a time when you will be quiet. It will be time for you to keep your mouth shut because it will be God's turn.

Right now you can swell your chest, choose any words you want, and make great swelling expositions of theology as though that makes you right. You can say anything you want. But Jesus said, "I did not come into the world to judge it. There is one that judges you already: the words I have spoken. You will be judged by them in the last day."

Right now you are a freewill moral agency. You can say anything you want and act any way you want. But when you stand before Him, it will be His turn. You are going to be quiet and take what He hands out.

Your turn is now.

Every person hell belches up will stand before Him for their works. Their names will not be in the book of life. He will tell them the hour of their visitation and why He is sending them back to the lake of fire.

He is absolute sovereignty.

He is sovereignly immutable. Immutability means He cannot change, nor can you change Him. The devil, you, the flesh, and the world do not have the power to alter Him even slightly. There is no shadow of turning in His whole being. He cannot change, and you cannot change Him.

He is the same yesterday, today, and forever. What He said a thousand years ago is as good now as when He said it. That is why you can have faith. The moment you believe Him, He is sovereignly under obligation to come and do it, even if He wanted a way out.

## God's Love Explains Both Healing and His Restraint Toward Human Will

Someone says, "My Aunt Susie believed God and still died. I know He had a purpose for letting her die."

Sure He did. He could not heal her.

If a friend of mine were going to go to hell, and he was only saved through a window of time because of difficulties, I would rather God take him home than let him recover, fall back into sin, and go to hell. In that case, my estimation is that God could not heal him and bring him to heaven.

When I found out that God is sovereignly love, I began to find out about His character. That was when He began opening up salvation under every dispensation. I saw the lengths God went to so people would be full of the knowledge of Him. I was astounded.

In fact, He would not even let me into the Old Testament at first. First I had to assimilate our covenant. Then when I found out our covenant turned on sonship and inheritance, and after I came to that understanding, He began opening the Old Testament to me.

I said, "Father, why did You wait so long? I could read the Old Testament."

He said, "Because you would have used all those spiritually dead examples wrong, like so many have."

I said, "What protected me?"

He said, "Your praying in tongues, boy."

## Tongues, Peace, and Assimilation Prepared My Mind for Old Testament Revelation

I started talking about praying in tongues boosting your immune system by thirty-five to forty percent.

Is that my finding? No, sir. That is Dr. Peterson's finding. It activates a part of your brain that is not used for anything else. That part is dormant until you speak with tongues. Then it begins producing a chemical response that strengthens your immune system.

I have never missed a preaching engagement in twenty-seven years because of sickness. I can testify to the glory of God that what He said is true.

For two and a half to three years, my mind has been absolutely out from underneath pressure. I did not understand the effect pressure had on the mind, except that it breaks the mind down and you begin to have senior moments.

Have you ever had a policeman stop you and ask your name, and you forgot it? You cannot find your license. You cannot even find your purse. If a temporary pressure system can do that to your mind, what is the long-term effect? What are you doing to yourself?

On the other hand, if your mind is absolutely bathed in peace, what happens to your body?

It took two and a half to three years after I got out from under enough pressure for my mind to stop being subjected to it, and then it began healing itself.

When God began to teach me the Old Testament, I had never seen anything like all those genealogies. He made a simple statement in my spirit:

> "I want you to go back and find out how long from Noah backward to Adam. I want you to find out how long their sons lived and preached this gospel."

I went back, and to my amazement, it was all in there. They preached this gospel longer than their fathers. It was all in there.

I figured it out with pencil and paper and thought, Boy, I am going to introduce this on the Born-Again Trail. Then I thought I had better bring those notes with me.

Now, you know I do not have notes. My notes are little dots by Scriptures. The Scriptures are my notes. I assimilate chapters so the Scripture tells me where I am. Why do you need notes to understand what the Scripture says when the Scripture is trying to tell you what the chapter says?

Notes are fine, but the Scriptures are my notes.

## The Genealogies Show God Preserving the Gospel Across Generations

I began introducing the genealogy. Adam had Cain and Abel. Cain killed Abel and was banished to the land of Nod. He built a city after his son named Enoch. Then came Irad, Mehujael, Methushael, and Lamech. Lamech married Adah and Zillah, and they had Jabal, Jubal, Tubal-Cain, and a sister named Naamah. Of course, you already knew that.

Then we went back to the bloodline of Seth. With Seth, men began to call on God again. Seth lived 912 years and preached the gospel 112 years longer than his father Adam, who lived 930 years.

Seth had Enosh, who lived 905 years and preached the gospel 105 years longer than his father. Enosh had Cainan, who lived 910 years and preached the gospel 90 years longer than his father. Cainan had Mahalalel, who lived 895 years and preached the gospel 60 years longer than his father. Mahalalel had Jared, who lived 962 years and preached the gospel 162 years longer than his father.

Jared had Enoch. Enoch lived 365 years, and his father preached the gospel 435 years longer than his son because Enoch was taken. Enoch's son was Methuselah. Methuselah lived 969 years and preached the gospel 604 years longer than his father. He was Noah's grandfather and lived to the year of the flood.

Methuselah's son, Noah's father, was Lamech. He lived 777 years. Methuselah preached this gospel five years longer than his son Lamech.

Noah lived 500 years and had Shem, Ham, and Japheth. They rode the flood out. Noah lived 950 years and preached this gospel 355 years longer than his father Lamech.

Shem lived 600 years. He had Arphaxad. Arphaxad lived 438 years and had Salah. Salah had Eber, who lived 462 years. Then came Peleg, and the lifespan dropped by about 200 years. Peleg lived 239 years. Reu lived 239. Serug lived 230. He had Nahor, Abraham's grandfather, who lived 148 or 149 years. Nahor had Terah, who lived 205 years. Terah had Abraham, who lived 175 years. Abraham had Isaac, who lived 180 years. Isaac had Jacob, who lived 147 years.

Noah lived fifty-eight years into Abraham's life. The first thing Noah taught was sacrifice from the clean beasts that were on the ark by sevens. He taught Shem, Ham, and Japheth sacrifice and all his ways. He stayed with them until Abraham came. They could take a pilgrimage any time and sit at Noah's feet and hear the gospel.

Shem lived 108 years into Isaac's life. He rode the flood. Job knew the Redeemer was coming and would stand upon the earth in the latter days. Job lived around the time of Abraham and came from Shem's bloodline, as did Abraham. Shem's son was Arphaxad, and that is the line Abraham came from. Arphaxad had a brother, Aram, and that is where Job's people came from. They lived about the same time.

Even though Abraham carried the bloodline and had the promise, Job knew all about God.

You talk about the tender, loving mercies and kindness of God. What made me discover these things? Finding out His attributes. Finding out what He is self-governed by made me know how much He loves me and what He went through for the salvation of the world.

I cannot tell you what finding out about His character and attributes has done to my faith and my trust in God. There is nowhere you can put me that I cannot believe Him.

## The Father Seeks Worship That Only the New Nature Can Give

After understanding some of His attributes, I began to question my Father, and He began to talk to me about things that, for me, had been hidden since the foundation of the world.

Now pick this up where we left off. Go to John 4. All creation was waiting for this hour.

Jesus sat down at Jacob's well and talked to the Samaritan woman. This conversation changed my life forever. John 4:23-24 says:

> "But the hour cometh, and now is, when the true worshipers shall worship the Father in spirit and in truth: for the Father seeketh such to worship him.
>
> God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth."

There was no hour before this one. Not since the spiritual death of the human race at the hands of Adam had there been such an hour. Now the hour had finally come when true worshipers could worship the Father in spirit and in truth.

Notice that spirit has a little "s," which is correct, making reference to your human spirit. The true worshipers worship the Father in spirit and in truth, for the Father seeks such to worship Him.

God is a Spirit. What you can give Him that He wants from you, you can only give Him through your spirit. They that worship Him must worship Him in spirit and in truth.

When He said God seeks such to worship Him, remember there is not much we can give Him. Most of His attributes flow to us and through us. Praying in tongues is Him ministering to our spirit. Fasting is us ministering to our own flesh, shutting it down so it will not seal off the operation of our spirit. Speaking the Word changes trouble spots by bringing God's Word and what He said about the problem onto the scene. Angels have come to minister to you in presence, anointings, and atmospheres.

But all these things are to you and through you, for your benefit and the benefit of others.

There is not much you can give Him that ministers to Him.

When Jesus said, "God is a Spirit," He was making reference to the nature God has, the kind of Person He is. These are the people He is looking for to worship Him in spirit and in truth. There is something about you that is like Him.

If you cannot and will not give that to Him, you are not giving Him one thing He wants from you.

First, He sought you out of spiritual death. Somebody must have prayed, because He found you. He sought us, found us, and saved us.

The next thing He is seeking for is what you have that He wants: the kind of fellowship and rapport that can only come from a nature you have received that is like His own.

## "Seeketh Such" Means God Waited Through the Dispensations for Born-Again Fellowship

"Seeketh such to worship him" does not mean God was running from church to church, camp meeting to camp meeting, looking for people who worship Him better than other people.

It means He waited out the dispensations of four thousand years of spiritually dead men who could not give Him the fellowship He longed for. They could only give Him in part through their souls, in the measure John talked about at the end of chapter 3. The Holy Spirit would rest upon them for the purposes, pursuits, and plans of God in their lives as the clock ticked down to D-day, when the first spiritually alive Man in four thousand years would stand on this planet.

Hebrews says it is impossible for the blood of bulls and goats to take away sin. If those sacrifices could take away sin, they would have ceased to be offered. But the worshipers were not purged from the consciousness of sin, which is part of the death nature.

They could not give God what He wanted.

When I was in Israel following Abraham's journeys, we came to a city gate that had been uncovered. Everyone who passed that way, according to the record, including Abraham, would have passed through that gate.

It was crumbling and falling down, and I thought, My God, these stones are old.

All of a sudden the Holy Spirit spoke to me:

> "Your accounted-righteous experience, your born-again experience in accounting, walked through that gate."

I nearly melted right there. I have been born again.

They could only give Him so much through the veil of animal blood, but they could not take that last step.

## Relationship Without Fellowship Is the Tragedy of the Elder Brother

When are we going to wise up?

The disciples said, "Jesus, even the devils are subject to us in Thy name." To be honest with you, the disciples were more shocked than the devil was that he had to leave when they told him to go.

Jesus said, "That is right. But rather, shift your rejoicing to the fact that your names are written in heaven."

He was saying, "I have come to pay the price. Have you figured it out yet? I created and saved you for fellowship."

If God had a weakness, it would be you, now that you have a nature that can fellowship with Him. Why will you not give Him what He wants?

Do you think you are fulfilling His desire by sitting in church listening to some man's opinion from the pulpit? Do you think you are fulfilling His desire to know you individually and fellowship with you by gracing Him with your body in a chair for a few hours a week?

The teaching on the prodigal son was not only about the younger brother. It was also about the elder.

Music and celebration broke out because the younger son had come home. Servants went out to get the elder brother from the field. He asked, "What is all the music?"

They said, "Your brother who was lost has come home."

He got angry and would not come in until the father finally went out and entreated him.

He said, "I have never once transgressed any of your commandments."

That is right. He based his relationship with his father on his works.

"I have always done everything you ever told me to do. And this son of yours, who wasted your money on harlots and riotous living, comes home and you kill the fatted calf for him."

You have to understand, the younger son broke relationship with his father. He also broke fellowship. But five minutes after he restored relationship, he restored fellowship. The elder brother never broke relationship, but he never had fellowship.

The father said, "Son, all that I have is yours."

The problem was that the elder son never knew him well enough to know the father would have killed a fatted calf for him every day if needed. He did not know his father.

Religion gets mad because the man down the street is getting more saved than you are.

## God Would Not Break Adam's Will Because Love Cannot Rule as a Dictator

There are many things I want to know about God. As I learned His attributes and began getting to know Him, many times the mysteries held up before Him by my spirit began to open.

I will tell you one thing: if God were going to break your back, He would have broken Adam's back on his way to the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. Then there would be a whole lot fewer people in hell, because He would have stopped him.

I have heard testimonies, especially in ultra-holiness, where people could not figure out why they were all broken up in car wrecks and their children were in sin. They had to reason out some logic on God's behalf:

> "He put me down on my back because I was living in sin. He broke my back, glory be to God. He knew what it would take to get me to serve Him."

You think that is a testimony?

"He broke my back. The only way I can look is up."

If God were going to break anybody's back, He would have put Adam down on his way to the tree. So if He would not put Adam down, what makes you think He is putting you down? What do you think you are worth?

So you got saved after breaking your back. If I were you, I would give Him the glory for being there the whole time while you were too blind to see it, and then start calling your healing down.

If God were going to put anybody down, it would have been Adam, because there are a whole lot of people in hell because He did not.

Why did He not?

Because He could not.

God does not delight in the sacrifice of animals, much less the sacrifice of His Son. Ten million equations later, when Jesus cried out, "If it be possible, let this cup pass from Me; nevertheless, not My will, but Yours be done," He meant, "If there is any other way You can save mankind, I will not drink this cup."

But there was not. After ten million equations, there was not. There was only one way God could salvage humanity, and Jesus had to go to the cross.

God could not stop Adam's journey to the tree. If He could have, He would have. There would not be multitudes screaming eternity out now, would there?

He did not stop Adam because He could not. God is sovereignly love. That means He cannot operate outside of love. God cannot exist as a dictator over you and me forever in heaven.

He is self-governed by His nine attributes. Ten billion eons of millenniums from now, He will not get angry and wipe you out, because He cannot change. You are safe. He will not change His mind. He will never hurt you. He will not destroy you because He gets irritated about something. He is the same yesterday, today, and forever, and your future is secure.

When you know Him, you can have everything He has here.

In His love, He could not and will not be a dictator. There is something about your will and your individuality that God holds higher than anything else in creation. He protected it so severely with His attributes that He could not stop Adam.

Your ability to choose is worth so much to Him that hell is full, and yet you can sit in the middle of testing, trials, and hell and still choose God, lift your hands, and worship Him.

## The Passion Play Vision Showed Worship Filling a Place With Presence

A year before I broke into worship, God tried to get me in. Some people kept wanting to go see a passion play, and I finally went with them. Everything was perfect: the costumes, the acting, the music. Angels flew. Hell was hot. Every singer was exactly on key.

There was just one problem: no anointing.

God showed up at the altar call. On the way back, I was musing in my heart: What would it take, God, to get an anointing into a passion play like that?

To my surprise, because I was musing in my spirit, He immediately answered and said, "If you want to find out, go home and worship Me all night."

At the same time, I saw a rock landing in a pond. Ripples from that single rock landing in the pond began with one ripple, then another came from it and pushed the first one, and another and another, until every nook and cranny in the pond was filled. Wave after wave filled it.

I said, "What was that?"

He said:

> "When you learn to worship Me, first My presence will permeate your spirit. Then it will permeate your soul and your senses. Then it will permeate your geographical location. Wherever you are, I will come."

## Private Worship Broke the Ceiling My Spirit Had Never Passed

I can remember worshiping Him privately. When I finally broke in, after about three months of a certain amount of time each day, I believe it was a year or a year and a half later that I was standing in front of my church.

I had prayed in the Holy Ghost until, on the Born-Again Trail, it was time to put the worship limb on. There was no going past that date. Even though a year and a half earlier He had tried to get me voluntarily to go into it, because I had prayed in tongues, I reached the point where it was time to put it on and I could not pass it up.

So it released God to do what He did in my life.

I was standing in front of the church when a breeze came up and blew past me. It blew on my spirit, and my spirit became so light that I did not know whether I was going up or down. I ended up on the floor, totally out of it. As soon as I hit the floor, He started talking to me.

I will be kind to myself and say He said, "You do not know anything about worship."

My spirit began to protest. I was on a certain day of a fast, so I asked, "What about my fasting?"

He said, "I did not say not to fast. But fasting will not give you what private worship does, nor can private worship give you what fasting does."

He said, "Son, when everybody puts all their eggs in one basket, such as fasting, there are many things I can do in their life. But there are other areas I cannot reach because they do not employ the other keys."

Immediately I felt guilty. I thought, If I put all my eggs in one basket, it would be praying in tongues.

Immediately He responded, "Yes, son, but fortunately you chose the key that unlocked the door to revelation knowledge. That is why you are now enjoying these other limbs you are beginning to walk in."

I felt quite good for a few minutes.

Then He began to lay out in detail how long He wanted me to worship Him in private worship. I did not know how to do it. My private worship consisted of an hour now and then. I did not know what was beyond an hour.

He said, "I want you to learn all about it." He gave me a certain amount of time, and He knew I would do it when He told me to do it.

I went to my soundproof radio room, turned the lights out, and closed the door. At that time I had a half couch in there and the chair for making tapes. Since then, Tim replaced it with a small bed that fits in there, because I was lying on that couch with one leg hanging over the end and one off on the floor.

I had no music. I cannot hire a worship team to follow me around to create an atmosphere conducive to worship. It was me, myself, and I.

I knew enough to pour myself into every statement of adoration. Before, when I tried to worship, I thought it would be the nice things I said. But it narrowed down to three or four short statements that I poured my whole self into, knowing it was my soul I was offering up and my soul I had to get past.

I knew the soul is the part of me I communicate with my neighbor through accumulated knowledge: "Hello. How are you doing?" I knew I would worship in the language I knew. I would not let myself pray in tongues. I wanted to find out how to worship Him in straight English.

I would worship Him five minutes, and my mind would wander down the street. I would say, "No, you do not." I would stop, go get my mind, and bring it back.

I would say, "I am in the presence of a King. You are going to show respect and protocol. As long as I am worshiping Him, all of me is going to worship Him."

Then I would pour all of me into every short statement:

> "I love You. I worship You. I glorify You."

Now it is no problem. As soon as I raise my hands, I am in. But not then.

My mind must have wandered off forty times in that first hour. Hour after hour dragged by. I had highs and lows, but I stayed in there because when Jesus said "in spirit and in truth," He was talking positional truth. He was telling the Samaritan woman, "You can do this. Old Testament saints could not give Him what He wanted, but you can."

I knew to stay in there whether I felt like worshiping or not, because God had given me a certain amount of time.

After I got past the first hour, the one I was familiar with, I started drying up. I did not know what was beyond an hour. Some days I would lie there and say, "I love You. I love You," just as dry as could be.

But I stayed in there because He told me to. Little did I know He was talking positional truth: "in spirit" means out of the new nature He has given you, out of the new creation. Old Testament saints could not give that to Him. You can.

All the books I read on worship told me that "in spirit and in truth" meant you started worshiping in the flesh and finally ended up in the spirit as an experience. That does happen, but that was not what He was talking about.

Those teachings lead you to believe that when you say, "I love You, I love You," and feel nothing, you are not getting through. If I was not getting through, I had a lot of hours ahead of me. Drinking water did not help. I would drink a little water and get dry again.

> "I love You. Hallelujah. I really love You. Oh, I mean I really love You."

Little did I know there were ceilings on my life. My spirit had never fellowshipped with Him beyond those ceilings except under gifts of the Spirit that drew Him. But not my own spirit. Not me. I had a ceiling over me. I had never fellowshipped beyond that before, and now I was up against it.

I kept saying, "Hallelujah. I love You. I really love You. I praise You. I glorify You."

That ceiling of flesh was there. I could not feel Him. I could not feel anything. But little did I know I was getting through even more than when I was feeling things, because He was letting me feel where I had already walked. Now I was getting to worship Him beyond anything I had ever walked in, and I had run up against the flesh.

Did I stay in? Yes, sir. And He put me through this for teaching's sake. I can save you time now.

I did not know what I was up against, but I stayed in to learn. Each day, I wore that flesh down. Some days I would have a good hour or two and then some bad ones, but pretty soon the whole time was good.

Then I can still remember the day He came and followed me around all day.

I thought, All I have to do is think about Him, and He surrounds me. What is this? This is glorious. Nothing bad likes to live here. This is glorious.

He followed me around all day. He was never more than a microfiber away. I would raise my hands and say, "Glory."

One time I was standing in a friend's church. I lifted my hands and started worshiping, and a lady in front of me fell down on the floor. The pastor looked at me, and I looked back as if to say, What?

Some kind of baptism of the senses came.

## Worship Brings the Missing Ingredient to Analytical Faith

Some of you have analytical, clinical faith. You look into God's Word, know it works, and know He will work it for you, but you do not know Him very well.

Let me save you some time. The missing ingredient you are looking for that will cause your faith to come alive is private worship.

Why? Because a baptism of the senses comes. He will so baptize your senses in His presence that the part of you resisting what faith says without seeing begins to come under ascendancy.

Things just start happening.

I did not know there was healing in that anointing. In the last March camp meeting we had, every person who came in with braces left without them. One woman said it looked like the anointing was on demand. Every person who came with braces or crutches left without them. That was the first time ever.

I did not understand there was healing in the presence of that anointing that comes when you worship.

After March, when all those people were healed, I went to my next camp meeting and discovered healing was in that presence, a geographical wave of presence that comes through.

I was ministering in the camp meeting as usual. There was a man whose arms had been burned and chemically poisoned several years earlier. They had never healed. He would wrap them with bandages in the morning, and by night blood would be through the bandages.

Compassion came on me with that presence, and I prayed for him. The next day it was all dried up. The next day after that, he had pink skin. He was their cameraman, so they had known him like that for years. Now he was healed.

I was recovering in the back room, drinking a cup of tea, tired, with the anointing lifted. The pastor's wife came back. She had a thumb that had been jammed for four or five years and would not bend. She sat by me and reached over with her thumb and said, "Would you pray for my thumb, Brother Roberson?"

Out of sheer habit, I lifted my hands and began to worship. I reached over, took her thumb, and started to pray. It just bent with my fingers.

I thought, What is this? Is this a presence that is present like an atmosphere in a hospital room when everybody is crying? Is this what Smith Wigglesworth walked in when he said he never prayed more than thirty minutes at a time, but never went thirty minutes without praying?

Then a sixteen-year-old boy, the son of one of the elders, who had been born blind in one eye, saw her thumb. He came running over and threw himself down on his knees in front of me. I was recovering, but the presence was still there. I put my hand over his eye and worshiped Him. I took my hand off his eye, and he was looking back at me.

He went out and told his mother and father, and you could hear the screaming for twenty to thirty minutes.

I said, "Father, explain this to me a little bit. I have operated in the gifts for years. I have come out and worshiped God, finally putting carnality aside enough for the gifts to flow through my spirit for the needs of others. But this seems to be only a microfiber away. What has happened?"

He said, "You have touched Me with your spirit, son. When you touch Me with your spirit, all that I am can flow through you. You cannot suspend your spirit in My presence for hours and not touch Me. And when you touch Me, I will just flow through you."

I said, "Who can have this?"

He said, "This is not the giftings I am talking about. This is something available to these signs that follow them that believe."

## From Glory to Glory Means the Spirit Is Led Where the Miracles Live

Second Corinthians 3 says we are changed from glory to glory as in a mirror, into His image. Not like Moses, who had a veil over his face. That veil was spiritual death, and they could not see.

Not us. We look with an open face. We do not have that veil of spiritual death over our face anymore. We get to look into the face of God through Jesus with no veil of spiritual death. Therefore we can be transformed from glory to glory into His image.

From glory to glory is what He is doing with your spirit.

What we have tried to do is operate where we are in the spirit, read the Word that says these signs will follow them that believe, and then try to get God to go out and do those miracles for us. But according to John 16, He keeps coming back and getting us, trying to guide us into all truth. He tries to get our spirit to go where those miracles live.

We keep trying to get Him to leave us the way we are and go out there and do those miracles.

Anybody can get there. Fishermen on the Amazon River who can hardly read are pouring in by the thousands because others broke into worship.

People say, "Brother Roberson, that is the hardest thing I ever broke into. My God, I hated myself. I thought I was going to die."

He says, "It is a crutch."

Thank You. Thank You.

## Worship Chosen Under Pressure Ministers to the Father in a Way Heaven Cannot

Listen to me. After I had broken into worship, Tim, my associate, got one of Rick Joyner's new books, photocopied a page, and pushed it under my door because I was locked up. It described how God receives our worship in heaven.

The scene depicted God watching a little struggling church filled with people going through many trials. Hell was backing them up. Nothing was going right. Persecution was on every hand. Yet they were lifting their hands and worshiping Him, and the Father literally broke down and wept as He watched them.

Tim slipped it under my door, and I melted. It nearly destroyed me.

I said, "Father, if I had known You received worship like that, I would have gotten into worship a long time before now."

He said, "No, you would not have, son. You would not have realized it. I had to give you a direct command. A lot of people read it, and it does not move them."

When I saw how He received it and saw the tears, I could not understand it.

"God, You really love us like that? You are really moved that strongly by us?"

I am telling you right now, there may never be a time in the millenniums to come when you can minister to your Father like you can now. Your spontaneous worship in heaven will be no problem. There will be waves of glory in heaven. But there may never be a time when you can give Him what you can give Him now.

I will tell you why He weeps. I will tell you why He cries.

At the cost of untold millions in hell, you have the ability to choose. Hell has built its barricades in front of you. Its armies are on your left and right, and there is nowhere to retreat. When things cannot get any worse, you can choose to lift your hands, worship Him, love Him, and glorify Him.

You choose. At the cost of hundreds of thousands in hell, you make that choice and decision. Yes, it moves Him.

There is no way out. Hell has indicted God for unfaithfulness. Things could not look worse. Your children are in sin. Your finances are in shambles. Nothing is going right, and you are dying.

Still, you lift your hands and worship Him.

Things will not stay the same.

You choose to lift your hands and tell Him how much you love Him, as long as you want to, as often as you want to. There is nothing hell can do about it. There is nothing it can conjure or bring against you that has anything to do with your personal fellowship with Him who saved you and made the path.

I choose.

I choose.

I am at the helm of this ship. I am the bit in this horse's mouth.

I choose.

I choose everything.
