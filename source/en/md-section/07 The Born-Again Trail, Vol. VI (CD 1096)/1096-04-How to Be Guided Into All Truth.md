# How to Be Guided Into All Truth

## The Glory Jesus Gave Us Belongs to His Humanity, Not His Deity

Open to Hebrews 1 and put a marker there, even if it is your finger. Then go over to John 16. We will start in John 16 and then be in Hebrews after that, if things go according to plan.

In review, briefly, in Palm Springs last August I slipped into the Spirit while standing in front of a girl whose picture Gary held up Friday night. Her birthday is coming up. She has never walked or communicated. I was standing in front of her and slipped into the Spirit, and God did some things, such as telling me that I had become accountable for what I know in that area.

When He got through with that, I slipped a little further into the Spirit and saw His glory. But the emphasis when I saw His glory was on the glory given to Him in His humanity. Sure enough, when He began to break down John 17, "I in them," "Thou in Me," and that we all might be made perfect in one, the key was this: the glory I saw was linked with His humanity as the first begotten, the firstborn of God.

He said to me that the key to breaking all those verses down was looking not at Jesus' deity, but at His humanity.

So those verses began to be broken open, including this sixteenth chapter. In John 17, Jesus says, "My glory I give unto you." The glory He gave us was the glory of the position He held with His Father as His Son, the first begotten, the firstborn of the Church. That position and standing is the glory He has given to me and you.

To understand that glory a little more, Arnold Schwarzenegger's glory is his muscles. He glories in them. They are his life. His muscles are what he is. If he gave you his glory, you would end up looking and acting with those same muscles just like him.

To go to the other end of the pendulum, take a computer expert. We call them computer nerds, though in social circles you may call them computer experts. Generally, their brain is putting out so much power there is not enough left over to run their body. That is why they got the reputation in the last century of coming out with one pant leg stuffed in their sock, a pencil pack leaking on their shirt, and bed head. If that man gave you his glory, what would he give you? The next day you could hack into the FBI files, because that is his glory. He lives for it.

This is what Jesus was talking about in John 14. Remember, break this down according to His humanity:

> In My Father's house are many mansions.

That means rooms. "If it were not so, I would have told you." He ought to know, because He occupied one of those places in God as His firstborn Son. "In My Father's house are many rooms, yet I go to prepare a place for you."

He went by way of the cross. He prepared a place for us so He could come again and receive us unto Himself, that where He is, we may be also. When He said, "Where I am," He meant that place, that room He held and occupied with the Father as His Son and firstborn.

He said, "I will come and receive you unto Myself, that where I am, you may occupy the same room also. My glory I give unto you. Where I am, you may be also."

Of course Thomas jumped in there and said, "How can we know where You go, and how can we know the way?"

Jesus said:

> I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by Me.

No man can come to the Father but by that life.

His glory is that position He holds with the Father in His humanity.

## The Spirit of Truth Guides Only the Willing

This helps you understand these verses in John 16. Jesus was just about to go home, and He was making the promise of the Holy Spirit, who would come, and telling of His ministry when He did come.

Look at John 16:12:

> I have yet many things to say unto you, but ye cannot bear them now.

You cannot bear them now because you do not have a new nature.

Then verse 13:

> Howbeit when He, the Spirit of truth, is come, He will guide you into all truth.

There are people who have the Spirit of truth but do not have much truth, because all He will do is guide. He will not push.

You can take your will and choose to believe anything you want. You can stand up and fight Benny Hinn, fight the Holy Ghost, fight the miracles, and do it as a champion of God. He will not push. If He were going to push anybody, He would have pushed Adam away from the tree. He does not push.

You have time to decide whom you are going to serve. He does not push. He guides.

Can anybody tell me something you can do every day that will kick that guidance system in? You are a sharp crowd.

He has come to guide you into all truth. That is why James 3 says:

> Be not many masters, knowing that we shall receive the greater condemnation.

Do not be many masters, or teachers, experts in everything. He that offends not in word or doctrine is a perfect man, able to bridle the whole body.

As faith people, we taught that mostly as, "With my words I turn myself and my body out of disease. With my words I turn myself into problems or out of problems because I have what I say." But Jesus was covering a broader scope.

Be not many teachers, receiving the greater condemnation. A man offending not in word or doctrine is a perfect man, able to put a bit in the mouth of the body of Christ and bridle it. The same way the helm on a ship and the bit in a horse's mouth are much smaller than the thing being guided, a teacher has power to snap a bit into the body of Christ's mouth and steer it, even out of Christ and all the way to hell, much less into doctrines that defeat you.

It is not uncommon that He said that man would receive the greater sentence of condemnation and loss of reward.

But He will not push you. When you decide in that new nature, that decision is what He has to work with. Thank God He will honor people's prayers and send a steady stream of people to you who walk in the same authority on this earth as you do, people with a new nature. He will send them to you because they are His servants. They will talk to you and talk to you, and He will not quit until the day you lay down and still say, "I do not want any of it."

He will not push, but He will guide. You cannot guide something unless it is willing to be moved. He did not say drag. He said guide.

## The Holy Spirit Glorifies Jesus by Showing What Belongs to Him

Look again at John 16:13:

> Howbeit when He, the Spirit of truth, is come, He will guide you into all truth.

How much truth? Half? Three-quarters? No. All truth.

Who did He send to see to this? Him, the Holy Spirit.

> For He shall not speak of Himself; but whatsoever He shall hear, that shall He speak: and He will shew you things to come.

Now notice verse 14 very closely:

> He shall glorify Me: for He shall receive of Mine, and shall shew it unto you.

That is how He glorifies Jesus. He receives what is His and shows it unto you.

Is there anything you know you can do every day that causes that process to come? We have been strong on that for twelve years, would you say?

This is His ministry: to guide me by receiving of His and showing it unto me. Notice the next verse:

> All things that the Father hath are Mine: therefore said I, that He shall take of Mine, and shall shew it unto you.

What does a man in a traditional church do with something like this? "All things that the Father hath are Mine. Therefore I said that He shall take of Mine and show it unto you."

Where did He get it? He got it from the Father. So this certainly could not be talking about His deity. It is talking about His position in His humanity as the firstborn.

He is saying, "All that the Father gave Me as the firstborn of the Church, in My humanity, that glory I give unto you. I sent you the Teacher so that all the Father gave to Me can be shown unto you, because My glory I give unto you."

The key to breaking that verse is again His humanity. Thank God He gave us a practical way, independent of ourselves, where we cannot even mess the knowledge up: communicating to Him hour after hour in prayer.

Can anybody tell me what you can do every day that releases this process of being guided into all truth?

## Jesus Prayed That His Glory Would Perfect Us in One

In John 17, in that magnificent prayer He prayed for us, beginning with verse 19, He said:

> And for their sakes I sanctify Myself, that they also might be sanctified through the truth.

He separated Himself to finish what the Father gave Him to do, so they could be separated by the truth and enter into this life.

Verse 20:

> Neither pray I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe on Me through their word.

So this prayer is for me. I do not think Jesus is in much of a habit of having His prayers not come to pass. I know I am not. If you believe what you pray, then you will ride it out until it comes to pass. You will pray according to the Word too, because you will not pray stupid things.

This is why He can grant you the desires of your heart: a man who delights himself in God can only do that through the foundation of His Word. There is no other way. My prayers will be accurate because they are founded on His Word, and He can grant the desires of my heart because I delight myself in Him.

Of all people to have their prayers answered, I would expect Jesus would. I want that prayer answered in me. He is the One who prayed it, so I do not think there is enough power anywhere to stop it except with me. I am not going to do it, and I am not going to let the devil do it. My flesh is not going to stop me.

He said something to me the other day about my flesh. I hate it when I react to anything in the flesh. In fact, as the days go by, I hate it worse and worse. The other day I was condemning myself, saying, "You have no right to react in the flesh. It does not matter what anybody else does. How you react is not dependent on what they are doing."

I hate myself more than I ever have when I react in the flesh, and I have a tendency to condemn myself. God said, "That is not necessarily a bad sign, because the closer you get to Me, the worse you are going to hate your reactions in the flesh."

I said, "Thank You for that little piece of meat. I am not so bad."

Jesus prayed this prayer for me, and I know there is a standing out there the Church knows very little about. He said the Spirit of truth would come and guide us into all truth.

There is a place waiting where, when Jesus can get turned loose in a city like ours, that city will suffer two things: great revival and great opposition. But at least we will have the revival.

Amen. Great revival, great opposition. Hallelujah, Jesus.

There is that place for the Church to walk in, and it has been available for two thousand years. When He put us on the born-again trail, the first things we taught were only what He had said to me: "You will find all I have ever promised you on this trail."

He spoke the truth. I am understanding it now and obtaining it.

## The Same Love the Father Has for Jesus Is Meant to Be Known Through Us

John 17:20 says again:

> Neither pray I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe on Me through their word;
>
> That they all may be one; as Thou, Father, art in Me, and I in Thee, that they also may be one in Us: that the world may believe that Thou hast sent Me.
>
> And the glory which Thou gavest Me I have given them; that they may be one, even as We are one.

Hello, Arnold Schwarzenegger. Hello, computer expert.

> And the glory which Thou gavest Me I have given them; that they may be one, even as We are one:
>
> I in them, and Thou in Me, that they may be made perfect in one.

To help you understand that statement again, by one man's offense, Adam, sin entered into the world, and death by that sin. Until the law, sin was in the world, but sin is not imputed where there is no law. Nevertheless, death reigned over those who had not sinned after the similitude of Adam's original transgression. That means every human being born of Adam kept dying even though the sin was not imputed and they were not responsible for Adam's fall.

Every human being was made imperfect in one. But now Jesus says He is in Christ, Christ is in Him, Christ is in the Father, and the Father is in Him.

How so?

As the Father had life within Himself, He gave to the Son to have life within Himself, and the Son took that same life and gave it to you to have life within yourself. Just as we were made imperfect by spiritual death in one, now by the resurrection of one Man, we have been baptized into His life and perfected in one.

By what? That life He received from the Father. He was the first Man to be born spiritually alive since the fall of Adam, four thousand years later. In that life, in that human spirit He received from His Father, I received from Him. So I am now being perfected in one as you are, and the One we are being perfected in is Christ, who received His life from the Father.

Hallelujah.

Then verse 23:

> I in them, and Thou in Me, that they may be made perfect in one; and that the world may know that Thou hast sent Me, and hast loved them, as Thou hast loved Me.

We probably need to go over that one again:

> And hast loved them, as Thou hast loved Me.

This is almost incomprehensible. The same love He loved Jesus with, He wants the world to understand He loves us with. The same compassion, the same love, the same glory He had for Jesus, He has for me.

He wants the world to know that the Father loves us with the same love He loved Him. I would say so. It altered the position of the Godhead forever. A Father became a Father. The Word became His Son, deity in humanity, and the precious Holy Spirit had a regeneration work to carry out.

You talk about love. To make sure we had everything available to find these things out, He sent the Teacher. Yet, like Adam, He cannot push you. He cannot drag you. But He can lead you into all truth.

We have some things to find out. I think in this phase we are going to find out more about His power and glory. I have been enjoying the Father's heart being poured out on me. The feelings are unexplainable. All of a sudden I find myself crying almost before I even think. That is just a little unveiling of His heart.

I do not know what is out in front, but I know this: if He can get more of His compassion in me, twisted-up children will be no problem.

## One Onion Skin of the Father's Love Can Change People Quickly

I am also pleased that He was so ready to transfer by His grace. That is what happened in Gatlinburg or Cleveland, Tennessee, where I could not come home for six weeks. He just kept pouring it out and changing people like I have never seen in my whole life.

Just a little bit of the Father's heart being present and transferred by grace can start a stillness and fast change in people's lives.

What are we entering into? I want more of that love He loved Jesus with, even though He can only peel an onion skin at a time. As I keep telling you, it was amazing to me to have a vision of an onion being peeled. When He gave me the first wave of His love, I understood why I could not take any more. Nine hours of crying.

I tried to call Gary and Tim to get them to pray for me. I got Gary to pray for me and to tell the church to pray for me that night. I could hardly talk, and there was no sign of it letting up.

I had to come out of it to do the conference. It showed up again on Friday night. It has been showing up in almost every service, not necessarily all the time heavy in the crowd, but in me. To a certain point, I can tell that I am not going to go any further in God until I finish whatever He is trying to bring me into.

So I have been trying to stay home. The more He gives you, the more impossible it is not to. As far as meetings go, everything goes to a certain point and then there is no more information. It does not do any good to pray about them because there is no information. I only have what He told me before I went into the seeking time, and He is not saying anything else.

I say, "Should I do this meeting in silence?"

There is a dead spot in front of me, and I have to finish finding out what He is doing. At least I should be home.

Amen. That makes me happy.

## Hebrews Says God Now Speaks by the Revelation of His Son

Let us go to Hebrews 1.

Hallelujah. Forty-five minutes warming up. Fifteen minutes to introduce Hebrews.

Stand up and help me for a minute, please.

Oh, hallelujah. To find out the Father loves you with the same love He loves Jesus. You are being perfected in that One, the last Adam. Everything He is, the Holy Ghost is planning on making you. He would like to take what He brought from heaven and do it with that instead of waiting for you to get to heaven. Why not take what heaven sent and use it before you get there?

Hallelujah.

Glory, glory, glory.

I magnify You and glorify Your holy name. I give You all the praise and the glory. How can we honor You? How can we thank You for that Teacher and for Your Word, for a shot into the darkness of spiritually dead men?

We get our shot in the dark. Hallelujah. And I have an infrared scope on my gun. I have God's sight. I told my kids, "Devil, I have something for you. It is called crosshairs."

How can you take a shot in the dark? Because I found the light. I am adjusting my scope according to the new nature. I am a duck that was raised as a chicken and found out I can swim.

"How do you know you can swim?"

"There is something on the inside of me. I am not a chicken."

It is the nature of God. Jesus has come and received me unto Himself, and He gave me a Teacher to find out what and where He has received me into, and where I am.

Somebody says, "I just do not believe that."

I know you do not believe all things are possible to him that believeth either. You do not believe you can say to the mountain, or that the works He does, you shall do. Impossible? Well, if it takes believing this to believe the other, I am in.

Give me Your glory, that I may glorify You. Jesus, I love You.

Hallelujah.

Someone says, "Brother Roberson, this seems impossible. Are we imbalanced?"

No. I understand this body. I understand the flesh. I understand He has given me the Holy Spirit. I understand also that He is Lord. I understand Jesus is also deity. We are not talking about anything to do with His deity. The position He gave us is in His humanity.

"Can you prove it?"

Sure. Begin with Hebrews 1:1:

> God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers by the prophets,
>
> Hath in these last days spoken unto us by His Son.

The revelation of Jesus Christ by the Holy Spirit is God's voice. He has nothing else to say to you.

Hallelujah.

He has in these last days spoken to us by His Son:

> Whom He hath appointed heir of all things, by whom also He made the worlds.

Make no mistake: in His deity, He made the worlds.

This is why when I said, "God, show me Your love," I was a stickler for the Word. The revelation of Jesus Christ is God's voice. That is how He is speaking to the world now. He has spoken to us by His Son. So whatever I think I get, if it is not confirmed by the canon of Scripture and the revelation of His Son, I will not receive it.

Hallelujah.

## The Son Who Made the Worlds Washed the Feet of Fallen Men

I said, "God, show me Your Word. Show me Your love. You peeled a layer off and it almost destroyed me with Your compassion. If I am walking so carnally and so close to the earth that one level of the Father's love almost destroyed me where I am walking, show me from Your Word."

That is when He began to show me. I got into a shortcut to the heart of a servant, saving years. Jesus washed the disciples' feet as an example. "As I have done, do ye also," He said.

When I saw Him washing the twelve disciples' feet, I saw it pyramid to all humanity. All He was telling me was, "Son, if I washed their feet, I washed yours."

We found out what Satan was after from Matthew 4. In his creation, he was the sum total of everything as far as beauty is concerned. He was full of pipes. It was not pipes sticking out; it was the harmony his beauty and his voice brought when it opened creation up to worship. When you saw him, the sum total of God's creation invested in him, his beauty struck off for God, and the way he sang opened creation up to God.

That is why God goes after everyone called to deliver the body of Christ into worship very, very young. I believe one of the missing keys for His geographical dwelling is found in worship.

When Satan could not get Jesus to fall over His flesh, turning stones into bread, he moved to the emotional realms of the soul and tried to get Him to commit suicide by misquoting a verse: "The angels will gather You up so You will not dash even Your foot against a stone. Jump off the pinnacle of the temple."

Jesus said, "I will not tempt God."

Then Satan took Him up onto the high mountain of religion, the mountain over the religions of the world, social systems, New Age moves, and every other damnable thing he sends people to hell with. He showed Him the kingdoms of the world and the glory of them and said, "This is what I really want from You. Fall down."

Satan, once known as Lucifer, saw how God received glory and worship, and how it pleased Him. Obviously, worship was something so prized by God that Satan began to take it for himself. He tried to get Jesus, offering Him the world, the kingdoms, and the glory of them.

Jesus would not. He said, "Get behind Me."

Then, three years later, Jesus was on His knees before a fallen race. He would not bow before Satan, but He was on His knees before a fallen race, washing filth and betrayal.

Hebrews says:

> God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers by the prophets,
>
> Hath in these last days spoken unto us by His Son, by whom also He made the worlds.

When He washed your feet, the grains of sand He knelt on were made by Him. You were born by His creation. Who are you that He would get down in front of you?

## The Son Was Born Into Inheritance, While Angels Were Made

Hebrews 1:3 says:

> Who being the brightness of His glory, and the express image of His person, and upholding all things by the word of His power...

This is who He was before He was poured out into humanity. In His deity, He was called the Word. He upheld all things by the word of His power.

> When He had by Himself purged our sins, sat down on the right hand of the Majesty on high;
>
> Being made so much better than the angels, as He hath by inheritance obtained a more excellent name than they.

By inheritance. By inheritance. By inheritance He obtained a more excellent name than they.

> For unto which of the angels said He at any time, Thou art My Son, this day have I begotten Thee?
>
> And again, I will be to Him a Father, and He shall be to Me a Son?
>
> And again, when He bringeth in the firstbegotten into the world, He saith, And let all the angels of God worship Him.

What did He say about the angels?

> Who maketh His angels spirits, and His ministers a flame of fire.

Notice the difference between Him and the angels. He was born. They were made.

What is the difference between you and an angel? You were born. They were made.

When Jesus told Nicodemus, "You must be born again," Nicodemus asked Him to explain. Jesus said, "You are a teacher in Israel, a good one too, probably sitting on the Sanhedrin court and judging the affairs of the people, and you do not understand this?"

He said:

> The wind bloweth where it listeth, and thou hearest the sound thereof...

The word for wind there is the same as breath. When God formed Adam's body from the dust of the earth and breathed into him, He reached inside Himself and took life. There was no creation here. There was a beginning, but the beginning was born. He breathed into him.

That is why Jesus said the wind blows where it lists, you hear the sound, you do not know where it comes from or where it goes. So is every man who receives the life from God. They are born of the Spirit.

You are a new creation, all right, because you have been born of God. The difference between you and angels is that they were made. You were born.

He has by inheritance obtained a more excellent name than they. Here we go into inheritance again. Covenant or inheritance? Once the will was read, the covenant was with the sinner. I got born again. After I was born again, it became inheritance.

Thank You. Inheritance.

## Adoption Completed Makes Sons Heirs, Not Porch Beggars

Remember the way God finally demonstrated this to me. Five children, three of them biological and two adopted. He said, "Do you treat your adopted children differently?"

I said, "No. They are bone of my bone and flesh of my flesh."

When everyone saw Tony, being Filipino and Chinese, with me and Rosalie, two big old white Gentiles, the sharp minds of the kids at school said after they saw us, "Are you adopted, Tom?"

She said, "I used to be. I used to be."

Now she is bone of my bone and flesh of my flesh, because the adoption was complete.

He said, "When your oldest son raids the refrigerator, what does he do it based on?"

I said, "Trust me, God: sonship."

He comes in and does not ask. It looks like an elephant burial ground when he is done, bones strewn all over, meat on the walls, in the sinks, and in the kitchen. Does he ask? No. He has rights.

God said, "What if Tom or Daniel got their adoption papers out and said, 'We have an agreement. We have a covenant. You better feed me,' and laid it out on the table?"

I told God they would not sit down for a good month.

Why not get off His porch and quit yelling at Him, and go in and eat?

The Son by inheritance obtained a more excellent name than the angels. What about us?

Hebrews 2:11 says:

> For both He that sanctifieth and they who are sanctified are all of one: for which cause He is not ashamed to call them brethren.

He that sanctifies and those who are sanctified are all of one. For this cause, Jesus is not ashamed to call us His brothers.

There is no old man left. I have been born of God.

What did He say He would do with us?

Verse 12:

> I will declare Thy name unto My brethren, in the midst of the church will I sing praise unto Thee.

He will worship with us.
