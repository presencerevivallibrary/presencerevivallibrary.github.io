## Three Mantles Reached Fullness Together: Love, Miracles, and Judgment

Now we have a couple of mantles in fullness here. One is that love and joy and unity, so much so that they went past servant. I mean, they made themselves slaves. This was it for them.

Later, when the twelve apostles were arrested, they got out supernaturally. What I was shooting for was when the apostle Paul came with persecution. He was hailing Christians out and locking them up, and everybody fled except for the apostles. They stayed in Jerusalem. The believers were set free to the cities round about, and they began to minister the gospel, such as Philip in Samaria.

Two mantles reached fullness. One was that love, joy, and unity. I have not seen anything like that. I have seen cults with consequences. They own your whole life and death, and there is nothing free about it. These people did not have their possessions taken by force. They ran them up there themselves. The apostles did not preach for them to do it. This was an attribute of the new nature in manifestation.

The second mantle was miracles. Two absolute mantles were at fullness, and Ananias and Sapphira did what they did in the face of all of this.

A third mantle was at fullness, because God has no respect of persons. This is the part we have not understood. One mantle was love. Two was miracles and deliverances. The third mantle was instantaneous judgment.

We looked at the judgment of Alexander and Hymenaeus, because they began to corrupt the foundation and speak blasphemy against the truth. They were getting into law, genealogies, basing salvation on it, and greed. Paul turned them over to Satan that they might learn not to blaspheme.
