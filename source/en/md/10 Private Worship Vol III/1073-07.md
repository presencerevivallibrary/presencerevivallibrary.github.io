# Following God's Path Into His Compassion

It is this faith that bypasses the mind. Crying is a universal language. It bypassed her mind and went right for her heart.

I know the difference between self-pity tears and God tears. God tears change things because He is moving in your heart in such a way that you cannot help but melt down. Before you know it, you jump up and start forgiving your enemies instead of shooting them.

Hallelujah, Jesus. I worship You, glorify You, and magnify You.

Several years ago, I came to the end of one key. I had been through fasting, meditation, and other things. Then He visited me during a meeting in Oklahoma City. I was at a passion play, and there was no real presence there. I wondered what it would take to get presence into a meeting like that.

His answer was, "Go home and worship Me all night, and you will find out."

I did not do it. I went home and went to sleep.

It was not until a year later that I was standing here and ended up on the floor. I do not think anybody touched me. I was just standing there, and He revisited me with that same key.

"Are you going to obey Me now?"

I said, "Yes."

So I put instructions before Him: "This much? This much?" I was finding out how much I had to do. It ended up being between a certain amount of hours and another amount, and He did not want me to drop below that until I found out what it was about or could move on my own.

I was a little scared of private worship. I had studied people to see how they got the anointing they carried. I found that some of the most anointed worshiped God excessively in private worship. Some talked about ten to twelve hours a day locked up in their rooms under persecution. I watched magnificent power flow through crowds under them.

But their doctrine was not good.

I made it a point to find out when they received that anointing and why they got it. But I also wanted to know why they could be so far off in the Word.

"Were you judging them?"

No. I just knew what the Word said.

I had put other keys on. Every January, after eating everything in sight through Thanksgiving and Christmas, we would begin the fast. We would go through, "I could not cast him out because of your unbelief; howbeit this kind goeth not out but by prayer and fasting," and we would stop eating.

Fasting was a marvelous key. It is one of the last things people do, so He waited a while. Then I put that key in and unlocked the fasting door.

He also unlocked meditation. He unlocked how to offer the soul on the altar of God's Word, how to meditate, and how to talk to what is trying to come on you.

If you get a pain in your arm, you say, "No, you do not. I got healed of that. You cannot stay in my arm. Get out." What would you do if the only thing you could move was your mouth and the rest of you was pinned down? Would you confess every time you thought about not being able to move?

I had to know what confessing the Word over and over would do. It was not enough to know it doctrinally. He did it, and I saw God do things.

One child in Brazil had his feet turned backward. I prayed, and nothing happened. But at that time we were teaching absolute confession and the law of confession. We taught them about confession. A year later I came back, and the child's feet were on the front.

The man said, "My God, it took me a year to talk those feet back around to the front."

I was amazed. I had to know the difference. I knew that worked.

At first it seemed like vain repetition. "You think you will be heard for your much speaking." But that is when He began unlocking the laws of meditation, what happens inside you when you speak the Word over and over.

If I ask you to read a paragraph once and quote it back, you probably cannot. But if you read it thirty or forty times, you can. What happened in your mind? Can we harness that?

Yes.

That is where we learned the laws of meditation and assimilation, and why we refuse to take a verse out of context. Every verse is a stroke of the brush, building toward the completed picture.

I learned all these keys. Then the last one He put on was love.

I wondered why another man could receive God's love and be used powerfully, yet go wrong. I heard one man who operated in tremendous power say that the tree of the knowledge of good and evil was the tithe, the ten percent. "Touch it and you die."

I thought, "Why do You even use him?"

I cannot tell you how glad I was that I had established the revelation gift inside me.

I can save you a lot of time if you will believe this. When you establish the revelation gift, which is praying in tongues, nothing can take that away from you.

Who is speaking? You are. It is your authority, because the Holy Spirit transfers the language He creates into your spirit. You are doing the praying, and He is answering you.

What mysteries are you speaking? The mysteries hidden in Christ since the foundation of the world, Christ in you, the hope of glory.

Do you think God is praying those mysteries through you because He does not understand something? Are you going to sneak up and tell Him something He does not know? No. It is for your benefit.

As you pray in tongues and assimilate the Word, one Scripture picks up another, and they lock together. Suddenly the meaning comes rushing up into your understanding.

That used to happen to me before I knew who was doing it. I would say, "Hey, Earl, have you ever seen this verse?" Earl would tell me what he thought it meant. Then I would say, "This is what came up in me." He would ask, "Where did you get that?" I would say, "I do not know. It just came up inside. Does that not happen to you?"

I did not know the source.

You have already established the revelation gift. Nothing the devil does can take that away from you. When you begin to operate in the power of God so powerfully that he does not even know his own name, remember this: the path to the power is as sovereign and finished as the cross itself that provided it.

God put love at the end of the ladder for me because He knew that if I had not established the revelation gift to discern and understand all of this, I could have been destroyed by His power.

He gave me the other gift first.

If you trust me, establish yourself in the revelation gift and never get far from it. Then you can launch headlong into the other key, and it will produce the same thing every time: His presence of compassion, manifested in glory and love.

If you are not established in that revelation gift, establish it at the same time.

He unlocked the worship key. It was not really a door. It was a big bank vault with a rolling door. It unlocked, and I cracked it open. Glory came out from behind it in every direction, but I could not open it all the way.

I used to walk back and forth and say, "I do not know. Is this the last key?"

I can tell you now: it is.

God does not have love. He is love. It is the last key because when it takes you into the fullness of what He is, it takes you into love. That is what your new nature is going to carry: the love and power of God.

Paul said no other foundation can be laid than Jesus Christ crucified, the chief cornerstone. Everyone is a building block, but take heed how you build on that foundation. Some build with hay, wood, and stubble; others with precious metals. Everything will be tried by fire.

If everything you are is burned up except being born again, nevertheless you will be saved, yet so as by fire. But he that defiles the temple, him will God destroy. To defile the temple, you must destroy the foundation.

How would you destroy the foundation? By taking Jesus Christ crucified out of the equation.

If He had given me that love too soon, before I was established in the revelation gift, I might have started thinking, "God's love is too great. How can there even be a hell? Jesus died on the cross. It was sufficient. He redeemed all mankind. Everyone is redeemed."

Then the next step is to say that the devil will be saved too.

It only takes the deception of one charismatic person to lead many down that path. To destroy the foundation of the church is to remove the necessity of Jesus Christ crucified.

You see the same danger with some who say Jewish people have their own covenant and do not need Christ. No, they do need Christ. If you tell people they can make it to heaven without Jesus Christ crucified, you are crumbling the foundation.

John knew about love. Jesus entrusted His mother to John. John lived well over a hundred years. He said there is a sin unto death, and he did not say to pray for that. He was not talking about some ordinary sin in a believer's life. He was talking about a sin that gives people no hope by telling them Jesus Christ does not have to be in the formula.

I thank God for the time I spent learning what the revelation gift was. With the revelation gift flowing full force, praying mysteries up and then understanding them, God is getting freer to talk to us about His love.

We are going to be downloaded on by the love of God, and we will have enough revelation to govern the move and the power that will show up when hospitals are emptied.

Compassion can be harnessed. When Jesus was moved by compassion, He healed them all. We are not going to stop short.

With the revelation gift intact, He does not have to pull up short because we go off saying things that cannot be reconciled with His Word.

When you walk into His presence and all restrictions are gone, death has been put away, and the new heaven and new earth have come, you will have capacity to understand why He had to do everything the way He did.

He is just. He is justified in every decision He has ever been forced to make. He does not merely have justice; He is just. He does not merely have love; He is love. He does not merely have sovereignty; He is sovereign.

He moves the way He moves because of who and what He is. If you cannot understand it now, you will when you get there. He cannot lie, and because of that we have truth. When you truly believe truth, He cannot not move.

Keep the revelation gift tuned up. Its brother is meditation and assimilation. Keep both tuned up, and you release Him to pour out His love on you.

The little bit I have received has taken me for a ride I would die for.

When He began manifesting love, it came with forgiveness and other things you would expect. But that love also has power to manifest itself as presence.

In Brazil, He showed me that this presence was His compassion. When He manifested it as a literal presence, something began happening to people.

I searched history and found indications that this had happened before. It is another anointing that belongs to the healing family.

One healing anointing touches the physical body. But another touches roots in the natural that have taken hold in you: hurts, wounds, and things hard to get over. The gifts of healings are plural because He has a gifting and anointing so full of grace and love that He can go in and heal you in a minute.

I was in a powerful meeting and heading toward the door through the crowd. A lot of people had been healed under that man. God said to me, "You know what was healed on them?"

I said, "Sure. Their bodies."

They had received tremendous physical miracles. But then He said, "This anointing you are working with is just beginning as a product of My love."

The physical anointing heals the person you send to church, the one who puts on a nice suit and a nice face and is kind to people. But this anointing heals the one who stays at home.

It heals the one who stays at home.

If you get on the path that goes there, just as surely as tongues works your future by praying the plan of God out ahead of you, and just as surely as the mysteries you need are brought into understanding, God will bring you down the path to what He has called you for.

This other thing is part of that path.

If I had listened to someone's teaching and gone after love first, without the revelation gift, I would have been trying to figure God out. I would have wondered why babies suffer, why He does not heal them, why my wife was taken away, why loved ones were lost. I might have said, "This is a strange way He shows His love," or gone all-inclusive and said, "Do not worry. We are all going to make it."

But now I do not have to be afraid. I have established the revelation gift. I love to divide my time between tongues, worship, and the Word.

There is a deliberate walk into as much of the love of God as you want to walk in. It is as available to you as the cross itself. Otherwise, why offer it?

We have different calls, but loving God and loving people are not separate calls. They belong to all of us.

In your walk, He cannot love you more than He already does, because He is love. But He loves to shed that love on you in manifestation when you can receive it. There is no greater joy for Him than to fellowship with you on the level of His love.

He said to me years ago, "I am love. I cannot love you any more, but you can stop and learn how to receive more of the love I have already given you."

That is what I did. I stopped and found out how to receive the love He has always loved me with. I am on that road now.

When you come back next year, I will not be the same.

Will you?

When I went through my first horrible time in private worship, I do not know how many devils were shipped in, but they were all in that little prayer room. I felt pressed down all over, from heart to toe.

I had already been in there for days saying, "I praise You, worship You, glorify You, and magnify You," while feeling worse every minute. It felt as though when I said, "I love You," the words turned into a hotcake and fell flat on the floor, and I needed a spatula to pick them back up and offer them again.

When it broke off, I understood why Paul did what he did when all hell was loose on him.

I even had a little glimpse of heaven. I could not see the whole background, but I saw angels watching. I kept my hands up in a gesture of worship and said, "I love You. I worship You. I praise You. Hallelujah. Glory."

Some books say that when you hit those times, you are not breaking through. But my stubborn streak was in gear. I was not going to quit.

Later, when I came out the other side and it all lifted, I saw the angels. They were saying things back and forth: "Did you see that? Did you see how much hell was stacked on him to get him to quit? Did you see that he would not put his arms down? He would not stop."

When I thought I was making no progress, I was actually deemed strong enough to plow. Because I would not stop when hell became intense, I tore down the walls by force. I expedited it. I sped it up. I tore down everything between me and Him, and then He came.

Now I am finding out that love is the worthy foe. You cannot worship God and mammon. You will love one and hate the other. When the love of God comes in and slays mammon, it is the worthy foe. It is one of the missing ingredients to everything we have been looking for.

You are not supposed to give with yourself on your mind. You are supposed to give with the sinner on your mind. That is where the love of God brings you.

If you stay with both keys, we will show you Scriptures and put you in the closet so you can start your journey into a presence of the power and love of God that will manifest to sinners with every kind of problem you can think of.

Sometimes I feel so good that I feel guilty.

Stand up and lift your hands.

He cannot lie. His justice is absolute. His immutability means He cannot change, nor can He be changed. Truth is truth. When you truly believe truth, He cannot fail to move.

Just as you could memorize a paragraph after thirty or forty times, do you not know you cannot keep calling Him blessed, worshiping Him with all your heart, and pouring your heart out hour after hour without being affected? Do you think you can soak your spirit in the presence of God, especially His love, and stay the same?

How would you like to have an abiding presence go with the compassion you feel when you see a suffering child?

Lift those hands and worship Him for more.

He is. When they asked Him, "Who are You?" He said, "I am." He is the sum total of everything that is.

Jesus died, and even in our lifetime He put everything right. We get to walk through this body, guaranteed up to a hundred years, and go after our lost brothers and sisters while He finances us.

For those with entrepreneurial callings, do not sit around on a dry limb. If you do not know what to do, we are telling you. He has been looking for you.

Along with all of us operating in the revelation gift, when you take this step, you will not burn out, because you cannot die of something you love more than life.

Hear what the Spirit would say:

Even through this, you will be assisted in coming to the forefront of the operations and diversities of tongues. In this gifting, I use this for all kinds of information. I use it to inform you, establish you, and bring revelation knowledge forward.

There are many diversities to the supernatural language, even addressing the heathen in their own language. Rejoice and know that the closer you draw to Me in My love, the more I am able to trust you and turn you loose with different things.

You have said many times, "I have made up my mind to discipline myself," and each time you failed. But this time you are saying, "I have to get beyond this. I cannot fail this time. I have to stay in until I reach my course."

Do you think for a moment that I do not hear the request of a heart like that? Rejoice, for I have grace sufficient for these things. You will not draw on Me in your own strength. You will draw on Me in the strength of the grace I give you in the standing of My Son.

Do you think I am preparing you for nothing? I am preparing you for these last days. To obey Me now is to take a step forward so you will be on time in your timing. If you discipline yourself now, receive My grace and move forward.

Do you not know that the more you mature in worship, the more fear you will defeat? Be a champion and come forward without fear. Strengthen yourself in Me and say, "I have Your grace; therefore I am strong."

You do not understand the amount of time I put in, praying in tongues, learning to move back and forth by hearing Him and making each move according to what He was saying. But it is a benefit of those who edify themselves through the simple gifting.

When you move forward with this key that casts out fear, you will be able to tell, because your confidence will be lifted higher and you will lose your fear of failure.

He waited long enough for me to understand this so I might teach it to you in a way that you will not fall over it.

Now I am happy.
