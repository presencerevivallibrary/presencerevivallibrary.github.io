# The Spiritual Man and the Spiritual Mind

We have been examining in some detail what Paul meant when he said the Holy Spirit teaches spiritual things to spiritual by comparing them.

He says He compares spiritual things with spiritual. In that word "comparing," when you compare twins, each one has to have attributes that are alike. Without something alike, there is no comparison.

Paul was saying:

> "Which things also we speak,
>
> not in the words which man's wisdom teacheth,
>
> but which the Holy Ghost teacheth;
>
> comparing spiritual things with spiritual."

He is saying that the Holy Ghost teaches by using that language to set up a comparison between the spiritual things of God and spiritual, meaning the part of you that makes you spiritual because you have been born again and received a new nature.

We came into the kingdom of God, translated out of darkness, not knowing everything. But thank God, the day we were born again, we received the fullness of a nature that could be taught and could understand spiritual things.

When you are first born again and receive the Teacher, the Holy Spirit, He compares spiritual things with spiritual. The thing you have that is like God and enables Him to freely make comparisons from one to the other is the new nature you received.

That new nature makes you like God. The best word for it is species. You belong to His species now. You have His nature.

If you want to see something funny, slip a duck egg under a chicken sitting on her nest of eggs. Then watch when the old hen goes down by the pond with her brood.

All of a sudden, that little duck breaks and runs for the water. The mother stops him and says, "You are going to drown."

He says, "I am not going to drown. I can swim."

"No, you cannot. You cannot swim. You are a chicken."

He says, "No, I just know I can swim for some reason."

As many times as she tells him he is a chicken, there is something in him that makes him know he can swim. It is his nature. He does not have the nature of a chicken. He has the nature of a duck.

You have been born again. You do not have the nature of the first Adam. You have the nature of the last Adam. Trust me, you can swim.

The third Person of the Godhead has stepped up inside you. He has come to make the comparison, to cause you to understand the Word that has been spoken before you and to understand spiritual things.

He has come to take the legacy of the great men of God who went before us, left behind in the canon of Scripture, and cause you to understand it.

Kenneth Hagin said when he started the Bible college, "You do not have to start all this from the beginning. I had to start this in the beginning of my life. I fully expect you to pick it up where we left off and take it further."

Why reinvent the wheel? Why start with nothing, void of revelation knowledge, and not pay attention to the canon of Scripture? Men gave their whole lives to leave a legacy behind, hoping that once Paul could get you on the foundation he established in Romans, Galatians, First and Second Corinthians, Colossians, and Ephesians, you would understand that foundation.

The great Teacher has come to take everything they said and cause you to understand it, so you can get off milk and have your foundation. Once you have that foundation, you do not have to fall from it.

Whatever your call is, whether apostle, prophet, teacher, pastor, evangelist, the entrepreneurship of the simplicity of giving, the ministry of helps, governments from president to meter maid, all the armies in between to get the job done, all the way to the prayer ministries of the diversities of tongues, the foundation remains the same.

Very little is said about those offices. We try to go to the Old Covenant and look at the prophets there. We try to derive most examples for men who have been born again from underneath the shadow.

But it is another story now. They operated in the shadow of the rebirth. We operate in the new nature itself. There is a difference.

Yet those ministries are lying there, waiting to be fulfilled. Once a man understands they are in the Church and are the foundation of it, those foundational truths God gave are never meant to be varied from. Never.

When God takes one of these offices you are called to, who are you left to develop that call?

We are sadly lacking in the mighty apostle's ministry. Contrary to what many believe, most equate the apostolic call only with power. In reality, he has all the callings and giftings working in him. He can go anywhere, do anything, and leave any ministry behind in his wake.

He is also used by God to go into trouble spots, because one of his most powerful callings is doctrine. His most powerful call is doctrine.

That is where the Church is mostly defeated: doctrine.

Once you get a man like Joseph Smith who starts the wheel rolling, you have all the sheep that will follow him. Doctrine is a very powerful tool. Satan loves to form doctrine and religion because there is usually one man faithful to keep people in bondage and train his disciples to keep them in bondage generation after generation.

Once you receive your calling and have the foundational truths given to you by the Word of God, for God's sake, how do you think you are going to get by without prayer? How do you think you will get by without praying these mysteries Paul is talking about?

> "Not in the words which man's wisdom teacheth,
>
> but which the Holy Ghost teacheth;
>
> comparing spiritual things with spiritual."

How do you think you are going to get by in your call without using the foundational truths as your standard to grow and live by, and then letting Him separate you to an office people know very little about?

How are you going to do that by sitting under people who say you can pray too much in tongues and get weird? Who do you think they are working for?

There are many people who love God and would do anything for Him. That is why Paul warned against being one who beats the air.

You can be full of energy and willing to do anything for God. You might even establish a monastery and lock people up in it. You might make them take vows of silence.

How much further can you get from:

>

>

Lock yourself up in a monastery and take vows of silence. You wonder, who got to these men?

If you are real, it takes a lot of energy, consecration, and dedication to lock yourself up in a room and shut your mouth.

You say, "At least they are not talking unbelief."

No, they are not. They are living in the greatest amount of it you can live in.

Satan knows how to handle those who are full of energy. He will get you beating the air. It does not matter how much energy you have; what matters is what you spend it for.

If you took that same amount of consecrated time and began to speak those mysteries, letting the Holy Ghost use a language not taught by man's wisdom to set up a comparison between the spiritual things of God and your new nature, you would need that energy when the devil comes and says, "Another troublemaker."

These men got in the way of the Church, got in her path, and said, "This is not right. This is what God said," and turned the tide of history.

You have a new nature, and any way God can get teaching to you, He will get it to you. If God did not want you to know what I know from the Holy Ghost comparing spiritual to spiritual, then what am I doing standing up here?

If He is trying to get into you what He taught me about the Word, which He is, then I am not the only vehicle. If God the third Person came to be your Teacher, this means He is desperate to get this into you.

It does not matter the source. Whether through praying in tongues, through teaching, or through something else, if it is truth, are you able to understand it? Praying in tongues, by this comparison, causes you to begin to understand spiritual things.

That is why the Teacher carries that tool with Him and uses it. It is His power to get our authority to change anything that needs to be changed about us, and that includes filthiness of spirit, which is wrong believing.

If a child rides by on a tricycle and is speaking truth, I can be taught. He wants you to know.

He is no respecter of persons.

The devil has just had a little more time to study this creation. He knows what can defeat a person. He knows how to get zealous people to sit in the mountains of Tibet and stare out a little square window, burn a candle, and challenge the world to get them now.

He knows how to get a man of great energy to spend it doing nothing, to beat the air where it makes no difference.

What he is most afraid of is you who do not beat the air, who spend every moment of that energy doing what produces the change from glory to glory.

Hallelujah.

I love being called, and I love being in the ministry. At times people ask me, "How do you stand up under that stuff?"

I say, "I occupy my two-by-two-foot cubicle. Wherever I am, I bring it with me. I live in it. I live in peace. I carry it with me. I change the atmosphere wherever I go. And if they threaten me, I will let that atmosphere out of that cubicle."

There is peace in it. Hallelujah. There is peace in it.

Why would you walk into anything else?

Torment and fear are the absence of that love. God does not just have love; He is love. I have Him.

Why would a man tear his body up worrying? Why burn the ends of your hair off because your brain is putting out so much energy worrying? Some of your hair even dies, has a funeral, and waves goodbye at each other.

Why would you do that to your body?

"I cannot help it."

He has a lot of instruction in there about how to help it. So why would you stay not helping it?

"It is just the way I am."

Is that some catch-all statement for not changing?

"It is a cultural thing."

Yes, until you met Jesus. Then you became a new creation.

Peace is wonderful. Sometimes I feel guilty because when people are screaming and going down in great arrays of defeat, I do not want to go with them. Sometimes it is hard to feel sorry for them. You can have compassion, but that is different from the limitations of natural love.

With compassion, if you could take their place, you would, because you know that if you could change places with them, you could turn it. You wonder, "Why are they going there? Why do they not believe what the Word says?"

Sometimes I have to go back to where He brought me from and remember a little bit of those feelings before they died, before they were pushed out, transformed by the renewing of the mind. I have to remember how those feelings felt when I am watching someone fly apart like a cheap clock, springs flying everywhere, going to pieces right in front of me.

Then I remember it was a process. You just have to keep them doing the right thing. It is a process of transformation by the renewing of the mind, to prove the good, acceptable, and perfect will of God.

The same way He reveals His perfect will in my life in the call is the same way He transforms me out of torments and fears and reveals peace in me.

But if I am not walking toward one, I am not walking toward the other. It is hard for Him to reveal absolute peace in you without revealing His plan for your life, because it is impossible to walk in the fullness of His peace while saying, "I am going to do what I want."

I love peace. Glory be to God, I love it.

Most teachings keep us in an area where that kind of peace is almost impossible because they do not let us know God enough.

"I need one."

"I have a reason. I have a reason."

People's solutions are okay, but most of them are temporary. God's solutions are permanent.

The only things I am still locked in are the things I did not yet know how to come out of. Every time I learn how to come out, the thing to do is obey, renew myself, and strengthen myself until I come out of those areas.

In a language the world did not know, not in the language man's wisdom teaches, but in the language the Holy Ghost teaches, using that supernatural medium to compare spiritual things with spiritual, He wants us to understand the mystery hidden from ages but now revealed to the great Church of God: the mystery of everything Christ is in you, the hope of glory.

Paul said he was filling up in his flesh what was behind of the afflictions of Christ for His body's sake, which is the Church. Nobody preaches on that verse because they do not know what it means.

What he was actually talking about is this: he did not care what kind of sufferings befell him, and at the time he wrote this epistle, he was in prison. He says, "Meanwhile, there is being filled up in me of the sufferings of Christ, that which still lacks in my flesh."

He said, "I can tell you why. It is for the Body of Christ, for the body's sake, which is the Body of Christ."

Everything in my flesh that Jesus suffered and died for, and yet is still alive, Paul says, "Fill it up with Your sufferings, Jesus. Fill it up for the body's sake, so I will be counted worthy to minister to them, fully equipped and qualified."

He was.

Does it make you want to pray in tongues?

It is this Man, this God-Man, the mighty Word by whom all things were created, that the Holy Ghost is trying to reveal in you.

The substance for the chair you are sitting on came from which Person of the Godhead? Him, the deity of Jesus. All substance came from His Person. Through Him, thrones, dominions, and invisible things belonging to another world, such as angels, were created. The substance and consistency of what things consist of came from His individual being.

The worlds, universes, and vastness of God seen in creation came from the second Person. He became this Man whose spirit was born from the Father, so He would become the firstborn of this creation also, and could be the Head of your creation.

In all things He has preeminence and authority as the Head of the Church, to be revealed and to become everything He said He is and will be, as revealed by the Holy Spirit in you.

So for God's sake, the Holy Ghost wants to pray to reveal Him in you.

Turn Him loose.
