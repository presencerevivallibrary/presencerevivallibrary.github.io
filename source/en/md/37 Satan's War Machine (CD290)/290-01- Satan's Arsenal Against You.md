These spirits seduce, tempt, harass, and try to wear you down like a drop of water until you concede and begin to walk the path of least resistance.

You end up sedated in a chair. You and those who teach you have been slapped so hard that you are tired of charging hell with a water gun. You just sit there, be a nice person, go to work, go home, and die and go to heaven.

"Is there any way I can avoid being slapped? Do not make waves."

Then the devil singles you out for practice. He says, "I am a good killing devil. This person has potential because they heard too much Word. What if they returned from the vomit they have been eating to the gospel? What if they returned and tore hell up? I would have to answer to Satan. I had better get those cancer devils to pay them a visit and get them out of here."

Then you will wish you had charged for God full bore while His Word worked for you. It always worked for you.

I can tell you one thing: if you get into strife and rebellion, I do not care what God has called you to, you will have to straighten that up before He can heal you. Do you think He will heal you to dance with the devil? I am not kidding, my soul.

Let us take a final scenario. Suppose that as pastor you decide one day that God meant what He said:

> Give yourself continually to the word of God and prayer.

That is your job: feed the flock.

So you decide to let governments govern while you feed. You say, "I am responsible for eternity for these people. What a responsibility. What a calling. I am going to feed."

You lock yourself up in a pure vein of the Holy Ghost and stay there until feeding time. You pray six to eight hours a day with flurries of worship in between.

Consumed with God, He teaches you how to spend the day with Him if you knock on His door. You learn to flow in and out of things nobody can teach you but Him.

Two years go by, and things in the church may even get worse. Bad services, people leaving. Two or three years go by, and suddenly things begin to change a little. Four years go by, but your faith is what delivers you to that closet because the Word of God says it.

All the time, God has been out ahead of you, working glorious realms, getting ready for an outpouring. Finally the Holy Ghost moves every mountain and gets it into place.

One day you are standing behind the pulpit like normal. You may have long ago dropped the soul emphasis in your preaching. Whether it is shouting with an evangelist or teaching melody with a teacher, it is not what your soul does to the words. It is what the Holy Ghost does. Whether you are shouting or whispering, please be anointed. Go home or go home.

Suddenly, in the Spirit, you hear a noise. The ceiling rolls back like a tuna can. A big hand has the key. Liquid sunshine pours out, splashing in every direction with rainbow colors all over the auditorium.

There is a deaf section over here, forty or fifty people being signed to. Over here, people have been bringing in the homeless. Over there are people from the rest home, sitting in wheelchairs connected to bladder bags. We have been preaching miracles, so they are bringing them in.

When that liquid sunshine pours, chaos follows. Deaf people begin to hear. There is a commotion. Bladder bottles and IVs drag along as people walk out of wheelchairs. People who were destroyed by drugs wake up and say, "Where am I?"

"What is the last thing you remember?"

"Haight-Ashbury. I was licking the stamp."

"You are in church, and this is thirty years later."

In the natural, deaf people call their mothers: "I told you God would do it, Mom. Let me call all your deaf friends." Soon airplanes are loading up. Elderly people walk out and say, "I shed my bladder bottle yesterday and thought I would drop in for tea. Do not put me back in that place or I will have you arrested." People delivered from drugs go out collecting their friends, saying, "Something is going on. His glory has been poured out."

Hallelujah. In the natural, we know how that escalates. God opened the portal to heaven, and you did it because you found out somebody can do that.

If you are going to go somewhere you have never been, you can do something you have never done. If what you are doing is not working, get a clue. Before you call me wrong, taste the water.

What happens in the spirit? Thousands of unclean spirits have come out. They are coming out by the hundreds of thousands. They step over in the corner of the church and say, "We have to do something about this. We had better report to the ruler of darkness. We told him he should assign more people to stop the pastor from praying. Is this our fault? No, this is not our fault. Now we have to petition seven more wicked spirits for every one of these people getting free."

The ruler of darkness then has to collect devils from one end of the state to the other and start sending them in to try to break this up. That is where the fight begins.

Do you remember a few years ago when people were going up in hot-air balloons?

"What are you doing up in that hot-air balloon?"

"I am binding the principality of the air."

They went up in airplanes. "What are you doing?"

"I am getting up where the principality of the air is."

Let me tell you something. If you cause revival like that through prayer, even before you are done praying, like Paul, you will not have to go up in a hot-air balloon to look for him. He will come looking for you. He will find you. Forget the balloon.

His low-level spirits will report to a ruler of darkness, and he will come crashing into your room. He will try everything he can. If he fails, he says, "I have to appeal to a power. I have to go higher."

Then they will try to counterfeit something. "We have to bring this move into sensationalism. Get some devils in there to crow and bark. Drive it past the Holy Ghost. If we can do it, we can take a lot of people with us."

Finally, the power appeals to a principality. He tries to stop it, but he cannot stop you, like Paul. At that point, you find the sufficiency of grace. But if you do not know what is happening, he will stop you.

This is why William Branham, with religious spirits and destructions of reasonings, began to think he was Elijah to come. This is why John Alexander Dowie, after God used him mightily in healing, began to think he was Elijah to come. The day he announced it, they tell me, he sat down and never preached again.

These spirits destroy reasonings.

George Jeffreys was another. I asked an intercessory woman who lived here before she went home to be with Jesus, "Who was the mightiest healing evangelist you ever saw? Was it Kathryn Kuhlman? I know she stayed in your home."

She said, "No."

"Was it Jack Coe?"

"No," she said. "It was a man from England who made a pass through America by the name of George Jeffreys."

They brought fields of people in. He would say, "No man has focused this man, Jesus," and people began to get up all over the field. Ambulances and everything. Blind eyes snapped open. God used him especially for arthritis.

After thirty years of ministry, destructions of reasonings came and convinced him that Britain was a lost tribe of Israel. He started preaching British Israelism and also lost his life over it.

Satan knows his business.

Paul said the messenger of Satan was after him because of the abundance of his revelations. He said, in effect, "You only know what I found out. I lost everything. I was stripped of everything. I lost family. In the middle of being stripped, I found out I had nothing else left, nothing to turn to. Three times the Lord told me, 'My grace is sufficient for thee.' When I found out what that grace was, it switched my dependency off the flesh and onto the Spirit. When there was nothing left, that was when I found out how strong He was, because I depended on nothing else."

Nothing else.
