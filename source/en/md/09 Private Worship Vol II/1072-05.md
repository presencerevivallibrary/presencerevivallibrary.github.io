# He Cannot Love Himself in You for You

Tonight we will designate 10 o'clock as the official time, and then we will release you. Leading into this, He wanted me to do some teaching, but He came and has been taking the service.

Wednesday night I was trying to recall the prophecy. Near the end of the line, He came and began to talk to me about the kind of love this worship is going to develop in you. Prophecy is in proportion to the faith given you, and all prophecy is given for edification, exhortation, and comfort. In order to exhort from something, there has to be a basis for the exhortation.

Romans 12 says, "He that prophesieth, let him prophesy according to the proportion of faith." What I prophesy must be in proportion to what I am walking in of faith and revelation. Last night I understood the principle He prophesied, but I would never have been able to put it together had He not come in the spirit of prophecy and exhorted on what I already knew.

When He pulled it together, I got a look at love and God's position in it that I had never known before. I did not know what to do for about ten minutes. I had to ask Gary, "What did I say?"

It went something like this: of all the things He has given us grace for, He has traded our standing for His, our weaknesses for His strength, our old nature for His new one, and on and on. But then He said something about love and its position in grace.

He said, "I cannot love Myself in you for you."

Say that again. The one thing He cannot do by grace, this one is up to us. He cannot love Himself in you for you.

I had never heard anything like that in my life. It made me know that the basis of the new nature has everything in it and has the capacity for you to develop your love for Him, but you have to do it. He cannot love Himself in you for you.

This means we have a nature with the capacity to hold His love. Private worship is a key that begins to develop that nature, where it comes forward and you begin to love Him out of the nature He gave you. It has to be developed.

So now these newborn babes coming into the kingdom have ordained strength to still the enemy. He made the emphasis on both: "Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings thou hast ordained strength" and "thou hast perfected praise."

When you come into the kingdom, unless you are converted and become as one of these children, you do not come in fully developed, knowing everything. But you do come in with a new nature and a capacity to be developed and to renew your mind. You have a mind that can discern spiritual things.

Even more than that, as a newborn babe in the kingdom, you have a spirit with the capacity to reservoir the love of God, to develop it and hold what He pours into you.

If you want to know the truth, you would not be sitting here if you did not have that capacity. But as you have found out, the ability to discern truth and knowledge is not enough. Even a high IQ is no protection against a mystery-destroyer of reasonings. One person's IQ may be off the board, but that is no protection.

Knowledge is not enough. All that is written of God is so that you can know God.

When He says faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the Word of God, there was no way to be saved until you heard the Word of salvation. Then you acted upon it and were born again. The standard is the same for everything.

Faith comes by hearing the Word of God because you look at a problem, disease, or something you are facing and find out what God said about it. In a simple form, you are saying, "He wrote down what He will do about that problem. Now I know what He said He will do. Now believe it."

Do not make a big mystical thing out of faith. Faith is, "He will do that." But believing He will do something is still different from knowing Him. You can believe a business associate will do what they say because of reputation, but when you are talking about getting to know someone, you are talking about something else.

When He made you a partaker of His divine nature, you received all the equipment you needed to get to know Him in fellowship. That is connected with love. In that connection, when you get to know Him who said these things, your faith will go off the board.

If you get it backwards, you try to develop yourself to believe somebody you do not know very well. What are you going after here? You are going to end up going after private worship until you get enough of a dose of it that it becomes a lifestyle, something you can hardly do without.

Basically, we will do corporate worship and praise. Praise is when you sing about the mighty works of God and who He is. Pure veins of worship are generally you to Him. It is your adoration to Him, how much you love Him, you and Him.

He rises up, and there is a groaning that says, "I hate where I am in my life." The Holy Ghost says, "Good, because when you did not care, I had no faith to change it with."

Because almost all of them tried it and were turned back. They believed what they saw, heard, felt, or faced in circumstances, or they had things in their lives they were going to hold on to. They got tired of being delivered to the problem over and over to deal with it.

We have had people say, "I am not going to pray. I do not want to pray. I do not even want to want to."
