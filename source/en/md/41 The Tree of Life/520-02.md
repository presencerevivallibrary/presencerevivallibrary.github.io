# Lucifer: The Anointed Cherub That Covereth

Go back to Hebrews 9. We will begin at verse 23, then we will be in John 8 again, then Ezekiel. This time we are going to make it to Genesis, because this is just review.

Hebrews 9:23 says:

> It was therefore necessary that the patterns of things in the heavens should be purified with these; but the heavenly things themselves with better sacrifices than these.

He is speaking of sacrifice, animal blood. The pattern, the copy, the figure of the things in heaven, had to be purified with these. But the heavenly things themselves had to be purified with better sacrifices.

Verse 24 says:

> For Christ is not entered into the holy places made with hands, which are the figures of the true; but into heaven itself, now to appear in the presence of God for us.

The thing made with hands was the figure of the true. It was the pattern. But Christ entered into heaven itself.

He did not offer Himself often, as the high priest entered into the holy place every year with the blood of others. If that had been necessary, He would have had to suffer or continue to offer Himself year after year since the foundation of the world. But now, once in the end of the world, He has appeared to put away sin by the sacrifice of Himself.

He has not been offered since the foundation of the world. He only came once. Just one time, retrospective, and it took the entire future in.

Notice His coming was so final that Hebrews calls it the end of the world.

I understand that I am a new creation, old things have passed away, and all things have become new. But the depth that the fall and the curse reached was so great that God said the ground was cursed. When this thing is over in the last dispensation, the elements are going to pass away with fervent heat. There will be a new heaven and a new earth.

When the ocean yields her dead, and death, hell, and the grave deliver up their dead, and the great white throne judgment comes, everybody is going to be told why they are where they are. God is just and justified in every judgment He is forced to render. Nobody will go to hell without God being able to explain the hour of visitation and the way out.

Somebody says, "What about an island where they never heard?"

If one person lifted his face to heaven and said, "I want to know the truth," God would have translated another Philip in a moment.

That place is so final. The old things have passed away. You and I have become citizens of the kingdom. We live in a natural world subject to natural law, but we serve the Spirit. We serve the One who is the Father of all natural law. We are not its servant. It is our servant.

He so ended the world the day He died that it carries into the dispensations to come. At the great white throne, God will look one look into the universe and it will flee away, replaced by a new heaven and a new earth. All the old came to a central end somewhere in this universe when one Man, the Son of God, died and ended it so it could yield up another one.

That is the place where you leave your prayers. My God, to think they would not be answered.

I have been prophesying more and more out of this place. The difference now is that the Holy Spirit has taken the love of God and magnified that love in my nature. It has caused me to understand how God operates out of the love in His nature.

I am prophesying more and more out of a place of peace, a place called done, a place where God changes things because the things that should not be were ended right there.

If you can believe it, your old man died right there. Your diseases died right there. Your defeats died right there.

You can walk into a thicket of jungle with the witch doctor's face painted white and a machete made in Taiwan and receive it. He cannot kill you because you already died.

Someone says, "Brother Roberson, when should I go into a place like that?"

When you are ready. Otherwise, you might leave the planet there.

I am prophesying, praying, and leaving things there because Hebrews says this Man only had to offer Himself once at the end of the world. God formed the earth, put us on it, set the fiery center, the axis, and the poles. Under the old covenant, the priest would have had to die year after year. But not this Man. Once, at the end of the world.

If you believe it, it all ended there.

Is it not interesting that two thousand years after Adam, the flood ended it? Two thousand years later, the cross ended it.

We understand then that everything here is a copy and a pattern. Hebrews was talking about redemption and things made with hands. But Jesus had to go into heaven itself to purge the things all of this was a copy of.

Would it not stand to reason that the house we live on, this planet, started out with the Garden of Eden and is going to end up with something much more elaborate than that?

You are going to be thirty-three years old in a glorified body, good-looking. We will have all our hair back. Our pear shapes will be back, and not an upside-down pear either. When you see old prophets running around heaven with sticks, they are not old. Jesus was cut off at thirty-three. You will not get a day older than the Substitute got.

What most people hate when they find this out is that Jesus said they neither marry nor are given in marriage in heaven, but in that respect are like the angels. You will look really good, but you are not going to marry.

Moving right along.

Since the things that had to be redeemed were a copy, a figure, and a pattern, would it not stand to reason that the house we live on is a copy? It is a little house exactly like His. Why would there not be a garden He lived in?

When God called Lucifer the anointed cherub that covered, and He put Adam in the garden to dress it, the thing Lucifer covered was the garden.

At this point, let us look at God's house.

Go to Ezekiel 28:13. Once again, briefly passing in review, God was prophesying to Lucifer. Usually He identifies the seat of operation, where Satan was operating from. If He had prophesied in this last century, He might have said, "O Lucifer, I know where you are: Hitler." That is the way He is talking here.

In verse 9 He says:

> Wilt thou yet say before him that slayeth thee, I am God?

Then in verse 13 He is still prophesying:

> Thou hast been in Eden the garden of God; every precious stone was thy covering...

Sardius, topaz, diamond, beryl, onyx, jasper, sapphire, emerald, carbuncle, and gold.

Then:

> The workmanship of thy tabrets and of thy pipes was prepared in thee in the day that thou wast created.

Verse 14 says:

> Thou art the anointed cherub that covereth; and I have set thee so.

If you are going to underline anything, underline the class of angel he was: a cherub, cherubim. He was the anointed cherub that covered.

God says:

> Thou wast upon the holy mountain of God; thou hast walked up and down in the midst of the stones of fire.

Then:

> Thou wast perfect in thy ways from the day that thou wast created, till iniquity was found in thee.

Why would God's house not be like ours? Why would the creation be so different?

I always wondered what Lucifer covered. I was not yet in a place to understand it. Knowing that the pipes were his ability to worship or lead worship, I thought he went out and covered the universe and opened creation up to worship. That is partly true. But the thing he was really covering was the garden.

Whatever his description was, it must have been incredible. When God built our house, the design sounds so close to His, especially when we see the things that are coming: the new heaven, the new earth, the holy city, the Tree of Life, and the river.

Everything is so close. Why would our house, if we are His sons and daughters, not be like His? When He placed Adam in our garden to dress it and cover it, it was the same reason He placed Lucifer, a cherubim archangel class, to cover His house.

This is amazing.

There is only one origin for iniquity, and that was Satan.

I wish I had some idea how old this creature was. But in combat with him, the wisdom he has and the way he operates tell me we are dealing with someone very lethal. He terminated the first creation in two thousand years. If we do not do something, he is going to have more people than we do before this one is terminated.

He is lethal. God only knows whether it was millions of years that he was with Him before iniquity was found in him.

But I do know that when Satan went after us, he already knew, from holding the position he held and covering what he covered, what we were worth to God. He knew who we were and what we were to Him. He mostly knew because of the difference in our creation. When God called us sons and daughters, Satan understood there was something different about us.

When he instigated the fall and carried it out, one thing he knew was that if God could come after us and get us, whatever God used to come after us with, there would be no better way.

Do you think God does something secondary? Do you think He does not have the ability to see whether something is perfect? Lucifer knew this about God.

So when God came after us, Satan knew there could be no other way.

When Jesus wrestled through that moment in the garden, you would not even want to know the forces of hell in play at that moment. He prayed until His sweat became as great drops of blood. You know as well as I do that there is not a prayer Jesus has ever uttered that was not answered by God.

This one was answered too.

He said, "If it be possible, let this cup pass." Possible is the opposite of impossible. It meant, "God, exhaust Yourself. Let this cup pass, but only if there is another way You can save mankind."

You have to know God answered that prayer. If there had been another way, the only thing that would have been damaged is you and I. We would have had to lose something. We would not have come out as sons.

There was no other way.

Do you think the devil did not watch Him? He knew all he had to do was study how God went after you and counterfeit it with religion. He gives you another way out, unbelief, or not even making it past wanting to sell out. He can take you to where you will not even try because you do not have enough faith to believe that if you tried, you could ever go on.

He is lethal. He is satanically wise. He has been around a long time. He is a murderer from the beginning of our creation, full of iniquity, a liar, the father of it. There is no truth in him. None.

When he comes into your presence, he only does it for one reason in three parts: to steal, kill, and destroy. He is not good at anything else. He does not do anything else.

So what did the Holy Spirit come for? To substitute truth for you? So that if you did not know any, He could say, "That is all right. I am God. I will just take care of it"? No. He came to teach you what God said so you could believe it.

If someone full of the Holy Ghost has a disease, the Holy Ghost did not fail in His job. Listen, if your brain was a power plant, I can hear it working. What do you have to say in all of this?

This Man only had to offer Himself once at the end of the world.

Hold it. That was two thousand years ago. My old man ended there. My defeat ended there. My disease ended there. This planet is collapsing into a finish, and it ended there. I am not going to wait until everything is handed to me to operate out of the victory He got for me there.

Hallelujah.

This town is my home, and I am not going to let the devil have it. Not without a fight? No. There is no fight to it. He has already been defeated. I am just flat not going to let him have it.

Go to John 8:32. Jesus was speaking to the Jews, and some of them believed in Him. When they did, He said:

> And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.

That got a response out of them. They answered, "We are Abraham's seed. We were never in bondage to any man. How can You say we shall be made free?"

Jesus answered:

> Whosoever committeth sin is the servant of sin.

It does not matter if you are Abraham's seed.

Verse 35 says:

> And the servant abideth not in the house for ever: but the Son abideth ever.

The slave of the household, the hired servant, is not going to inherit the house. Only the son will inherit the house.

They might be Abraham's seed. They might be under the law and all those things. But as long as they have a nature of sin, they are not going to inherit the house.

Jesus said they would know the truth and it would make them free. That is the truth He was talking about: they would be born again, set free from sin, receive a new nature, and become sons and daughters of God.

Verse 36 says:

> If the Son therefore shall make you free, ye shall be free indeed.

He is talking about being born again.

Then He made reference to the nature they had:

> I know that ye are Abraham's seed; but ye seek to kill me, because my word hath no place in you.

Then:

> I speak that which I have seen with my Father: and ye do that which ye have seen with your father.

They answered, "Abraham is our father."

Jesus said:

> If ye were Abraham's children, ye would do the works of Abraham.

But they sought to kill Him, a Man who told them the truth He heard from God. Abraham did not do that. Abraham could not have done what they were doing, because he lived by what was revealed to him.

Jesus said:

> Ye do the deeds of your father.

They said, "We are not born of fornication. We have one Father, even God."

Jesus answered:

> If God were your Father, ye would love me: for I proceeded forth and came from God.

Then:

> Why do ye not understand my speech? even because ye cannot hear my word.

They were spiritually dead. They had no capacity.

Then He said:

> Ye are of your father the devil, and the lusts of your father ye will do.

I like it because when Jesus got onto this teaching, He opened the other part for me.

He said:

> He was a murderer from the beginning, and abode not in the truth, because there is no truth in him.

Passing in review: what beginning? Ezekiel said he was perfect first, so he could not have been a murderer from his own beginning. What beginning was Jesus talking about?

Whichever beginning He means, He says Satan was a murderer from the beginning and did not abide in the truth because there is no truth in him. When he speaks a lie, he speaks of his own, for he is a liar and the father of it.

Not the father of liars, but the father of it: the father of whatever caused lying to come into existence, iniquity.

The beginning Jesus was talking about could not be the beginning we have been talking about for months, the Genesis beginning, the one John talked about in John 1:1 and Genesis 1:1:

> In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.

That was the beginning before you, me, or anything we know to exist had a beginning: our creation, our planets, our Milky Ways. God existed eons before that beginning.

The beginning Jesus was talking about was ours, and Satan was already a murderer when he came into our creation.

Look around you. Look at religion. Look at what has been robbed. Are you going to let him rob you from what you can walk into?

You are up against something older than our universe. That is why God came to tell you, "When I teach you truth, I come and personally do it. Then I come to live with you in the person of the Holy Spirit to teach you who you are."

That is where your victory is based. Who are you? What has He put at your disposal? Who are you up against? How wise is he? What are you doing? Who are you leaning on, and whose wisdom are you seeking?

Look at the substitutes Satan has offered. Look at the alternative forms of religion he has turned to. Look what he has done in reincarnation. First the devil gives you a death nature, then tells you that you cannot live according to God's standard, then sells you some form of religion that sends you to hell. He tells you that you are not good enough to see God, and it is all right if you die in residence enough times, reincarnating and coming back, until eventually you earn an audience with God.

Who are you up against? Whoever he is, you are well equipped to handle it.

You talk about a nursery. "I cannot read. I do not have a Bible. You got me saved on an island, and I do not know anything."

God says, "That is all right. You can be filled with the Holy Spirit, and for you He will bring a language from heaven to do all your praying for you."

I cannot make it any easier. Not only will God originate your prayer, He will answer it after He passes it through the authority you have and prays it.

Go to Genesis. Do you think the devil is happy with this? He is always trying to interrogate me.

Genesis 2:7 says:

> And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul.

Man found himself to be a spiritual being with a physical body, living through a soul.

God is not dependent on air. Air is a creation of His. He is not codependent; He does not need to breathe something He created. He was spawning a spirit inside this man He would call His son.

Verse 8 says:

> And the LORD God planted a garden eastward in Eden; and there he put the man whom he had formed.

I like that word "formed." It is the same as telling you that He took a single strand of DNA, the blueprint, to form the man.

Verse 9 says:

> And out of the ground made the LORD God to grow every tree that is pleasant to the sight, and good for food.

Then:

> The tree of life also in the midst of the garden, and the tree of knowledge of good and evil.

Catch the wording. The Lord God planted a garden eastward in Eden. There He put the man He had formed. Out of the ground He made every tree grow that was pleasant to the sight and good for food.

Then He pauses and says, "the Tree of Life also." It is in conjunction with what was going on there, but He puts it in another category. The Tree of Life also came into the garden, in the midst of the garden. And on this side of the fence also was the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil.

There seems to be a pause between the trees that were good for food, made to eat, caused to grow out of the ground, and the Tree of Life. He put them in another category.

Then verse 10 says:

> And a river went out of Eden to water the garden; and from thence it was parted, and became into four heads.

The first was Pison, which compassed the land of Havilah, where there was gold, bdellium, and onyx. The second river was Gihon, which compassed Ethiopia. The third was Hiddekel, which goes toward Assyria. The fourth, the one we hear the most about, was Euphrates.

Then verse 15 says:

> And the LORD God took the man, and put him into the garden of Eden to dress it and to keep it.

Lucifer was the anointed cherub that covereth. Adam was put into the garden to dress it and keep it.

Go to the end of Genesis 3. This is after they fell. I want to leave you with something.

Verse 20 says:

> And Adam called his wife's name Eve; because she was the mother of all living.

Eve means life. Do you want to know where every human being came from? Somebody asks, "Where did the sons and daughters get their wives?" Eve was the mother of all living. Everybody came from her. She came from Adam.

Verse 21 says:

> Unto Adam also and to his wife did the LORD God make coats of skins, and clothed them.

This was the first animal bloodshed. Something had to die for even a basic need like that to be met. It was the first sacrifice.

Verse 22 says:

> And the LORD God said, Behold, the man is become as one of us, to know good and evil.

When the devil used that as bait, he was not lying in that part. He said, "You will become as gods, knowing good and evil." God acknowledged that this had happened.

Then God said:

> And now, lest he put forth his hand, and take also of the tree of life, and eat, and live forever...

People sometimes think this means Adam and Eve would inhabit the earth forever, living like they were and living like they were. But what would happen is that they would live beyond their body and, in a spiritually dead state, go into incarceration with no redemption.

At the end of this dispensation, they would not hop around on this planet killing each other for eternity. Whatever happened there, if they ate of the tree, it would have caused them to live forever in an unredeemable state.

It is hard to imagine walking over to some kind of tree, picking food off it, eating it, and having that happen to you. But notice again, God said the man had become as one of Us, knowing good and evil. Satan was not lying in that part of the temptation. Now, lest Adam put forth his hand and take also of the Tree of Life, eat, and live forever:

> Therefore the LORD God sent him forth from the garden of Eden, to till the ground from whence he was taken.

Verse 24:

> So he drove out the man; and he placed at the east of the garden of Eden Cherubims, and a flaming sword which turned every way, to keep the way of the tree of life.

I will leave you with this thought: what were these cherubim doing there?

Lucifer was the anointed cherub that covered. These angels were of his class, his stature, his power. Why did God have to place these at the Tree of Life to keep Adam from eating?

Is that not amazing? Adam was a little spiritually dead man now. Why did God need these angels?

It was not just to stop Adam from gaining access. It was to stop Lucifer from gaining access for them. Once Adam and Eve became accountable for evil, Satan wanted them to go ahead and commit whatever they could commit to seal their doom forever and ever.

Whatever it was, Lucifer committed it. That is why there was no redemption for him, nor for the third of the angels who did the same thing.

What makes you think it was so different in his garden from this one?

We will stop there.
