# Comparing Spiritual With Spiritual

How many of you have been getting that letter we did and had time to go through it a little bit?

Hallelujah. Barbara was sure excited about it. I have the finish of it again in the last ten pages or so, so I am going to go into that a little bit. Last week we introduced another level of depth through the new nature, and I want to continue from that.

How many remember when I first broke through and God visited me in my office, and I got my first look down the Born-Again Trail? I can remember before the first camp meeting when I began to preach it. Sue, I think, asked me, "What are you going to teach on in the camp meeting?"

I said, "I am going to preach on being born again."

It was to a bunch of preachers, so Sue said, "Yeah, right. Can't you see he is just kidding?"

I was a little lost in the Spirit, thinking about the revelation and how it was going to unfold, and it just came out of my spirit: being born again. Of course, the Born-Again Trail was tagged on later.

I am not going to make it very far, am I?

Hallelujah.

I remember when I was in that rapport with Him, communing in my heart, and He was answering everything as though the gift of faith was on me. The communion was over everything He said I could have, and I related it to those great verses:

> "All things are possible to him that believeth."

I said, "Where is that? We know the Bible says that, but so few ever break into anything even similar to that."

I asked, "Where are the verses that make the transition from just falling into the crowd and having a normal, mediocre operation with God? I am not talking about what men can accomplish in the natural. I am talking about bringing God here, instead of just following in mediocrity, complacency, and being beaten into submission by pressure until you finally give up. Where are those verses?"

That is when He told me they are in most of the verses people leave out.

As you know, the Born-Again Trail consists mostly of the pieces that have been left out of the walks everybody has given us. It is not that the trail is so great in itself; it is that these are the pieces left out. When you begin to understand what you have, where you can go, how to fellowship with God, what He is after, and the part of you that can understand Him, the limit is off.

I get to go as far as I want to in God.

I was having a good time up there because I found there was healing in the presence that comes from touching Him with your spirit. I have been having the greatest fun with that healing that is in the presence when it comes.

I say, "Come over here," and they get healed.

One man brought his fiancee. I think she had never been in a service where people fall down. He brought her over and said she had kidney problems, and she had pain in her kidneys then and had it all the time.

I said, "You had better come over here and see."

He came. The presence just filled the whole atmosphere. He took all her pain. Meanwhile, she fell down on the ground. Her knees buckled and she fell.

Later, when she got up, I said, "Do you know about the falling down and everything?"

She said, "No."

I said, "Why did you fall down there?"

She said, "I do not know."

But she was healed.

We were finding out that the devil reigns through the death nature the same way God reigns through our new nature. Satan used the fear of death to reign over the human race.

It is no harder for Satan to get a man who has the sin nature to sin than it is to get a swarm of piranhas to eat a cow after you throw it into the Amazon. That is the nature of sin.

Satan used the fear of death to manipulate the human race. He kept giving us answers that were not answers, such as Buddhism, Mohammedism, and all these things that promise some kind of life after death because of the fear of death.

Spiritual death is what Satan uses to reign with. That is where he gets his power.

Thank God, God reigns through the hidden man of the heart, the spirit man, with the new nature. The sin nature moved on us, and we obeyed it on different levels, from Hitlers to nice tax-paying citizens who keep the law, have their beer busts, run their four-wheel drives at each other, cuss a lot, and are just good old boys who take care of their families. They yield to the sin nature in all degrees. It just depends on who got hold of you and trained you.

There were little grandmas who baked bread and sent little Hans off to war. They did not know what was going on in the war until they were sent into the concentration camps as part of the cleanup program. They made the grandmas look at heaps of bodies piled up and starved to death. The grandmas were nauseated and sick.

But not little Hans, whom they had baked bread for and sent off to war when he was thirteen. Not little Hans. He had already been trained in the fathomless depths of that unregenerated sin nature. They were over there joking and eating while the little grandmas were throwing up.

Their spirits were trained. It just did not bother them.

All of you who had a sin nature had that same capacity. The sin nature moved on our reasonings and our mind and got us to obey it on one level or another.

But now my new nature is moving on my mind on one level or another. It is my decision. I am a free-will moral agent. How much I yield to it is my decision.

The more I pray in the Holy Ghost, the more I turn that new nature loose in my soul, and the more my decisions are based on how free I am in that new nature.

You can just sit in church, barely make it into church, barely make it into heaven, and barely get out of bed. You are a free-will moral agent. It is your choice what you want to do with what He gave you. Absolutely your choice.

You can yield to that new nature moving on you at any level and stop at any level. You can enter heaven with nothing more than being born again, sitting around in church saying, "I am okay. I am going to church."

Yes, you are okay as far as making it to heaven, but you are not okay. You need a good dose of what it feels like to lose your reward.

Or you can yield to the new nature, conquer, and reign through righteousness.

I love this stuff.

How many are going to reign? I happen to know I am.

"How do you know you are?"

Because He said I am. I have what I say.

Hallelujah. Look out, devil. You already had your time with me. My turn. I am going to get so much of God that I will make you turn loose of my friends, disease and everything else. In fact, I am going to make you turn loose of a whole lot of the population of humanity.

Devil, I do not like you very much. I plan on taking a lot of your friends away from you.

Glory be to God forever.

There was not even a microfiber of anything anywhere in her entire being that did not totally succumb to a wisdom so strong it did not just leave her surrendered; it left her stunned.

Yes, they were exceedingly happy.

I said, "Why particularly? Just hearing a man's wisdom ten or eleven hours a day, would you not get tired?"

He said, "Son, let Me ask you something. How many preachers do you know that you could sit in their presence that long every day just to hear what they had to say?"

I said, "None. I get tired of myself."

He went on to explain, "Your spirit is not like your body. If you quit feeding your body, it will die. But if you quit feeding your spirit, it will continue to exist. It does not need substance to survive. It is eternal and self-existing. If you do not feed your spirit, it will not quit existing; it will quit developing."

You can be in the same spiritual place your whole lifetime that you were when you were first saved.

If you do not feed your spirit, you will not develop. The only reason our rewards will be different in heaven is not because of the difference in our calls, but because all of us received the same thing when we were born again. At the high price of our individuality, we choose what we will do with it.

There is something about our will and right to choose that God values as highly as anything else He does in all creation. If you think we are all going to get to heaven as generic little copies of each other with no individuality, think again.

If we did not have the power to choose more or less of God, then the reward would all be the same. But it is not.

Before I was born again, I inherited a sin nature from the first man Adam. I did not inherit Adam's individual human spirit, but I did inherit the spiritual death that was in his spirit. Adam has his own spirit. I have mine.

After I was born again, I did not inherit Jesus' individual human spirit. I have my own. But what I did inherit was the spiritual life that was in His spirit. Jesus has His own spirit. I have mine. What I received from Him was a new nature.

As individual as I was when I had a sin nature is how individual I am now that I have a new nature.

The same way the old nature would move on my emotions and reasonings to sin, the new nature now moves on my emotions and reasonings to do righteous works.

All sinners move on different levels of sin as they respond to the sin nature, from good productive taxpayers and citizens to the overcrowded conditions of our penal systems. In the same way, all believers respond on different levels to the new nature, from those who serve themselves all the way to those who have given their whole lives as servants of God.

Our individuality is still intact. We are just being influenced by another nature.

Hallelujah.

The only part of us the Holy Spirit has to work with is our new nature. It is the only part of us that can understand spiritual things and grow in God.

The reason our spirits can grow in God is because God is a Spirit, and He has given us a nature like His own. If He wants to teach us something, He can do so by getting us to understand the things of His nature by using the new nature He gave us to understand it with.

Something hard to understand is the new nature. It is not my brain, mind, intellect, or emotion. It is a nature. It is a nature that gives me capacity to understand God.

When Mama Goldfish adopted Baby Piranha, she tried to teach Baby Piranha the ways of the goldfish. But Baby Piranha was having a lot of trouble understanding what Mama Goldfish was talking about. When she tried to teach him how to make goldfish houses, layers, and nests that goldfish live in, he could hear her words and understand what she was saying with his brain, but he could not discern what she was talking about.

Finally she said, "I do not know what I am going to do with you. You keep having your friends for lunch, and the neighborhood is afraid of you."

Then they found out Mother Piranha was not dead, and Mother Piranha came back to claim Baby Piranha. The adopted Mother Goldfish was kind of glad about all this.

Baby Piranha said, "You are my mama?"

"Yes, I am, son."

"Mama Piranha, what big teeth you have. You are not like my Mama Goldfish. Her mouth was round and bubbles came out of it. I did not understand her. Where are we going, Mama Piranha?"

"We are swimming underneath the fence and going through the river, because a bunch of cows live down there. We are going to hang around with the others and wait for one of them. In fact, we are just going to really pray that one of those cows falls in the water."

"Then what are we going to do, Mama?"

"We are going to tear all the flesh off its bones and eat it."

"Oh yes, I can understand that. I know exactly what you are saying. I do not know how, but my mouth is already watering."

Why? Because Baby Piranha has the nature of a piranha. Therefore, he can understand the ways of a piranha. He knows exactly what his mama is saying.

When you were born again, you received the nature of God. Now you can know the things of God that are freely given to you because they are spiritually discerned. There is a part of you that can understand God. Now He can teach you because you can understand Him.

Discernment is more than knowledge. Knowledge you can have in your brain, but to discern means you understand why something is the way it is. It is not just knowledge.

> "Comparing spiritual things with spiritual."

For the Holy Ghost to teach you, He has to have something to compare it with. Draw a line. On one side is heaven and all the mysteries of Christ. On the other side is you. God will compare something He has to something you already have, or there is no comparison.

To compare something, there have to be two sides of the fence. There has to be something to compare it to.

This is what Paul is saying: the Holy Ghost can now take all the things of God and the nature of God, cross over to your side, and compare them with your nature, because you have been born again. You have the nature of God, so now He can use your nature to compare the things of God with. Because you have His nature, you can understand them.

He identifies the spiritual things of God to the part of you that now has that capacity, the part of you that makes you a spiritual man because you have a new nature.

The Lord told me, "No matter what you do in school, the things you write and the things you teach, always leave them with an answer."

If, for some reason, we are off on our millennial timing and somebody celebrated about a thousand years but did not change the calendar, just in case it is going to be a hundred years longer than we thought, wherever our material finds anybody, it will keep launching preachers into power and changing people's lives.

That is what He told me, and I just wanted to weep.

First Corinthians 2:15-16 says:

> "But he that is spiritual
>
> judgeth all things,
>
> yet he himself is judged of no man.
>
> For who hath known the mind of the Lord,
>
> that he may instruct him?
>
> But we have the mind of Christ."

"He that is spiritual judgeth all things" means you are able to discern things in the light of the nature He gave you, according to His nature and teaching. It is pretty hard to be judged from the outside when you are being taught from the inside.

Then Paul says:

> "But we have the mind of Christ."

In case you think Jesus' mind mystically floated down on the end of a string, and suddenly when you were born again you received His mind and know everything He knows, that is not what he means.

Some base teaching on this, saying that once you are born again and receive a new nature, you already know everything there is to know about God and only your soul needs to be transformed.

Wrong.

Your spirit has to be taught. It does not already know everything.

"But it says we have the mind of Christ."

I know he said that. The closest thing you have to His mind, in the way you are thinking, is the Holy Ghost, who does not speak of Himself, but whatever He hears, that He says. The Holy Ghost represents the mind of Christ in you. That is why He is teaching you.

But that is still not the mind of Christ that Paul says you have. I have the mind of Christ in the Holy Ghost, yes, but I do not yet have the knowledge of all the mind of Christ. I plan on having all He can teach me before I go home.

Everything pertinent to this walk in this body before I am glorified is mine. I want to line up all the children in wheelchairs and with Down syndrome. I know what was in His mind, and I want Him to transfer it to mine.

The question is, can I receive what is in His mind? Yes, I can.

Why? Because I have the mind of Christ.

What do I mean? I have the spirit of Christ. I used to have the spirit of Adam. I did not have Adam's personal human spirit; I have my own. But I inherited the death nature from Adam. By one man's transgression, sin entered into the world and death by sin. The whole human race inherited the spiritual death that was in Adam.

I do not have Adam's individual spirit. I have my own, but I had a spirit like his because I had his nature.

I do not have that anymore. Whose spirit do I have now? I have the spirit of the second Adam, which is the spirit of Christ.

I do not have Jesus' personal human spirit. I have my own. But I now have the nature that is in Christ. It is in me. So I have the spirit that is of Christ.

I have my own spirit, but I have His nature. I also now have the mind of Christ.

Do you think your spirit floats around without a mind? I have the spirit of Christ, and I have the mind that goes with the spirit of Christ. That is a spiritual mind. I have the mind Christ has in His spirit, in my spirit.

It is not His personal mind; it is my spirit. It goes with my spirit. But it is the mind that goes with the new creation, the mind of Christ, a mind now able to discern spiritual things because I have His nature.

So the Holy Ghost can teach me what is in the mind of Christ because I have that mind born into my new creation spirit when I was born again.

I have a mind like Christ, a mind with the capacity to receive the things of Jesus Christ.

Did you get that?

The mind of Christ is the mind that goes with the new creation, the hidden man of the heart. When you were regenerated, that is the mind you received. It is the same kind that is in Christ. It looks like His, and in its humanity has a capacity like His.

Now the Holy Ghost can take the mind of Christ and put it into you because you have a holder just like His to receive it.

It makes me wonder what I can know. Somebody stop me before I get too much further down the road.

One thing I am going to find out is what He had that could empty wheelchair after wheelchair. I am going to find out because I have the capacity to understand it.

We quote the verse, "Eye hath not seen, nor ear heard, the things God prepared." Yes, but then the man got born again and said:

> "But God hath revealed them unto us by his Spirit."

So that day is over.

The mind of Christ is a mind just like Jesus had when He first appeared on this earth with a sin-free nature and a mind in that spirit. That is what you received. It looks just like His, and it will receive just like His.

Hallelujah.
