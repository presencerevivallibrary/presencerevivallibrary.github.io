Because you would still rather walk with the flesh than nourish what He can use to set you free. He does not have two things to free you with, only one:

> "By his Spirit that dwelleth in you."

"That is the Holy Spirit."

No, it is not. That is why everybody has been so bound. The Holy Spirit can use that, but if you do not want to be free, He has nothing to use. It is by His Spirit. When your spirit gets to the place through nourishing it, then it is stronger than your disease or your sin, and He will quicken and cleanse you, and you will be free.

John G. Lake said:

> "You cannot get healing for the body as far as you are personally concerned. Someone else's faith may, but until your spirit is right, you cannot get healing for your body."

Now, you can get it in a prayer line by somebody else, but usually you cannot keep it.

Faith, love, joy, and hope all spring from the spirit. Being the hidden man of the heart, they are all products of your spiritual life.

The reason people do not have rich, beautiful faith is because their spirit is denied the privilege of communion and fellowship with the Father.

Do you understand me? You do not read your Bible. You do not pore over it. You do not live in it. You do not spend any time fellowshipping with the Father. Consequently, your spirit is depleted and weakened, and faith springs out of it. Your faith grows out of a sickly plant.

On the other hand, your spirit life is fruitful, built up, and enriched by communion with the Father and by reading the Word. That communion includes worship, praying in tongues, and confession. That is what he listed.

He says that on the other hand, your spirit life is fruitful, built up, and enriched by communion with the Father and by reading the Word, and your spirit becomes strong and vigorous. There issues from it faith that is triumphant and creative.
