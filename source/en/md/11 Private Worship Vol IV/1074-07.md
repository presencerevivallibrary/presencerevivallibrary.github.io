# Private Worship Vol IV - 7

For I love to exhort and edify when I get to speak My mind for this moment. I love these times, saith the Lord. There is so much that I want to say.

He is expressing His joy that so much of the training has been through edification of the Holy Spirit, through the understanding of My Word, which is listed as the mysteries. The mysteries were hidden in Christ, but now are being made manifest.

For you who are speaking these things, it pleases Me much because I am able to edify the crowd when My mind is able to be manifested for the moment. I am pleased with you who have edified yourself to the place that you can yield to Me, that you might exhort what is in My mind for this hour and for this city, saith the Lord.

I had to add that last part by an unction so you would know I was not just making some kind of great speech.

Many of you are going to be abundantly happy, and satisfaction will dwell deep in your soul. As things move forward, know you not that you will move forward with them? I will use what is working in you to exhort, instruct, and even develop beachheads.

You need to know enough that all that I have and am can be exhorted until others begin to burn with revival fires too. Many of you will be surprised, very surprised, at how much you know when I am coming on you and the people in front of you need to know what you know. Some of you will find out at that time how much you know.

I was going down a normal prayer line that was not normal. I had taught what happened to me last week, that one realm stopped and another began. Or I should say I graduated that realm and became capable of entering another.

What I preached last Sunday was what I met at the door concerning opposition. Until that time I had never seen a fallen angel manifest where they communicate, threaten, and tell the great things they are going to do to you, like kill you. They were parked at that door. This was their second major attempt to keep me from entering it.

The first was when I was supposed to get into the revival of sensationalism. I survived that one, even though those angels came as gods. But I had never seen one. These were not disguising themselves. They were ugly as a stick.

There were no more little locked key doors in this realm. I saw a great big safe with an iron door. I had seen it once in the other realm, but I did not stay there and it did not open anymore. This time it was down a corridor, and it contained the love of God. You turned the handle, and the big safe door came open. More of it leaked out, then more.

It looks like the same kind of love that moved Jesus by compassion and caused Him to heal every single one of them. It is available if you can go there.

After I prayed for a man for a minute, I stepped away, slipped into the Spirit, and saw a beachhead. All of a sudden I was lifted up in the Spirit. For one second, love explained to me that in preparing to go somewhere, especially to establish a beachhead, it is nice if it is Hawaii and they sing and greet you and you are heroically received. But when they have snipers waiting for the airplane to land, that is a different story.

He caught me up, and a merger took place. He said, "You will have no need to fear where you go. In this place I can fully equip you. Whatever happens, you will be fully equipped, and you will have no fear, and you will not fail your mission."

I said, "You can do that here?"

He said, "Yes, I can. You can walk over to the beachhead with that package operating in you: no fear, and every single thing there you will be able to handle because of total preparation and receiving of that place, absent of fear and fear of failure."

I was in it for a minute. I had to step back and come back to myself. Missionaries have always gone into danger, but this place had absolutely no fear and full equipping for the task at hand.

Paul did not die until he was ready. Was he afraid of the way he died? No. From the sewage prisons of Rome to a beheading, he said, "I have fought a good fight. I have kept the faith." He was the one who was ready to go.

So I am thinking, that kind of equipping is on this road? Yes, sir. Yes, it is.

All that is necessary is coming into place. We give You glory, praise, and honor for it.

For you must understand that all things are possible to him that believes. I can do these things, saith the Lord. It delights Me when I am able to take a people beyond what is most and into what is more, and turn you loose on darkness.

Your Father seems to think you can win.

Many of you are looking and testing. Your heart says, "I want to move forward," but your enemy says, "I am going to stop you here." What has the enemy to do with what I have said? For what I have said is all-powerful and unchanging.

He who hangs on to what I said and does not abandon it will receive what is in My truth. I cannot walk away thinking I failed you. I cannot fail you. You fail yourself.

Hear what the Spirit would say. Did I not give you a Teacher? When you did not know how to pray, did I not create, organize, and teach you a method and a way that you would not have to stop just because the path stopped? You could continue on until you understood the mystery of what was stopping you and move forward.

These are exciting times. Some will run out ahead. Some will say, "I totally believe it now," and they will adjust themselves. Soon they will be running out ahead.

Do I punish you for this? No. I am not against you. I am for you. Why would you think you are capable of only so much when I said all things are possible to him that believes?

He took me to the birth: Mary, the manger, and before the birth, the overshadowing of the Holy Spirit. When the Holy Spirit overshadowed her, she conceived. It was in the same path I had stepped through into the love of God that caused me to see what I am about to tell you.

Do you remember when I had someone follow me around saying, "I love you, I love you, I love you," until I turned him off like a robot? Click. Because it was love I gave him that he was just giving back.

But in that conception, along with your new nature, which is what Mary carried in Jesus, there was some kind of DNA that programmed the love you would grow up in. Whose love was it? Yours. It is yours just like your nature is yours. You got it from God, but that is you.

I know what is in that nature. I know it can walk into the love of God because that is what it is patterned after. These little strands of DNA contained the love, and it grew up and manifested itself as much as it could.

I used to spend time trying to get God to love me more until one day He finally said, "I cannot, because My love was maxed for you at the cross. You can find out how to receive more of the love that I have already given you."

Some people operate in this much love and this much of everything else. That love grows maybe up to the rib cage, and they get kind of nice. But you can let that grow. You can nurture it, bring it forward, and keep increasing it like everything else He has given you until it is maxed.

What do you think will happen when it is maxed?

I already had a short experience with this on the road to this realm. When I saw the big safe crack open and just a little light came out from behind it, I got a feel for what it was like. Miracles increased during that time, and people were weeping uncontrollably.

One day the Holy Spirit said, "Do you know what that presence is?"

I said, "Sir?"

He said, "That is the tangible manifestation of My love."

"Where is it coming from?"

"Right now I am using you."

There is a tangibility to it. The tangible presence of His love can be felt, and it manifests in compassion. What moved Jesus to where "all were healed"? He was moved by love, manifested as compassion, and He healed them all.

You may say, "That was Him, and you are you." How can you account for the revival under Peter where every single person was healed? How about laying people in his shadow as he passed by?

We do not have to sit here with billboards pasted over people in chairs who come to church for twenty or thirty years, teaching them how to reconcile more of the world to themselves and still go to heaven while the crowds grow.

If you have a child with twisted legs, old enough to know why other children can run around and play while he cannot move, where can you bring him in Tulsa and take him home healed? Do you know a church? I do not, but I know one that is on the path.

Media is responsible for many large crowds. When I first broke out and these kinds of things began happening, I did something I should not have done. You might not think it was horrible, but I did. I was holding my breath for the next service, wondering whether He would use me or not. He did. He used me the same way, even though I had done something horribly wrong.

I had a choice there. He loves people so much. I could have stopped and created a doctrine: "Maybe that grace they are teaching is right. Jesus dying on the cross covers every sin you ever did or ever will do, and once you accept Him, that is it." That all-inclusive doctrine is being preached too.

How do those things start? I am glad the love was at least enough to love God more. When that was wrong and I knew it, because I loved Him more, I chose to stop. I did not test the waters to see how much more wrong I could do and still be used by God.

I am finding out now that the love He gave me is in the DNA, and it is developing like anything else.

The more you pray in tongues, the more you confront strongholds. A stronghold is a system of thoughts empowered by emotion that is not right. It usually has an origin.

I can remember when a presence would come and I would feel sticky and ugly. I would think, "What did I do?" I did not know a presence came in with stronghold information, not to possess me, but to harass me.

One day the Lord said, "Treat it like a person."

I said, "What?"

"Treat it like a person."

I said, "Get out. Get out in Jesus' name. You get out and do it now."

All of a sudden the atmosphere changed.

But if it is an enticement, you have to overcome yourself and turn the enticement off.

The more you let the love of God develop in you, the more fear you lose. The more fear you lose, the less you fail. Perfect love casts out fear and has no failure.

When that safe door opened and the love started flooding out a little more, weeping fell on the people. There were twenty or thirty people weeping all at once because of the manifested power and tangibility of His love.

I went on and finished my course as best I could, until I came to the end of that realm and knew now that the door I am walking toward is that last door: the operation of the fullness of His love. Possibly it is the fullness of the love in me that grew up to receive the fullness of the love tangibly that He has for me.

I know God does not have any weaknesses, but if He did, I think you would be one of them.

If we went back in history past Satan's creation, back beyond the universe, time is a creation to Him. It only means something to those who are terminating. The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit always were.

Why did He work so hard on our creation? When we fell, He marked out a legacy of time to get us back. We know it has been about six thousand years.

In the angelic creation, preachers say Lucifer has no redemption because he did not have a tempter. He was perfect in his ways until iniquity was found in him. If he has no redemption, why is he roaming around? Why not destroy him right there?

He evangelized a third of the angels. Did they have a tempter? If we had a tempter and He had a plan for us, He probably had a plan for the third that followed Satan. This helps you understand when Scripture says they are reserved in chains of darkness unto judgment. The chains of darkness are not that they are being held somewhere; they are locked into death and the fire waiting for them, because whatever provision there was, they rejected it.

God is all-sufficient and immutable. He cannot change nor be changed, which means He cannot lie.

So why is it so important to Him that He programmed my new creation? In that program is also your glorified body. He is not going to pop it on you like a glove. You will be transformed. We are going to look good. You are not going to be over thirty-three and a half. Jesus was cut off at thirty-three, so you are not going to get any older than that. You will be a thirty-three-year-old running around ten million years from now.

He programmed that DNA in me, and in that love I get to develop with the other attributes in my nature. What does He want from us? What can we give Him?

When I was writing a letter on this, I coined the phrase: the most costly thing heaven has ever sustained is your right to choose. Without it, He has no fellowship. You would be some kind of robotic creation.

Because this love developing in me is receiving His love, when I get full, look out. Horrible, contagious diseases and other things: no fear, no failure.

I saw a place with that love where you were so equipped with Him and with His presence that you were not afraid to walk into the middle of a hostile camp. No fear.

He made us family. He is the Firstborn among many brethren. He is our Father now. We are His sons and daughters, although there we will not operate as sons and daughters as we do now. We will be more like the angels, but still His children.

What does He want? He wants fellowship. When you raise your hands, adjust your heart because you know it is true, and say, "Father," do you think that as this love grows in us, our fellowship will get richer and richer? Do you think He wants to leave people suffering and cities unsaved because He was never turned loose in the city?

If your child had twisted, mangled legs, and we took him behind a curtain, prayed, and brought him back healed, how many of your relatives do you think would survive that? They have survived our speeches. How would they survive that?

I can preach new messages from Genesis to Revelation, and they will be good and anointed, but I will not convince everybody.

I taped Ronald Coyne's eye up. I taped it and put stuff over it, and it was checked out. There was absolutely no seeing past that eye. Then he reached up and took the glass eye out. I looked into the back of an eye socket. But while I was looking into the back of his eye socket, he was looking back at me.

He said, "Give me your driver's license." He read everything off of it. No eye.

What God did was a miracle in reverse. We need an eye to see into the natural world through our spirit. So He opened the spiritual eye that was still there to see without the natural seeing device. Science checked him out and went away shaking their heads. It was an unexplained miracle, and many people were saved.

Tulsa is in revival. Jesus is Lord over Tulsa. I knew that from the day I walked off my job in Oregon, that I would end up here fighting this fight for Him. I knew it after only three months of praying in the Holy Ghost.

I know it is going to be accomplished because I made it to this path and survived all that other stuff. Now I am operating in some manifestation of it.

You know how the devil works. You get so many good miracles going, and everybody says, "I have never seen anybody like you. I am so glad my child was healed. There is nobody like you on the planet." After enough of that, tell me what happens.

Fallen angels can manifest tangibly because they were created to serve both worlds. You can entertain a stranger unaware. One of them manifested as a woman in my hotel room, and the others were there with her. She was putting the curse on me with smoking flax, as if I was supposed to get scared.

Then comes the voice: "Nobody is used like you. Do you understand that He separated you from the rest? There was one Adam, one Noah, one Mary, one Elijah." If you get egotistical enough, you will buy it. You get drunk on yourself.

I got past it. Love takes care of that as it keeps growing.

Can I quote some of my favorite verses to illustrate?

> You can say unto this mountain, Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea, and shall not doubt in your heart, but shall believe that those things which you say shall come to pass, you shall have whatsoever you say.

But before you do, if you have ought against anybody, go make it right and come back.

If I am bringing an offering to the altar and have ought, I might as well pick it up and put it back in my pocket until I solve the ought, then come back and offer it.

Do you know what is waiting out there? Do you know why a mighty man like William Branham still has people waiting for him to come back? A special pronunciation, a special palate, a special role. How did that happen? Because he was able to believe it.

I made it past a lot of that. There were about ten or twelve devils camped at the door that opened up. That great big safe with the love of God was still cracked open, and there was a journey path to it. The first thing He showed me was that the love programmed into my DNA belonged to me. That is what I give back to Him. That is what can keep increasing and what He can inhabit. When He inhabits it, He can inhabit it in the power of compassion.

Do you like praying in tongues? I seem to like private worship too.

One time I called someone known for miracles. I had preached with him once or twice. I left a message because I wanted to ask one question. In the early days, when he was alienated by family because he left the faith, he was in his room a lot. I wanted to know whether he prayed in tongues all the time when he was locked up, or whether he was primarily worshiping. I did not get a call back.

Have you figured it out? I think so. If tongues had been producing the other, I know the doctrine would not have gone where it went. But there was a lot of presence and glory.

I have been taught by the Teacher. I took the discipline He gave me. Whatever door He opened, I practiced it. Whatever key He opened up, I put it into practice. That is where I learned things like meditation and assimilation.

In teaching me how the mind worked, He started like this: "How come you cannot read a paragraph and quote it back? How come if you read it thirty times, you can quote it back word for word?"

He said, "Meditate day and night. What happens when you read it again and again? These words put it there, and those words put it there, and it comes automatically. Do you not know that when you scan over little truths, this truth lifts up and explains that one? What do you think would happen if you read Romans thirty times in a row?"

That kind of meditation takes place day and night. You do not even try. It is like being thrown into a strange country and picking up the language because that is all you hear.

That was against this day. I had to be taught. I cannot be taken out now.

When He started revealing His love, that was a tough one for me because I was not exactly raised in it. I understood enough to know that when He was teaching on it, He showed me baby Jesus and Mary to get across to me that this love I love Him with is from me. It was not thrown into me so I could receive God's love and give it back mechanically. It is mine to give back in fellowship and in power.

Hold me up. I made it to new ground.

Gary was talking the other day about seeing a dark belt around the planet and a bright one. He thought the dark belt was principalities and powers. The Lord said, "No, that is your flesh."

People began to break through part of it. Some got their mind through and stuck it into the glory area and could teach. Some got their hands through and were anointed. Some made it all the way through and shot like a spark to a dark place of the earth, igniting like a forest fire.

I know what is coming.

If we called a blind night, how many people would come, especially if they saw some communication about it? How would you like to see a person see for the first time? What will be the strength that you do? Pray in tongues. Keep speaking those mysteries. Worship, and throw fasting in when He hints about it.

I had a Scripture: "The first shall be last and the last shall be first. Many are called, but few are chosen." In the chapters that talk about it, a Jewish mother heard that and asked Jesus to make her sons sit at His right and left hand. He said that was for the Father to choose.

Then He explained that advancement comes differently from the Gentiles. They take glorious positions by force. But he who is great among you shall be your minister, your servant. He who is chief shall become your slave. The one who makes himself the servant comes in first.

Many are called, but this is the basis on which they are chosen. Exalt yourself and make yourself first, and you come in last. Make yourself the servant, the slave to Him and His cause, and you come in first.

Right now He is starting to change.

I would that you could understand the importance of the indwelling of the Holy Spirit in all of this I have to offer in My purposes and My plan. From day one, you would immediately receive Him upon receiving Me.

I strongly encourage you: if you do not have the baptism of the Holy Spirit, let me put it this way. If you believe you have Him, but you do not have Him to the level of being able to speak in tongues, particularly the tongue that is for your edification, then if you are not filled with the Holy Spirit, come up here.

I do not know if there is anybody here or not, but I sure want to give you a chance if you are.
