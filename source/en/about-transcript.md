# About the Transcripts

These transcripts were produced from the original audio recordings of Pastor Dave Roberson's teachings using automated speech recognition and AI-assisted transcription and editing tools.

The goal of this project is to make these teachings more accessible for reading, study, search and translation.

Two transcript versions are provided on this website:

* A transcript-only version without section headings.

- A version that includes AI-generated **section headings** to improve readability and navigation.

The section headings are editorial aids generated by AI and were not part of the original teachings. While they are intended to reflect the flow of the teaching, readers may prefer the transcript-only version if they wish to read the message without added headings.

Because this archive contains hundreds of teachings, the transcripts have not been manually proofread in their entirety. While every effort has been made to improve accuracy and readability, occasional transcription errors, formatting issues, misidentified words, punctuation errors, or other inaccuracies may remain.

Readers should regard the original audio recordings as the authoritative source whenever precise wording is important.

If you discover a transcription error, correction suggestions are welcome and appreciated.

The original audio recordings and related ministry resources remain available through [www.daveroberson.org](http://www.daveroberson.org).
