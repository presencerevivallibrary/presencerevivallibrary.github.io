1072-PS030917B Loving Him With the Nature He Gave You

Grace Can Help Your Standing, but Love Must Come From Your Heart

The day that you search for Me with all your heart, we interpret as this: the day that you love Me with all your heart is the day that you will find Me.

Even as My grace is poured out, I can help you with My standing for this, and I can help you with My standing for the receiving of that. I can pick you up out of the mud, brush you off, and trade your standing for Mine. I can even trade your weaknesses for Mine and help you along the path.

But he that searches for Me with all his heart is he that turns to Me with the nature I have given him. Within that nature is the capacity to love Me.

You will go from where you are now to that place of loving Me. That love is something I gave you in the nature.

I am trying to understand this now, and I am going to say it the best I can: He cannot love Himself in you for you.

Did you get that? He cannot.

He cannot love Himself for you. In other words, this cannot be replaced by grace. This has to come from you, from the work He has done in you.
