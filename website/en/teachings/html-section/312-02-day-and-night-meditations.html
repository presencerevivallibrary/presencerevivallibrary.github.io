312-02 Day and Night Meditations

God Put Him on a Trail Into Born-Again Realities

As you know, and those of you who have been coming regularly know, I think it was about five months ago that I made a statement that God was going to put me on a trail, because He had just told me He was. He said if I would walk that trail out, it would change my whole life and bring me on into everything He said I could have, that I would be eating of the fruit of the vineyard soon.

Little did I know He was going to open up more born-again realities. I had never dreamed there were chapters and chapters on our freedom, and on letting the Holy Ghost come to enforce and bring everything forward that we have become as new creations. When He brings Him forward, that is when the champions emerge. The champions.

He said, "You are going to get on that path." Besides that, He said, "I am going to teach you a new way to meditate."

It is not really a new way. I had been skimming around it, but I did not understand the fullness of it. When He told me what to do, I really did not know what it would do. I only began to do it as an act of obedience.

Some of you will remember that at times I said if I did what He told me to do, it would change my preaching. He wanted me to draw more from the mind of Christ and be able to yield to Him anytime, anywhere, and pull out what He had invested in me through meditation and through my teacher, the Holy Spirit.

So I began.

He wanted me to spend time going through the whole Bible, but then to isolate different books that He wanted me to assimilate. That is the wording He used. He wanted me to assimilate the mind that was in them, to absorb them.

Books like Ephesians, Galatians, Colossians, Hebrews, and Romans are not very big, and all of them have a central theme. There is a mind of God in each one. He has said something to the Church. In the information in the book of John, in the book of Ephesians, in new creation realities, is God's mind to the Church. It is gold. It is pure victory in its purest form.

When God sat me down and said, "When you start doing this now, you are going to pull from a reservoir you did not know you had," He was telling me that any person who would do this in their business decisions, in their walk, and in the love they walk in, would begin to pull from a reservoir they did not know they had.

I said, "God, do You want me to assimilate these books, absorb them?"

He said, "Yes."

I said, "Well, You know, I kind of do that. We get a few favorite verses in those books, we skim them, and once in a while we will read through them a little bit. Maybe there is some good part in it."

If you will let me interpret for God--He does not act like I do--but it was as if He grabbed me and said, "Look."

John Poured a Lifetime of Revelation Into the Books in Your Lap

The book of John, those five chapters to the Church, are different from the testimony of Jesus where John recorded His testimony. John walked closer to love, as close as a man could walk in love. He could not even understand agape until later, after he was born again. That is when he recorded the Gospel of John, the testimony of Jesus.

Because of the love he had already fostered in his soul, he was able to understand more revelation knowledge than probably any apostle, especially about the Holy Spirit, the Teacher. He is the one who came and taught John love, and John was able to write:

When He is come, He will guide you into all truth.

None of the others recorded anything about that. Not Matthew, not Mark, not Luke. John seemed to have insight into the Holy Ghost and the love of God that nobody else had.

Now here it is, culminated in this book that is sitting in your lap right now. John ministered to maybe the age of one hundred and five. At one hundred, he was on the Isle of Patmos, a penal colony off of Turkey. He was literally breaking rocks in slave labor at almost a hundred years old.

He outlived the Emperor Domitian and was brought back. For the next hundred and fifty years, Christians used that island as a pilgrimage site to get closer. Then Muslims took it over, and after two hundred years it became theirs.

Regardless, John came back after probably eighty years of ministry. Everything God deemed pertinent to pour into a book, He poured into the one in your lap. This is eighty years of a man's life.

What are you going to do with it? Dance on a verse once in a while? Eighty years, everything this man learned that God esteemed as his contribution to the canon of Scripture, the mind of God after eighty years in that man, He poured into these words you are going to look at.

And you have no respect for them?

When a guy like me wants to teach you how to meditate, you should put your ears on. I have been broken. You know, I have been broken.

Different Callings Need the Mind of Christ Assimilated in Their Area

When people come to me and say, "Do you know what I am called to be?" if I do not have a word of knowledge or discerning, I can watch them and watch what they gravitate toward. I can make a pretty educated guess.

They want to know what books they should begin to assimilate, to absorb. If you have the ministry of a psalmist, or whatever kind of problem you are going through, you need to absorb the mind of Christ in that area.

The book of Romans is an incredible book. If you are not able to get free from sin with the new nature that is in you, if you were able to assimilate the first eight chapters of that book--particularly chapters four, five, six, seven, and eight--then you could jump over to chapter twelve if you are having trouble. But I will not do that anymore. I will study them, but I will take the whole book now. I will just take the whole thing. I do not want to miss anything.

The Apostle Paul also ministered something like fifty years and poured himself into these epistles. This man made a great contribution to the foundation of the Church. Everything he learned that God deemed pertinent to the Church is poured into those few epistles.

And you do not have time?

Are you loving me?

So people come to me. Some I will say should meditate in the book of Psalms because of their ministry. Others have the entrepreneurship of the simplicity of giving, which is one of the roughest ministries. If I cannot get them on the rock enough to outrun the power of money, it is not that they will not achieve money. But by the time they achieve it, there is a good chance it will have them. In fact, that is usually the normal, not the exception.

There are not very many giving multi-billions into the gospel. If they are, you are probably not hearing about it, because to give that much, they usually were purged from bragging about it.

If it is the helps ministry--because helps is the highest after you leave the fivefold, like the apostle is to the fivefold--the very highest gift you come into on the second level is the helps ministry. It just barely clears the fivefold, and helps is next.

It is the steward. It is the few whom God can use to pour billions out to. They are just big fat funnels that suck it off of the world and put it into the kingdom of God. They are smarter than the devil. They are smarter than Wall Street. They are smarter than the world's entrepreneurs, because the Holy Ghost and all His counsel is sitting at their table and helping them make their decisions.

It is not cheating. If He gives you an inside tip, it is okay. The sinners could have the same tip if they would serve God.

Proverbs and John Train Stewards in Wisdom and Love

Of the two books I usually assign to assimilate for the upcoming stewards, the entrepreneurs, the first is the book of Proverbs. Usually these are the instructions: I assign them to read it fifty times. I cannot make you do anything.

I can read it in two hours the way I am about to tell you. Read each verse deliberately and precisely. You must acknowledge the content of each verse, even if you do not understand it.

"I do not know what this verse means, but I understand each word."

If you run across a word you do not understand because King James did some kind of flip side on it, then go find out what the word means so that the next time you cross by that verse, you can understand the content of it.

Read directly, deliberately, and precisely each verse.

I have developed to do it while I am praying in tongues. He said, "My spirit prayeth, but my understanding is unfruitful." I have developed to read precisely and deliberately while I am praying in tongues. I can do it. I could not do it overnight, but I have developed to do it because I wanted my spirit to be edified at the same time I was offering my soul on the altar of God's Word.

So I will just pray in tongues. Sometimes something will start to come to me, and I find myself not praying for a little bit, but I go right back. I do not pray out loud. I usually whisper, because I found out that praying out loud will wear your vocal cords out in a matter of a mere twelve or thirteen hours.

Maybe I will not read my letter to you tonight. I will just set you into it and read it next time I meet you. Hallelujah.

So I have learned to do this. I read precisely and deliberately, acknowledging what is in each verse. If I find myself slipping into daydreaming or solving some problem from yesterday in my thinking, while my intellect is still reading this thing, I stop. The moment I realize I am doing that, I stop. I back up to the last verse I remember reading deliberately and precisely and pick it up from there again.

It used to happen to me all the time, but I wasted so much time getting through a book that it will cure you after a while. "This is the fifth time in the same chapter I have had to back up." That is right. It will cure you. After a while, you will pay attention because you do not want to back up.

I will tell you, funny things will begin to happen in your soul as you do this.

For the entrepreneurs, I make Proverbs fifty times, and then I make them go to John, 1 John, 2 John, and 3 John and do that a hundred times.

Reading like that, subjecting myself like that to the Word of God, I can go through the book of Proverbs in two hours. Usually, if I am in Proverbs and assimilating the book, I will only go halfway through in an hour. Then I might do something else and come back to the second half. But if I do it in one setting, it takes me two hours, because I do not go back very often now and have to read eight verses over because I somehow departed from them.

Assimilation Equips the Soul for Day-and-Night Meditation

In doing this, you are preparing yourself. You are offering your soul on the altar of the Word in preparation for meditation.

In reality, the instructions He gave to Joshua, and the instructions David gave--"Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful; his delight is in the Word of God, and in His law doth he meditate day and night"--are the same.

He said to Joshua:

This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate therein day and night.

Day and night. Day and night.

I cannot study, memorize, and read day and night. But I certainly can meditate day and night, if I equip my soul for the day-and-night kind of meditation by assimilation.

When a person offers their soul on the altar of meditation, they are preparing themselves for the day-and-night kind of meditation. Believe me, it can take place at any time.

The part I really did not understand was the night part. I am thinking, "What are we going to do? Be like some monk in the monastery, giving yourself to the Word long hours of the day and then burning the candle at night? How is a housewife going to do that in this unforgiving world of forty-hour workweeks, schedules, and running everything?"

Yet the instructions to these great men are to give themselves to the meditation of the Word of God day and night.

When God taught me how to assimilate the Word, and this is what I am teaching you to do, you store it into your soul in such a way that you give the Teacher of all teachers access at any time, with all the raw material He needs to take that paintbrush and paint the revelation of the mind of Christ in that book on the canvas of your heart.

Hallelujah. That is a pretty flowery outbreak of words there, would you not say?

Sleep Sorts the Soul, and the Word Can Reclassify the Mountain

But I did not get the part about day and night. I am going to talk about the night part just for a little bit.

Science has always tried to figure out what sleep is. I do not know what it is exactly, but I sure enjoy the fire out of it.

Have you ever thought about sleep? Have you ever really thought about it? All of a sudden something reaches up from in you and turns you off like a light bulb. It severs your conscious mind from its duties. The reason it does it is because your mind is needed somewhere else.

Your mind does not stop just because God severs it from outside contact. All of a sudden, click, just like turning the light bulb off. I do not even know where you go. Where do you go? I do not know, but I have developed it pretty well.

Something reaches up and goes click and severs your mind from conscious duties. As near as science can figure, your mind is needed somewhere else. I know this to be true; God has taught it to me now.

Your mind prioritizes things. It begins to file them in categories of importance, into short-range and long-range memories.

For instance, how many in here are fifty years young, fifty and older? Where were you when Kennedy was shot? Why did you not forget that? Because when it was prioritized and filed, it was filed somewhere you would never forget it. Things are filed away according to importance.

Have you ever gone to bed with something really troubling you? The reason it is troubling you is because the devil and you went and got a little molehill and made a big fat mountain out of it, especially if it is something the Word of God says you should not have made a mountain out of.

Now suppose you have absorbed an entire book a hundred times. Here is this hateful little mountain. It took a molehill and exaggerated it into this horrendous thing that says you are not going to receive from God and you will never be able to forgive anybody. You go to bed troubled.

During the night, after He reaches up and turns your mind off--click, and you are gone--your mind takes the information you fed the most into your soul and what is important to you. It starts filing it in long-range and short-range memory, and it looks at that molehill.

Because it has absorbed the book of John a hundred times, it says, "Hey, he has taken this little molehill and made a mountain out of it." So it takes the little molehill you made into a mountain, drags it way off somewhere, and stores it under unimportant.

Now, because it is not exaggerated in your thinking anymore, the next morning when you wake up, you go, "My God," and revelation knowledge hits you. You know exactly what to do about the problem because the mountain was made back into a molehill. In the absence of it, you are thinking clearly again.

That happened because you fed yourself for day-and-night meditation.

Of course, you could go to bed with some guy chopping another guy's head off on TV or something. I think some of your minds ran ahead and figured out everything I was talking about in a split second. Then you wonder why you sit up in a cold sweat.

The preachers I counseled who crashed had to occupy their minds all the time before they crashed, because the wrong part of it had so taken them over that they had no peace. Unless they occupied their mind all the time, they could not rest.

When I would start telling these preachers how they were broken down, they would look at me. I would continue to tell them, and they would look at me more. Then they would say, "How did you know that?"

I would say, "How far do you think I went down that road before I recovered myself?"

I was getting 1,400 or 1,500 born again and filled with the Holy Ghost in a five- or six-week period. I ran the time out on three twin-engine airplanes and preached in three camp meetings a week before He stopped me to settle down with you and receive revival. Do you not think I know what I am doing?

While I was getting all those people saved and filled, the devil did not like me at all. He began to move into my life in an area I was unfamiliar with, just coming out of the woods of Oregon.

The first thing he took away from me was prayer. The second thing he was going to take away from me was my life. He was trying to convince me that everything is ultimate grace, that I could do anything I wanted with my life. He worked on it:

"It is all by grace. The way your ministry grew, prayer had nothing to do with it. God just came along and did it for you. Has your anointing not stayed the same?"

So when these preachers say, "How did you know that?" I know because there was a time when my mind gave me so much unrest that I could hardly sleep unless I had the TV on. I could not occupy myself unless I was watching it all the time. I could hardly even approach the Word of God to open it. Something in me resisted it so hard.

Resistance to Assimilation Reveals What the Devil Fears

Let me tell you something. If you have not experienced him resisting you approaching the Word of God like I am teaching you now, you will. When you start offering your soul to the assimilation of the whole mind of Christ that is in each of those books fifty times, if you have never been opposed in your meditation, you will be.

I am talking to people all the time now at this stage who were fought like I have been fought but did not want to tell anybody.

"I do not have a desire. Something is fighting me. There is nothing in my emotions that wants to pick this up. It is World War III just to go into it and start reading it. Something is wrong."

Something needs to be transformed.

I had not been fought real hard with the Word until these last four or five months, when He taught me how to go on in assimilating it in its fullness. The Holy Ghost lives in great hopes in you that you will give yourself to the Word of God like that so He can meditate with you day and night.

When I began to assimilate the Word like I am teaching you now, I hit some sore spot, some out-of-tune string on the devil, because he really came at me with a vengeance. He got so irritated. I could tell how irritated he was because emotions just wailed and screamed around me, and for the first time in a while I had to fight to open the Word. I had to fight to do it, to begin to assimilate.

But folks, if you really want to know what mustard seed faith is, the seed form of what is to be, it is enough faith in you to do the right thing.

My emotions say, "Do not meditate."

Yes, but the mustard seed that carries the promise said, "Do."

If you have faith as a grain of mustard seed, then you will have enough faith to meditate no matter what your emotions say. That is what produces the tree that is in the seed. You will have enough faith to pray because you have the mustard seed. If you do not do those things, generally you never get a harvest.

He fought. After I began to assimilate these books, I found out why he was fighting me. The first time the Holy Ghost took me and began to teach me with this new meditation, I found out why he is afraid of me. If he is fighting you like that, he is afraid of you.

He fought Augie and took the Word of God away from Augie. Augie responded in rebellion.

Augie, stand up.

When he was on the edge of insanity, he had to fight every moment to keep from suicide. Lee was fighting the same fight. He does not mind; it is in his testimony. Sometimes Lee would have a breakdown on the plane, and he would have to wrestle her down. That was the state they were in just before they came, because he was doing the same thing: getting as many born again and filled.

The strong man came, knocked him out of prayer, and only took him that extra mile he was not able to take me. Thank God I was able to recover. Had I not, I am sure I would have ended up fighting the same fight you fought to get back.

It is not that he did not have cause. He had cause. But you do not have cause because Jesus is sufficient. The mustard seed says, "I can do what I am supposed to do, and I have enough strength to do it." It will produce the tree.

What do you think about that?

Augie Rebuilt His Mind by Letting All Scripture Pass Through Him

This is how Augie rebelled. He moved back home to India. His dad was alive then. He was in the fight for his life, and he knew it. Thank God for families that love you like that.

He went to help with the Christian school those folks had. Augie said that to survive, he had to have everything God said to mankind pertinent to you and me.

You know He runs the universe, but for us He wrote the Bible. Everything God said pertinent to your victory and salvation is somewhere in those sixty-six books. It is in there. You do not need any more, or there would be more. It is all you need. It is enough.

Everything God said pertinent to Augie's salvation and to humanity passed through Augie every two weeks.

I said, "How long did it take you a day, Augie?"

He said, "Five or six hours."

I said, "I want you to show me how you did it. I want to know everything about it."

He said he would go to the front room, and when I was at their house he showed me the couch. I will always remember these things because it was deeply embedded in me now. When Augie is shaking the world, I will get to go look at the couch where he was recovered.

Hallelujah. Ship it to America. Do not let them throw it away, Augie. Make an icon.

I said, "How did you do it?"

He said, "I went out and kneeled in front of the couch in the front room and put the Bible on the couch in front of me. I started with Genesis and read all the way through to the book of Revelation six hours a day. Every two weeks, I started over."

All the mind of God running through his mind every two weeks turned his mind from the destruction and started him on the path back.

I said, "Augie, when you got to the details of the law and how boring different books were, what did you do when God was so boring with the detail to build this and that?"

He said, "I just praised God that He cares that much about detail. If He does, then that means He cares that much about the details of my life."

He could find something everywhere to praise God for.

When Augie and I sat down and had a talk yesterday about the assimilation of His Word, he got so excited. He got as excited as I did over what God has taught me.

When He taught me, the devil got real mad. Well, I made the devil mad again. He seems to really hate how I am giving myself now to the Holy Spirit for the kind of meditation that offers my soul on His altar and turns the greatest Teacher that has ever lived, the Holy Ghost, loose on the inside of my spirit.

Now I am equipping Him with what He needs to give me all the revelation knowledge He wants to give me.

Hallelujah.

First John Shows How Assimilation Pulls Verses Into One Central Revelation

If I had the witness to go on, I would go on. I am only going to show you a few isolated verses in this book just to show you, in God's mind, what He was doing in this book.

When you read a book like 1 John a hundred times, the central theme of the book will begin to crystallize on the inside of you. The satellite verses that are floating around it, eventually the Holy Ghost will be able to reach out, grab that verse, bring it in, until the whole mind of Christ and the whole revelation and the faith that are in that book come together on the inside of you, because you equipped your spirit with the raw material you need for meditation day and night.

A whole new world is waiting for you.

May I remind you one more time that John poured eighty years of his life into those chapters. Eighty years. We are just going to skim a couple of words here to see what this man thought was important after eighty years, and how important you think you are, and how much you think you know, that you can treat his information lightly.

Is anybody having as good a time as I am? The Holy Ghost is dancing in me.

Let us take a couple of verses out of prime places, about four of them, and just skim through the whole book and see what John thought was important and what assimilation will do.

Let us begin with 1 John 1:6-7:

If we say that we have fellowship with Him, and walk in darkness, we lie, and do not the truth: But if we walk in the light, as He is in the light, we have fellowship one with another.

Is that not something? What darkness is he talking about? You have to run ahead for just a second to 1 John 2:9, to a stroke of the brush over here:

He that saith he is in the light, and hateth his brother, is in darkness even until now.

Now we can go back. That stroke of the brush is done.

Then go back to 1 John 1:6:

If we say that we have fellowship with Him, and walk in darkness, we lie, and do not the truth.

If you hate your brother, you do not have fellowship with God.

"No, I have had a wonderful..."

No, you did not. Notice the seventh verse:

But if we walk in the light, as He is in the light, we have fellowship one with another.

In other words, if you do walk with God like you claim you walk with God, there are no ifs, buts, or maybes. You will have fellowship one with another. You will.

How can you tell somebody is out of fellowship? They hate people and are in strife and unforgiveness. He says if you walk with God in the light as He is in the light, and walk where He walks, you will have fellowship one with another. You will.

Then He said the blood of Jesus cleanses us from all sin.

The Inner Anointing Guards Against Seducing Spirits

Let us go on and pick out a couple more verses. What would happen to you if you read this thing a hundred times? What would He paint in you? My God, what could the Holy Ghost do with you?

Go to 1 John 2:27:

But the anointing which ye have received of Him abideth in you, and ye need not that any man teach you: but as the same anointing teacheth you of all things, and is truth, and is no lie, and even as it hath taught you, ye shall abide in Him.

Now, is this not interesting? Back up to verse 26:

These things have I written unto you concerning them that seduce you.

But you have an anointing on the inside of you that is truth and cannot lie. Concerning being seduced, he says it cannot lie and teaches you all things.

Most seduction takes place in the body of Christ not from the sheep first, but from the pulpit first. All division and all incorrect doctrine is taken from people who have taken verses out of context and could not find the central theme of God's mind and Paul's revelation. They created one and ended up believing anything they wanted. Very dangerous.

But notice what he said here. We have an anointing in us concerning those that seduce us. If I stand up here and begin to seduce you with incorrect doctrine, you do not have to go with me because you have an anointing that is truth. It inoculates you against deception if you know how to walk in it, activate it, and offer your soul on the meditation of God's Word.

How do you know? He said it against false teachers.

Go on to 1 John 4:1:

Beloved, believe not every spirit, but try the spirits whether they are of God: because many false prophets are gone out into the world. Hereby know ye the Spirit of God: Every spirit that confesseth that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is of God: And every spirit that confesseth not that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is not of God: and this is that spirit of antichrist.

Is that not interesting? In the second chapter, he says concerning those that seduce you, you have an anointing. They cannot seduce you because it will teach you all things concerning these antichrists, these false spirits, this false teaching.

Then he goes on to say that any spirit that confesses not that Jesus has come in the flesh is not of God, and any that does is of God.

This is how much we knew about this in ultra-holiness. We would be casting the devil out of somebody, and he would go, "I am not coming out." That is just like saying, "Sic 'em," to me and Candy and stuff like that.

"Say it again, devil."

So they jumped on him and said, "Did Jesus come in the flesh? Did He?"

They are not supposed to be able to say that, you know. "Did He?"

That spirit really confused me, because that devil was talking, and he said, "Yes, He did."

I am going, "Whoa. Now what do we do? I know this is a tormenting devil."

See, what he was talking about was the spirit that is on that ministry. The Isle of Patmos off of Turkey, that penal colony, after it was not a penal colony, became a pilgrimage retreat for Christians to go where John received the Revelation. Then the Muslims took it over, and that is a spirit of antichrist that does not believe God was manifested in the flesh.

What he is saying is that any spirit on any ministry, and they are preaching under the anointing of it--like New Age and those kinds of things--that says, "Well, yes, Christ is one way to God," is antichrist. No, He is the only way to God.

Hallelujah.

We think we are so smart in our culture, educated people with computers, but it is the same old devil, just using a little more sophistication in his deception.

Any spirit, any anointing on any ministry, that does not recognize Jesus as the only true and living God, manifested in the flesh, died for your sins, and resurrected, is calling Jesus accursed, as he said in Corinthians. You are saying He died for His own sins. He did not die for His own sins. He died for your sins. He did not die under His own curse. He died under a curse for you.

Jesus is not accursed. Jesus is the Son of God, made to be my sin, and resurrected to the glory of the Father.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

Any spirit that comes and says, "There is another vein of salvation," John says that anointing which is in you--and he was teaching the early Church, and Christianity was so young--concerning those that come to seduce you and divide you into a thousand parts, this is what he has to say to you.

Come on, people. Eighty years of his life. It is worth you reading it a hundred times without going to another book. Get it in there so your Teacher can activate. Equip the Holy Ghost.

Are we not smart enough to realize, out of the thousands of preachers just in this town, that they cannot agree with each other? Why? We only have one mind in each book. Why? Is somebody not letting the Holy Ghost have His way?

God Guides Willing People but Does Not Force Their Beliefs

"Well, you know, there is good, acceptable, and perfect."

Not concerning the revelation of His Word. That good, acceptable, and perfect in Romans 12 is talking about the plan of God. What He was saying is, "I will let you get by with this, and I will let you get by with this. If you do not want perfect, you can get by with acceptable, and good will get you into heaven."

He was not talking about your level of understanding of revelation knowledge. He was talking about the level of the plan of God you are walking in. It will get you into heaven, but it is not what is really there for you.

Why can we not agree? I can tell you, God is not going to push anybody around. He would have pushed Adam.

Do you know how many people are in hell because God would not, in the nature of everything He is, impose Himself upon Adam? He let those people choose what they wanted. If He was going to stop anybody, He would have stopped Adam, and He did not stop Adam.

Because He did not stop Adam, that guarantees you from the eons of millennia to come that you are a free agent. You can think and enjoy your life. You are an independent entity beside God. You are there because you want to be there, and He respects you as an individual in this. He gave you your life, and you will have it then.

God is not the mafia. He does not shoot you up and break your arms to get you to do something. I will grant you that a lot of you did something because you broke your arm, but He was there before you broke it, and anything you asked He would have done.

He likes it when you lift your face and say, "I have fallen in love with You just because I am wearing these shoes and looking up into heaven. You do not have to break my back."

He loves it when you go to the Word and assimilate it because you have fallen in love with Him. He loves it.

Hallelujah.

If He was going to break anybody down, it would have been Adam. But He let him go, and hell is full. It is your decision. You are making it. Your decision.

Have you not noticed how He will let you believe anything you want?

"I just believe in ultimate grace. I believe you can do anything you want and stay saved."

You can believe what you want. It does not really matter. He will let you. We have all the preachers and the sheep to prove it. He will not push you. He guides you, and you can only be guided as you move. Pushing is by force. Guiding is, "Come with Me. Are you willing to come?"

Yes, sir. And His promise to you, John said this--that book is worthy of you--John said this: He has come to guide you into all truth.

Then John 17 says:

Thy Word is truth.

He will not push you. He will let you believe anything you want. You can open your mouth and swell the words. All your swell words mean nothing. He lets you believe what you want.

Where did you get it? There is only one standard for truth. Respect it. Assimilate it.

Now you have the Teacher of all teachers in you. He has been commissioned by God. If you want to go, He will take you on the trip. He lives in great hopes on the inside of you that you will learn how to offer your soul on the altar of God's Word, the same way you would parachute a five-year-old child into a country where he does not speak the language. He will not try, but he will assimilate the entire language.

If you do what I am about to teach you in these next weeks, you will parachute into the book of Ephesians. You will parachute into the book of John. It will not be long before you pull every verse into the central revelation of that book, and you will be speaking a new language. It will be automatic because you assimilated it.

Offering the Whole Soul to the Word Uses More Than Intellect

I have thirty-seven pages or something here. Do not worry; I am just going to use a scare tactic. I will just open it anywhere.

The difference between a person offering their whole soul on the altar of God's Word and just offering their intellect is the difference between a person sitting down with a cassette tape and trying to learn a whole new language by comparing one word of English to one word of the language they are trying to learn, and taking a five-year-old child and dropping him off in a country that speaks another language.

That child will be transformed by the language within a year. It is not that he will try. He will not. But his soul has the power to assimilate the entire language within a year, because he is not just using one attribute, but all the attributes of the soul: his mind, his will, his emotions, along with his intellect.

I am going backwards on this.

Little did I know that when I followed the Holy Spirit into the principles of meditation, He would teach me the most effective way to offer my soul on the altar of His Word. Not with just my intellect, because even though my intellect is effective, it is only one attribute of my soul. I still have three more to go.

If I am ever going to get past the limitations of trying to meditate using just one attribute, then I am going to have to learn how to incorporate the other three: my mind, my will, and my emotions.

This is fun going backwards.

As we learn to offer our soul to the meditation of God's Word, never underestimate the power of the soul.

There is only one thing more powerful than the operation of your soul, and that is the nature of your born-again spirit. When a person begins to subject their soul to the meditation of God's Word, then they have all the power of their soul working for them and not against them.

The most common mistake people make in underestimating the power of the soul is not realizing that your soul has the power to transform you to whatever you subject it to the most.

Hallelujah.

Stand up. Let us worship God.
