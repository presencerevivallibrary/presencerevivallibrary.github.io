620-01 The Glorified Body - 1

Immutable Righteousness Shows the Price God Paid to Speak to Us

I noticed the biggest difference when love delivered me to the Father. Then I started understanding different attributes, like His inability to change because of His sovereignty and immutability. Then I got a quick glimpse of His righteousness.

We have always heard about our righteousness. We would get excited and say, "I am the righteousness of God in Christ." We were excited, but we did not really know what we had.

We have His standing with the Father. The Father had life within Himself, and He gave to the Son to have life. I have His standing. I am the righteousness of God in Christ. Yes, He put me back into right standing, but I have to have faith in Him.

Just that glimpse into His righteousness went both ways in me. When revelation like that goes off, it feels like eternity. It went both ways, to what He has always been and to what He is. Suddenly you see something, and your mind tilts. You have to get it back straight and take it all in.

I kept trying to talk about it and would get partway through. But I have been dating these things back to when they started happening, one after another. It goes back to when love let me know how much righteousness loved me. That is when I got a glimpse of His standing, His righteousness, and what it took for Him to walk up to me and say, "Hi."

The price He had to pay to do that.

He is sovereignly immutable. He cannot change. He cannot be changed. He is absolute in righteousness. There is no darkness in Him at all. He is impenetrable. He does not vary. There is no shadow of turning. He is impenetrable by darkness whatsoever: absolute righteousness.

And the price He had to pay just to talk to an Old Testament saint:

"God, I need to talk to You."

"Excuse Me a minute. Wait. I am going to take your death, the penalty of death you are under, your nature, and put it on this animal. He will die in your place."

That is fine, except we are talking about a sovereign God who can do anything. The molecular structure of the planet you stand on was created by Him. Anyone who knows enough to create a world like this for your home, take a strand of DNA and form you, does not need help. He is Creator. He knows exactly what He is doing.

He is sovereign. There is no higher counsel, no higher power, no decision after His. He is the end of all of it, and He cannot change.

What kind of person has it together like that, yet to talk to you has to stop and say, "Wait. Hold it. This animal is a credit card. I am going to put your death on him and kill him, and it will charge it to the cross"?

Who, having it together like God does, would go through something like that? It does not seem like anything a reasonable, sane person would come up with.

The reason it had to be that way is because He is impenetrable. His righteousness has no darkness at all. He is immutably unchangeable. He cannot vary. He cannot receive even a little darkness. As together as He has it, to talk to you your price had to be prepaid. Your death had to be carried out.

If God Cannot Vary in Judgment, He Cannot Vary in Promise

If that is true on that side, how about the things He said He would do? How about when He said all things are possible to him that believes?

The things He is talking about pertain to redemption, to us, to miracles, and to everything He has made available to us. If He cannot vary in the negative, how much less can He vary in the positive?

When you touch Him with your faith, He has no recourse. It is not a question of whether He will do it. He will.

What kind of sane businessman would look at a world like ours, every continent populated, and have a baby born in a manger in an obscure village with a whole world to face for salvation? Could He not drop one off in every city? Was there not something He could do to get this done?

No. The reason it was done that way was because there was no other option, no other recourse. He already admitted He took no delight in animal sacrifice. "A body hast Thou prepared Me." If He took no delight in slaughtering animals, charging your darkness to the cross so He could fellowship with you, then He certainly had no delight in His own Son being sacrificed, except to look beyond it.

What kind of sovereign person, doing anything in an organized manner, would choose that way to save the planet?

If that is not frightening enough, He had to leave the work of it to you and offer Himself to you only in the proportion that you could believe Him. What a frightening thing. If He is so smart and sovereign, why would He do it that way?

Because He is unchanging. There is no other way He could do it and remain who He is, and you remain who you are.

I am a free son of God. I am free, and I have the Holy Spirit. All heaven has been offered as an earnest against the final receiving of you as the purchased possession. If something gets in your way, you have the earnest with you, ready with all that heaven is. But you have to believe it.

If you do not, you will let your generation slip into a dark age.

"He is God. He can do something."

No. Since He is inalterable, sovereign, immutable, and cannot change, if He could have saved three or four of the dark-age generations, He had to save them. Why did He not? Because He could not. He lets nobody go to hell without trying to stop it.

Now I am starting to hear statements out of that realm of righteousness I had not heard before.

If God were going to go against anyone's will, slap the living daylights out of them, break their back, and knock them to the ground, He would have done that to Adam. He would have stopped Adam on his journey to the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. He no more did that than He slaps you down with a car wreck to teach you something, or sends a little child born with no eyes so you can learn a lesson.

If He could superimpose Himself over your will in this dispensation, there would not be anybody in hell. He would have done that to Adam and saved the mess.

Truth Lets the Holy Spirit Out of the Prison We Build Around Him

I belong to this creation. I belong to His family, and all that He is can be made available. But you are the one who has to bring Him in.

You can have the Holy Spirit be as powerless as most sinners I know because you lock Him up.

What lets Him out? Truth. He has come to guide you into it. If you let Him, He will let you out, and you will let Him out.

God would like you to get into His power while there are still people to be saved, if you do not mind.

Is there condemnation? No. There is no condemnation in the sense that a person has to lie down and die. The new nature is what is required to get you in. For all the messes you cause after you have it, there is a dispensation of mercy that follows you to heaven and burns the final trash.

Paul said to be careful how you build on the foundation. If any man builds hay, wood, and stubble on it, that work will be burned. Your contribution to the body of Christ is what you build on the foundation of the apostles.

What is your contribution? "The Holy Ghost has passed away." "Healing is not for today." A great act of mercy is going to follow us. He is going to take care of a lot of trash after we get there. Thank God that, included in redemption, we get there.

But if you make it as far as receiving the Holy Spirit, then you have received the Teacher who will reveal the standing of Jesus in you. You can burn a lot of that trash now, and that will constitute reward.

I would rather burn the trash now than suffer the loss of Him having to burn it for me.

Praying in Tongues Lets Heaven Write and Answer the Prayer You Need

Tim got me excited on the phone. Sometimes when I talk to him, I end up running off the road. It is not good to be driving when you talk to Tim.

We have been feeding The Walk of the Spirit, The Walk of Power to somebody in a recovery place for drugs and alcohol. We have been giving them tapes and the book. A man checked in, and the person we were giving the books to gave his copy to this man. The man read it and got so excited that he started getting free.

Now he is getting so free that we have to ship him a book quickly before he checks out, because he thinks it is one of the best books he has ever read on getting free.

As we sit here, that book has been translated into twelve different languages. It is just starting. The reason it is doing these things is because it is foundational. It goes right back to the foundation of the apostles, starts with your new nature and the power of the Holy Spirit, and builds you out of any problem that exists on this planet.

The whole reason He took the language away from you when you pray those hours is so He can pray what you really need.

How much love does someone have to know your life completely and then write out what you need to say so He can answer it?

The devil has to hide something like that. That is why God is so behind the book. A man can be staggering out on the Amazon in a grass skirt, get born again, be handed the book, and come out of it.

Who would write the prayer out for you, borrow your authority to pray it with, and then have heaven answer it? Someone like that wants you to succeed. That is why the devil is so mad at the book. But it does not do him any good, because we have revelation on him too.

God Could Not Heal Through Me Until I Accepted the Truth About Failure

That glimpse of righteousness helped me to know His love even more, to understand the price He paid. Anybody who would go through all that to get me back will certainly do everything else He said He would do.

Leading up to that, He said things to me like, "Job's comforters. Many people today are saying things about Me that are not true."

The big proving time came when He took me into a transition and said, "Is there any religion left in you? Anything that says I can and I will not?"

I said, "No, no."

Not until I got to look into His face a little more did He say, "I am not talking about your mouth, boy. I am talking about your heart. Is there anything in your heart accusing Me, saying I can do it but I will not?"

"As in what?"

"A blind baby."

The baby is not accountable. The parents may be linked with the problems a child has, but when you bring a baby to God and that baby is unaccountable, He discludes anything the child inherited. The baby is blind. Here is the question: you hold the child up before God. You have already marked everything else off the list. He wants to heal the baby. Parents do not matter. Culture does not matter. He wants to heal the baby.

Is there anything in you that says, "God can do it because He is God, sovereign and immutable, but He went ahead and left this baby groping in darkness for some reason beyond my understanding"?

He said, "You are saying I can and I will not. Or are you saying I could not heal him, so I did not because I could not?"

This is the press He put me in concerning religion. I like to leave a reason built in somewhere for failure, especially if it switches the failure off me.

But if I am going to take my journey out of this, it has to start with truth. I accepted the fact that He did not heal the baby because He could not find enough in me to do it with.

Could that bring condemnation? Quite the contrary. I am coming under absolute liberty and freedom. If I had to change God into doing it, I would be in trouble because He is sovereign and cannot change. But if He has already done it, then I do not have to do the impossible, which is change Him. All I have to do is go where He has done it.

I thank God I cannot change Him. But if I cannot do it, I can change me. I can go where I believe He has done it, and He will do it.

He is not letting Tulsa go to hell. We are sitting around playing cards or something.

If you get condemned over this, you have slammed a door on truth. I am not going to live under condemnation, saying, "I could and I did not." The first door to freedom is truth.

The Two Strands of DNA Explain the Merger of Spirit and Natural Creation

When God starts unfolding who He really is, a lot of questions come up, including questions about children. On The Born Again Trail, that is where we got to: children.

Someone asked me, "Brother Roberson, when you are thinking in religion and going down a wide highway, and God wants to make a correction or change something you believe, how does He snag you? How does He get you out of the river? How does He stop you and get you to listen, especially if it is against the current?"

They asked if He gives me a vision. They were right.

The kind of vision I am talking about materializes in your spirit for a moment. It is not something that fully materializes in front of you. It is a picture created by the Holy Spirit, not an image created by your mind. I know the difference between the ear of my spirit, my mind, and when the Holy Spirit creates an image He wants me to see for teaching or ministry.

For instance, a woman was crying, and suddenly I had a vision of a room. The cupboards were empty, and an old mattress lay there. Her problem was not only that she wanted prayer. She was crying because that was her house. She was sleeping on a mattress, had no food, and had a baby. When I told her what I saw, she broke down and wept. I was able to minister to her, and it changed her life.

When I was teaching on little children, creation, and why children are born spiritually alive or dead in The Born Again Trail, I was looking into Genesis to pick up anything I had missed through assimilation. While I was looking at Adam's creation, I saw the spiritual kingdom of God merge with the natural kingdom.

The whole animal kingdom, and most things that grow or move, come from a strand of DNA. One strand of DNA constituted the whole natural creation: man, animals, and so on. What was different with man's strand of DNA was that another one came from the kingdom of God. This second strand wrapped around the first one, and immediately in my spirit I knew that was how He merged the spiritual with the natural and came up with the son of God, which is what you and I are.

Adam's Creation Joined Natural DNA With Spiritual Life

Genesis 1:27 says:

So God created man in his own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them.

Verse 28 says:

And God blessed them, and God said unto them, Be fruitful, and multiply, and replenish the earth...

Take note that verse 27 says, "male and female created He them." The actual account of forming the man is in Genesis 2:7:

And the Lord God formed man of the dust of the ground...

The word formed is interesting. To me, form means you take clay and form it into whatever you want. But what God used to form this body from the dust of the earth was a single strand of DNA. When He merged the spiritual DNA with it, the spiritual body began to grow at the same rate as the physical body.

When He finished forming Adam through a single strand of DNA, in our creation as sons and daughters of God it included spirit and natural. God held him up and breathed into him the breath of life, and he became a living soul.

God does not breathe because He is dependent on His own creation, air. What He did was create a natural strand of DNA, then take a spiritual strand and wrap them around each other. Then He activated the process, and it formed the man, both spirit and natural.

When He breathed into him the breath of life, God brought him to consciousness. His creation was done. Then God activated what He had created and formed, and the man gained consciousness.

Can you imagine two strands of DNA, one spirit and one natural, forming and forming until suddenly there is a complete man? God gives him His breath, Adam gains consciousness, and God says, "Hi." Adam says, "Hi." He did not even have to learn the language God spoke.

Eve Existed in Adam's DNA Before She Was Built From His Side

Where this gets interesting is Eve.

Genesis 2:21-22 says:

And the Lord God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept: and he took one of his ribs... And the rib, which the Lord God had taken from man, made he a woman...

The word made there is a poor choice. A better word is builded, but how would they understand that if they did not understand chromosomes and building blocks?

When Genesis 1 says, "male and female created He them," Eve existed in the man's DNA. That is why God took a rib. He had to have a strand of DNA in which the woman existed, and He built her.

Every human being has come through that single strand of DNA, including Eve. That was the doorway into God's family and into our creation. There is no human being who did not come through that strand of DNA. Even Jesus' body came through the strand of DNA Adam had.

Hebrews says He did not take upon Himself the nature of angels, but the seed of Abraham.

God took Adam's rib, put him to sleep, activated the DNA sequence, and built a woman, because the woman existed in the man in his DNA. There is one chromosome difference between a man and a woman. Just one chromosome.

Male and female created He them, because the woman existed in the chromosomes, or DNA, of the man.

Jesus Received Abraham's Body and God's Living Spiritual Seed

Luke 1 says the Holy Spirit overshadowed Mary, and this holy thing conceived in her was the Son of God. We know He took on the seed of Abraham, but we also know He had to be born spiritually alive to be offered as our living sacrifice.

God used the natural process of birth to bring Jesus into the world. Who gave Him His body? The seed of Abraham. Who gave Him His spirit? God.

How did He do that? He left the natural strand of DNA in there that clothed His body, and God replaced the spiritual strand of DNA with His own. The man who came forward and was born was clothed by Abraham, but His spirit was a direct result of God when He was conceived in Mary's womb and born as the Son of God.

When I saw the merger of the spiritual and natural worlds, I saw it merge in one strand of DNA. We had the DNA strand that made the natural body, and another that made the spirit man. They both grew together.

For Jesus to be born spiritually alive, God only had to replace the spiritual strand that would have produced a natural man with the one that produced Jesus' spirit. Abraham clothed His body, but God gave Him the sequence that made His spirit spiritually alive.

Why did Abraham's body come to Him? Jesus could have stood the temptation Adam stood with a body like Adam's. But you and I get born again while still dragging around a body fully capable of sin because death works in it.

Jesus may have been able to stand Adam's test with an eternal body and His spirit, but you and I have to stand everything hell throws at us with a born-again spirit and a body that has death working in it.

So He was clothed with Abraham's seed, yet His spirit came from God. When He stood the test, everything hell can throw at us was also thrown at Him. He was tempted in every point as we are, yet without sin.

He stood the test on both levels so you and I could come out victorious. Nothing can overthrow or overpower us because Jesus gave us His standing. It was good with a body like this, and it was good with a body like Adam's.

The Spirit Man Has Anatomy and Continues Beyond the Body

Luke 16:22 says:

And it came to pass, that the beggar died, and was carried by the angels into Abraham's bosom...

The rich man also died and was buried, and in hell he lifted up his eyes.

You mean your spirit man, independent of the body, has eyes? Yes. When a child dies, what age do they get to heaven? Whatever age they left the planet. Then spiritual DNA grows like natural DNA. That little spirit is growing in that body at the same rate the body is, and if you kill the body, that spirit goes home to be with God but continues growing at the same rate.

In hell, the rich man lifted up his eyes. That means the spirit man has eyes. He was in torment, so he had emotions. That means the spirit man must have a soul. He saw Abraham afar off and Lazarus in his bosom. He asked that Lazarus dip the tip of his finger in water. People in heaven have fingertips.

If I have eyes and fingertips, then I must have ears. I must have hair, even if I lost it here. Your spiritual DNA is hanging on. You are going to look good. You will be thirty-three and a half years old, but the problem is that you will not marry there, so looking good will not matter.

If I raise my hand, my spirit arm raises with my hand. Otherwise I would not have one when I step out of my body. If we both stepped out at the same time, we could say, "Hi, Gary." We could shake hands if we were both in that realm.

You have a spirit man. He is the result of spiritual DNA wrapping around natural DNA, merging the two worlds. He grows at the same rate as your natural man. The natural body gives him authority and contact in this world. If you destroy the body, you destroy his operation here, and he steps out.

Make no mistake: if you are ten years old, you will grow to thirty-three. If you are twenty, you will grow to thirty-three. You will never get a day older, because Jesus was cut off from the land of the living at thirty-three as your substitute.

The Graves Opened Because the Saints Received Bodies After Jesus Rose

Matthew records that some kind of resurrection took place after the resurrection of Jesus. The graves opened, and many of the bodies of the saints who slept came out of the graves. It explicitly says their bodies. They were seen walking in the holy city and appeared to many.

That would have been during the time when Jesus was seen by about five hundred brethren, by James His brother, by the Twelve, and others. At that same time, many of the old saints who slept were making their appearances in Jerusalem, probably people from a recent century whom others would have known.

This is interesting because it says the graves opened. When Jesus comes back, what is He coming back after? Paul says we will all be changed in a moment, in the twinkling of an eye, at the last trump. If you are wearing your body, something will happen to it: from corruptible to incorruptible, right there on the spot.

Yet after Jesus rose, these people came out of their graves with some kind of body and appeared in Jerusalem.

What if a person was ten feet away from an atomic bomb, where the heat melted rocks? Would any DNA survive? What if a shark ate you? There has been controversy about cremation because religious people said Jesus is coming back to resurrect our body, so do not burn it. But what if a believer was accidentally burned? What if an atomic bomb took him out?

People say, "That is God's problem," and they are right. Many people take by faith things they do not understand. But sometimes, in the how, God opens up things about Himself that you need to know.

The Seed Sown in Christ's Death Contains the Blueprint of the Glorified Body

First Corinthians 15:35 asks the same questions:

But some man will say, How are the dead raised up? and with what body do they come?

Paul answers:

Thou fool, that which thou sowest is not quickened, except it die.

Whose death were you baptized into? His. Then you were sown into His death, and you came alive with Him. That was the seed you were sown into. Unless a seed dies, it will not come alive. You died in His death and came alive in Him.

That means in Him is the seed that not only produced rebirth for you, but also produces the glorified body. Each seed has a body as it pleases Him.

Even if the body was destroyed by an atomic bomb, burned, eaten by a shark, or completely destroyed, it does not matter. Your seed died when it was sown into His death, and you were quickened together with Him in His resurrection.

You have natural and spiritual DNA. Even if the natural DNA was destroyed, you have been born again of Christ. Within that spiritual DNA is also the blueprint for the body you will receive. When that day comes, He will take that DNA and activate it, just like He did the first man Adam, and your glorified body will appear.

That is why, if you are wearing this body, suddenly it will be glorified. If you do not have one, suddenly you will be clothed upon by your body made without hands in the heavens.

First Corinthians 15 continues:

And that which thou sowest, thou sowest not that body that shall be, but bare grain... But God giveth it a body as it hath pleased him, and to every seed his own body.

Contained within the seed is the DNA for the body. Because you were baptized into His death and quickened together in His life, your rebirth contains within that seed the blueprints for your glorified body.

It is sown in corruption and raised in incorruption. It is sown in dishonor and raised in glory. It is sown in weakness and raised in power. It is sown a natural body and raised a spiritual body.

The Heavenly House Is Ready Even If the Earthly Tabernacle Is Dissolved

Second Corinthians 5:1 says:

For we know that if our earthly house of this tabernacle were dissolved, we have a building of God, an house not made with hands, eternal in the heavens.

This earthly house is the body that merges us with the natural, our earthly part. If it were dissolved as in acid, gone, we have a building of God, a house not made with hands, eternal in the heavens.

Paul says:

For in this we groan, earnestly desiring to be clothed upon with our house which is from heaven... Not for that we would be unclothed, but clothed upon, that mortality might be swallowed up of life.

While we are at home in this body, we are absent from the Lord, for we walk by faith and not by sight. We are willing rather to be absent from the body and present with the Lord.

When you step out of this body, you immediately go into His presence. But Paul says we do not desire to be unclothed. We desire to be clothed upon, meaning we want the whole redemption work to be done. We want our glorified bodies. We want all things restored.

But he gives confidence here: if the earthly house of this tabernacle is dissolved, rest assured you have one made without hands in the heavens.

When your seed was sown into His death, you died with Him, and then you were quickened together with Him. Every seed has its own body. It may not look like the seed that was sown, but every grain has its own body. Contained within that seed is the DNA for the glorified body that will come out of that seed in due time.

You and I were sown into His death and quickened together with Him in His life. All we have to do is hang around long enough, and we are going to see what kind of body it produces.

Hallelujah.
