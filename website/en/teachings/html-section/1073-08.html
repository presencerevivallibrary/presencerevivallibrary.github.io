1073-08 Don't Lay Down the Revelation Gift

The Love Walk Must Be Governed by the Revelation Gift

When I listened to people talking about the love of God, I wondered how much was being heard. The other day while ministering, I passed a certain point and almost went numb. But I knew that just before that, in the teaching, I was talking about the danger: a person can go after the love of God with all his strength, with everything in him, as long as he powerfully maintains the revelation gift.

That revelation gift is praying in tongues. It is a gift given to you because authority is transferred from Him to you. It is actually you doing the praying, using the authority you have on this earth when you pray in tongues to Him.

One member of the Godhead creates the prayer because He knows your call, your life, your failures, your shortcomings, and your strengths. He does the praying for you, and another member of the Godhead answers it.

That is very good prayer. One member creates it, another answers it, and I get the benefit. That is hard to fail.

But with the love walk, as I studied it along the way, I saw the danger in some teachings and conclusions people reached. I can tell you a little about the journey into it.

I do not know where I was going or what was happening when He invaded me with this key. I know it started at a passion play, where He wanted to put the key in my hand so I could begin receiving on purpose, on the sovereign path to the cross, the love of God.

He said, "You can find out how to get the presence into that if you will worship Me all night."

I did not do it. It was a year later before I attempted anything, and that came through another supernatural visitation.

The Father Sought Worshipers Who Could Fellowship From His Nature

When I began to search the Word, I remembered Jesus coming to the temple. Children were inside making noise, crying, "Hosanna." The Pharisees tried to stop it: "Do You hear what these children are saying?"

Jesus quoted Psalm 8:

"Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings

thou hast perfected praise."

Psalm 8 says:

"O Lord our Lord,

how excellent is thy name in all the earth!

who hast set thy glory above the heavens.

Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings

hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies,

that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger."

I did not know much about worship, so I looked around until I found Scriptures I remembered reading over and over. One was John 4, where Jesus speaks to the Samaritan woman:

"Ye worship ye know not what:

we know what we worship:

for salvation is of the Jews.

But the hour cometh, and now is,

when the true worshippers shall worship the Father

in spirit and in truth:

for the Father seeketh such to worship him."

If you calculate that hour by the millennial time clock, it is His generation. The hour had come and now was. The true worshipers would worship the Father in spirit and in truth.

What caught my attention was, "The Father seeketh such to worship Him."

As I have said many times, He was not hopping from church to church looking for a good worship team. When He said the Father seeks such, He meant He waited out the dispensations, four thousand years, for the first Man to appear on the planet with a nature like His. Finally there could be fellowship on the level of the same nature, the first member of His family.

He was looking forward to someone who could give Him something that would fulfill His longing for fellowship and worship.

Worship in Spirit and Truth Gives Him the Fellowship He Wanted

To worship Him in spirit means communion with Him from the same nature. To worship Him in truth means I am His son. I have been born again. I can worship Him in truth by working the miracles He said I could work, walking in the power He said I could walk in, and proclaiming what He said is truth.

What was He after? Fellowship.

Why would God create a creature with a choice? He must have had a weakness in that area. He did the same thing with the angels. We know very little about that priesthood except that one said, "I will exalt my throne above the stars of God; I will be like the Most High." But it seems he was capable of making a decision.

There is something about God and eternity that makes Him pay the price for the right of His creation to make choices and decisions. I would not want to be with one creature forever unless I knew that when it lifted its arms and told me it loved me, it was its decision and not a program I put into it.

So God seems to have paid that price more than once.

There is a communion between my spirit and His. When He sought such to worship Him, He sought those who could truly choose Him.

When I form words and enter into a flow of fellowship, I tell Him, "I worship You, glorify You, magnify You, and exalt You." Eventually you can get to where you hear Him while you are telling Him those things.

You are speaking the Word. He tells you to love the Lord your God with all your heart, mind, soul, and strength. That is in the commandment. So when I worship Him, I am speaking truth. I have Scripture over it.

Do you think I could worship Him using that truth, loving Him and telling Him, without affecting Him? No.

But He was not talking about your intellect. He was talking about the nature you have that can put you into genuine fellowship with Him, like fellowship with family.

Paul Learned to Glory in the Middle of the Attack

The children crying out in the temple were stilling the enemy. I knew worship hurt the devil, but at the same time it brings tremendous fellowship with God.

When I broke in, it took me a while. I told you about the wars and fights. I was privileged to go to Paul and find out how he handled it when all hell turned loose on him.

James says:

"Count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations;

knowing this, that the trying of your faith worketh patience."

Paul went beyond that. He was stoned and left for dead, but did not stay dead. He was thrown into prison. He wrote Philippians from a terrible prison situation and still wrote, "Do all things without murmurings." How would you like to be tied up in something foul and then write to somebody else, "Please stop murmuring"?

They did not like to keep people within sound of Paul's voice because he would get them saved. They hated sending soldiers to transport him because he could get them converted along the way.

Three ships sank under him. That was not accident. He had an assignment.

You cannot pass through the way Paul did and create chaos in hell for the devil without getting an assignment against you.

Five times he received the thirty-nine lashes. Three times they put his feet in stocks and beat his feet with rods. Starvations, false brethren, prisons, shipwrecks, and danger pressed him.

Three times he called on God about the assignment against him. God's answer was the same:

"My grace is sufficient for thee."

Finally Paul got it. He said, in effect, "I will rather glory in my infirmities, in necessities, distresses, and persecutions, because when I am weak, then am I strong."

He was not glorifying God for those things. He was worshiping God in the midst of them for being who He is: his strength and grace, not his defeat.

Tongues Brings Up Mysteries Worship Alone Cannot Teach

One thing I never let up on too much was the revelation gift. Praying in tongues could not give me what fasting gave me. It could not give me what reading the Word, meditation, and assimilation gave me. But tongues did promise to edify me, and I found out why.

The mysteries you pray up before the Father, the mysteries of Christ in you, the hope of glory, come alive at some point. You start to know what causes things.

Different people have different callings and giftings. I knew one person who did almost all worship and very little else. The glory of God did come, but you would not want to know what happened to him. He knew almost no Word.

I can worship God and call Him wonderful names and be in absolute fellowship with Him. But when the Holy Spirit prays, He is the Spirit of truth, the One Jesus said would guide you into all truth.

Angels are not your teachers. Sensationalism is not your teacher. The Holy Spirit, a member of the Godhead, is your Teacher.

The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit make up the one true and living God. He is my Father, and the Holy Spirit is my Teacher. He guides me into all truth, even if He has to pray the path out for me and then put me on it.

He is not going to let me down, because I am my Father's son, and the Holy Spirit is God.

Each Key Unlocked a Door Toward the Final Key

The first path I established was praying in tongues. That was the first key. It produced the revelation of praying in tongues, and I finally figured out where revelation was coming from.

Then He started teaching me about fasting. Fasting executes the position the flesh has already been declared to have. Romans says:

"If Christ be in you,

the body is dead because of sin;

but the Spirit is life because of righteousness."

If flesh is dominating you and sealing over prayers you have been believing for, fasting drives the flesh down under your spirit's operation. Suddenly those prayers start being answered, and you think, "My fasting moved God." No, it did not. It subdued the flesh enough for what already was available to come through.

Then the meditation and assimilation keys came online. I was understanding mysteries, but having trouble helping other people understand them. He taught me how to offer my soul on the altar of God's Word and how to harness the power in the mind.

Your mind prioritizes even while you sleep. Have you ever gone to bed with a problem and awakened knowing what to do? When you sleep, things prioritize.

Why can you remember where you were when a major historical event happened, but not what you were doing two Thursdays ago? Because it had an effect on you. How would you harness that?

He taught me not to take Scriptures out of context. When you read a passage over and over, one verse rises up and connects with another. Eventually the mind of Christ in that passage comes forward.

After the revelation gift, He taught me how to teach people to receive revelation knowledge.

Then I came to the final key. It was not a little key. It was like a big combination lock on a bank vault. I got it a little open, and light flashed out everywhere.

He said, "That is the love of God. It is the last key because He is love. Perfect love casts out the rest of fear, and the empty hospital you are looking for is in that level of love."

Love Without Revelation Would Have Taken Me Out

I asked why more people are not walking in it.

He said, "Because they are taken out."

When I realized the revelation gift had brought me to that point, then when He began pouring bits of the love of God on me, He was able to teach me.

I lifted my hands and thanked God He did not pour that dose of love on me at the beginning, because I might have helped others preach error. I would have asked, "How can God be love and still be the way He is? How can there be hell?"

But I kept the revelation gift intact.

Habitual worship lets you bypass your mind and speak with your heart. You can suspend yourself in His presence for hours, worshiping and thanking Him. You are speaking the Word, but bypassing the mind and speaking from the heart. You cannot suspend your heart in the love of God like that and stay the same.

The love in Him is magnified in you. The revelation you walk in keeps you on the right path. You will cause much trouble for the devil and bring many people home because of the place you are walking in.

The Twisted Child Became a Call Not to Stop

Do you know what I am going to see? I am going to see that little child who held his hands up to me, twisted and mangled, and said, "Mister, please do not leave me like this."

Are we a church with a Bible full of promises or not? If He said these signs will follow those who believe, why would I stop?

This worship is for you. You may say, "My call is this or that," but your call is believer. These signs will follow you: you will lay hands on the sick and they will recover. You will cast devils out. You will speak with new tongues.

Those are signs for the believer.

That child said, "Please do not leave me like this."

I do not intend to.

The Reward Vision Showed the Opportunity of Earth

I had a vision of an exaggerated reward. It was very exaggerated by God, and I do not blame Him. I would not even tell it to you if He were not making me.

I got to heaven, and it was my day of reward. There was a King's Highway, and to the right were staircases up to a throne. I knew it was my reward. Crowds were on both sides of the road cheering. It was exaggerated to make a point.

I ascended the steps, and there was the Lamb. I was about to receive my reward. Suddenly a little hand slipped into mine. A little girl had gotten through the crowd.

She looked up at me and said, "Mister, I am a child of abortion. I am a child of famine. I am a child of war. I grew up here. You have no idea how much the Lamb loves us. He loves us so much that I cannot even describe the treatment He gives us. He showers us with love. We would do anything for Him, but He does everything for us."

Then she said, "Do you understand? I would give anything to have the chance you have, to be born there and grow up there. We all want to do something for the Lamb so badly because He is so good to us. I would give anything for the opportunity you have to do something for Him."

Then it was over.

I am still here. I still have opportunity.

I am going to drive the revelation gift right up alongside the fullness of the love of God. I am going to have revival, and hell is going to pay for everything it has done to those children.

Keep Tongues Beside Worship Until Love Manifests as Compassion

The path to the cross, and the love it provided through Jesus Christ, is as final as the cross itself. Why would it not be?

Because you have a revelation gift and know you must maintain it, add worship to it. Worship will cause the love of God to continually increase in you until it manifests as compassion. Miracles will flow like water, and you will never jump out there and think you are something you are not.

Keep praying in tongues. He will keep bringing you mysteries and the truth of the Word of God. You will continue to grow in love.

You can get on that path now and go there. You can divide your time. Give Him a couple of hours a day in tongues and a couple of hours in worship. Give Him a season to break into worship, then go back and divide it with praying in tongues.

But do not stop the revelation gift, or you will probably be taken out somewhere along the way.

How many are going to worship God a little more than you used to?

How many are going to pray in tongues more than you used to?
