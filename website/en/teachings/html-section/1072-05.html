1072-05 He Cannot Love Himself in You for You

Prophecy Revealed That Love Must Be Developed in Us

Tonight we will designate 10 o'clock as the official time, and then we will release you. Leading into this, He wanted me to do some teaching, but He came and has been taking the service.

Wednesday night I was trying to recall the prophecy. Near the end of the line, He came and began to talk to me about the kind of love this worship is going to develop in you. Prophecy is in proportion to the faith given you, and all prophecy is given for edification, exhortation, and comfort. In order to exhort from something, there has to be a basis for the exhortation.

Romans 12 says, "He that prophesieth, let him prophesy according to the proportion of faith." What I prophesy must be in proportion to what I am walking in of faith and revelation. Last night I understood the principle He prophesied, but I would never have been able to put it together had He not come in the spirit of prophecy and exhorted on what I already knew.

When He pulled it together, I got a look at love and God's position in it that I had never known before. I did not know what to do for about ten minutes. I had to ask Gary, "What did I say?"

It went something like this: of all the things He has given us grace for, He has traded our standing for His, our weaknesses for His strength, our old nature for His new one, and on and on. But then He said something about love and its position in grace.

He said, "I cannot love Myself in you for you."

Say that again. The one thing He cannot do by grace, this one is up to us. He cannot love Himself in you for you.

I had never heard anything like that in my life. It made me know that the basis of the new nature has everything in it and has the capacity for you to develop your love for Him, but you have to do it. He cannot love Himself in you for you.

This means we have a nature with the capacity to hold His love. Private worship is a key that begins to develop that nature, where it comes forward and you begin to love Him out of the nature He gave you. It has to be developed.

Newborn Praise Carries the Ordained Strength to Stop the Enemy

He said, "Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings thou hast perfected praise," talking about new entries into the kingdom of God. Psalm 8 says:

"Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings

hast thou ordained strength

because of thine enemies,

that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger."

Out of the mouth of newborn babes He ordained strength to still the enemy. The ordination of that strength is there in the new entries into the kingdom of God.

God was prophesying of the coming of Jesus:

"What is man, that thou art mindful of him?

and the son of man, that thou visitest him?"

We know He was born a man, and God visited Him. He made Him lower than the angels for the suffering of death, by which He destroyed him who had the power of death, the devil.

So now these newborn babes coming into the kingdom have ordained strength to still the enemy. He made the emphasis on both: "Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings thou hast ordained strength" and "thou hast perfected praise."

When you come into the kingdom, unless you are converted and become as one of these children, you do not come in fully developed, knowing everything. But you do come in with a new nature and a capacity to be developed and to renew your mind. You have a mind that can discern spiritual things.

Even more than that, as a newborn babe in the kingdom, you have a spirit with the capacity to reservoir the love of God, to develop it and hold what He pours into you.

Knowledge Is Not Enough Without Knowing God

If you want to know the truth, you would not be sitting here if you did not have that capacity. But as you have found out, the ability to discern truth and knowledge is not enough. Even a high IQ is no protection against a mystery-destroyer of reasonings. One person's IQ may be off the board, but that is no protection.

Knowledge is not enough. All that is written of God is so that you can know God.

When He says faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the Word of God, there was no way to be saved until you heard the Word of salvation. Then you acted upon it and were born again. The standard is the same for everything.

Faith comes by hearing the Word of God because you look at a problem, disease, or something you are facing and find out what God said about it. In a simple form, you are saying, "He wrote down what He will do about that problem. Now I know what He said He will do. Now believe it."

Do not make a big mystical thing out of faith. Faith is, "He will do that." But believing He will do something is still different from knowing Him. You can believe a business associate will do what they say because of reputation, but when you are talking about getting to know someone, you are talking about something else.

When He made you a partaker of His divine nature, you received all the equipment you needed to get to know Him in fellowship. That is connected with love. In that connection, when you get to know Him who said these things, your faith will go off the board.

If you get it backwards, you try to develop yourself to believe somebody you do not know very well. What are you going after here? You are going to end up going after private worship until you get enough of a dose of it that it becomes a lifestyle, something you can hardly do without.

Our intention is breaking through the flesh. By the time we are done, there will not be this many people sitting here who are the same people. Some will have gotten worn out. Some things said about the flesh will make people say, "Oh no, no, no." But before we jump into this, I need to give you things to set yourself against immediately so you can begin winning these worship sessions.

Praise, Worship, Confession, and Celebration Each Have Their Place

One area we may not get into much is celebration. That is when your body gets involved, all the running and things like that, which is fun. But we are not going to come and practice getting into celebration.

Basically, we will do corporate worship and praise. Praise is when you sing about the mighty works of God and who He is. Pure veins of worship are generally you to Him. It is your adoration to Him, how much you love Him, you and Him.

Then there is a kind of worship tied together with confession. When I do confess, my confession is generally tied around worship, as in Ephesians 5: ministering to yourself.

Who are you ministering to? Yourself. How?

"Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs,

singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord;

Giving thanks always for all things unto God and the Father

in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ."

I found out confession works better when you make your strong confession, then worship Him for forty-five minutes that it has come to pass, then make it again and tail it with forty-five minutes more worship. Watch out; you may trip over the package before you leave your house.

The more things I ask Him that enable me to serve others better, the more willing and quicker those things seem to come.

We will cover many benefits of private worship. One of them is that you will no longer have to wonder what that verse means when it says, "These signs shall follow them that believe." That is not talking about Brother Robeson only. It is talking about those who believe. They will lay hands on the sick, and they will recover. They will pray in tongues and cast devils out. I really like the "tread on serpents" part.

You will not have to wonder what that means when you start getting acquainted with God.

Joy Becomes Strength on God's Deliberate Path

The days to come, as you know, a whole realm has changed in the atmosphere and in Tulsa. You will find out that joy really can translate to strength. His joy is my strength.

Why would He say that if somewhere in your walk you could not possibly need extra strength? If joy translates to strength, how many of you could use some of that on a day-by-day basis?

What you ought to do is get to know God so well that He does not go home. The question is, can a person plot a course that really ends up there? Fortunately, yes, because God does not do things the way you do. He never fails. He always finishes. There are no flaws in His trail.

Celebration in worship is usually spontaneous. It is not planned with dancers and a microphone. Celebration comes when praise and worship reach momentum, and your body says, "I have had enough of sitting this out," and gets in it.

You do not plan this one. A lot of people preach, "You need to shout for joy." But that means you get joyful first and cannot restrain the shout.

His plans are not like ours. Ours are like the plans of mice and men; they do not amount to much. But when you get on His path, you end up going where He is going.

Why would Jesus redeem you out of something and into something if God did not have paths going to all the "intos"? Do not listen to religion that makes God into a sovereign Person who feels good some days and does it, and some days He just will not. You can know what God will do if you have been taught the Bible by the Holy Spirit.

God is as deliberate as you can get. He loved you when you were yet in sin and came after you. He gave you a nature that can love Him back. Would He then hold you off and say there is no direct way to come and have everything He has?

There is an admirable foe in the process of your redemption. We who have the firstfruits groan within our new nature, waiting for the adoption process to be made complete at the redemption of our body. Meanwhile, we are left here for the ministry of reconciliation, and we have an enemy.

Strongholds Fight Prayer Because Prayer Exposes What Feeds Them

I remember teaching the first group to pray extensively and long in the Holy Spirit, like all day Saturday. Testimony after testimony came in.

Do you know what usually stops people? Totally legitimate things. Things that need to be done. How come your lawn does not grow an inch while you are watching television, but it does while you are trying to pray? When you are watching television, you do not care. But when you say, "I am going to pray all morning," suddenly the lawn looks urgent.

I have seen people driven to get busy because something in them was fighting prayer. Strongholds do not work well if they are obvious. They like to lie below the surface and feed the action of your outer limbs. You say, "I do not know where that anger comes from." Maybe you do not, but something is feeding it.

Praying in tongues was rough enough because, first, the devil does not know what you are praying. Second, he knows if he does not get you to stop, you will eventually find out everything God wants for you and what He will do for you. He has to stop you while your emotions still work in his favor and while you are still partially ignorant about it.

If you say, "I am going to pray all Saturday in tongues," your lawn may seem to grow an inch every hour.

The devil knows that after you begin praying, you are going to uncover what has kept a ceiling on your life, and there will be a fight. If you do not stop praying in tongues, God will eventually build you up above those things so you can look back and see how ugly they were. For the first time, you and God together will agree they need to be moved out of your life.

That is the groaning Romans 8:26 talks about. The Spirit helps your weaknesses, because you do not know how to pray as you ought. He makes intercession with groanings that cannot be uttered. He rises up, and there is a groaning that says, "I hate where I am in my life." The Holy Ghost says, "Good, because when you did not care, I had no faith to change it with."

Why do more people not pray extensively in the Holy Ghost? If Jude says, "Ye, beloved, building up yourselves on your most holy faith, praying in the Holy Ghost," and we are running everywhere looking for mountain-moving faith, why do more people not do it?

Because almost all of them tried it and were turned back. They believed what they saw, heard, felt, or faced in circumstances, or they had things in their lives they were going to hold on to. They got tired of being delivered to the problem over and over to deal with it.

We have had people say, "I am not going to pray. I do not want to pray. I do not even want to want to."

Private Worship Unlocks What Praying in Tongues Delivers You To

When I approached the door He wanted to unlock to me, the private worship and presence gift, it had one of the biggest padlocks I had ever seen.

Some of you are hungry for God to occupy the space you walk in, in a way that resembles the Word of God in the sweet presence of His fellowship. When I approached this door to private worship, whatever flesh praying in tongues had left behind and had not dealt with was dealt with in private worship.

For His presence to come the way it does and to fellowship with you on the level He wants, He redeemed you and made every provision. Even though the devil tries to access you through the flesh, God cleared the path for that kind of fellowship so you can enter into it with Him. He would not redeem you out of and into without cutting a direct path into the "into."

I found a big padlock on the private door of worship. Something about that door frightened the devil badly. He attacked up front and delayed me an entire year. Since then, I have tasted enough to understand why he is so afraid of you sitting here and hearing the possibility of turning God loose in your life with the anointing of the believer.

Six months into it, I wondered why I had been so slow. I needed to know about private worship because He is turning the river back now. From January on, this will be the clearest year I have had in all my years of ministry. This is not just for a season of fasting and praying. We are turning this river back until we get down here what He has called us to do.

He wanted to put the worship limb on because it is absolutely necessary for Him to deliver in Tulsa what He wants delivered. I know exactly what He wants, what to do, and how to go in there. He wanted me not just to talk and preach about it, but to teach it and start walking you into more and more of it, until you graduate in fellowship with Him and His presence so much that it becomes a way of life.

Is there a way to walk into God where His joy becomes your strength, and the anointing of the believer becomes so powerful you could devastate a children's hospital? If these signs follow those who believe, and you are the believer, who are they supposed to happen through?

The power does not work best when God is cornered in a building and the same people keep coming back trying to receive the same thing. You are not supposed to just hold together and try to get things from God. You are supposed to bring them out to the world.

The reason we mostly do not do that is because He has never used us that way, so it does not seem like He ever will. In the old days, what I hated about my walk was unanswered prayer. I am starting to enjoy this now.

The presence is changing hour by hour because I found out how to take the new nature and yield it to Him more and more, until the things of His love, compassion, virtue, purity, holiness, and brotherly kindness become part of you. If these things be in you, you will never fall, and you can proclaim, "I am a believer," with virtues that substantiate your claim.

Praying in tongues is a revelation gift, and it will deliver you to worship. Worship will be fellowship between the nature you have and God's nature until what you received when you were born again expands like a reservoir, holding so much of Him that the signs of a believer become an everyday walk.

Will everybody make it? In crowds, there are always casualties. Some will get mad. Some will be bored. Some will not believe the end result will be what we say. Some will think the price is too great and would rather live in mediocre Christianity until they stand before Him.

But there may never be another time in history when God needs me like He does now. He has made all that He is available, but He must get turned loose. If winning a city were easy, all your churches would already have the city won. The battle usually gets so intense that it aborts long before it becomes more than a program.

Will we? My confession is no. And if we lose you, you will come back. It is going to happen because God does not have bad plans.

Commitment Carries You When Emotion Cannot Be Trusted

This step we are taking into private worship will include a revelation of God's transition from God, whom He was to the Old Testament saints, to your Father. Many things will happen when you start growing close to Him like this.

Some of you are questioning inside, "Is this going to be a hard thing that makes my walk no fun?" No. There is no temptation taken you but such as is common to man, and God is faithful. You cannot be tempted above what He has provided for you through the cross, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the avenue He makes for your escape.

There is nothing that can happen, no matter what the devil thinks he is, that is stronger than God. Hang on. It will be over much faster for you than for the devil.

In private worship, there was a time I could not trust my emotions, but I did not care. I was in on a commitment because I was lying on the floor and God said, "Go in." So I stayed in on sheer commitment, not because I was dancing in the strength of my joy.

One thing I do have is a stubborn streak. It took me down wrong roads that took a long time to get off, but when a stubborn streak is a servant of God, that is a good thing. Everybody else quits, and you are the only one there, but you are there.

I could not go by anything I felt for a while, yet I stayed in until the first waves came and I found out what He wanted me to know.

Short Adoration Trains the Soul to Stay in the King's Presence

Now I want to clear up one thing, then we are going to worship in a song, and then I am going to kick you into what it feels like to have about an hour of private worship, you and Him. There will be people around you. You might as well get used to it.

Some say, "I cannot do private worship when people are around me. They might hear what I am saying."

What do you want us to do, hire a band to follow you around all day and create an atmosphere? What if you were thrown in jail and every time you worshiped they hit you with a chain and broke your little Walkman? What is conducive to your worship then?

When I first broke in, I would not pray in tongues because I was so accustomed to praying in tongues hour after hour. If I wandered off into some foreign country in my mind while praying in tongues, my mouth was still praying in tongues, so it did not matter much because God was creating the prayer. But private worship was different. I went in there to learn how to worship.

Now I can fluctuate because both are solid in me, but not at first.

My mind would wander down the street and see some problem from yesterday while my mouth was saying, "I love You, I love You, I love You." Suddenly I would realize, "Wait a minute. What are you doing? This is lip service. Your soul has wandered off down the street."

I would stop worshiping until I got my soul back: my mind, will, intellect, and emotions. "I am in the presence of a King, and you will pay attention."

That is when I shortened my statements down. At first they were great philosophical masterpieces, but those will drain you. I found that He was looking for a connection in my nature. The more I could pour everything I am into short statements of adoration--"I love You. I worship You. I glorify You. Hallelujah"--the more response I got out of my nature.

"Hallelujah" turned out to be a good word. I would switch over to that, and something would jump inside me. Days were spent practicing pouring myself into short statements of adoration until the time came that He started coming.

Sometimes I would get a big cup of hot tea and walk around worshiping. I would set it down, take a little sip, get lost in worship, then reach for another sip and find it cold. The longer I stayed in that fellowship, the more it transformed me in spiritual realms to a spirit that could yield in seconds.

Miracles Began Moving Outside the Service

When this first showed up out of the ordinary, it was in a camp meeting here, and then it followed me to Phoenix in a camp meeting with people who were Kingdom Now. Their doctrine was not good, but they loved God.

The preacher's wife had a thumb that had not worked in several years. She saw what He did in the service, and I did too. I was almost as shocked as she was. The anointing that comes on my calling and office had lifted off me, but not the anointing of the believer, not that presence of God.

I was dead tired and drained, collapsed in a chair. She came with her thumb like that and said, "Would you pray for me?"

I said, "Sure," just out of habit. I was about to reach over and pull on it to see what would constitute a miracle. When I raised my hands, He came. In twenty-five years of ministry before that, He did not come like that except in the service.

What happened between me and Him? Who is this for, and what is it designed to do? It is certainly designed to bring miracles out of the four walls. So will the devil fight it? Of course.

He swept in, and when I reached over to test her thumb, it went straight up. She said, "Oh my God." I had to keep my composure, but inside I was thinking, "This is getting too frequent. It is scaring me."

Then an elder's child, about fourteen and born blind in one eye, saw it. He came over, threw himself down, and said, "Would you pray for my eye?" By that time I had lost it, and He came. I took my hand off his eye, and he opened it with the most amazed look on his face. He stood up and said, "Oh my God, I see!" Then he ran into the auditorium screaming to his mother and father, and it was over.

I do not mind telling you, the devils continued to attack in every way possible, including my house. Contractors said the burned-down house was supposed to be given back to me in six months, and that was two years ago. But I am catching on. I am not the one who is afraid. I am not the one worried about it. I am not the one so fearful that I am launching an all-out onslaught.

So it has come time to point you down the same road, not just by teaching, but by walking you down part of it. It has everything to do with what is coming and what God does.

I also remember the Baptist lady with the split arm and partial cast. She was scared and wanted to go home. I promised I would not scream, nobody would yell, and she would not fall down. I took her hand and said, "Father, You love her. Thank You, Jesus."

The next day she called the woman who gave her a ride. She said, "I woke up this morning and my arm does not hurt anymore, and I can move it. They are really funny people, but God hears them." She came back to an afternoon service, got in the line with only six people left, landed on the floor, and became one of us. What a witnessing tool.

John 4 Says the Father Waited for Worship in Spirit and Truth

Before we turn you loose until ten o'clock, you can walk. You do not have to scream. You can if you want. You do not have to talk out loud if you do not want to. I found prayer to be long enough that I preserved my vocal cords by whispering, and surprisingly, He seems to hear me.

Go with me to John 4. In verse 23 Jesus says:

"The hour cometh, and now is,

when the true worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit and in truth:

for the Father seeketh such to worship him."

Verse 24 says:

"God is a Spirit:

and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth."

When He said, "The hour cometh, and now is," He was telling the Samaritan woman, "You are in the generation I am talking about. This is the hour I have come into." He was talking about the hour He would die and rise from the dead. Then true worshipers would begin to worship the Father in spirit, out of their spirit, and in the truth of the new nature He had given them.

When He says the Father seeks such to worship Him, it does not mean He is jumping from church to church looking for a good worship team. It means He waited out four thousand years of dispensations for the first Man to be born spiritually alive on this planet who could give Him the fellowship He desired out of a nature like His own.

That first Man appeared, and Jesus said, "This hour now is. I am going to die, you are going to be born again, and you also will be able to worship the Father in spirit and in truth."

The bottom line is this: Old Testament saints could not give Him what you can. You have been put in fellowship with Him because you have received a nature that made you a partaker with His, and you can finally give Him the fellowship He waited four thousand years for.

Dry Worship Is Still Moving Heaven

When I first broke into worship, I did not know what was happening to me when the well dried up. I would say, "What does it mean to worship in spirit and in truth? What are You talking about?"

I was trying to break in, and I was hitting dry times. I would spend two or three hours saying, "I love You." I am not saying you have to hit those times, but if you do, do not let them be the reason you abort.

I searched books from people known for worship because I wanted to find out what He meant by spirit and truth. Almost all of them said the same thing. One top teacher said if you were worshiping Him and it was dry, and you never broke through or felt anything, then you were in the flesh and not in the Spirit, and you really did not get through to Him.

Wrong.

There is definitely a difference between feeling Him and not feeling Him, but you cannot gauge whether heaven is receiving your worship by that. That is why most abort, but you are not going to. You can be dry and keep on until He comes like He said He can come.

Where is the battleground? Where are great men and women of God formed? Not standing in front of a crowd. They are formed in the privacy of their own prayer closet, where nobody is watching.

Am I getting through when it dries up and does not feel like it? Hang your feelings. They got you in trouble in the first place. We had enough sense and faith to teach, "I am not moved by what I see, hear, or feel." Why are you suddenly moved because you got dry in worship?

Your confession is supposed to heal you too, even when you still have pain. So when it dried up, was I getting through? It did not feel like it. Some of you have spent weeks dried up. You may feel like you are saying, "Twinkle, twinkle little star, how I wonder where You are, God."

But quite the contrary, my friend. When the flesh is stacked up against you, Pharaoh's army is behind you, the desert is on both sides, and the ocean is in front of you, and you throw those arms up and worship Him and glorify Him, all heaven gathers and shouts, "Look, the devil is against them, the flesh is against them, their circumstances are against them, but they will not quit."

You are moving Him even more than when you are feeling Him. When you are feeling Him, it is evidence that He has come to fellowship with you because the things that were stopping you before cannot stop you now. The dry times were moving Him even before you had evidence.

Private Worship Clears the Mind and Strengthens the Body

You have exciting times ahead of you. We have more to say. Do you want clarity of mind? Worship eventually causes that sensual baptism where the cobwebs clear out and the moments of confusion get further apart.

Private worship added a dimension to my health that I wish I had when I was fifty.

Are you ready to take a little maiden flight for about forty-five minutes? They are going to lead you in the first songs. Initially you will stand, then you can be seated any time you want. After they quit leading and we go into private worship, I will lead you into a portion of it and then turn you loose in here.

You can wander around, get down on your face, go to the altar, or lie on the floor. I do not care what you do. I just do not want to see your mouth stop.

If you cannot think of anything else, say, "Hallelujah. Glory." Try to pour yourself into it for the time we are here. At the end, when we close around ten o'clock, we will come back with one more public worship and see the difference. The building is yours. Stay on, my friend. Stay on and keep worshiping.

Conviction Is Not a Reason to Stop Worshiping

You need to know that if there are things not right in your life, moral things you know should not be there, private worship will declare war on those things. His intention is not to bring a convicted lifestyle and say your ship is sunk. His intention is to build you up in fellowship with Him until you rise above them.

So when the devil starts pulling that stuff--"You do this and you do that, so you are not worthy to worship Him; He does not hear you"--and you feel conviction because you know you are doing wrong, do not let him use that to get you to stop worshiping.

God's intention is for the light to dispel that darkness and eventually become part of your answer. Do not let it stop you. You are going to love where you come out of this.

The devil does not need to tell you that you are not supposed to be doing those things. You already know that. Besides, your business is not his business. You are bringing these things to God, and worship is the vehicle.

The First Guided Private Worship Session

After this point, she is going to play softly, and I am going to begin leading you. Choose your own words of adoration. Try to pull yourself into it. Eventually you will survive and go on to hours and hours.

If your mind wanders, you are still going to arrive there, even if it wandered for half an hour. But if you want to get in quickly in a thirty-minute session like this, bring it back and tell your soul, "You are in the presence of the King, and you will pay attention."

When you go privately into longer times, if your mind wanders, do what you can with it, but do not stop worshiping because it wanders. Eventually you are going to become and feel the effects of what you keep saying, even if you lose forty minutes of paying attention.

Are you ready? You do not have to yell, but you do need to say it. You cannot just think it. It has to come out of your mouth.

Lift your hands. Let's begin.

Hallelujah, my Father. I love You. I magnify, glorify, exalt, and lift You up high. I want to tell You how much I love You, Father. I glorify, magnify, and exalt You to high heaven.

With all my heart, I magnify and exalt You. I worship and adore You. With everything in me, I bless You. I magnify You and give You all the glory. I bless the day that I can call You my Father. I bless the day my heart could love You and express from the pureness of the new nature my adoration.

Sometimes you will feel a quickening. Follow it. It is a good line.

Jesus, You paid the price. You love me. I love You. Holy Spirit, You are supposed to teach me how to glorify Him. Teach me how to worship Him.

Feel free to move around. You can say anything you want to say.

Jesus, I love You. I glorify You. I magnify Your holy name. With all my heart, I love You, Jesus. I glorify You and magnify You.

I am breaking into tongues a little bit and letting it happen on purpose. When you break in, let it come. You can fluctuate. The only reason I am telling you this is because He is pleased. He is very pleased with your hearts reaching out for Him the way they are.

Now we are going to leave you on your own for the next twenty minutes. We will come back at ten o'clock.

Some things belong to God, and all the glory is one of them. All the glory for worship belongs to Him too. Lift up your hands and thank Him with all your heart. You are walking down a path of presence, glory, miracles, faith, answered prayer, and all that He is. It is a direct path.

We have many things to walk you into, and we will establish each one. This worship will affect your confession so strongly. You will spend one of those hours with the things you are believing for, backed up by forty minutes of solid receiving worship and another hour of private worship.

We will spend five or six hours a week, many of these Friday nights. If we have to move things or change things, we will find time somehow to accomplish this once a week. This was not something He asked me my opinion about. It was, "First find out what it is. Establish yourself. Then lead them in."

Did you know some of these things have been hidden for hundreds of years, and you are going to experience them?
