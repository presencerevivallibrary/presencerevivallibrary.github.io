Divine Order Is a Hedge the Serpent Tries to Breach

Are you ready? I cannot get away from this. I thought that prophecy would last another forty minutes, but it did not.

Let us begin with 1 Corinthians 11. The apostle Paul was talking about divine order and what happens when there is a breach in divine order.

Ecclesiastes 10 says that a man who digs a ditch will fall in it, and whoever breaks a hedge, a serpent will bite him. If a man breaches divine order, he is digging a ditch. He will fall in it. If he breaches divine order, there will be a gap in the hedge that surrounds him, just like a chink can be put into the armor of God.

He said to put on the armor so you could stand against the wiles of the devil. There is a hedge around you of divine order. If you dig a pit and put a breach in the hedge, the serpent will come through, just like if you run around with one of the pieces of armor off. The devil will come in behind the armor, get on the inside of your operation, and try to kill you from the inside.

As you know, the first piece of armor was loins girded about with truth. Then there was the sword of the Spirit, which is the Word of God. That is the one you wield aggressively. But the loins girded about with truth is you standing behind the handle of the sword. That is your handling of the truth, your transformation to it.

If a man is a liar, exaggerator, misleader of truth, or lets lies stand in his presence, there is a chink in the armor. The devil likes to get behind it and then destroy you.

Just like a man who digs a pit will fall in it, and a breach in a hedge lets serpents crawl through and bite, Paul is talking about divine order here.

This is probably one of the most misunderstood chapters in the entire Word of God. The only reason I really understand it now is because it opened up Ephesians 5, and Ephesians 5 turned around and opened up 1 Corinthians 11 again until it went deeper and deeper.

There is protection in divine order.

Covering Explains Why Prayer and Ministry Can Still Be Dishonoring

Notice carefully what he says as he gives this outline for divine order:

Be ye followers of me, even as I also am of Christ.

Now I praise you, brethren, that ye remember me in all things, and keep the ordinances, as I delivered them to you.

As long as I follow Christ, you can follow me, because my actions will be of Him.

He says:

But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ;

and the head of the woman is the man;

and the head of Christ is God.

Another word for head there is covering. So let us read it with that:

The covering of every man is Christ.

The covering of the woman is the man.

The covering of Christ is God.

That is why he went on to say:

Every man praying or prophesying, having his head covered, dishonoureth his head.

But every woman that prayeth or prophesieth with her head uncovered dishonoureth her head.

The head the man dishonors is Christ. It is very dishonoring to Christ when a man falls down on his position in Christ and allows a woman to cover him, that is, to provide for all of his needs.

It is not that this man was not prophesying and praying. He was fulfilling a ministry. But it is very dishonoring to Christ when a man lays down, receives his supply from a woman, and lets her cover him.

It is dishonoring because Christ is the head of the church and the Savior of the body. Another word for Savior is provider. Not only is He the covering of the church, He is the provider of the body.

For a man to let a woman cover him and supply his needs is a breach. It is an insult to Jesus Christ. It stands against everything He stands for as our covering and the supplier of our needs.

Did you notice that the man or woman is praying or prophesying with a breach in divine order? They are still praying and prophesying. They are fulfilling ministry. But as long as there is a breach in the hedge, those serpents are going to crawl through and make your life hell, just as that devil tried to put a thorn in Paul's flesh because he could not get to him.

He will try to get to you, your children, your wife, your husband. He will try to weaken your front lines and assault you because there is that kind of breach in your hedge.

You Cannot Walk for Another Adult, but Christ Covers the One Who Did Not Breach the Hedge

God only requires that you fulfill the Word of God. You cannot live for somebody else. If your husband or wife will not be covered, rebels, and will not go with you, God does not cause you to stand in their place in judgment.

All you can do for another adult is believe for them, stand for them, walk where you are supposed to walk, and Jesus will make up the difference in your covering.

Hallelujah.

You cannot walk for somebody else. You can do what it says toward your children, but there is no adult you can walk in this for, no more than Adam could walk this walk for Eve or Eve for Adam.

Nevertheless, God is full of grace. These people were still prophesying and praying.

That is different from what you may esteem it to be. He linked prophesying and praying together as though these were two things people usually did. Anybody who had any degree of ministry and handled the Word usually prayed and prophesied.

In the early church, they did not have a Bible to flip open like you do right now. They did not have Ephesians and Colossians in a collected work in their laps. Paul would write to one church, another to the Ephesians, and another to this one or that one, but everybody did not have a collected work. Many people could not read. That is who the scribes were: record keepers and readers.

The great church of God was full of apostles, prophets, teachers, and pastors. Those who operated in tremendous revelation knowledge were your prophets and apostles. The apostles were charged by God to set the foundation of the church. The prophets received revelation from God within that foundation and went from church to church, establishing them for the present hour in the mind of Christ.

These prophets would pray, and then they would prophesy, because praying, they would receive an answer to the mysteries they prayed. They would walk into a church, and if any prophet sitting by received something from God, the first one had already had his turn. The spirit of the prophet is subject to the prophet. Let him wait. Then the great mystery he received from God could be spoken to the church.

Understanding that, you can understand the statement in Corinthians. A man or woman praying and prophesying with their head covered or uncovered means they are not fulfilling the position Jesus created them for in divine order. They weaken the hedge and give the devil a chance to crawl through and weaken the church and family unit's chances of making it through unscathed.

I know some of you, not by your choice or doing or undoing, have had hell. Jesus knows that. Where anyone falls down, He steps in and is your covering. If you are not the one dishonoring Him by putting a breach in divine order and tearing a hole in the hedge, He covers you.

So he says there is neither male nor female, absolute equality in God. Only in the natural did He have a divine order and create us for respective roles to fulfill the will of God.

Christ Did Not Consider Servanthood Robbery Because He Was Equal With God

Let us take it a little further. Go to Philippians 2:2:

Fulfil ye my joy, that ye be likeminded, having the same love, being of one accord, of one mind.

Let nothing be done through strife or vainglory; but in lowliness of mind let each esteem other better than themselves.

Look not every man on his own things, but every man also on the things of others.

That means look to somebody else's needs as something to meet, even more important than yours.

Then he says something incredible:

Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus:

Who, being in the form of God, thought it not robbery to be equal with God:

But made Himself of no reputation, and took upon Him the form of a servant, and was made in the likeness of men:

And being found in fashion as a man, He humbled Himself, and became obedient unto death, even the death of the cross.

Wherefore God also hath highly exalted Him, and given Him a name which is above every name:

That at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of things in heaven, and things in earth, and things under the earth.

Jesus subjected all of this unto God as His covering.

Jesus, being in the form of God, already in the form of God, thought it not robbery to be equal with God. If you are already in the form of God, that means you are God. You could not have the form of God and not be God.

So where would the robbery be?

This is why we have missed what is in these verses. Jesus was in the form of God and coequal in every way with God. Yet He did not consider it robbery to find Himself humbled in the fashion of a man, submitting Himself to the death of the cross. Because He did this, He was exalted above every name.

What Paul was talking about was not that Jesus considered Himself equal with God and that was not robbery. The robbery was considered when He took on the form of a servant, obedient to the death of the cross. Though He was in the form of God, He did not consider Himself robbed because He took the form of a servant.

In His glorified humanity, He was exalted to the right hand of the Father as Lord, Advocate, and High Priest over the entire church. But there is still divine order here. The head of every man is Christ, the head of the woman is the man, and the head of Christ in His glorified humanity as High Priest of the church is God.

Yet in the Spirit, He is still the second member of the Godhead and equal with God. In His glorified humanity, He subjected Himself under God, who is covering Jesus Christ. In all of this, He does not consider Himself robbed, even though He is tri-equal with the other two members of the Godhead.

When you say, "I love You, God," you are talking to all three of Them: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost as we know Them now.

The beginning here is the beginning of everything you and I understand to exist: this natural universe and everything in it. Before stars, heaven, interstellar space, the Milky Way, the universe, and the great void, God was already here forever.

In the beginning, before anything had a beginning, God created it.

He is the thoughts of God. He is God's will for us. He is the Word of God. He is everything God is.

In the beginning was the mighty Logos, the second person in the Godhead before we ever knew Him as Christ. The Logos was with God. The word God there covers the other two members of the Godhead. He was with mighty Jehovah and with the Holy Ghost, and He was God.

In the beginning, before anything had a beginning, was the mighty Word. He was with the other two members of the Godhead, and He was God.

He stood tri-equal with the other two members of the Godhead.

Even though He had the answer for everything and knew the formula for the molecular structure of the cross, He would not open His mouth and give the wisdom.

Why? So that in any business deal, any situation at the chief places of concourse, the business intersections of the city, when you need wisdom, you can open your mouth. Wisdom cries and waves red flags.

When the devil indicts you and starts putting you on trial for the trial Jesus was already condemned in, He refused to open His mouth in substitution for you. He knew everything, so you can rear back and open your mouth during the indictment with the wisdom He would not give, because He died in your place.

Hallelujah.

Jesus was already condemned, judged, tried, and executed for my failure. He would not defend Himself, and then He handed His defense to me. He would not open His mouth so I could, and I am getting out of this.

Hallelujah.

I will come suddenly, and from that day you shall see My glory poured out upon this foundation.

Hear what the Spirit would say, and follow closely the instructions. All that I tell you to do, do with all of your might, and I shall come suddenly, saith the Lord.

Let me tell you something. There are a lot of people who walked in the flesh, who took the avenue of the flesh to do things, who are going to be hurting in the years to come. Those who have abundance but walked in the flesh are going to lose it. There is going to be a switch.

If I were you, I would pull my old saddle off the fence, go on and get it in there, put it on the bronco, and ride that thing into the ground.

Hallelujah.

Glory be to God.
