1074 Bonus Worship Prophecies 1

Worship From the New Nature Releases Faith That Works by Love

As He told the Samaritan woman, He waited for this hour, or this generation, when you and I would begin to worship Him and fellowship with Him out of a nature that has been made a partaker of His divine nature. He waited through the dispensations for us to be put into that kind of fellowship with Him.

In fact, many righteous men and many prophets longed to see this day: the day when the Holy Spirit would be preaching and ministering Jesus through vessels yielded to Him.

The greatest operation of faith can really only be obtained, received, and understood through the new nature. Truly, as you love one another, the love of God is developed in you.

He told the lawyer that the first and great commandment was to love the Lord your God with all your heart, your mind, and your strength. He said the second commandment was to love your neighbor as yourself. But He said the second one was like the first, meaning the same love with which I love God is the same love with which I love people.

To say, "I hate people and love God," is an impossibility. To say, "I am going to develop the love of God in me and not develop in love for people," is an impossibility. The way you can tell your love for God is increasing is that your love for people also increases.

Therefore, again I say to you that the greatest faith is developed in the human spirit that is based on My compassion and My love that I have for you.

He looked forward to this day when that group of people would appear to whom He said, "All things are possible to him that believeth." Yet there are those who look to exploits to develop their faith. There are those who look to exaltation to develop their faith. There are also those who look to works to develop their faith.

But again I would say to you that what you are looking for cannot be found outside of who I am. In these last days many will find out that their faith and confidence have been misplaced in things that avail not.

The greatest faith is given to those who themselves become servants as they serve Me. What is the purpose for all faith that can move mountains? What is the purpose for faith by which all things become possible to him who believes? Is it not to administer My love? Is it not to administer My power? Is it not that the world might be saved?

So do not spend your time in that and for that which avails not. Give yourself to My Spirit. Is it not He who works on the inside of you and brings forward in you all that I am? Have I not expressed more than once that all that I have and all that I am is yours if you only believe?

The Narrow Path Satisfies Those Who Taste His Love

The narrowing path becomes difficult to walk because of the flesh imposed on those who walk after Me. Their vision is not clear, and many say, "This could not be the path I am looking for, because the kind of fulfillment and what I want is over on this other path."

But I say to you that you have not gotten past enough of the flesh to taste and see that I am good. He who tastes My love, and the faith that works by My love, also finds the peace that keeps him forever returning back from the difficulties, the flesh, the turmoil, and the hopelessness this world has to offer.

Do not be a child of Mine who has been adopted by the world system. Come in and see that I am good. When you taste that which is on the narrow path, you will be satisfied. Your thirst shall be quenched, and you will know that I am good.

For line upon line and precept upon precept, in these last days I have built this building made out of the spirits of born-again men being made perfect. Hear what the Spirit would say: because the walls have been put in place, indeed I am free to put a ceiling on it. Because the ceiling is in place, I am able to furnish the insides.

Hear what the Spirit would say: what I am released to do in this next year is because those who heard Me in the past laid a foundation, and those who constructed the walls. Oh, I am free to do things now because you have built the building where it has released Me to do the things I am about to say to you.

For those who looked, tasted, understood, and saw that I am good, and have come to a place of accountability, yet used their free-will choice to turn and walk the way of the flesh, the difficulties that arise will be quite apparent. They will reach out to Me and find out that what they are wanting from Me is fellowship. Even though I will respond and move toward them, the immediacy they are looking for they will not have.

But those who can hear with the ear of the Spirit are even now preparing for what is coming. Truly, darkness will get darker, but light shall get lighter. They will run to and fro, crying because of the things they will see. They will say, "Who has answers?" And I will say, "Those who have been walking by My Spirit and gave themselves to Me when it was not a popular time to do so."

Did I not say to hate even the garment spotted by the flesh? For those you are hanging on to, hang on. Know you not that you give Me an avenue into their lives that I have no other way? So hang on. Be strong and speak My Word, for in doing so you release Me to move in dark situations and impossible fronts where I would not be able to move any other way.

Know your authority and be strong, for you are the ones I will use to move through, saith the Lord.

Yielded Vessels Carry the Faith of Servants

It is not perfect vessels I am looking for, but yielded vessels. Have I not said to you many times that the spirit of surrender is the spirit of holiness? I look for those who are yielded to Me, for he who is surrendered and walking in a yielded spirit may have problems, but he also has obedience and faith.

Hear what I would say to you, church: move forward in Me and take that time to get acquainted. Again I tell you that the faith you are looking for is the heart of a servant and is moved upon by My compassion.

He is talking about His love for you and His willingness, His being there for you. But again He wants you to know that the faith you are looking for works by love. When you begin to understand the love He has for you, then you will begin to understand the power and faith available to get His work done.

Because it is not only you whom He loves. When He shed His blood for you and gave Himself for you while you were yet in sin, did He not love you then? When you responded to that love, you stepped inside with God and were born again. He loves all those people as much as He does you.

He said, "As you are finding out, son," and He told me that the persecution does not even begin to measure up to the consolation.

All through the history of ministry and individual ministries, people God has used have always been marked by persecution, testings, and trials, but they have also been marked by consolation. God showed up. I found out that it was at the point when you did not care how many people hated you or what your circumstances said; it was not going to detour what you do, how much you worship, or the cadence of the drums you are marching to.

He was right. I wish I had paid less attention to the persecution and more to the consolation, but I have now found that out. He said, "I want you to repeat that, because I have something else to say."

Consolation Comes When Pressure Reaches Its Brink

In many of your own personal walks, you have been brought to the point of breakdown. You have been brought to the point of turning back and looking away. Know this: the enemy will call in recruits and turn the pressure up. It is a design and a plan he operates by. Just before everything turns loose, he turns everything up, because he has passed this way before and saw the consequences of letting it slip by.

So I have a word from God for you. If you are on the brink of turning back, if your emotions have been stretched so far that you do not know whether you can hang on to them anymore and you feel like a slingshot in some child's hands, hang on. Hang on. Do not turn to the left or to the right, but hang on, because of the consolation.

First, you will find out that you are stronger than him. Second, it will show up in answered prayer and results.

The reason the church as a whole has been a degree off is in not understanding My love. The reason they lacked faith was in not understanding My love. The reason they lacked confidence was in not understanding My love. Because they did not understand My love, most looked at themselves not in the light I look at them, but in the light of their own shortcomings, and those are the least things I look at.

So be strong and come into the inner court. Begin to fellowship with Me. When I begin to saturate you with My love, it will do as much for you as anything has ever done. Be strong, for when you learn to see yourself through the eyes I see you through, then you will know. You will understand.

So come. What I do, I do for you.

Worship Now Becomes the Place of Escape Later

It is a time to lie upon your bed and worship Me in the nighttime. It is a time to worship Me in the daytime. It is a time to rejoice and come to know Me and who I am, because of the bad things that are in the process of manifestation.

For as the conclusion of all things has begun, know this: My testimony and My standards, by which I judge the world, will be at an all-time high. Anyone will be able to look from anywhere and see that this gospel will be published in every nation. Every person who has eyes and ears to see and hear will be able to look in the direction and become accountable. Indeed, those are the days that I will return, but meanwhile many sequences have begun that will lead to that day.

So rejoice and be exceedingly glad, and let your escape be into Me, for there is no other place to hide, saith the Lord.

Many of you will first be dismayed and have a tendency to condemn yourself because you heard things and knew things, yet waited to implement them. Do not be dismayed, nor condemn yourself. Enter in while the door is open and all things are not yet in manifestation.

It will cause hearts in the near vicinity to fail for looking on the things coming upon this world. But wait no longer. Take what you have heard and enter in when everything is not conducive to entering in.

Come to know Me and worship Me with all your heart for who I am, because in that day who I am will be what sustains you. Come to know Me now in the free will that you walk in, and you shall rejoice and be exceedingly glad in the days to come, for you shall have answers.

Prepare yourself. That is not the finish; that is the start. Prepare yourself with much worship and spending of time in My presence. For any who will seek Me, I will make the time, and for those who sacrifice their time, I will see to it that they have the time.

For all things are possible to him that believeth. Is it not a strange thing to you that you exercise faith in the "all things" to move circumstances and pull down strongholds that keep you from spending time with Me? These are the first great moves of My faith in the hearts of those who hear Me.

The Keys of Fasting, Worship, and Tongues Open Their Own Doors

Have I not designed each key to unlock its particular door: fasting, worship, tongues? Will I not move according to your faith and place these keys within your hands to unlock your particular doors?

Have rest within yourselves. Be full of peace, and know that he who desires these keys, I will move for him. I will see to it that he who is of sober mind and plots his course to My heart will have what he needs to make his journey.

It is my call and my job to go out ahead of you and find out what is out there. I am supposed to do that, and then you are supposed to receive it in such revelation and grace that you begin to walk in it surely.

There is no substitute, no substitute, for the fellowship He desires with you. There are no shortcuts. The worship limb requires your presence. He does not care if He comes and raises eighteen people from the dead. He did that before He entered into His rest. But fellowship with you is the now. That is the now. Nothing replaces it, so do not try to shove in a substitute. Nobody can worship God for you.

For in leaving, many of you shall leave differently than you came. In you I shall reach a place of places. That capacity will change. When capacity changes, you will be able to receive of Me what you have not been able to receive before. You will be able to go where you have not been able to go before.

So rejoice and be exceedingly glad, and worship, worship, worship. Indeed, all who walk the place you walk and understand what is approaching are approaching their finest hour.

Cities Will Come Under His Presence as the Door Opens

I have many things yet to say to you, but therefore another time, for not many of you are able to hear yet. But I have things that belong to this realm. I have things that belong to this age and this dispensation, and indeed I will set them forward as a pattern.

For you who are strong and endure, they will come from other places to see. They will come to know Me in the teachings of My foundations and the establishment of all that I am.

In all cities, cities will come to Me overnight. My presence will cover certain areas as waters cover the sea. In the magnitude of who I am, in mighty signs and wonders, I will establish Myself with a wayward and wrong-direction generation.

Do you think that I have not heard your prayers concerning this time? The devil has nothing that I have not absolutely rendered powerless.

In the dope culture, for those who have been overtaken by these things, know you not that I have anointings of healings, deliverances, and power, so that in one single move of My Spirit I will remove all traces of what was?

But do not stop here. I have opened the door. Oh, do you know the times I lived to open these doors? Now that they are being opened, do not hesitate, but be strong. A whole new realm of manifestations I have been released to bring to pass, and that will be in your lives, in your times, and in the level of blessing you walk in.

For you who are faithful in little will be faithful also in much, because your most valued possession is the time you spend with Me. I will come and answer prayer quickly, saith the Lord, for those who desire to go beyond these doors. I will provide the time for you. Be faithful in little, and I will give you much.

Personal Fellowship Becomes Strength When the World Has No Joy

In these times to come, know this: your personal fellowship with Me will hold a strong and great place. In that day, your joy must be based in your fellowship and relationship with Me, for in the world they will not have much to have joy over.

But you who remain in close fellowship with Me, even My joy shall be your strength. Therefore, in these times to come, as you look around, do not forget to look up. As you look up, keep your eyes upon Me and stay in fellowship with Me. These things will come and pass you by as though they do not exist.

For I am your provider, and happy is he who has learned in these last days that I am your source. Therefore again I would say to you that your personal fellowship with Me in your private worship and your times with Me is a source of strength. Be a partaker of that which I have provided for you against these days, saith the Lord.

You who have ears to hear, take heed and pay close attention to the things that worked because of My grace.

Many have gone their own ways. Many have not matured, but instead have divided themselves against each other. Many are called by many names, even as he said, "I am of Paul," and, "I am of Apollos."

But who are we, says the Word, except those who delivered to you Him who is all in all? For in the strife and contentions, is My body divided up against itself? Are you not on milk?

I have sent My teachers, and I have sent My strong ones who demonstrate and move in My Spirit after My power, and said, "Will you not grow up? Will you not come and mature?" Many things are happening, and it is the time allotted to move on. I have many things to say and many things to accomplish, but so very few to accomplish these things through.

At the end of any wave and realm, when it is time to enter into the next, many will hold on to the old and drink the milk. Yet as My mind is coming with the sets of instructions, you who have ears to hear, hear what the Spirit would say.

Moving Beyond Milk Keeps the Body From Feeding on Division

Many, many will camp on those things that speak of milk, and many shall prey on the body of Christ as she continues to die. They will live off her corpse. Yet there will be those who take Me as their source and move on.

In these last days, those who hear Me and move on shall do great exploits. So be strong, for this is a day that I emphasize those instructions that put you in fellowship with Me. Indeed, I am your source. But in the days to come you will have to know this stronger than at any time before in this last generation: I am your source.

Therefore enter into that fellowship with Me, so that no matter what you see and what the circumstances dictate of your generation, remember it is My joy that is your strength. It is My peace that causes you to be immovable. As your feet are shod with this peace, you will be able to stand toe to toe with the worst your enemy has, and not only will you not surrender your ground, you will take ground away from him that he previously held.

But do not stay behind and feed on the dying body and the corpse of that dying body, as many shall do while they teach error. Move on from milk, and do not divide yourselves against each other. Who is your Lord? Who is your Master? Who is your Teacher, if not I?

Watch those who do not say what I say and do not teach what I teach, for they are least in the kingdom who teach not what I teach and teach other men to do so. The greatest in My kingdom are those who receive, understand, and teach My commandments.

Therefore brace yourself up, for the path is being laid out ahead of you. When you hear the shout of victory, leave those things behind and move forward, for indeed great revival is at hand.

Many things will seem strange to you, but hold on. Some things you will recognize from My Word as the fulfilling of prophecy. Yet some things you will not understand because they have not been understood in many centuries. Be strong and listen to the inner witness.

Again I say: your fellowship with Me, with great joy and peace, is the cultivation of the ground where I speak and lead you through. It is My peace in troubled times that will cause you not to move in the wrong direction but in the right one. He who walks in peace and cultivates that ground can hear My inner witness in the most troubled times. That will be your truest guide.

Therefore again I say, enter into fellowship with Me now. Do not wait for the times when things will change again, but take advantage of these years of grace that I will give you to prepare yourself for what is coming.

Loved Ones Will Be Harvested When They Have Nowhere Else to Turn

For all of you who relentlessly hang on to your lost loved ones and will not turn loose, their harvest also is at hand. There will be a time when they cannot turn to the left or to the right, nor can they move forward or backward, like the day Moses stood before the sea. There was nowhere to go. In that day I will reach down and harvest them.

Therefore, as you call them out, do not think that I have forgotten or that heaven has not been in operation concerning all those you held up to Me. For all those you even passed through in a season in prayer and forgot yourselves, I have not forgotten them. I am the Lord God that healeth thee.

Do you not know that those strongholds that existed with many of you because you were born again under circumstances not conducive to your freedom, many of those strongholds, when you get to know Me, will be pulled down with the destruction of the reasonings that give them power in your life?

Therefore fasten your eyes on heaven. Enter into that fellowship with Me that no man can give you. Be of brave heart and strong.

For these loved ones who cannot back up, cannot go forward, cannot turn left, and cannot turn right, they will lift their face toward heaven and receive Me. As the revival spreads, many, many will come to know Me because of the times. These that you have held up in My presence will look up to Me and receive Me as Lord.

Do not be afraid. Do not let fear grip your heart for the things that seem to be happening to them. As I bring all of this to another level through the prayers of those who are praying and those who are giving their lives to Me and to My Word, even though the devil will seem to have them cornered, know this: no test will overtake you and all those you are holding fast for except such as is common to man.

He will not be able to kill them outright. The devil, knowing that he has a short time, will cause all kinds of havoc. But be not afraid for your loved ones, for I will have My hand and My protection upon them. No matter what it looks like, do not faint in your heart. Look up to Me and know the day is coming when they will receive Me as their Lord.

A City Can Be Delivered by Wisdom and Prayer

I would not have been able to talk about the possibility of Tulsa and cities experiencing great, great revivals had not the truth of it been understood in prayer. It is even for this reason I am able to say these things.

Hold fast and know that these ways are the ways that I think. These things are the things in My mind. For I so loved the world that I gave My Son, and it is not My will that any should perish. No, I do not think like fallen men think, and I do not think like those who are on milk and leaving the world behind.

I think as those who received Me and received My peace. To those I am able to talk of those things which are possible. When I see a city, I look through the darkness and do not see defeat. I see nothing but the victory. These things I am already speaking, and I am waiting for those who have ears to hear, who get close enough to Me and in Me to hear what I am saying.

There was a small city, and a great king had it under siege. The city had no help and no hope. Yet in the city there was a poor man, and his wisdom delivered the city. They did not become slaves. They did not go into bondage. Nobody was killed. The city remained intact. This man said something, and that man got the city delivered.

That is what I heard in the beginning of the tongues when I could not translate it very well. He went on with it to speak of how He thinks in His mind, and He wanted me to speak to you of how possible this is.

His Love Makes the Impossible City Revival Possible

Much of what many of you are looking for is My love for you. You attempt to experience My peace without experiencing My love, but My love is part of My peace.

Therefore hear what the Spirit would say: there are places in Me where entire cities experience revival. But who is it that can come to this place? Do I hesitate? Am I the One who holds back? Did I not demonstrate My intent when I spared not My own Son but delivered Him up for you? I am not the One who withholds.

For the times I desired to talk with you, you who had ears to hear, the times I desired to speak with you, oh, My delight is when you continue on and on in the revelation gift of understanding My mysteries. Time after time, know you not how I longed to move forward and speak to you in that still place where you can hear?

But many could not. Why? Because of the religion they found themselves indoctrinated to. Whose voice is it in your mind that is louder than the One I am uttering in your spirit?

Oh, how I desired to speak with you, but religion has blocked so much of what I had to say and what I had to do. Yet I look with delight upon you. Know this: many of you are close to a place where it has been many generations since any of My children walked.

Can a city be won in a day? Stop. Stop and behold. Watch what I will do in this year to come, and you will know that it is not I who withholds. Indeed, all things are possible to him who will believe.

But who will come to Me without price? Who will come to Me and surrender all that you are with a surrendered heart, ready to become the reservoir of My love?

Surrender Opens Anointings for the Children of the City

Hear what the Spirit would say: all that I am has been made available to win the lost. As you have spoken many times of the children of your city, these all live in a state of innocence. You who know Me and the rights I have given you, know that those same rights belong to those children. Anyone who can carry My miracle-working power to them, I will move on their behalf.

Where are you? What cloud do you stand behind? What has clouded your judgment? Where has your procrastination come from? What is within you, that hearing what is being said, you cannot even want to cross the lines and surrender yourself to Me?

Know you not that in places of freedom I have anointings for those things? Know you not that I understand the enemy's ploys and how he works? I have grace and I have anointings.

Many are intent on coming to Me where I am so I can go to you where you are, and My anointings indeed shall shine in that day. You will come one person and leave another. You will come procrastinating, and nobody will stop you after that day.

Hear, hear: for those who have surrendered themselves to Me, all that I am and all that I have is in the process of coming.

Building His Kingdom Is the More Excellent Way

For he who desires the building of his ministry, this path will take you to many things, through many things, and through many doors. But it will not give you the best I have until you make it a place in your heart to build My kingdom.

More build their ministries than build My kingdom. Most take the paths of least resistance. Hear what the Spirit would say: to conquer all that I have scheduled you to conquer, do you not know that those resistant things in your path are meant to be conquered by you long before you meet the giant with the stone in your sling?

Stand fast in the liberty wherewith I have set you free, and be strong. I said, "Desire spiritual gifts, but I will show you a more excellent way."

Even though a man has the capacity to speak with tongues, if he will not persist until he has obtained the more excellent way, which is to walk into My love and offer himself on My altar to be My servant and build the kingdom I am building, that kingdom cannot be built void of My power.

For you who desire to stop, that stopping is the place where you stop and minister to your flesh as much as you attempt to build My kingdom. Those who lay hold on Me and walk down this path of excellence, your excellence is measured by the love operating in you and the lengths you will go to minister My grace and My power to those who are captive in dark places.

Why do I train you to walk in My footsteps? Is it not to walk the paths I walked? Where did I walk? Did I walk in the high places to be seen of men, or did I take the lowly place, illuminate dark places, and pay prices so that one day you who could hear My voice in your spirit would have a cleared path to walk where I walked and illuminate the dark places I illuminated?

Let it be said in hell, in the places you go, that a son of God is in the village, and they will have to make an account over here.

Train yourself. Find yourself on your knees long before you find yourself in places where you can receive glory, lest you fail to let that glory go to Me.

Hearing His Voice Requires Compassion and Love

In these days to come, during revival, it will be most important to learn and know My voice. To be led by My Spirit will be imperative, for there are many things the enemy has prepared for this generation that have not been seen for many, many generations, and some things not for centuries.

Hear what the Spirit would say: people fall short of hearing My voice because there is a ground they do not let Me prepare. It begins with My compassion and My love. I have called them to Me to manifest Me through them, but many will fall short of that, for they will not cross that line and begin to walk in that part of the nature I have given them. They could hear Me also in those areas.

Many would not do the things of the leadership of the Spirit pertaining to love and sacrifice had their hearts not been prepared by My compassion. So many will fall short. But those who give themselves continually to edification, to building themselves up on their most holy faith, are those who will also keep themselves in that love.

Hear what the Spirit would say: when I said, "Build yourselves up on your most holy faith," it is the faith that comes from Me. Your most holy faith will not be the faith people attempt to employ to serve their own exaltations, personal ambitions, and greed. The faith I develop from that line forward concerning My love is the most holy kind. It is developed through edification and will not respond to those things that are asked amiss.

For many of you, I began to graduate you to a place you have not walked in before. I even came with My grace, laid hold on you, and began to pull you through, knowing it would be a time of temptation and trial for you. The enemy would come to the bridge of your strength and try to stop you, but I have all confidence in you that you will complete that which I have given you to do.

Hear this: these things have not been heard in many generations. To hear My voice in the greatest detail in this decade to come, you must accomplish that walk of love. It is in those areas that I led those men of old who could not be detoured, who would not give way to temptation, who did not regard their life dear to themselves, and who were not afraid of their enemy.

They could hear My voice because they were governed by no fear. They were governed by no greed. They were only led by My Spirit, sent where I sent them, and went where I said go. Their lives were laid down on the line. For a person to surrender all, you must have the ingredient of compassion and love to hear My voice in those areas. It will cause you to finish your course in difficult times.

Indeed, you shall continue to build yourself up on your most holy faith. Your most holy faith is that kind I give you that is sanctified from all things that faith was not designed to serve.

The Holy Spirit Lights the New Nature for Dark Paths

Hear what the Spirit would say: come, come, for you are facing the greatest days of harvest. People will be looking for the answers that you have. An excitement was struck, and in many a light was lit. Oh yes, this light will begin to illuminate your path.

Your new nature in the hands of the Holy Spirit is all that you need to see down the darkest paths. Is a candle lit to be hidden under a bushel, or is it not to light the very room you are walking through? Your new nature is to your spirit what the candle is to that dark room. You shall be led through those dark places, for He will not fail you.

I have a song that even the angels cannot sing. Oh, but I have waited for those to be baptized in the baptism of My love. In My archives I have tunes that have never been heard, and I have words that have not been spoken. They will cut to the quick and go straight to the heart of a person, and they will fall on their face and say, "Truly, He is in your midst."

But who can hear these songs? Those who sanctified their garments, washed their hands from all wrongdoing, purified their hearts, and walked into My holiest of holy. I will wait for those and delight in the giftings I have in store for them who will hear Me.

Know you not, you who have chosen the path of mortification through the power of My Spirit, that this includes not only praying in tongues, but it will deliver to you in fastings, in times of assimilation of My Word, and in times of worship? All these play their roles in you offering yourselves to Me in the fullness of equipping for what I have prepared for you.

You who choose the path of mortification by the power of My Spirit have chosen the school that I have given to you, and you will be equipped.

For those who believe circumstances are their teacher, they get worn out by circumstances until, in their weary state, they turn to Me, and then I begin their journey. Be not as those who are governed by their circumstances, but listen to the school I have put you in. My mortification and My teaching are not grievous, for My peace is with you all the way, saith the Lord.

The Heart of a Servant Receives the Shortest Route to His Throne

You take a step toward Me, I will take many toward you. You open yourself up to Me, I open Myself up to you. It is in My presence and to those who yield themselves to Me that you are giving Me what I need to do all that I can do on the inside of you.

These are the days of establishing My grace, the days of the movings of My Spirit, and the days when many of you will receive all that you have been preparing for. So be strong. The enemy is between nervous and uneasy. He is a little uneasy because it has been too long since he has seen this many saints going where they should go at one time.

So hang on. Glorious days are ahead, and you who are faithful will reap the reward.

As you know, all that He is is in these kinds of presences, where He can come in not only with His grace, but many times He will pass through you with His faith, with the gift of faith. Even while you stand in here, you will receive something done out there. As surely as you walk these days out, it will come to pass.

So be in a mind to receive what I have for you. There is no holdup on Me except the restrictions you impose. Release yourself and be free, for I have much to do. Get on and go with Me, that you all might go together and no one will have to go back and get you.

This is My mind: all that I am would do all that I can every time you meet together with Me. This is My mind. I hold no good thing from you, for I so loved you that I spared not My own Son. How shall I not freely give you all things? I have never withheld. It has not been on My own part that these things have been withheld.

Hear what the Spirit would say: let this mind be in you which was also in Christ Jesus. He thought it not robbery when He fulfilled all that I had for Him to do. The Firstborn has given you His standing. Do not think yourself robbed when I ask you to do all I have called you to do, when I ask you to take the role of a servant, and when I ask you to do those things in an hour when no one is doing them.

Be exceedingly glad, for you are taking the shortest route to all that I am in My throne. Come and receive, for I always pour out on the heart of a servant, says the Lord.

The Fight in Your Emotions Means You Are Close to Breakthrough

The devil has put up a smoke screen. He has clouded and muddied the water. He does not want you to see what is on the immediate side of what you are going through.

Know this: he only has a short time to try to turn this, to get your heart to fail, and to have you give it all away in hopelessness. But if you remain strong and trade those garments for My garments of worship, you will find out what I can do, because you are very close to something. This is why the enemy is putting up such a fight in your life.

Therefore hold on and spend your time with Me, and shortly I will come, for it is over, says the Lord.

All of you have been called to come to that place in Me where you take your place as an able minister in My atmosphere. But you say, "God, I am not worthy. I did this and I did that."

I say to you: who is it that changes his faith without actions that correspond? Who is it that has anything from Me who never acts on what I say? Therefore take what you have, however little it is, and give it to Me. Pour yourself out, and you will see that not only will I use you and give you a reward for all that I do and any service you are in, but I will change you too.

Why sit here and do nothing? Those who come in and do not know will be the bystanders. They will be those who look on, and because you add yourself to what I am doing, those who look on will be established in knowing that I am the Lord your God who is present here.

Therefore yield yourself to Me. Be of those who are wise and take this next step with Me.

Many of you have grown weary because even though you go through the motions, you do not fellowship with Me the way you once did. Still others have never fellowshiped with Me. Religious things become weary when they do not have the life of the new nature and My fellowship in them.

Therefore repent and come to Me, that you might walk in My peace and the gladness of joy.

Meat for the Mature Requires Leaving the Normal Behind

I have spoken to you many things, and still many things need to be said. You must understand that line must be upon line and precept upon precept. Though I desire to say certain things, I cannot, for you are not yet able to bear them, even as prophecy is in proportion to faith.

I have very few voices in this world who can speak My mind. As I have said many things, I have many things to say, saith the Lord.

Know this, and excel: those who have touched Me, even prematurely, and received My glory, and many of you who studied those mighty ones from the past, know that many did not finish their course with the same strength with which they went through it.

Hear what the Spirit would say: many of you desire My presence. You desire unhindered answered prayer. You desire the knowledge of the movings of My Spirit, My giftings, and at least the signs that follow My believers.

Hear, you who have ears to hear. Oh, I desire to pour out those things, and many things that have been hidden from the foundation of the world but were manifested in Christ in you, the hope of glory. I have great hope that many will receive in this decade. But so many are upon milk and cannot bear the things I would say.

So hear these things: you who leave the normal behind and pull ahead will be those who embrace your fellowship with Me.

The first phase and line of attack that Satan launches against those determined to receive My anointing and My outpouring will be against you in your emotions. His intention will be to turn you back through hopelessness and despair, both from looking on the things changing in the nation and in the world.

But be strong. This is only his first line of attack. You whose desire is to pull ahead, be strong. By then, through your fellowship with Me and worship, your joy should be your strength. You should be able to sustain what the enemy would do and move on ahead.

Oh, I have great things for you. Receive the foundation that you have received through the fastings, the praying, the revelation gift, and the staying power from understanding My mind, the operation of your flesh, and strongholds that would seal in your life. Many of you are determined to be stopped by nothing this world has.

Move on ahead now in fellowship with Me, for I am desiring to pour anointings out that few have seen in this city. Brace yourself up and get ready to be equipped, for you are drawing very close.

For those who survive this wave, I have many things waiting for you, even the strength to go back and carry many of those who desire Me but were not strong enough to cross the line. Not those the enemy sent into the camp who are haters of men and lovers of pleasure; you will know the difference. They will be those who want Me but have fallen to the wound of the enemy.

So rise up and be strong, for My joy is your strength. Hear the instruction that is to come, for a time of great rejoicing in the camp is at hand. Great victory is the order of the day.

The Obvious Simplicity of Fellowship Clears the Mind

I saw a large cross section of people coming down God's corridors of anointings with amazed looks on their faces. There were sounds of fellowship going on between them. It was great joy, amazement, and excitement that they could have so much of God on such a personal level. The excitement was very high.

I especially heard a cutaway for a moment. People were so excited that they had discovered private worship. Let us not even call it that; let us call it fellowshipping with God. They discovered fellowshipping with Him in an unusual way that caused His joy to become their strength, cultivated the grounds of peace, and cleared their minds so they could hear Him. They were excited and wondering why they could not hear Him before.

One stronghold the enemy builds in the thinking of many believers is against the obvious. The obvious seems too simple. The devil loves to make it complicated, and people miss it because it is too simple.

Just enter into fellowship with Me and go from where you are to where I am, where these things exist.

Hear what the Spirit would say: the devil would like to get into your reasonings and build a stronghold. He will give you all the reasons, all the strongholds, the emotions, and the systems of thoughts of why I will not move. The devil is the best at getting you to overlook the obvious and reason things away.

Look in front of you. The obvious is simple. He just wants you, and you are all He has ever wanted. You need to stop in your busy schedule and schedule a little of your own time to spend with Him.

Delight yourself in the Lord, and He will grant you the desires of your heart.

For am I not your strength? I am your strength if I am your joy. Therefore there is a place in Me where your emotions and all that you are are brought under subjection to My Spirit and My peace. It is not something you try to walk in. There is a position and a peace that is in Me, and that is a place you conquer from.

My peace, My peace, can become the loudest thing you know: louder than the devil, louder than your flesh, and louder than sickness. This is something the devil works overtime to cover up and hide in programs and religion.

All of you can know Me, from the least to the greatest. Therefore set your course. Set your course. Another can set your course for a certain distance, but the day comes when you must set your course. Therefore set your course and come into My things, and those things of peace are the first steps you need to take.

He Delights in a Body Ready for Meat Instead of Milk

What did He say then this last time about the things He desires to teach you? He has desired, as Paul wrote, for you to be on meat and not on milk.

He has so moved toward you. He is so pleased that you understand certain things, such as the revelation gift, which enables you, if you want it, to open yourself up to make a journey into meat. He has a shortage of people teaching the things that cause the perfecting of the body of Christ in these last days and bring His return.

So He is delighted with how far this body has come. He has taken great joy and delight in as much as has already been said. He said, "I desire to give you meat, but I cannot because you are on milk. Are you not carnal?"

He said, "You are still wanting to follow men instead of God." One says, "I am of Apollos," and we are talking about people dividing themselves up to follow a man. There are gifts He puts in the body; they add to your perfecting and things. But they were never designed to split the body and divide it against itself. That is what He was addressing.

But I cannot tell you the pleasure I feel from the Father right now. We seem to be on a schedule. Maybe I do not know: here, and then we get here, and then back here. But we seem to be close enough to what He has called us to do to please Him.

The day that you search for Me with all your heart, the day that you love Me with all your heart, is the day that you will find Me. Even as My grace is poured out, I can help you with My standing for this, and I can help you with My standing for the receiving of that. I can pick you up out of the mud with My standing. I can even trade off your weaknesses for Mine and help you along the path.

But he who searches for Me with all his heart is he who turns to Me with the nature I have given him. Within that nature is the capacity to love Me, and you will go from where you are now to that place.

To love Me is something I gave you in the nature. It is one of those things that I cannot do for you. He cannot love Himself in you for you.

Did you get that? How many of you heard that? He cannot love Himself in you for you. I have never heard anything like that before. In other words, this cannot be replaced by grace. This has to come from you in a work He has done in you.

Grace Produces the Fellowship That Invokes Blessing

For it is not by your works, lest any man should boast of himself, but it is by My grace. What you have been made is by My grace. It is not how long you have been saved or how hard you try, nor is it even how hard you work. These good works are born out of the new nature; they do not cause it.

Hear what the Spirit would say: fellowship with Me, fellowship with Me, fellowship with Me will cause to be invoked in your life what you are saying and believing, and the understanding of what you are saying.

What many of you are looking for is this fellowship with Me, where you can find that kind of grace, especially in this area of blessing. This is what you have been looking for.

Many of you have a negative program, for you have failed to earn His blessing and inheritance. All the tithing and giving that you did to everything they said to give it to, and your life has not changed that much.
