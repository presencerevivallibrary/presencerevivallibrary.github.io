520-03 The Garden of Eden After the Fall

Our Eden Was a Copy of God's House

Last week we covered how our Eden is just a little copy of God's Eden. I call it our house. Why would God not build a house for us like His?

What manner of love hath the Father bestowed upon us, that we should be called the sons of God?

Why would He not build a little house for us to live in, like the one He had?

When He talked about the pattern of things being cleansed under the law, under the shadow, all those things were cleansed with the blood of animals. But the things they were patterned from in heaven had to be cleansed by a more perfect sacrifice: the blood of Jesus Himself.

He called the shadow a pattern and a copy. But the thing making the shadow is Christ. When there is a shadow here, it means the thing making the shadow is coming. All the movement in the shadow said one thing: the body making the shadow is on His way here. That is why every part of the shadow speaks of His coming.

I like this "impounded provision," the teaching we receive in our journey from being children of God who know He is right, to servants who love God and love the Father, and finally to slaves.

When they nailed a man's ear with an awl to the doorpost of his master's house, he was a slave who had been freed by Jubilee. Our Jubilee is Jesus Christ. Jesus picked up the scroll of Isaiah and began to preach the acceptable year of the Lord. He was talking about Jubilee. He was saying, "I am here to fulfill it."

Israel was basically married to God by the law. When He separated the nation, they came under the law. Because they were spiritually dead, they had to be governed by it. He made them His servants by law, and not serving Him resulted in punishment.

Then Jubilee freed the slave. The slave walked away a little ways, looked back, and said, "My master was good. I think I am going to go back to his house and become his slave forever." He went back to the one who set him free, and by his own free will made himself that man's slave.

The master took his ear and nailed it to the post. It meant, "You are my slave. You are my servant. I am the only one you are going to take instructions from."

Hallelujah. God could get excited.

This is a copy. All the activity in the shadow said that He who was making the shadow was on His way here. But once the thing making the shadow gets here, you cannot live in the shadow and the thing making it at the same time.

He is our Jubilee. He is our Sabbath. He is our freedom. He is the eldest Brother in our family, and God is our Father. I serve Him because I turned around and made myself a slave.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

This house we live in is a copy. God put Lucifer in His garden as the anointed cherub that covereth. In our garden, He put man in it to dress the garden. The descriptions are similar: the anointed cherub that covereth, and Adam placed in the garden to dress and keep it.

Jesus Identified Satan as the Source Behind Their Nature

In John 8, they provoked Jesus. They could not understand what He was saying to them, even though they had a basis to understand it from the law. He said:

Why do ye not understand my speech?

Then He went into where they were getting their information from and who they were like, which was the devil.

John 8:41 says:

Ye do the deeds of your father.

They said to Him:

We be not born of fornication; we have one Father, even God.

Jesus answered:

If God were your Father, ye would love me: for I proceeded forth and came from God; neither came I of myself, but he sent me.

Verse 43:

Why do ye not understand my speech? even because ye cannot hear my word.

The kind of hearing He was talking about is understanding. "Why do you not understand My speech? Because you cannot hear My word."

Then He said:

Ye are of your father the devil, and the lusts of your father ye will do.

If you were a religious leader in that day and somebody talked to you like that, it could ruin your breakfast.

He continued:

He was a murderer from the beginning.

When I assimilated the Gospel of John, and later when God wanted to reveal these things about the Eden of God, our Eden, and truths that go back before Genesis, this phrase stood up inside me: "a murderer from the beginning."

The ability to lay back and meditate day and night is possible because I have offered my soul on the altar of God's Word for meditation. I do not know how many times I have read this book. People ask me different questions all the time. Sometimes they say, "How much do you know?"

I say, "I do not know." Usually, when a demand is put on something, that is when I find out.

I do not know how many times I have assimilated John, but it was enough for that word to stand up when God was able to pull it together with what He was trying to tell me.

"A murderer from the beginning." I could not get it out of me.

The next thing I knew was that this could not be Lucifer's beginning, because Ezekiel says that in the Eden of God he was perfect in his ways when he was created. So that could not be the beginning he was a murderer from.

Knowing that, I knew Jesus was talking about our creation.

Assimilation Lets the Anointing Pull the Word Together

Verses are not merely committed to memory; whole chapters are outlined inside. I have to go somewhere inside to a verse to find out where I am in the chapter.

It comes through assimilation, and assimilation assists meditation. That is why we teach so severely on meditating the Word of God day and night. This is how you offer your soul.

Even when your brain has trouble remembering things, the anointing will reach in and pull all that out if you will put it in.

John says:

In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.

The same was in the beginning with God. All things were made by Him. Without Him was not anything made that was made. In Him was life, and the life was the light of men. The light shined in darkness, and the darkness comprehended it not.

You have to stop somewhere.

We know the beginning John is talking about was before anything we understand existed. Genesis 1:1 and John 1:1 talk about the origin of our beginning:

In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.

Our creation came into existence, and somewhere after that, we came into existence. Before there was a Milky Way, a galaxy, or anything pertinent to our creation, He existed. There is no way we could know how long. We can date parts of the universe, meteorites and things like that, and they are old. He is older.

Last week we camped on the ancient wisdom of Satan and how he knows how much God loves you, counterfeits what God is using for your redemption, and uses it for your destruction. We camped on someone who knew God well.

Satan knew both good and evil. He is satanically wise. Jesus said he comes for only one reason in three parts: to steal, kill, and destroy. He was a murderer from the beginning and the father of it. There is no truth in him. All he does is kill, and he is good at it. Millions have misjudged him, and he has killed them.

He is ancient, older than all the meteorites. He has been around a long, long time.

The Holy Spirit Leaves No Excuse for Defeat

You are God's son. He restored your nature. Maybe God was smart enough to have you born again back into the family you were outside of.

Satan says, "You lost them."

God says, "Watch this. I am going to receive the punishment for the old man, and they are going to be born again back into the family. How do you like that, devil?"

That happened to my spirit. Born again is me. I am not the Holy Spirit. It is me. I have a nature now like God's. I have been born again.

Then God looked at you and me in all our individuality. He looked at that ancient one, older than the oldest meteorite, trying to destroy us. He said, "I think one of the members of the Godhead had better go dwell in them and be their Teacher."

How hard will it be for you to justify your failures on that day when He says, "Was the Holy Spirit inadequate?"

"No."

"Did He not offer to edify you?"

"Yes."

"When the devil attacked that last time, was he stronger than Me and you?"

"No."

"Then did I not give you enough money to finish your course?"

"It did not come."

"Are you saying We did not give it at the cross?"

"No."

"Did He not teach you how to receive it? Did you go to school?"

If you ever really want to do anything, there is one luxury you have to give up: excuses.

I have heard my own voice after giving a whole stream of them. "I was tired..." I heard it, heard it, heard it.

When God started opening these things up, I cannot tell you what it did to my faith. Things I wondered about in Him were confirmed and justified. It let Him finish tearing down the things that are not supposed to be until all the debris is cleared off the foundation built on the rock.

Jesus said the man who hears His word and does it is like a man who dug deep and laid his foundation on a rock. If doing His word is the rock, then the deep digging must be the understanding of it, clearing debris and trash away, digging deep, and laying the foundation on the rock.

He did not say the stream would not come, rise, and beat on your house. Jesus guaranteed it would. What He changed was your outcome. When the storm is over, you have cleared the debris, and you are on the rock.

Beginning to know Him, who He is, I started understanding His sovereignty, His judgments, how He really moves, how He dealt with people, why He had to deal with them the way He did, what it means to me, how He came down through the ages, the price He paid, and what He has given me to understand and operate in.

Getting to know Him did all those things. The first thing it did was give me the strength to finish clearing the trash off so the reborn foundation will be the one I finish with.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

The foundation I get to build now is not going to be destroyed because of money, and it is not going to be destroyed because something bad happens along the way. Disease will not cancel me out. I have been coming out of it.

I was sick through my thirties and into my forties. I had back trouble, a tilted vertebra, and sciatic trouble trying to go bad on me just because I was a nice guy. No, it is important to me to whip whatever comes down the road, because I will whip the next thing too.

I get to finish my course. I am not going to offer excuses or apologize for any part of it.

When I am old, this is what Jesus says I am supposed to do: gather my children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren around me. I will not be on a morphine drip. I will not be in a coma. I will not be screaming because of pain. Why scare them about the end?

I will say, "Kids, you are going to be just as blessed as I am to come to this day. I get to put this thing off pretty soon, and it is not bad. It served me well when I got it under subjection. There is nothing to be afraid of here. I am telling you, He is faithful. Serve Him."

That is what the Holy Spirit has been doing. Sometimes He has to go back, and you have to let Him finish taking the trash off the foundation, shore it up, and build. Let Him finish in you what He has begun. He is able.

The Spirit Reveals the Firstborn's Standing to Us

Yes, we are up against an ancient enemy. We do not know what that creature knows. But we do know that a member of the Godhead stepped down inside us and offered to become our Teacher.

Jesus said the Spirit would receive of Mine and show it unto you. What is He showing? The standing Jesus has with the Father in His humanity, not His deity. His standing as the Firstborn is the one He gave to me.

Jesus is as much God as He is man. You are as much man as you are man, or as much woman as you are woman, and you have God. He is deity and humanity, and He can operate totally out of both. He says, "My standing with My Father as the Firstborn is what I am going to reveal unto you."

So who does He send? The Holy Ghost.

I cannot read. I do not have a Bible. I am abandoned on this island by a faulty boat. Who will be my Teacher? I was just born again, I did not get a Bible, and I heard something about the Holy Ghost, but I know nothing.

You can get everything from God that He has because you have God to get you with. One member of the Godhead has come with the ministry just for that. He will receive of Mine and show it unto you.

"But I know no Word, and I am too dumb. I do not know how to pray. How do you pray to God when you do not know any Word?"

The Godhead says, "We have another problem to solve. Should we go so far as to do their praying for them?"

Another member says, "That is a good thought, but we had better take the understanding of it away from them so we can build them up above some of those lustful problems. They need to get a good look at how ugly those things are, then they will turn them loose. We had better not let them in on it until they are ready."

So God gives praying in tongues. Much of it you do not know what you are praying for, but He lets you know this much: you are edified and built up on your most holy faith.

"Do you think we can get them to pray if we do it for them?"

"I have it from a good source that this is going to be hard."

"Do not tell Me you ran ahead and looked at the future, You member of the Godhead. Stay with us here."

"But I saw it. There will be people who jump up and say, 'Pray too much in tongues and you will get weird.' They will misjudge it, teach wrong about it, and talk people out of doing it. Some of them will have a Bible and still know nothing."

The Holy Ghost says, "Wait, I am the only One left. You want Me to go live with them?"

"Yes. Take up Your abode with them."

It does not matter what you know or how much you know. He will pray a mystery out, and that gives Him the authority to begin to move on your spiritual mind to understand what He prayed.

I got caught here last week, so I am not too concerned how old Lucifer is, how long he has been around, what he knows, or how bad he is. He cannot make me do it. He has to get me to do it. Greater is He who is in me than he who is in the world.

I am not too concerned.

Iniquity Was Found in Lucifer and Then Introduced to Our Creation

John 8:44 says:

Ye are of your father the devil, and the lusts of your father ye will do.

He was a murderer from the beginning. Our beginning: not just humanity's beginning, but our universe. He did not abide in the truth. There is no truth in him. When he speaks a lie, he speaks of his own, for he is a liar and the father of it.

Most people quote it as though Jesus said Satan is the father of all liars. He is more than that. Jesus said he is the father of it. He is the origin of all darkness, all iniquity, everything on that side. All of it had its origin with him.

Ezekiel 28:13 says:

Thou hast been in Eden the garden of God; every precious stone was thy covering...

Sardius, topaz, diamond, beryl, onyx, jasper, sapphire, emerald, carbuncle, and gold. That is the first time I ever got bored going past that much wealth.

Then:

The workmanship of thy tabrets and of thy pipes was prepared in thee in the day that thou wast created.

Notice: he was created. You were born.

Verse 14:

Thou art the anointed cherub that covereth; and I have set thee so.

Then:

Thou wast upon the holy mountain of God; thou hast walked up and down in the midst of the stones of fire.

Verse 15:

Thou wast perfect in thy ways from the day that thou wast created, till iniquity was found in thee.

He is the father of it: the father of all darkness and iniquity. There is no truth in him, even though before this happened he was perfect in his ways. But iniquity was found in him.

Usually, when something is found, it was not obvious until someone came across it. Something found is often something searched for. This insinuates that he probably operated for a while as this iniquity developed in him until it was found, exposed, and uncovered.

He had to be thrown out of heaven. He did not leave because suddenly it thundered and boomed and his iniquity lit up like a big light bulb and Jesus appeared on the scene immediately to throw him out. He had time to work with angels. A third of them went with him.

I would like to have heard some of the persuasive things he said to them when that iniquity began to surface.

Creation's Complexity Points Back to a Mind

When Scripture says iniquity was found in him, think about our race. Everybody is trying to find out where we came from, the origin of the human race. The brilliant intellects of our generations always lean toward evolution, though more and more are learning creation.

They are finding out there is matter even smaller than electrons or particles. Something has to make up an electron, proton, and neutron. Electrons are so small you can only measure their movement by equipment. You cannot even see them. Now they are finding something smaller than them, some kind of world that exists in the electron.

They said the basic unit of matter now discovered gets so small that when one of those particles floats through the planet, it touches nothing because the planet is great caverns to it. That is small.

You think computers are something now. Your phone about does everything for you. You could lose your memory depending on that phone. But they are developing some kind of photon cube, where particles of light will store information. They are finding that the smaller the particle gets, the more complex it seems.

Some of the most brilliant minds, now that they are getting further into creation, are starting to say, "This is impossible. There must be a mind behind it."

Evolution is like having a wrecking yard where a car has been deteriorating for a hundred years, blocks falling down, grass growing over everything, and a junkyard dog patrolling the area. Then a tornado comes through, and when it is done, every car has been restored. That is the chance of evolution. That is where you came from, if you believe that: a tornado went through your junkyard.

So Ezekiel insinuates that all this trouble had its beginning in him. Where did any of it come from? It was found in him. He is the father of all of it. All destruction, disorder, and disarray was found in him.

Again, the insinuation is that this process took place over a period of time.

The Guarded Tree Would Have Sealed Fallen Man's Doom

The fact that God had to put cherubim to guard the Tree of Life means they were stopping somebody more powerful than Adam. What shocked me was that Adam could get up and eat off the tree, even in his fallen state. The fact that God put cherubim there means it could be done. You do not guard something that cannot be done. You guard something that can be done.

What kind of Tree of Life is this, that it could seal your doom forever right where you are?

God said, "The man has become like one of Us." Hear, O Israel, the Lord our God is one. Where did "Us" come from? Who was He talking to? There is one God, only one God, but He consists of three Persons, and the three Persons are of one essence.

God said they had become as Us, to know both good and evil.

Now that they were capable of knowing evil and good, evidently the mistake Lucifer made when iniquity was being born in him is that he kept nourishing the iniquity. Obviously, that garden had a tree just like our garden.

People say, "Why did Lucifer fall? He had no tempter."

That is why he fell.

But he did have a will. He was a servant, created to serve. He was not created as family. The thing that makes us different is that we can procreate eternal creatures. Any baby you have, even if you have an abortion and think you killed it, you did not kill that child. Somebody else is raising that child right now.

Obviously, there was a tree there. When God stepped into our creation, He took cherubim, Lucifer's class, because Lucifer knew what would happen. It had to be an angelic class, his class, to stop him. He knew that if he got Adam and Eve, now that they had tasted good and evil, to eat off the Tree of Life, they would live forever in that state.

He should know. God said it out of His own mouth:

Behold, the man is become as one of us, to know good and evil: and now, lest he put forth his hand, and take also of the tree of life, and eat, and live forever...

The sentence trails off like the consequences are something you would not even want to know. God put cherubim with flaming swords turning every way to guard the way of the Tree of Life.

If Adam and Eve, like Lucifer, had begun to nourish themselves off the tree and held on to evil while knowing both good and evil, their accountability would have come to the place where there would be no way back. Their doom would have been sealed.

Some people think that means we would have populated planet after planet as sinners killing each other forever. No. Your body would return to dust, like God said. Your spirit would go to prison, just like at the end of this dispensation. You are not going to roam and hurt anybody. You are going to stay there forever.

They would not run around on top of the planet and live forever. They would have been incarcerated forever and ever.

Obviously, before Lucifer was thrown out, he came to that state, and the angels came to it with him. Why would a person even want evil when they had all the good for the ages Lucifer had? Yet we have people now who are born again and filled with the Holy Ghost, and they fight for the right to sin. The spirit of that is still here. If they keep it up, they are going to get what they want.

I knew there was a reason they fell. I knew God did not let Lucifer fall without putting up a fight to get him to turn.

We Receive Truth to Build a Foundation That Will Not Fall

Stand up and lift your hands.

Father, we worship You, thank You, and glorify You. You are giving us some idea from the eternities of times past. We are finding out again that You are just, and You are full of tender lovingkindness and mercies. You have taken care of us.

Father, we are going to reach this city for You. We know the common level where religion is being employed has not done the job. What everybody needs, and what we need, is for You to come. We need You to come. That will make the difference.

If we are going to have something we have never had before, I guess we are going to have to do something we have not done before.

Thank You for Your understanding. There is a group of us who are going to fast and pray this year, Father. We are going to receive the thing that will make the difference in this city, and we want You to know that You get all the glory for it.

We humble ourselves and bow down at Your feet. We surrender ourselves in all the glory and majesty.

Thank You for trusting us. Thank You for trusting us enough to commit these truths to us. We are building our foundation, and we will not fall. Especially at critical mass, we will not bring as much reproach as the good we have done. We will not.

Thank You for Your strength.

In Jesus' name, amen.
