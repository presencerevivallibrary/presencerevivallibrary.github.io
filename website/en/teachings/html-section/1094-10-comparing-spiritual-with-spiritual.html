1094-10 Comparing Spiritual With Spiritual

The Born-Again Trail Restores the Missing Pieces

How many of you have been getting that letter we did and had time to go through it a little bit?

Hallelujah. Barbara was sure excited about it. I have the finish of it again in the last ten pages or so, so I am going to go into that a little bit. Last week we introduced another level of depth through the new nature, and I want to continue from that.

How many remember when I first broke through and God visited me in my office, and I got my first look down the Born-Again Trail? I can remember before the first camp meeting when I began to preach it. Sue, I think, asked me, "What are you going to teach on in the camp meeting?"

I said, "I am going to preach on being born again."

It was to a bunch of preachers, so Sue said, "Yeah, right. Can't you see he is just kidding?"

I was a little lost in the Spirit, thinking about the revelation and how it was going to unfold, and it just came out of my spirit: being born again. Of course, the Born-Again Trail was tagged on later.

I am not going to make it very far, am I?

Hallelujah.

I remember when I was in that rapport with Him, communing in my heart, and He was answering everything as though the gift of faith was on me. The communion was over everything He said I could have, and I related it to those great verses:

"All things are possible to him that believeth."

I said, "Where is that? We know the Bible says that, but so few ever break into anything even similar to that."

I asked, "Where are the verses that make the transition from just falling into the crowd and having a normal, mediocre operation with God? I am not talking about what men can accomplish in the natural. I am talking about bringing God here, instead of just following in mediocrity, complacency, and being beaten into submission by pressure until you finally give up. Where are those verses?"

That is when He told me they are in most of the verses people leave out.

As you know, the Born-Again Trail consists mostly of the pieces that have been left out of the walks everybody has given us. It is not that the trail is so great in itself; it is that these are the pieces left out. When you begin to understand what you have, where you can go, how to fellowship with God, what He is after, and the part of you that can understand Him, the limit is off.

I get to go as far as I want to in God.

The New Nature Releases the Presence That Heals

I was having a good time up there because I found there was healing in the presence that comes from touching Him with your spirit. I have been having the greatest fun with that healing that is in the presence when it comes.

I say, "Come over here," and they get healed.

One man brought his fiancee. I think she had never been in a service where people fall down. He brought her over and said she had kidney problems, and she had pain in her kidneys then and had it all the time.

I said, "You had better come over here and see."

He came. The presence just filled the whole atmosphere. He took all her pain. Meanwhile, she fell down on the ground. Her knees buckled and she fell.

Later, when she got up, I said, "Do you know about the falling down and everything?"

She said, "No."

I said, "Why did you fall down there?"

She said, "I do not know."

But she was healed.

Satan Reigns Through the Death Nature as God Reigns Through the New Nature

We were finding out that the devil reigns through the death nature the same way God reigns through our new nature. Satan used the fear of death to reign over the human race.

It is no harder for Satan to get a man who has the sin nature to sin than it is to get a swarm of piranhas to eat a cow after you throw it into the Amazon. That is the nature of sin.

Satan used the fear of death to manipulate the human race. He kept giving us answers that were not answers, such as Buddhism, Mohammedism, and all these things that promise some kind of life after death because of the fear of death.

Spiritual death is what Satan uses to reign with. That is where he gets his power.

Thank God, God reigns through the hidden man of the heart, the spirit man, with the new nature. The sin nature moved on us, and we obeyed it on different levels, from Hitlers to nice tax-paying citizens who keep the law, have their beer busts, run their four-wheel drives at each other, cuss a lot, and are just good old boys who take care of their families. They yield to the sin nature in all degrees. It just depends on who got hold of you and trained you.

There were little grandmas who baked bread and sent little Hans off to war. They did not know what was going on in the war until they were sent into the concentration camps as part of the cleanup program. They made the grandmas look at heaps of bodies piled up and starved to death. The grandmas were nauseated and sick.

But not little Hans, whom they had baked bread for and sent off to war when he was thirteen. Not little Hans. He had already been trained in the fathomless depths of that unregenerated sin nature. They were over there joking and eating while the little grandmas were throwing up.

Their spirits were trained. It just did not bother them.

All of you who had a sin nature had that same capacity. The sin nature moved on our reasonings and our mind and got us to obey it on one level or another.

But now my new nature is moving on my mind on one level or another. It is my decision. I am a free-will moral agent. How much I yield to it is my decision.

The more I pray in the Holy Ghost, the more I turn that new nature loose in my soul, and the more my decisions are based on how free I am in that new nature.

You can just sit in church, barely make it into church, barely make it into heaven, and barely get out of bed. You are a free-will moral agent. It is your choice what you want to do with what He gave you. Absolutely your choice.

You can yield to that new nature moving on you at any level and stop at any level. You can enter heaven with nothing more than being born again, sitting around in church saying, "I am okay. I am going to church."

Yes, you are okay as far as making it to heaven, but you are not okay. You need a good dose of what it feels like to lose your reward.

Or you can yield to the new nature, conquer, and reign through righteousness.

I love this stuff.

How many are going to reign? I happen to know I am.

"How do you know you are?"

Because He said I am. I have what I say.

Hallelujah. Look out, devil. You already had your time with me. My turn. I am going to get so much of God that I will make you turn loose of my friends, disease and everything else. In fact, I am going to make you turn loose of a whole lot of the population of humanity.

Devil, I do not like you very much. I plan on taking a lot of your friends away from you.

Glory be to God forever.

Solomon's Servants Show the Joy of Standing Near Wisdom

You remember where we wound up.

When King Solomon was at his fullness, he was something to behold. Even Jesus made reference to his wisdom. Under the shadow, the wisdom King Solomon reigned with was a type of what the new nature is to your born-again spirit.

The Queen of the South was not lacking in wisdom herself and ruled a whole kingdom. When she heard the wisdom of Solomon, there was no more spirit left in her. There was not even a microfiber of anything anywhere in her entire being that did not totally succumb to a wisdom so strong it did not just leave her surrendered; it left her stunned.

Second Chronicles 9:3-7 says:

"And when the queen of Sheba had seen

the wisdom of Solomon,

and the house that he had built,

and the meat of his table,

and the sitting of his servants,

and the attendance of his ministers,

and their apparel;

his cupbearers also,

and their apparel;

and his ascent by which he went up

into the house of the Lord;

there was no more spirit in her.

And she said to the king,

It was a true report which I heard

in mine own land

of thine acts,

and of thy wisdom:

howbeit I believed not their words,

until I came,

and mine eyes had seen it:

and, behold,

the one half of the greatness

of thy wisdom was not told me:

for thou exceedest the fame that I heard.

Happy are thy men,

and happy are these thy servants,

which stand continually before thee,

and hear thy wisdom."

When I was meditating on this, I said, "God, what is in these few verses for me here about Your servants? Happy are they who stand in Your presence for ten or eleven hours."

They were happy attendants who got within hearing distance of Solomon and wanted to stand there ten or eleven hours just to hear what was coming out of his mouth. Yet he operated in the shadow of the rebirth, not in the rebirth itself.

Again, the wisdom Solomon reigned with was to Solomon's kingdom what the new nature is to your born-again spirit. So reign with Him. Why don't you reign with Him?

Hallelujah.

I said, "These servants were happy?"

Yes, they were exceedingly happy.

I said, "Why particularly? Just hearing a man's wisdom ten or eleven hours a day, would you not get tired?"

He said, "Son, let Me ask you something. How many preachers do you know that you could sit in their presence that long every day just to hear what they had to say?"

I said, "None. I get tired of myself."

Spiritual Growth Depends on Feeding What Is Eternal

He went on to explain, "Your spirit is not like your body. If you quit feeding your body, it will die. But if you quit feeding your spirit, it will continue to exist. It does not need substance to survive. It is eternal and self-existing. If you do not feed your spirit, it will not quit existing; it will quit developing."

You can be in the same spiritual place your whole lifetime that you were when you were first saved.

If you do not feed your spirit, you will not develop. The only reason our rewards will be different in heaven is not because of the difference in our calls, but because all of us received the same thing when we were born again. At the high price of our individuality, we choose what we will do with it.

There is something about our will and right to choose that God values as highly as anything else He does in all creation. If you think we are all going to get to heaven as generic little copies of each other with no individuality, think again.

If we did not have the power to choose more or less of God, then the reward would all be the same. But it is not.

Before I was born again, I inherited a sin nature from the first man Adam. I did not inherit Adam's individual human spirit, but I did inherit the spiritual death that was in his spirit. Adam has his own spirit. I have mine.

After I was born again, I did not inherit Jesus' individual human spirit. I have my own. But what I did inherit was the spiritual life that was in His spirit. Jesus has His own spirit. I have mine. What I received from Him was a new nature.

As individual as I was when I had a sin nature is how individual I am now that I have a new nature.

The same way the old nature would move on my emotions and reasonings to sin, the new nature now moves on my emotions and reasonings to do righteous works.

All sinners move on different levels of sin as they respond to the sin nature, from good productive taxpayers and citizens to the overcrowded conditions of our penal systems. In the same way, all believers respond on different levels to the new nature, from those who serve themselves all the way to those who have given their whole lives as servants of God.

Our individuality is still intact. We are just being influenced by another nature.

Hallelujah.

The Divine Nature Gives Capacity to Understand God

The only part of us the Holy Spirit has to work with is our new nature. It is the only part of us that can understand spiritual things and grow in God.

The reason our spirits can grow in God is because God is a Spirit, and He has given us a nature like His own. If He wants to teach us something, He can do so by getting us to understand the things of His nature by using the new nature He gave us to understand it with.

Second Peter 1:4 says:

"Whereby are given unto us

exceeding great and precious promises:

that by these

ye might be partakers

of the divine nature,

having escaped the corruption

that is in the world through lust."

This means I can understand His Word because His Word makes me a partaker of His divine nature, and I have a new nature to partake of it with.

Hallelujah. I can hardly stand this.

This is why the Holy Spirit bypassed your mind and emotions and baptized your spirit. When He begins to nurture your spirit and bring forth your new nature, joy, peace, and faith will be the dominant factors of your individuality as the new nature moves on you to make godly choices, serve God, and be filled with His power and glory.

If I ever lived in any kind of godly fear, it would be to miss this prayer and power conference. There is a new and stronger anointing than ever before.

I have found that if you can touch God with your spirit, everything heaven is and everything heaven has can flow through you.

The principle of the sower overtaking the reaper is a precious operation of God's grace. It has taken me twenty-seven years to learn about precious things that cannot be earned. In a conference like this, I can sow twenty-seven years of harvest into your field, so you can take my harvest and overtake it in a much shorter time.

My greatest year, we averaged 4,000 born again a week. So come on. I am waiting for your dust.

Oh, you were here when we did it, so you were part of it.

Hallelujah.

Baby Piranha Understands the Nature He Shares

I have never had so much fun translating revelation knowledge into parables you can understand as I did in this letter and teaching.

Something hard to understand is the new nature. It is not my brain, mind, intellect, or emotion. It is a nature. It is a nature that gives me capacity to understand God.

When Mama Goldfish adopted Baby Piranha, she tried to teach Baby Piranha the ways of the goldfish. But Baby Piranha was having a lot of trouble understanding what Mama Goldfish was talking about. When she tried to teach him how to make goldfish houses, layers, and nests that goldfish live in, he could hear her words and understand what she was saying with his brain, but he could not discern what she was talking about.

Finally she said, "I do not know what I am going to do with you. You keep having your friends for lunch, and the neighborhood is afraid of you."

Then they found out Mother Piranha was not dead, and Mother Piranha came back to claim Baby Piranha. The adopted Mother Goldfish was kind of glad about all this.

Baby Piranha said, "You are my mama?"

"Yes, I am, son."

"Mama Piranha, what big teeth you have. You are not like my Mama Goldfish. Her mouth was round and bubbles came out of it. I did not understand her. Where are we going, Mama Piranha?"

"We are swimming underneath the fence and going through the river, because a bunch of cows live down there. We are going to hang around with the others and wait for one of them. In fact, we are just going to really pray that one of those cows falls in the water."

"Then what are we going to do, Mama?"

"We are going to tear all the flesh off its bones and eat it."

"Oh yes, I can understand that. I know exactly what you are saying. I do not know how, but my mouth is already watering."

Why? Because Baby Piranha has the nature of a piranha. Therefore, he can understand the ways of a piranha. He knows exactly what his mama is saying.

When you were born again, you received the nature of God. Now you can know the things of God that are freely given to you because they are spiritually discerned. There is a part of you that can understand God. Now He can teach you because you can understand Him.

Discernment is more than knowledge. Knowledge you can have in your brain, but to discern means you understand why something is the way it is. It is not just knowledge.

Hidden Wisdom Became Speakable After the New Nature Came

Now we will understand these verses better than ever before.

First Corinthians 2:3-5 says:

"And I was with you in weakness,

and in fear,

and in much trembling.

And my speech and my preaching

was not with enticing words

of man's wisdom,

but in demonstration

of the Spirit and of power:

that your faith should not stand

in the wisdom of men,

but in the power of God."

Then Paul says:

"Howbeit we speak wisdom

among them that are perfect:

yet not the wisdom of this world,

nor of the princes of this world,

that come to nought:

but we speak the wisdom of God

in a mystery,

even the hidden wisdom,

which God ordained before the world

unto our glory."

He speaks wisdom among those who are mature, but not the wisdom we got from this world or from its kings, rulers, and princes, whose wisdom always comes to nothing at the end of their generation and reign.

Paul says we speak the wisdom of God in a mystery, even the hidden wisdom which God ordained before the world unto our glory. That mystery is Christ in you, the hope of glory.

He called it hidden wisdom because it used to be hidden in God on the other side of spiritual death. But the Apostle Paul went into God and pulled out that mystery, the mystery of who Christ is in you, the hope of glory. Now that wisdom once hidden in God can be spoken to you.

At one time, it was hidden in a mystery. Paul went into God, brought that mystery back, and now can speak the wisdom that was hidden in it to you because you are born again. It is not hidden on the other side of spiritual death anymore, because you and I now have a capacity to understand it.

Somebody stop me while you still have time. I am growing every day. If you are going to stop me, you had better do it early.

The Born-Again Spirit Searches the Deep Things of God

First Corinthians 2:7-9 says:

"But we speak the wisdom of God

in a mystery,

even the hidden wisdom,

which God ordained before the world

unto our glory:

which none of the princes of this world knew:

for had they known it,

they would not have crucified

the Lord of glory.

But as it is written,

Eye hath not seen,

nor ear heard,

neither have entered into the heart of man,

the things which God hath prepared

for them that love him."

Why could it not enter into man? Because the princes and their domains that came and went were all spiritually dead. It was impossible for it to enter into their hearts.

But now, verse 10 says:

"But God hath revealed them unto us

by his Spirit:

for the Spirit searcheth all things,

yea, the deep things of God."

Everybody says, "Is he talking about the Holy Spirit there, revealing it to us?"

No. Here he is talking about the born-again spirit He has given to you, and the nature to understand Him with.

In the verse before, when they were spiritually dead, kingdoms came and went, and eye had not seen nor ear heard, neither had it entered into the heart of man, the things God had prepared for those who love Him. But now that I have been born again, I have a heart those things can enter into: the mystery of everything Christ has in me, through me, and through me, the hope of glory.

"This is not talking about the Holy Spirit?"

No, it is not.

When God searches Himself, what do you think? Do you think the Holy Spirit searches the Father? Do you think the deity of Jesus searches the other two? They do not search each other. They are omnipresent, omniscient, all-knowing, without time, and omnipotent, all-powerful. All three possess the same attributes and know the same things. They do not have to search each other.

You are the one who received the spirit that can now search the fathomless depths of God because you have a nature to search Him with.

Hallelujah. I have a nature I can search Him with now.

Proverbs 20:27 says:

"The spirit of man is the candle of the Lord,

searching all the inward parts of the belly."

That was literally a prophecy given for our day, for when we would be born again. God says, "What is your human spirit to Me? The spirit of man is My candle."

When you were born again, what did He do? He stepped forward and lit your spirit with a new nature. Now He can use that new nature to teach you, searching all the inward parts of your belly.

God says, "Your new nature is the part of you that can understand Me. I can go in and search all the inward parts of your belly by comparing My nature to yours and showing you what you need from Me."

Hallelujah. Glory be to God forever.

We Received God's Spirit Nature, Not Adam's Spirit of the World

First Corinthians 2:10-11 says:

"But God hath revealed them unto us

by his Spirit:

for the Spirit searcheth all things,

yea, the deep things of God.

For what man knoweth the things of a man,

save the spirit of man

which is in him?

even so the things of God

knoweth no man,

but the Spirit of God."

That Spirit of God is the born-again human spirit.

When Paul says, "What man knoweth the things of a man, save the spirit of man which is in him?" he is saying, "How do you have a capacity to know anything outside of your spiritual depth? You cannot understand or know anything outside of your nature."

If I am spiritually dead, I am limited to know the things of my spiritual death. How do I have a capacity to explore anything outside of that?

That is why Paul said:

"Is the law sin? God forbid.

Nay, I had not known sin,

but by the law:

for I had not known lust,

except the law had said,

Thou shalt not covet."

A spiritually dead man did not even know what sin was until God came along and said, "Stop coveting." He tried to stop and could not.

Why? Because the spirit of an unregenerated man has no way to explore outside of that nature. He could not know God.

Now that you have been born again, what man can know the things of God except the new nature in his born-again spirit? That spirit can begin to search the deep things of God.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

First Corinthians 2:12 says:

"Now we have received,

not the spirit of the world,

but the spirit which is of God;

that we might know

the things that are freely given to us

of God."

We did not receive the spirit that is of this world. That is not the spirit the first man Adam gave us, which was spiritually dead. That is not what we got from God. We received the spirit which is of God.

If this is talking about the Holy Ghost, why did Paul have to tell you that the precious Holy Spirit was not the devil? Why would he have to take a verse and tell you that the spirit you received was not the spirit of this world? You would know that was the Holy Spirit you received.

The spirit of this world was the spirit I inherited from the first man Adam, spiritually dead. I inherited his death. The one we received from God means I now have His nature, and now I can know the things freely given to us of God.

Why? Because I now have a nature to understand them.

The Holy Ghost Teaches by Comparing God's Nature to Our New Nature

Now we can take the next verses further than we ever have before.

First Corinthians 2:13 says:

"Which things also we speak,

not in the words

which man's wisdom teacheth,

but which the Holy Ghost teacheth;

comparing spiritual things

with spiritual."

What things? The things we can freely know of God. Paul says, "Now we speak and teach them." These are the things once hidden in the mystery, but Paul went into God and pulled that wisdom out because we have God's nature now and the capacity to understand spiritual things.

He says, "Which things also we speak," or teach, not in the words man's wisdom teaches, but which the Holy Ghost teaches.

Paul is saying, "I was first taught these things by the Holy Spirit, and then I taught you." The words the Holy Ghost teaches are not the words of man's wisdom, the wisdom of kingdoms that come and go.

What he is really making reference to is that first he prayed in tongues, and God used that language and revelation gift to teach him the mysteries of God by comparing the spiritual things of God with spiritual, which means comparing the things of God's nature to his new nature.

The Holy Ghost, your Teacher, used that supernatural language of tongues to edify Paul and teach him the mysteries once hidden in God. Now Paul is taking those same mysteries and teaching them to anybody who wants to hear them.

When the Holy Ghost taught those things to Paul, He compared spiritual to spiritual, just as when Paul teaches you, he compares the spiritual things of God to the new nature in you so you can understand them.

The Holy Ghost used that whole supernatural language of edification to continually compare the spiritual things of God to the Apostle Paul's new nature until Paul wrote them down, and that is what you have in a book.

Notice:

"Comparing spiritual things with spiritual."

For the Holy Ghost to teach you, He has to have something to compare it with. Draw a line. On one side is heaven and all the mysteries of Christ. On the other side is you. God will compare something He has to something you already have, or there is no comparison.

To compare something, there have to be two sides of the fence. There has to be something to compare it to.

This is what Paul is saying: the Holy Ghost can now take all the things of God and the nature of God, cross over to your side, and compare them with your nature, because you have been born again. You have the nature of God, so now He can use your nature to compare the things of God with. Because you have His nature, you can understand them.

Comparing spiritual with spiritual. This is the capacity I now have.

No wonder the Holy Ghost bypassed your mind, will, intellect, and emotions and stepped straight into your new nature. He has a nature like the nature in your spirit, because He has the same nature as the Father and the Son.

You pray in the Holy Ghost and pray those mysteries down. The Holy Ghost then takes those mysteries and crosses over to your side of the fence, where you have the capacity to understand Him with your nature, and begins to compare the things of your spirit with the things of God.

The Natural Man Cannot Receive What Only the New Nature Discerns

There is no comparison unless there are two things to compare. You compare twins because they are just alike. The Holy Ghost would have nothing to compare the things of God with had you not received a new nature.

He identifies the spiritual things of God to the part of you that now has that capacity, the part of you that makes you a spiritual man because you have a new nature.

Get it. The new nature is the part of you that understands Him. It is the only part of you that can understand the things of God.

When you feed that new nature, it begins to grow. It starts influencing your reasonings, emotions, and choices. They become more godly, and you begin to conquer and make more right decisions as you learn how to walk out of that new nature, as the Holy Ghost becomes your personal Teacher and Nurturer.

I do not know how we missed this. These are the verses we leave out: comparing spiritual with spiritual.

Then Paul jumps back to the natural man to make another comparison. He shows you what he is doing in verse 13 by jumping back to the natural man in verse 14:

"But the natural man receiveth not

the things of the Spirit of God:

for they are foolishness unto him:

neither can he know them,

because they are spiritually discerned."

What part of the natural man is he talking about? Just the flesh? No. He is talking about a man who has not been born again, who has a sin nature, and that sin nature is the only thing his mind has to understand God with. He cannot understand Him.

The only thing he can understand is by faith: you need to give your heart to God because you are a sinner and Jesus really needs to save you. That is about the only thing he can have faith for.

The natural man does not receive the things of the Spirit of God because they are foolishness to him. They are foolishness to Baby Piranha when Mama Goldfish has him. She wants him to play with his friends, and he wants to eat them.

Neither can he know them. Why not? Because they are only spiritually discerned by your new nature, the part of you that is like God.

In case you did not catch it, Paul made one more comparison. We have not received the spirit that is of this world, but the born-again spirit that is of God, so we can know the things freely given to us by God. Those things we teach, not in the words of men's wisdom in the world, but in the language the Holy Ghost has taught us, who compared the things of God to our new nature.

Your natural, unregenerated man cannot understand the things of God because they are understood spiritually by your new nature. The more you pray in tongues, the more comparison will be made and the more you will understand God's Spirit to your spirit until your whole being is ordered by Him.

Are you listening to me?

Praying in tongues is a revelation gift.

Spiritual Teaching Leaves People With Answers

First Corinthians 2:15 says:

"But he that is spiritual

judgeth all things,

yet he himself is judged of no man."

Why can he judge all things? Because he has been taught by that new nature, the Holy Ghost comparing the things of God to his spirit.

I did not receive any of these teachings from any man alive on the earth now. I was able to get them from Paul, who received them from God. He is a man, but I did not receive them from anybody alive now.

When I taught you these things, you had a spiritual capacity to understand them. I got to be a teacher who took the things of God and compared them to your new nature, so you were able to discern them, understand them, and begin to operate in them.

I had to keep praying in the Holy Ghost until I understood what Paul said. I understand it now.

The other day I almost broke down and cried because I heard testimony after testimony. They came up to the tape table. We sold three or four cases of books, sold them all out. People grabbed them up and came up with testimonies about what the book was doing. They could not stand it, so immediately they thought of people having problems understanding the Holy Spirit.

It changes people where it finds them.

I heard testimony after testimony and wept because He said to me, "Son, what would you do if Paul had not left his legacy behind, his letters?"

I said, "Oh God, if there had just been some statue of him broken up in front of some pillar, we would only have heard about him as a saint, one of the apostles born out of due season. I do not know what I would have done if he had not left those teachings behind, because those teachings changed my life where they found me, two thousand years after he was gone."

He said, "Listen to Me closely now, son. Whether you are doing a one-minute teaching, do not just get up and say something nice. People have problems, and they do not have answers."

I hear so much preaching it makes me want to cry. I always judge it by walking away and asking, "What can I do with what I heard?" Most of the time, nothing.

He said, "I do not care if it is a one-minute teaching. Give them an answer. I do not care if it is fifteen minutes. Give them an answer. My people are dying from lack of knowledge."

Even though they are saturated with what we would call the Word, many people have gone on to take the very principles they were teaching and use them to serve greed and things like that. I am not the one who judged it. I am just repeating the judgment.

He said, "If it is fifteen minutes, give an answer. Give something that will change their lives."

That is what the Apostle Paul did, and that is why we do not understand him. They are spiritual letters, and they change your life from the core if you can understand anything he said. They set you free, whether by teaching us the danger of the law and how to get out from under it, as in his day, or by showing how the Holy Spirit has become our Teacher and can take the things of God and slowly feed them into our nature because we now have a nature that can discern the things of God and understand Him.

He has come to guide us into all truth.

The Lord told me, "No matter what you do in school, the things you write and the things you teach, always leave them with an answer."

If, for some reason, we are off on our millennial timing and somebody celebrated about a thousand years but did not change the calendar, just in case it is going to be a hundred years longer than we thought, wherever our material finds anybody, it will keep launching preachers into power and changing people's lives.

That is what He told me, and I just wanted to weep.

The Mind of Christ Is the Mind of the New Creation

First Corinthians 2:15-16 says:

"But he that is spiritual

judgeth all things,

yet he himself is judged of no man.

For who hath known the mind of the Lord,

that he may instruct him?

But we have the mind of Christ."

"He that is spiritual judgeth all things" means you are able to discern things in the light of the nature He gave you, according to His nature and teaching. It is pretty hard to be judged from the outside when you are being taught from the inside.

Then Paul says:

"But we have the mind of Christ."

In case you think Jesus' mind mystically floated down on the end of a string, and suddenly when you were born again you received His mind and know everything He knows, that is not what he means.

Some base teaching on this, saying that once you are born again and receive a new nature, you already know everything there is to know about God and only your soul needs to be transformed.

Wrong.

Your spirit has to be taught. It does not already know everything.

"But it says we have the mind of Christ."

I know he said that. The closest thing you have to His mind, in the way you are thinking, is the Holy Ghost, who does not speak of Himself, but whatever He hears, that He says. The Holy Ghost represents the mind of Christ in you. That is why He is teaching you.

But that is still not the mind of Christ that Paul says you have. I have the mind of Christ in the Holy Ghost, yes, but I do not yet have the knowledge of all the mind of Christ. I plan on having all He can teach me before I go home.

Everything pertinent to this walk in this body before I am glorified is mine. I want to line up all the children in wheelchairs and with Down syndrome. I know what was in His mind, and I want Him to transfer it to mine.

The question is, can I receive what is in His mind? Yes, I can.

Why? Because I have the mind of Christ.

What do I mean? I have the spirit of Christ. I used to have the spirit of Adam. I did not have Adam's personal human spirit; I have my own. But I inherited the death nature from Adam. By one man's transgression, sin entered into the world and death by sin. The whole human race inherited the spiritual death that was in Adam.

I do not have Adam's individual spirit. I have my own, but I had a spirit like his because I had his nature.

I do not have that anymore. Whose spirit do I have now? I have the spirit of the second Adam, which is the spirit of Christ.

I do not have Jesus' personal human spirit. I have my own. But I now have the nature that is in Christ. It is in me. So I have the spirit that is of Christ.

I have my own spirit, but I have His nature. I also now have the mind of Christ.

Do you think your spirit floats around without a mind? I have the spirit of Christ, and I have the mind that goes with the spirit of Christ. That is a spiritual mind. I have the mind Christ has in His spirit, in my spirit.

It is not His personal mind; it is my spirit. It goes with my spirit. But it is the mind that goes with the new creation, the mind of Christ, a mind now able to discern spiritual things because I have His nature.

So the Holy Ghost can teach me what is in the mind of Christ because I have that mind born into my new creation spirit when I was born again.

I have a mind like Christ, a mind with the capacity to receive the things of Jesus Christ.

Did you get that?

The mind of Christ is the mind that goes with the new creation, the hidden man of the heart. When you were regenerated, that is the mind you received. It is the same kind that is in Christ. It looks like His, and in its humanity has a capacity like His.

Now the Holy Ghost can take the mind of Christ and put it into you because you have a holder just like His to receive it.

It makes me wonder what I can know. Somebody stop me before I get too much further down the road.

One thing I am going to find out is what He had that could empty wheelchair after wheelchair. I am going to find out because I have the capacity to understand it.

We quote the verse, "Eye hath not seen, nor ear heard, the things God prepared." Yes, but then the man got born again and said:

"But God hath revealed them unto us by his Spirit."

So that day is over.

The mind of Christ is a mind just like Jesus had when He first appeared on this earth with a sin-free nature and a mind in that spirit. That is what you received. It looks just like His, and it will receive just like His.

Hallelujah.
