372-01 Breaching Divine Order

The Lord opened up Ephesians 5 to me by opening up 1 Corinthians 11.

It was after He opened 1 Corinthians 11 that I understood what He was talking about when He said a man prophesying or praying with his head covered dishonors his head, and a woman prophesying or praying with her head uncovered dishonors her head.

It does not sound like that has much to do with Ephesians 5, where He said:

Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave Himself for it.

He is the head, the covering of the church, and the Savior of the body. He said, "So ought men to love their wives."

Being the head of the church means that Jesus Christ, at the right hand of the Father, is the covering for the church. Savior is the same as provider. He is not only the covering of the church; He is the provider, the Savior of the body.

In Ephesians 5, Jesus went on to say, "I am speaking about a great mystery," one of the greater mysteries in the Bible. But He said, "I am not talking about marriage." He is relating to it, but not talking about it.

He said, "Husbands, you ought to love your wives as Christ loved the church." What He was trying to get over to you is how much Christ loves the church. It is a great mystery, He said, but I speak concerning Christ and the church.

He wants us to know Him from that position as Provider.

Christ's Love for the Church Is Revealed Through the Husband's Covering Role

In the same way a husband is supposed to cover and provide for his wife, Jesus wanted you to understand that He loves you. There are no problems with Him. He wants to provide for you.

He said, "Husbands, love your wives as your own bodies, for no man yet ever hated his own flesh, but nourisheth and cherisheth it."

He said, do yourself a favor and love your wife, because to love your wife is to love yourself. Yet Jesus was speaking of the mystery of the love Christ has for His church.

What He was saying is this: for Christ to deny you your needs would be to deny Himself. To not meet your need would be to not meet His need. To deny you would be to deny the fellowship He desires to have with you.

He is trying to get a position over to us that most of us do not understand very much because we do not know Him very well.

If He uses marriage as the institution to teach on the love Christ has for the church and man has for a woman, then we have a problem. The divorce rate in the church, even in the church, is higher than fifty percent. Broken homes, single-family dwellings, second, third, fourth marriages. People do not see commitment. They do not see love. They see betrayal. They see people forsaking each other.

And we are trying to understand the love Christ has for the church, measuring it and basing it on the marriage institution.

This is why the two churches that were pagan, the Corinthians and the Ephesians, had to be taught as they were. They had no background in the law. The first thing Paul had to teach the churches was one wife, commitment, and family, because of paganism.

By the time he taught on the relationship the church is supposed to have with Christ, he was able to use the marriage institution as an example in a way that we often cannot today.

A Wrong Spirit Has Attacked the Family Unit and Distorted Commitment

A wrong spirit has gotten in.

You say, "No, there is not a wrong spirit in."

I do not need any proof outside of a fifty-three percent divorce rate. I do not need any more proof.

In our generation, people are divorcing at a rate they have never divorced before, more than in the last generation, and the generation before that. It has been progressive.

Why? Because we are doing things right as a church? No. Not because we are doing things right, but because as a family unit, we are doing things wrong.

There is a spirit that has gotten in. When I talk about that spirit, man, I get dagger looks. People have left the church and said, "I am going to go somewhere where God will make me what I want to be."

Just because some man tells you something does not make it so. The ostrich syndrome does not work. You think if you stick your head in the sand, it will go away. The devil will come up behind you and kick you right in the behind.

He loves ignorance. His power is deception. As long as you do not know you are deceived, you will continue beating the air. You will continue down that path, because deception is not deception the moment you know you have been deceived. When you know, it breaks his power.

I do not need any other argument except the statistics. A wrong spirit has gotten in.

With the dope culture and emotions, people are either all or nothing. "You are my best buddy. I will be faithful to the end." The next day they have a gun at your head. There is no middle line with dope. Emotions are all the way, one hundred thousand percent, or they are nothing. There is no in-between.

That is why it is hard to hold a job when you are messed up on dope, much less commit to something like the family unit. Your family does not excel to the forefront. Your habit does.

If we are going to study the family unit to understand our relationship with Jesus, my God, we are going to have to exclude our psychiatrist case histories and go to the Word.

"My mother did it to me. My father did it to me. It was my mother. That is why I am depressed."

I know. But let me tell you something about Jesus. Freud was wrong. So what if you found out your mother did it? What are you going to do about it? Finding out your mother did it is not enough. You have to stand up and face the responsibility.

Here is the good part: he whom the Son has set free is free indeed. Greater is He that is in me than he that is in the world.

The Devil Breaches Divine Order to Pull Down the Protective Hedge

Let me tell you what the devil is after. He is after divine order. He wants to breach divine order. He wants to pull the hedge down so the serpents can come through and bite you.

He is after divine order. He wants the woman to wear the britches and the man to lay down and live off her, destroying divine order, breaching divine order.

There is no hedge that God builds with divine order that was meant to be breached. If it is breached, there are going to be consequences. It does not matter what you think about it. If there is a breach in a hedge, then serpents are going to come through it.

God instituted divine order for a reason. He knew there was a maniac in this world called the devil, and he is crazy. He is flat crazy.

When Jesus gave the warning, as we said last Sunday, He comes not but for to steal, kill, and destroy. There is no other reason he comes. One reason divided into three parts. He does not just want to stop you. He wants to destroy you off the face of the earth. He wants you to be in an unmarked grave, and people will walk on it and never know you lived.

You can meditate a million years, and I will tell you right now, you will never come up with any other reason for Satan to come. He comes not but for to steal, kill, and destroy.

Since his light has gone out, he has no other nature. He does not do anything else. He is not good at anything else. All he does is kill.

If you breach the hedge or weaken any area of it, then the least he will do is destroy anything you are doing.

God institutes divine order for a reason. To breach it does not mean you will not carry on with your life and do what you are doing. To breach it does not mean you cannot fulfill a ministry to a degree. To breach it does not mean you cannot go on in a certain level of blessing for a time.

But any time there is a breach in what God has instituted, something is going to come in through the hole.

The Attack on Marriage Is an Attack on the Next Generation

There has been a spirit against the church as a whole. That spirit is to destroy the family unit. You know as well as I do that this is what the devil wants to do. He wants to divide it and break it down.

He does not want committed parents raising the next generation of children. No, he wants to raise them. Because if he raises them, he will get them when they are adults.

The best way to breach the hedge and come in for your children is to let a bunch of sinners staring over a desk, beating on it with a mallet, decide where your children are going to go.

How come there is not as much shouting as last week? Aren't you excited?

How many know that when He drops something in my spirit, I cannot get away from it? Every time I slip into the Spirit, I go back to it. So what is the use?

"It will not make you very popular."

Heaven loves me. I am popular.

It was this eleventh chapter that opened up Ephesians 5. I began to understand Jesus as Provider.

Paul Names the Divine Order of Covering in First Corinthians Eleven

Let us look at that spirit trying to get into the church. Paul already nailed it here.

Beginning with 1 Corinthians 11:1:

Be ye followers of me, even as I also am of Christ.

This is Paul talking.

Now I praise you, brethren, that ye remember me in all things, and keep the ordinances, as I delivered them to you.

Keep the order as I handed it down to you, because there is divine order outlined by these ordinances as I delivered them unto you.

He says:

But I would have you know...

Is that not something, how he enters into this?

In these ordinances he delivered, he set these things into divine order, and now he is setting them in concrete in the form of teaching:

But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ;

and the head of the woman is the man;

and the head of Christ is God.

People have hated these verses for I do not know how long.

With that spirit trying to get into the church, women believe they have been handed a bad deal. They believe they have not been handed a rightful position of authority in Christ. But that is not what he is saying here.

He is setting both natural and divine order into place as far as our creation order and the position He created us for. He was not talking about our equality in God as new creations and born-again people, for we are all one. The woman is not diminished from the man, and the man is not diminished from the woman.

But concerning these verses, he says something very powerful. The head of Christ is God. The head of the woman is the man. The head of the church is Christ.

Another word for head is covering. The covering for Christ is God. The covering for the church is Christ. The covering for the woman is the man.

This is in order of creation. This is divine order. This is the hedge He has built up.

It is not that we cannot function in God, fulfill a ministry, and do things for God. But any time you breach that order, you weaken your chances of making it through this life intact, with your whole family, and going to heaven.

A Man Who Takes His Covering From His Wife Dishonors Christ

Verse 3 says again:

I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ;

and the head of the woman is the man;

and the head of Christ is God.

Then in the fourth verse he says:

Every man praying or prophesying, having his head covered, dishonoureth his head.

Notice this man is being used by God, praying or prophesying.

The word prophesying is not what we have made it to be in the church today. Here it also meant when God dropped a message by revelation into a prophet, pastor, or preacher, and he came out and gave that message by the Spirit. That was a form of prophesying.

He is talking about fulfilling a ministry, praying, and ministering. But he says doing so with his head covered dishonors his head.

Not understanding this, men have made their wives wear hats to churches for hundreds of years. Finally the wives got tired of pinning on a hat, so they got down to a little scarf thing that just covered a little of their head so they could be legal. Because the next verse said a woman prophesying or praying with her head uncovered dishonors her head.

When God opened these verses up, what they meant, then Ephesians 5 opened up, and I finally understood through the fullness how much Jesus wanted to meet my needs as Provider. I can depend on Him. He has established Himself as my covering and my Provider.

He wants me to fellowship with Him like a wife fellowships with her husband who is providing for her.

This is what he meant. A man prophesying or fulfilling his ministry with his head covered dishonors his head.

Who covers the man? Jesus Christ. The head he dishonors is Christ.

The reason he used words like dishonor is this: the man is prophesying or praying with his head covered, meaning he has laid down and taken his covering from a woman. She is out in the workplace tearing everything up, doing what is necessary, working two or three minimum-wage jobs, and he is taking his supply from her as his covering.

That is highly dishonoring to Christ, because it is a breach in everything He stands for as Christ is the head, covering of the church, and Provider of the body.

"Husbands, love your wives." That is why he said he dishonors Christ, taking his supply when that spirit has come in and changed the role. It breaches divine order, and it is highly dishonoring to Him. Highly.

A Woman Forced Into Provision Is Different From One Who Seizes the Covering Role

When this man lays down, and I hate lazy preachers worse than lazy husbands. I am not talking about the hate-hate. I am talking about the kind Jesus meant when He said, "If any man come to Me and hate not his father, mother, wife, and children." It means to love them less. I love a lazy preacher less than a lazy husband.

When the man has laid down and she has had to take that role, the role switches.

A woman prophesying or praying with her head uncovered dishonors her head now. The responsibility has fallen on her. Yet she herself may feel she has been slighted, that her mighty role she was called to be has been taken away from her. So she assumes the position of authority. Because he more than likely is some kind of penny waster, she takes it away from him.

That is highly dishonoring to Christ, because any breach in divine order pulls the hedge down. The serpent likes to get through.

Let me show you something today, women. The actual translation is also "wife." You should be so proud of your position. You should be absolutely flattered by Jesus Christ Himself that He created you in the position He created you in.

Men should be honored that Jesus Christ created them in the position they are in and gave them the added blessing of being able to provide for their wives and take care of their families.

There is a wrong spirit gaining ground on the church in the last day. It is not that this wrong spirit has not been in the church from the early church age. He is just gaining more ground as he finds more people to herald his cause.

But I uncovered something. Oh, I uncovered something. You talk about a Pentecostal fit. When He got done with me, I thought, Women should be so honored, so honored.

I know the hedge has been breached in the marriage unit, and the divorce rate is over half. I know that. I know it is hard to find yourself anymore in our society. I understand that.

I know many of you are forced out just under the covering of Christ Himself because you have had a lazy man lay down on you and think more of himself than of you, and abandon you with your children. I understand that.

I understand you have to get out there and fight that old spirit every day sometimes instead of doing what you want to do. I understand all of that. But it does not change the fact that you should bless God every day for honoring you with being born a woman.

Hallelujah.

Spiritual Equality Does Not Erase Created Roles

I found something that made me understand how Jesus looked at it. When I saw how Jesus looked at it, it set me so free. I hated to get these verses out. The devil would beat me so badly every time I mentioned these verses because of how the roles have become in the modern-day church.

Both are career people. We have to be, to keep up with the Joneses. So somebody else raises our kids.

Hallelujah.

Are you staying with me?

Do you understand how much God loves you when He does these kinds of things? If you keep the hedge, you will have longevity of ministry. You will be in full force when Jesus comes back, and you will be in full force when you are old.

If you keep His hedge up, keep divine order, honor everything God has taught you, and keep His ordinances, you will live a long time and be strong in ministry, because your lamp will burn brighter and brighter.

However, with these crazy devils that are loose these days, coupled together with dope and all those things, I know you cannot live a godly life for your husband. Husband, I know you cannot live a godly life for your wife. But you can live a godly life and do what is right before the Lord Jesus Christ.

"Show us, Brother Roberson."

Yes, sir. Go over to Galatians 3:26. Let me wind this up. I feel some miracles coming.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

For ye are all the children of God by faith in Christ Jesus.

For as many of you as have been baptized into Christ have put on Christ.

There is neither Jew nor Greek, there is neither bond nor free, there is neither male nor female:

for ye are all one in Christ Jesus.

My God, in God there is neither male nor female, for you are all one and equal in Christ.

Is that not interesting?

It was only in divine order and in the order of what He created us for that He asked us to take the roles we took in the order of the church and in our creation, which is male and female.

When Peter talked about it, he said:

Husbands, dwell with them according to knowledge, giving honour unto the wife, as unto the weaker vessel...

He certainly was not talking about in spirit, because in spirit we are equal with God, neither male nor female. What he was talking about is the outer part of the vessel, the soul and the body.

Being the weaker vessel, the inside of the clay pot holds as much of God and is in every way equal. It is the outside of the pot that was created different.

You were created for one role. We were created for another. We are built different. After you have children, you were created to nourish the children. They did not know about bottles and things like that. Sometimes they would call a nanny in, and the nanny who had milk would feed the infant. Rich people did that.

But even after you had the baby, you were created to nurture the baby, to be there with him. We did not create you that way. God did.

The emotions are different too. We are made more for the front line. We are the first line of defense. Our emotions are created different. Before dope, men's emotions were not so roller-coastery and were a lot more level. The whole world could fall apart, and we would look past its destruction and start rebuilding the future.

Women's emotions are more spontaneous. We are created different even in our soul. Men were created for the front line of defense. Women were created to bear the children and nurture the children. We were left free of that task because our calling was to cover and provide for you, and create a sanctuary in our home that is safe for you and the children.

Yet He is teaching Christ concerning the church, that He is our Provider and God loves us and has provided for every one of our needs. It is highly dishonoring to Christ when the role changes and a man lays down and takes from the supply of his wife.

Household Labor Is Not Diminished, but Provision Must Not Be Abdicated

Now they had farms, and the children and family worked the farms together. Do you think he excluded women from hard work? He did not exclude them from hard work. That did not mean she was a queen who filed her fingernails until noon, belched a couple of times, rolled out of bed in the afternoon, and took a little trip downtown with your credit card.

That is not what he is talking about.

If you have ever run a household, the husband hauls off to church and puts a few columns of figures together while she juggles eighteen lists with two kids crying on her hips. You come home and run off to the TV because you cannot take it for forty-five minutes.

Hallelujah.

I found something. I can stand up and preach this just as boldly.

I know many of you women are forced out to the workplace, and Christ is your covering now. When a man lays down on you and will not be the covering, Christ becomes your covering. I know what you have to do. He was not talking about you. His grace is sufficient. He will cover you and bless you.

But the woman prophesying or praying with her head uncovered, meaning she took the role herself, is different. In the other case, she was forced into it because the man laid down. But here we have a woman asserting herself.

Nature Teaches That Creation Roles Should Not Be Reversed

What do you think he said? Let us exclude all doctrine and customs in any churches in any ages. He says, take a lesson from nature:

Doth not even nature itself teach you, that, if a man have long hair, it is a shame unto him?

The long hair or short hair was symbolic in the early church of who was covering whom.

The man was in the image of God, and the woman was created in the image of the man with slight alterations. So he was saying she is not exactly like the man who was created exactly in the image of God. She is created in the image of the man. The man is created in the image of God.

He said, "Chop his hair off and let the woman wear her hair long." In the Corinthian church, because the temple prostitutes braided their hair and shaved their heads, he said you do not want to appear as a temple prostitute. So all he was saying on long hair is that for men it would be a sign they are in the image of God, and for women that they are in the image of the man and covered by the man, because it is the order he talked about.

But he says:

If any man seem to be contentious, we have no such custom, neither the churches of God.

If there is any contention, if anybody wants to get mad about it, I really do not have a right to tell you to cut your hair. If you came back with contention, he says we have no such custom, neither do the churches.

But throwing all customs aside, let us just take a lesson from nature. It is a shame for a man to have long hair.

That shame was not making reference to his hair, but rather to being covered by his wife. So he said, take a lesson from nature.

I was jogging on that nature trail, minding my own business, and I looked over in a thicket. There stood Bambi. I thought, I am about to take a lesson from nature.

Little Bambi reared up on his hind hooves, took his split hooves in front, and started going like this at me.

I said, "What is wrong?"

He went like this. I noticed my hair was bouncing off my shoulders.

I said, "What? What?"

He said, "Your hair."

I said, "Oh. This must be what that verse means. 1 Corinthians 11. It is a shame. Does not nature itself teach you it is a shame for a man to have long hair?"

No?

Then what was he saying? Take a lesson from nature. Do the animals change the roles they were created for? No. The black widow still eats her husband. Take a lesson from nature. Do they change roles? No.

When one of them starts acting funny, a wrong spirit is getting in and wants to breach the ordinances Paul laid out in divine order for ministry.

Equal in Spirit, Distinct in Role, and Responsible to Keep the Hedge

What did he show you? He says there is neither male nor female. It is just the outside of the clay pot that is different. Neither male nor female. We are all one in the Spirit.

There is no lessening of equality in Christ in the Spirit, and the woman can hold just as much power as the man. Our history is dotted with women who have shaken the world up. As many women as men have shaken the world up. They were unstoppable as they answered the call God laid out for them.

The woman can hold as much of God as the man and is called to do everything the man is called to do in the Spirit: work miracles, raise the dead, preach, teach, prophesy. We are all equal in God.

It is only the role He created us for that He asks us to fulfill, to shore the hedge up and make sure Satan does not get in and destroy everything.

What kind of attitude should I have?

It said that Jesus, though He was in the form of God, was as much God as God. He was coequal with the Father. Yet He considered it not robbery to be found in the form of a man and become a servant. Though He was equal with God, He considered it not robbery to take the form of a man and become a servant.

A woman who is equal in every way in the Spirit with a man should not consider it robbery to be created as who she is and to be covered in divine order by her husband.

Jesus became a servant and humbled Himself unto death. Even though He knew He was as much the second part of the Godhead as the mighty Jehovah, He did not consider Himself robbed because He became a servant.

As the High Priest of the church, in His glorified humanity, He covers the church. In His humanity as the head of the church, He subjected Himself unto God as His head in divine order. He considers it not robbery, even though He is equal with God Himself, to be covered by the mighty Jehovah in His glorified priesthood of the church.

No more should a woman consider it robbery to be created who she is and covered by her husband.

Oh, that freed me. That freed me.

So that spirit comes in: "You are being robbed. What are you, a domestic slave? You are being cheated of your chance to make your mark in the world, you domestic slave, you vacuum-cleaner jockey. Barefoot and pregnant, that is how I keep her, I tell you."

If Jesus did not consider it robbery to take His position as glorified humanity, then hear this:

I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ;

and the head of the woman is the man;

and the head of Christ is God.

The covering of Christ is God. The covering of the church is Christ. The provider for the family is the man. He says, keep these ordinances as I delivered them.

You will not find God lessening your anointing by giving the life you are supposed to give to your children. He will not lessen it. You repair breaches, and He strengthens it. Your life does not become less prosperous. It becomes more prosperous.

Does God have a call for you? He does. Does He have something out there for you to do? He does. When you establish divine order, God will send everything necessary for both of you to lead the most fulfilling lives that could ever be led.

But you cannot let that other spirit get in and change your roles. It will not work.

God cannot bless a man who is being supported by his wife.

Hallelujah. Glory be to God.

Knowing Christ as Provider Frees the Church From Lack

This makes me know how much He wants to cover me, the church. It makes me know how much He wants to be my Provider. It makes me know what kind of fellowship He wants me to enter into with Him.

I do not have any lack. I have a Provider.

I do not have any needs. I have a Provider.

As far as I know, the next time we meet together over our Provider, we are going to establish again how to enter into His presence and fellowship with Him as your Provider, which will free Him to meet every single one of your needs.

For He is your covering. He is the head of the church.

Stand up.
