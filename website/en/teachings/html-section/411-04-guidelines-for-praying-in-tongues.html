411-04-Guidelines for Praying In Tongues Guidelines for Praying in Tongues

The Spirit Man Has Real Faculties That Operate Through the Body

Look at Luke 16, beginning with verse 19:

There was a certain rich man,

which was clothed in purple and fine linen,

and fared sumptuously every day:

And there was a certain beggar named Lazarus,

which was laid at his gate, full of sores,

And desiring to be fed with the crumbs

which fell from the rich man's table:

moreover the dogs came and licked his sores.

And it came to pass,

that the beggar died,

and was carried by the angels into Abraham's bosom:

the rich man also died,

and was buried.

What part of the rich man was buried? His body. What part of Lazarus was carried into Abraham's bosom? His spirit.

Notice verse 23:

And in hell he lift up his eyes,

being in torments,

and seeth Abraham afar off,

and Lazarus in his bosom.

If his body was buried, do not tell me my spirit man has no eyes. In hell he lifted up his eyes. So we know people in hell at least have eyes.

Let us see what else they have. He was in torments, so he has emotions. The spirit man also has emotions. He lifted up his eyes, being in torments, and saw Abraham afar off and Lazarus in his bosom.

Then he cried and said:

Father Abraham,

have mercy on me,

and send Lazarus,

that he may dip the tip of his finger in water,

and cool my tongue;

for I am tormented in this flame.

We know that people in Abraham's bosom, and now in heaven, at least have the tip of their finger. So the spirit man has eyes and fingers. We also know the rich man had a tongue. The spirit man has a tongue, eyes, and fingers.

Is that not good?

Actually, my spirit man basically fits in my body. My hands fit hand for hand, cell for cell. If I stepped out of my body and my spirit man was standing here, and you could see him, and my body was standing here too, I could hold up my physical finger and say, "What is this?"

You would say, "That is your finger."

"What is it made out of?"

"Flesh, blood, bone."

"Can you break it down more?"

"Cells, compounds, atoms, protons, neutrons, electrons."

That is as far as we can break it down.

Now if my spirit man held up his finger, and I said, "What is this made out of?" you would say, "That is your inner man. Your spirit man. The hidden man of the heart."

Yes. It is made out of a substance called spirit. But it fits in my hand, not just hand for hand, but right down to a basic operation in creation.

My spirit man has authority in this world because it operates through this physical body. When I lift my arm, my spirit man lifts his arm and lifts my physical arm. I can see through my eyes, hear through my ears. Everything this spirit man functions through is the physical man, which allows me to operate in this world.

Miracles Show the Spirit's Faculties Can Function Beyond Natural Organs

At a meeting with Ronald Coyne, I held my hand and helped tape his good eye shut because he had had one eye put out. He did not have an eye there. In fact, he had a plastic eye. When you held the plastic eye up, you could not see through it. You could see light through it, but you could not see anything else.

He got ready to demonstrate his miracle because he could see. He popped the plastic eye out and held his socket open so you could see to the back of the socket. I was right there. I helped tape the good physical eye shut. I knew he could not see out of it.

Then he looked at me, got his driver's license out, and started reading the fine print on it. I got right under him, looking up, and there was nothing in there. No eye or anything. I was thinking, "He can see me."

He could see and read fine print, but he did not have an eye. How did he do that?

The bigger miracle to me was when he took that plastic eye, which was not transparent but opaque, popped it back in, and could still see. If you put your hand over it, it blocked the vision out. But he could put that plastic eye in and see through it.

I thought, "That should block his vision."

God did a miracle in reverse. He opened that eye in the Spirit so he could see into the natural world.

I know people who have no eardrums and hear. He did the same thing. What would really get people's attention is if a man whose legs had been cut off came walking in, moving around like you are. You would say, "Okay, I want to get saved. I am going to get saved or go crazy."

There is nothing there.

The Spiritual Mind Is Where the Holy Spirit Communicates

You also have a spiritual brain, a spirit brain. Some of you should show a little more sense in the spirit realm. He is so sensible. Sometimes you are not operating out of Him when you think you are.

You have a spiritual brain that fits into your natural brain, and He functions through that. We can go deeper into the connection between the soul, emotions, and the body's programming, which simulates emotions through chemical reaction. That is all an animal has: emotions based on chemical reactions. They do not have a spirit whose emotions are manifested through those reactions.

We can go as deep into that as you want. It does not make any difference to me, because the Holy Ghost took me and taught me all of that in prayer. He taught me how to operate in the Spirit.

You have a spiritual brain that fits into your physical brain. Science has isolated it as the subconscious mind. They know it has much more ability than the conscious mind, and they have never been able to explore its limitations. It does not seem to have any. Created in God's class, it does not seem to forget.

The spirit mind has much more ability, and it just happens to be the part of you the Holy Spirit communicates with.

"Brother Roberson, it feels like He speaks here, in my heart."

When men first operated in the word of knowledge and I wanted it so badly, I would pester them until three in the morning.

"Tell me, how did you hear God again?"

"He spoke to my spirit."

"Could you please get a little more specific?"

"He spoke to my spirit."

"I know that, because I know you heard Him and what you said was right. But how did He speak to your spirit?"

They really did not know how. They just knew when He did it because they had gotten to know Him.

They would say, "It comes way down deep from my spirit. It comes way up deep from my spirit."

"Would you please isolate the approximate region? Just above the belly button? A little lower?"

It does seem that way. But because your physical brain houses your spiritual brain, your subconscious mind, it seems recessed much deeper than the physical level of operation. It seems to come from the center of your being, from way down here. But it is not here. It is in the mind of your spirit.

"Belly" meant the center of your being, a power source. He talks to your spirit, and it seems deep because it is a deeper level than the physical mind.

This is where the baptism of the Holy Spirit basically took place. Of course, He is wall to wall. He filled you completely. But the mind of the Holy Spirit operates within your spiritual mind. He does not speak to you one place one time and another place another time, excluding the physical operation when God has to step outside everything and talk to you from the outside.

"Hello, you little carnal flesh creature who cannot be led. Sit down for a minute."

I think I heard His voice from the outside one time. I was in a Kathryn Kuhlman meeting. I had only been to two of them in my life. I was sitting on the steps, and somebody called my name three times. I turned around and said, "What? Did you say something?"

"No."

I had no sooner settled down than He called it again. The third time He said, "David." I turned around, and nobody was there talking to me. Then when I turned back around, He changed and started talking to my spirit. He told me things that would happen in my future. They have not happened yet, but I can just about guarantee you where they will begin happening will be here.

Hallelujah.

He does not generally like to talk to your outer ear. When He communicates with your spirit, there is one place He communicates.

Praying in Tongues Trained Him to Recognize the Spirit's Voice

Most of you come under an anointing, so you have no trouble knowing it is Him. You come under an anointing, He begins to talk, and you know it is Him by the fact that you are under an anointing.

One day I prayed an extended amount of time in the Holy Ghost. I slipped into the Spirit and had one of those inner visions. I saw my physical man in front of me, constituted by a dark form. Obviously my soul came online next, because there was some light to my body, but it was not very light.

Then came the spirit man, which fit inside all of it and was very bright. The brain part, the center of the mind that I could see, was very bright in the spirit man. Then there was a very bright area where the Holy Spirit operated.

That is when He taught me the first steps to knowing and how to listen for His voice. As I watched, I saw the Holy Spirit create and operate out of that place somewhere in the spirit man's mind.

He said to me, "Pray in tongues."

So I started praying in tongues. The moment I shut my mouth, those words did not come out of my mouth. They floated up to my mind, to the physical part of my mind, my brain. When I shut my tongue down, they kept coming.

I stopped for a minute and listened, and I could actually hear those words of tongues being created, coming into existence, and rising to my mind. I could hear them.

That is when He spoke to me and said, "Welcome to My voice."

The same place where He creates the words of tongues is also where He capsules a revelation, like downloading a whole revelation into a computer chip: a mystery on healing, a mystery on righteousness, a mystery. When He is done downloading that language, He sends it across to your spirit and releases the whole revelation at once.

Suddenly you understand a whole revelation in a millisecond. A moment ago you did not understand it. Suddenly the whole thing dawns upon you. Sometimes it takes me a month to preach what I received in one millisecond.

When you are praying in tongues, He creates those tongues in that same place. Most of the time you do not hear them because the moment they are created, they seize hold of your tongue and your tongue forms the linguistics for the word created in your spirit.

If it comes out your mouth, it does not come up to your mind. It is diverted through a gate that takes it right out. If you shut your mouth, it comes up to your mind. It will not come up very long, because what activates it is your tongue letting it out that gate. It is like a siphon. It will keep one sentence after another going. But if you shut your mouth, eventually it will stop.

Not only will it stop, but you will have to force yourself to keep trying to pray silently in the Spirit, because it will float up to your mind. If you are forcing yourself to do that with your mind, then your mind cannot do anything else. Those words of tongues will occupy your mind if you do not let the tongues out the gate.

That same place where He speaks tongues in my spirit is where I hear Him. If I stop, it feels like it comes from down here somewhere, but it actually comes forward. I hear the tongues.

I have isolated that place through I do not know how many hours of prayer. I have isolated the ear of the spirit, if you will allow me to say it that way. He can stop with the tongues and start speaking English, or God can stop me and my mind quiets down. When my physical mind opens up to the Spirit, I know He is ready to talk to me.

He will start talking in English, but the English comes into existence in the same place. I know it is Him because I have become familiar with that place from hundreds of hours of tongues coming from the same place. I know where that voice comes from, and I know the difference between that voice and the mental energy of my own thinking.

Silent Tongues Without Utterance Does Not Complete the Circuit

There is a question that arises: Is praying in tongues silently effective? I mean just sitting there praying inside, not moving your tongue, not using anything outward.

Even though there is no ironclad Scripture against it, all the other Scriptures that mention praying in tongues or speaking with tongues connect it with utterance. I have found that when I lock my tongue up, it stops. The tongue seems to finish the sequence.

It does not matter when I am praying quietly. After I just about exhausted my vocal cords, I decided to give that a try. Besides, I got tired of people turning me in at motel rooms and beating on the walls. So I started praying quietly, without using my vocal cords.

But it still finished the circuit. The tongue connected to that language was able to deliver the sequence, and when it did, the next phrase came. If I stopped it, I had to concentrate with mental energy. Faith and the Holy Spirit activate the language in me, but if it is not allowed to finish the sequence because the tongue is connected to it, it generally stops.

I have to exert a lot of mental energy to try to keep it going. Therefore, I cannot even use my mind for anything else because it floats up into my mind and occupies it.

So no, I do not think trying to pray with tongues in your mind and heart without finishing the sequence is effective. That is why He connected utterance with it.

The Holy Spirit Transfers the Prayer Language to the Human Spirit

Look at First Corinthians 14:14:

For if I pray in an unknown tongue,

my spirit prayeth,

but my understanding is unfruitful.

Whose spirit is doing the praying? My spirit. Would you go as far as to say my human spirit? If I pray in an unknown tongue, my human spirit prays. But at that point, my understanding is unfruitful.

If my human spirit is doing the praying, then there has to be a transference of that language from the Holy Spirit to my spirit. That transference is made at the moment of creation.

In other words, praying in tongues is as supernatural as raising the dead. The Holy Spirit literally creates that language on the inside of your spirit. At the point of creating it, He transfers it to your spirit.

If I, Dave Roberson, pray in an unknown tongue, my spirit prays. I am not speaking to men, because this is not a language to be interpreted for the edifying of the body of Christ. It is for my own edification. It is for the mysteries I am praying to be understood by me, and eventually I will understand them when I am finished praying them out.

When I pray in an unknown tongue, my spirit is praying and my understanding is unfruitful. Most of the time, when I go into these hours of prayer, my mind and brain do not understand the vowels and syllables I am speaking. I do not have any idea what I am saying, often for five and six hours of prayer.

Sometimes He begins a translation process, and I understand the mystery while I am praying. It is translated in my mind while I pray. My mind performs one function while my spirit performs another. I do not know word for word, but mysteries begin to unfold one after another when my Teacher kicks in.

I am glad my mind is unfruitful when I spend six hours in prayer, because I found out that I can employ my mind to take in the Word of God while my spirit is praying.

Reading Scripture While Praying Lets Two Parts Receive From God

It is automatic now when I pick up my Bible. I begin to read:

In the beginning was the Word,

and the Word was with God,

and the Word was God.

The same was in the beginning with God.

All things were made by him;

and without him was not any thing made that was made.

In him was life;

and the life was the light of men.

And the light shineth in darkness;

and the darkness comprehended it not.

I will pick it up and start reading. My favorite thing is to take Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians, and Colossians and read those books over and over while I am praying in tongues. Maybe eight times. Ten times. Twenty times. Maybe eight hours.

One of my favorite methods is to walk back and forth, then sit in a chair and pray an hour or two. I will lie on the floor, then get up and walk and pray. My Bible will be somewhere I can go look at it. In one setting I will read Ephesians all the way through. Then I will pray for a while, walk back, and read the whole thing through again.

I am glad two parts of me can take in from God at the same time. The Holy Spirit takes all I have meditated into my mind through accumulated knowledge and uses it for the day-and-night meditation process. This is where much of our revelation knowledge comes from.

My spirit prays. At that point, my understanding is unfruitful. If I am in church, Paul said I need to pray that I interpret.

I asked God, "Why are You transferring this to my spirit? Holy Spirit, I am talking to the other members of the Godhead in a language designed personally for me."

There is nothing closer to your life than this language. It is the mind of God planned for you, all the way through Romans 8. He who searches the heart knows the mind of God and makes intercession for you according to the will of God. Then we can know all things work together for good to them who love God and are called according to His purpose.

Why? Because He is working that plan. Paul goes in for the proof: those He foreknew before the cross, He predestinated to the cross. He shows you God's whole plan from one end to the other and says the Holy Spirit is involved in it. He is the One who knows the plan of God for you.

Jesus said:

All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth.

Go ye therefore.

In that statement was the transference of authority. So the Holy Spirit gave the language to you. He transferred it to your spirit. Now it is you praying. "Therefore you go." You are praying out of your authority to the other two members of the Godhead.

I might say, you are praying somewhat of a perfect prayer. What are you, a glutton for success? Surely one member of the Godhead is creating the perfect prayer and the others are answering it. You are sandwiched between the Godhead: Holy Spirit here, you here, and the other two members here. He creates the prayer, you pray it, and they receive and answer it.

If I pray in an unknown tongue, my human spirit is doing the praying because He transferred that language to my spirit. At the moment He creates it, your tongue shapes it. It is spontaneous. One is not without the other. To shackle your tongue will stop the process.

When I whisper in tongues, it is automatic. I start reading and I start praying. Sometimes it is so automatic that I start reading something in public, like an airplane magazine, and I pray under my breath where nobody can hear me. Sometimes I lock my jaw and teeth, leaving my tongue free in my mouth. It seems to need that much to finish the sequence and keep the siphon flowing.

Paul's Private Tongues Became Public Teaching in the Church

Let us go on to what Paul said, from First Corinthians 14:14 down to 18. Paul earned an illustrious reputation among the other apostles for having more revelation knowledge and more foundation. He had their respect.

Let me give you one of his keys. When he was a Pharisee of the Pharisees, he began memorizing the Old Covenant seven hours a day, beginning at the age of three. That was one ingredient.

After he was filled with the Holy Ghost, it is obvious that he prayed in tongues, probably on the backside of the desert and wherever he was, more than anyone. He made that statement. Since then I have found out that this was a major source of revelation knowledge because of all the mysteries he prayed.

He brought the Old Covenant together with the New. As far as I can see, he was about the only one of the apostles who thoroughly understood the relationship between the law, our covenant, and the law of the Spirit of life. That revelation was almost unheard of. It is commonplace to you now, but imagine having nowhere to get it and then going to get it.

His revelation knowledge was equivalent in detail to Moses receiving the measurements and facets of the tabernacle and the law.

Paul said:

I thank my God,

I speak with tongues more than ye all:

Yet in the church I had rather speak five words with my understanding,

that by my voice I might teach others also,

than ten thousand words in an unknown tongue.

He was teaching divine order in church because they were misusing tongues. In church, in an active service where we are touching the world, where evangelism goes on and the lost, unlearned, learned, and believers are present, he said he would rather speak five words with his understanding, in the language he speaks, that he might teach others also.

Notice the word "also." I would circle or underline that word. He said he wanted to teach others also. The unknown tongue is the one used in your prayer language, because it is not even the one to be interpreted in a public assembly. They called it the unknown.

In these verses you get two things: Paul's praying in tongues and how it benefited the church, and a glimpse at his private prayer life.

In his private prayer life he said, "I thank my God that I pray in tongues more than all of you." Then he gave the use of that tongue in church. "In church I would rather speak five words in the language we communicate in, so others may understand also, than ten thousand words in an unknown tongue."

He was saying, "From praying in the Holy Ghost, I received a revelation. I became edified, so I understand the revelation. But if you are going to understand it also, I cannot stand here and communicate it to you in tongues. I have to translate that mystery to English so you may also understand the revelation I received."

He had to be taught first, and the subject was tongues. Outside the church, his personal prayer habit was, "I pray more." Inside church, it would do no good to stand up and speak those tongues to you. How would I teach you what He taught me? But if I convert that mystery to your understanding, then you also understand.

The operation of tongues in church is for me to give you the mystery I received outside of church. That is our source.

God opened that verse to me in a funny way. I kept looking at it and saying, "There is something in here I am not getting." I kept praying, and He spoke one statement:

"Time how long it would take you to pray ten thousand words in tongues."

So I did. I prayed at an average rate and counted the words. I found out that ten thousand words is roughly an hour and a half.

He was taking the length of a service, about an hour and a half, and saying it would not do me any good to stand up here for the length of the service and speak in tongues. But if I speak in the language we communicate in the mystery I received, then you will also understand the mystery I received.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

Private Prayer Can Stay Quiet Without Becoming Merely Mental

What do you prefer to read when praying in tongues? I almost always like to read the Bible. Occasionally I will read something else, but the more the Holy Ghost teaches me, sometimes the less I trust the material I get into.

I am teachable. My God, I am teachable. It just has to be the Holy Spirit. I will take it from a five-year-old. It just has to be Him. I cannot abide the other anymore. I can, but I do not know why I would. I cannot give myself a good reason.

In verse 27, Paul is still talking about excelling to a place where the church is edified by your operation of tongues and prophecy:

If any man speak in an unknown tongue,

let it be by two,

or at the most by three,

and that by course;

and let one interpret.

But if there be no interpreter,

let him keep silence in the church;

and let him speak to himself,

and to God.

If there is no interpreter, do not speak the tongues out. We are talking about a public assembly with the unlearned and sinners present. This is different from a prayer meeting where you come knowing we are going to pray in tongues and that there will be teaching.

If there is no interpreter, let him keep silent as far as speaking out. Let him speak to himself and to God. That means he speaks in a very low tone, where he himself can hear. Once again, he is completing the circuit. Simultaneously with the creation of that language in your spirit, your tongue forms the language. When you speak it out, it releases it. He forms the next sentence, and the next, and the next.

If you stop your tongue, you have taken half the sequence away. That is why Paul said to let him speak to himself and to God. His spirit has entered into divine communication with God, and he speaks this language to himself under his breath. Nobody else around him is meant to hear it.

Warring Tongues Must Not Confuse Prayer to God With Talking to the Devil

Then came these teachings on warring tongues. You have to do that warring tongue, and all of that is fine if what we are trying to do is add our authority to His prayer. But you will not do that ten hours. You will not have any tonsils left.

People start that way, and eight or nine hours later they look like the rest of you.

You can add your authority to what He is doing if you want to by emphasis, but what are you warring against?

"The devil, really."

I thought it said you were talking to God.

He that prayeth in an unknown tongue

speaketh not unto men,

but unto God.

"Yes, but I am talking to the devil."

No, you are not. He took that prayer language away from your mind because you do not know what to pray. That is why He can take a failure and make a success out of him. Eventually, if you let Him pray long enough, He will get through to you.

To be quite honest, I think that is about the only chance some unusual people have. You know how they operate on another plane. They do not really mean to, but their left foot is always out front. All the other salmon are going upstream, and they are going down.

"Oh, I did not notice."

They are almost always doing something out of the flow of the Holy Spirit.

I am not saying this lightly. I have a deep-seated concern. If I could find one person God was not sufficient for, one person He could not take into Him, then I could not go as far as I want. But I have never found one He could not help if they will do what He says.

Eventually He will get through to you. That is one reason He took it away from your mind. You would be praying for things where God had locked the door, but He took it away because the Holy Spirit knows what you need desperately, much more than you do.

We form our prayers according to our own reasonings and what we think we need. But He was with God from the beginning. He saw Him plan your life. He knows what is better for you. That is why He says when He prays, you can be promised edification. But when you get in there with your own reasonings, sometimes He cannot promise you much, as some of you have found out by how many prayers you have had answered.

Persistent Tongues Can Bring the Strange and Bound Into Balance

I have seen people and wondered, "Is there any hope?"

Then I have seen them come online after a year or two or three of praying. You walk up to them and they start talking about the things of the Spirit in balance. What they used to think was God, whether it was the devil or just strange things, is falling off.

I go, "There is a God. There is a God in heaven. This is greater than the Red Sea."

Anybody can part water. If God could have just gotten Adam saved, He would have dusted him off and slammed him down and said, "Do not ever do that again." But He could not. It took four thousand years. He had to carefully undo it. God is not sovereign in the sense that He just does as He pleases and slaps you around.

It is harder than parting the Red Sea to deal with somebody's nature. That is a hard thing.

I have seen the Holy Ghost change people. I was a habitual liar, and it kind of followed me across the born-again line a little. My conscience had first been seared by the fact that I wanted to be greater in people's eyes than I was. If I could fix that quickly by telling a lie, I would.

But when you start praying in the Holy Ghost enough, pretty soon He starts dealing with that. It hurts you so badly you just want to lie down and die. You cannot get by with it. He follows you around all the time. He surrounds you.

You have one choice: stop praying and keep lying, or stop lying and keep praying. The two will not live together.

You are in for some exciting times. Many of you will jump up and leave over an emotional fit. It may be a stronghold. It will want to take you out before you get past it, so you will spend the rest of your life floundering.

The Prayer Ministries Underlie the Whole Order of Gifts

With one last statement about prayer, in the lineup of the operations of God and the callings, you have apostle, prophet, teacher, workings of miracles, gifts of healings, helps, governments, and diversities of tongues.

Apostle, prophet, and teacher are first. Workings of miracles and gifts of healings encompass evangelists and pastors. Helps and governments are six and seven. The eighth operation is the prayer ministries of the diversities of tongues, beginning with edification from the born-again experience onward into all the different diversities of supernatural tongues.

Did you notice that the gift of teacher took priority over the workings of miracles? These gifts are listed in the order God placed them in the body, in necessary and final order.

The teaching gift is listed before workings of miracles because the teaching gift is supposed to teach by example. In other words, the teaching gift is not really qualified unless it not only has the workings of miracles but is able to teach the understanding of those miracles from having them.

You do not get in a dual-yoke airplane for your solo, have the plane go into a nosedive, yell to the instructor, "Take the yoke," and have him say, "My God, I do not fly this thing. I just teach on it."

You are going down.

I would sure appreciate it, if you are going to teach me about miracles, that you have worked in them.

There is a priority there. Teacher is supposed to encompass the workings of miracles, be empowered by them, plus many more. Then it goes on to the prophet and on to the apostle.

The Prayer Center Multiplies What a Traveling Miracle Ministry Cannot

This is why the devil hates the prayer center. For years I made this journey alone, trying to exhort people to go, and they would not. I would go back to the church, and nobody was left. Out of the two hundred we excited into prayer, maybe three or four remained. I would go back the next year and excite four hundred more, and nobody would be left the next year.

So He commissioned me to stop here. He said, "You have taken other people's sheep as far as you can. You are ready to learn some things. I want you to take some people into this."

I understand what He is doing. I could go by myself. Whenever God sees fit, and we come into something, in the visions I have seen buildings and buses. I have seen the Holy Spirit sweep the whole crowd with miracles and salvation. Then I pack my bag and go home.

That is one thing. But I am not there as the teacher to do it and teach you. The devil knows that if he does not kill us or get rid of the prayer center, we might be multiplied. There might be a thousand of us. If God sees fit, and a type of mantle like we talked about comes, the glory pours out and ten thousand are saved.

Not only that, but with the teaching gift, guess what? I understand the mysteries. There is a chance a thousand of you will be out in different cities across the nation. He is not just trying to kill me. He is trying to kill you.

Workings of miracles are great. I can pack my bag and go. But the teaching gift means I can transfer the same thing into you, and there can be multiplication. That spells trouble for the devil, real trouble.

The devil would like it much better if we just went, worked miracles, packed my bag, got quiet, and went home. He hates it when you jump up and say, "Do this. Do this. Do this." Then the devil has to go to his whole war machine, expend a lot of low-level devils and a whole lot of energy to corral you and bring you back under control.

The more anointed we get, the worse it gets on him. The more we mess him up.

Repeated Scripture Reading Lets Related Revelations Come Together

All right, we will let you go.

Mostly, while I am praying in tongues, I feast my eyes on the Bible. I enjoy myself. I can spend the whole day on a book. Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians, and Colossians are books I like to read over and over again because they all say almost the same thing.

Take one of those and say, "I am going to give my day to this one." Then take another, and another. Pretty soon chapters will come together: a chapter over here and a chapter over there.

Did you know Ephesians 2 says the same thing as Second Corinthians 5? When Paul talked about abolishing the enmity in the flesh and the middle wall of partition being torn down, making of two one new man, he said henceforth there is neither Jew nor Greek.

Second Corinthians 5 says the same thing when it says old things are passed away, all things are become new. If any man be in Christ, he is a new creation. Henceforth know we no man after the flesh. We knew Christ after the flesh, but henceforth know we Him no more.

The old thing that passed away was the old covenant. Henceforth we know no man after the flesh. That means there is no more knowing a man through his flesh, his bloodline, or his heritage. Jew, Greek, Scythian, it does not matter. He has abolished the enmity, the partition, and made of all nations one new man in the new creation of Christ Jesus.

You will not pick those things up until you have thoroughly deposited the book of Ephesians. Then when you are over there, you go, "Where have I heard this before?"

Stand up, and let us worship Him in another song.
