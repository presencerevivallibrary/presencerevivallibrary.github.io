1074-04 Private Worship Vol IV - 4

Knowing His Nature Reveals What He Will and Will Not Do

For the purpose of worship, if you identify yourself by that nature as you fellowship with Me, know you not that you will get to know Me? You have been made a partaker of My nature, and you will get to know Me. When you get to know Me, you will know My character.

Because you know My character, you will know what I will do and what I will not do. If you can receive it and come to the place where you can know Me, then you will also understand how I will move, what I will do, what I will not do, and where many miss the mark by misunderstanding how I operated on the outer limbs of the tree.

Many aborted in the last stretch of their harvest. But those who get to know Me know whether I will do these things or not. They know whether My character has been outlined correctly or not, and they know whether those things said about Me are true. It is you who know Me who will have the final touches to finish your course, saith the Lord.

Hallelujah, if you want to.

What did He say? Through the exceeding great and precious promises, we were born again, and those promises caused us to take the step that made us partakers of His divine nature. Now that I am a partaker of His divine nature, He also said to add to your faith, to that word that caused you to be born again and come into relationship as a partaker.

He told you things to add that would cause you never to be unfruitful or barren, and you would never fall. He added things like virtue, brotherly kindness, and love. Now we are heading right for the core of the nature, the most powerful place there is in your walk of the Spirit.

Most of us, when put at the mercy of our own intellect, are at the mercy of a teacher who is at the mercy of his own intellect. If you can see emergence from the Dark Ages, tell me, where did false teaching come from? Who followed whom to believe things being taught in the body of Christ that one of our kindergarten children could tell you is not true?

Yet the most powerful place a person can walk is out of the core, the very basis of the nature that caused you to be born again.

Private Worship Suspends the Spirit Where the Reservoir of Love Opens

How many know He is not letting us off this key until somebody punches through? In the revelation He has given us on private worship, it is clear that God has charted a course. He taught us how to suspend our spirit in His presence. When we do, it causes God to come, and when He comes, He is able to empty Himself into a reservoir in me that is fathomless and depthless.

It is a pool with no bottom. In your born-again spirit, in that new nature, you have unlimited capacity to receive as much of the love of God as you will let Him pour into you.

What I am hearing more and more now is about the people who have touched God like this and made it to that place. It is certainly a place that will not hurt people. It is certainly the place described in 1 Corinthians 13. Does it seek its own first? Does it take offense at the evil done to it?

These things, He said, if we add them, we would never fall. There certainly has to be something in this that talks about finishing your course. He said we would not be unfruitful or barren.

He said, "Go on. Try to tell them about a place that passes all understanding. Go on and try to tell them about a peace that is not of this world. You will not find the words to do it, but all you can do is point the path to it. Those whom you convince to go in will find out what is waiting for them in that place in the Father's heart concerning this love."

I can go on and on, stumbling over myself trying to describe the place, and He is telling me I will still do it in this service. Some of you will decide to visit it, and you will find out what it is about.

I can understand this: it is a place that virtually nobody has fallen from. It is a place where virtually no one who attained to it was able to be turned back. It is a place where the most powerful part of what the Father is comes into manifestation, and nobody can stop it. It is also the least frequent place attained to in every generation.

The Born-Again Trail Leads to the Place Where All Things Become Possible

Those who have gone there will stand in that place I identified years ago in the born-again trail. When I told you that the journey from reading verses like, "All things are possible to him that believeth," to actually doing them seems longer than most of our lifetimes, He told me, "Son, you will find it on the born-again trail."

He told me I would find it in verses most people leave out, and that has been true. They are the bridge verses from common understandings and failures into a place where this really works.

Now He says, "Those things I told you then, this is part of that walk. Had you not understood who you were when you were born again, you would not have let the Holy Spirit do the work in you that He is commissioned to do."

Some of you are hearing, and you are letting Him do His work. Therefore it is He who will take you into that place of love. Those who have gone in experience that place of which I have talked, where all things are indeed possible to him that believeth.

I tried to get up and talk about something besides His love, and I made it three or four minutes. He said that most people are looking for this even though many of them do not know it.

God so loved the world that He gave. The word for love there is off the board. It is not a love that belongs to your language; it is a love that belongs to Deity. It is the kind God yielded up when He sent Jesus after you.

God so loved that it caused Him to take the initiative across the void of spiritual death and come over here and get me. Jesus became the captain of your ship. He jumped on the first ship and piloted it into spiritual death, told you to get on, and brought us back out.

You cannot understand a love like that because it is immeasurable.

The Godhead Gave Life, Paid the Price, and Came to Complete the Work

When He says death reigned over those who had not sinned after the similitude of Adam's transgression, Adam was a figure of Him who was to come. Adam was a figure of Jesus before Adam fell. The reason you cannot sin after the similitude of Adam's transgression is because you would have had to be alive and then go dead.

Adam was alive and went dead. Jesus was born alive, but because of His love for you and me, He went dead so He could bring us back alive. That kind of love is off the board.

The point is this: if you set your course for the core, the very essence God consists of and operates out of, you are going to pilot that ship in that love. You are going to get so far in that the love He has for you will start flowing through you. When God gets to do everything He paid the price of love to do, you had better back off, because you are going to see some things that are devastating.

He is not going to find that place outside the Spirit. I am born again. I have capacity. Then He gave me a Teacher, not just anyone, but one member of the Godhead.

It took Jehovah to supply the life. As the Father had life within Himself, eternal life forever abiding, and there never was a time when it was not, He gave to the Son to have life within Himself. That life from the Father became the life in human form that the whole second Person of the Godhead poured Himself into and gave.

One Person supplied the life. The second Person of the Godhead gave it. It took all three members to get you back. Why would you think the work the third Person of the Godhead has to complete is diminished from the first two? They provided the life, One paid the price, and the Other came to do the work.

He said, "You honor Christ, you have honored the Father." You let the Holy Spirit bring forward Christ in you, and you have honored the Father. You let the Holy Spirit do His work in you, and you have honored Him.

There is no greater work or honor you can give Him than letting Him do His work in you. There is no greater honor you can give Jesus than conforming to that work. There is no higher honor to the Father than becoming part of His work and His family.

Tongues Builds You Up Until Worship Can Unite the Whole Person

How many times do I have to say this? Two keys.

He made such provision for me that I could pass dumb up and jump on stupid. I could pass stupid up and jump on ignorant. I could pass every stop sign of unproductivity and go into fathomless failure. Who could get me out of that mess?

The Holy Ghost speaks up inside and says, "Okay, I will do your praying for you."

"But I do not know what to pray."

"Okay, I will pray what is best for you."

"I do not know how long to pray."

"I will pray as long as it takes."

"Will I ever come out of it?"

"Can I lie?"

By the time you build yourself up enough to pull yourself together, then you can add your emotions, your voice, your mind, and your will. You can bring your whole man, spirit, soul, and body, and unify it. Then you can say, "Now you are going to worship Him." Then you will start worshiping Him, and the baptisms of love will begin to take place.

There are some things He has been giving me, some areas where He has been ministering to me, and I do not mind telling you there is no small amazement in me. I am understanding more and more why men fall. I thought I had that pretty packaged in Satan's war machine. But the more I get to know my Father, the more I get to know His character through the new nature, and the more I get to know, according to the Word and the discerning of that through the new nature, how He operates, what causes Him to move, and what does not.

Prophetic Exhortation Points to the Direct Route Into Fellowship

Did I not say that prophecy was for exhortation, edification, and comfort? Those things I exhort, that I give you from the Spirit, that I give you from My mind, interpretation, the mind of Christ, listen to the exhortation. The exhortation is based on present truth. It is based on those revelations, those foundational truths that you stand on, those that have made you free.

Did I not say prophecy is for exhortation and comfort? When exhortation comes forward based on the Word, it will instruct you by the Spirit. It will bring together the current times you live in, My mind, and even where you are walking with Me. When I exhort based on the Word, I will not speak beyond you or below you, but I will give you that which edifies you.

So hear what the Spirit would say. When I say through exhortation that there is a direct route you can chart into those things you thirst for, into the fellowship you desire, into the walk that all who desire to walk in My power dream of, but beyond all of that to know Me by My nature in such a way that you will never be barren or fruitless, nor will you fall, this is My promise to all who know Me.

I think He is exhorting us. If many of us do not get into a whole lot more worship, I think He will keep pressing on us like that until we take this next step. He has already told me enough are taking it that He will accomplish what He said He will accomplish, because He is moving ahead with it.

He will keep talking about it until a company of you rise up and say, "This is a good land."

Your worship and rejoicing is over the price He paid and what it cost heaven to provide that for you. Your worship and rejoicing is over a love that loved you enough.

His Love and Grace Reach Needs That Survived Many Prayer Lines

Lately your need has been outweighing what I know and favoring what He has taught me to walk in and what He has given me. God can hardly come into the midst of needs and let them remain neutral. He does not.

There has never been a time that I have known as much Word as I do at this moment. I cannot remember a time when I have gotten to teach less. Nevertheless, when I understood His love for you, I found out that there is no other place but to go with Him. There is no other place.

When His love intensifies itself and His grace comes, what we used to call old miracles and old sicknesses that survived a hundred prayer lines can be reached. What makes you think you would be healed today? I am understanding much more about that now. I know about His grace, the combination of the gifts and His grace.

He can get things that have survived a lot of things because everything He has need of will come together in the service to reach in and do something. The good shepherd will leave the ninety-nine and search for the one that is lost in some kind of wilderness of sickness or failure. He will go get him. Nobody has ever loved you like that.

Would it not stand to reason that a loving Father, if He finds what He needs to reach down, would do this in the little time we have together?

For many of you have been scheduled to receive. You will call it change, because one day you were one way and the next day you were another. But you are scheduled to receive what I have for you in your accountability, in your strength upon My foundation. All these things will come together, and your day will come.

You have taken the low seat and said, "I will be Your servant, nor will this flesh rule over my spirit. I will continue in the things You have told me to do." You decided to take the role of the servant; therefore many of you have gotten past the worst. The enemy has more, but you will have more of Me to stand up against him with.

Did I not say, "I give you power over all the power of the enemy"? Even if he came today, My grace would be sufficient. You walk according to the accountability you receive from Me, and My grace reaches down and takes care of the rest.

In those days that face you, when you are doing all I have called you to do, revival is in the land, and there are so many people standing in the countrysides that they can find no more places to stand, My voice will go out through the land and My anointings shall be on you. From that day, the enemy will cry in dismay. He will not be able to stop you. It will be a great day.

So find yourself faithful now, for this mighty day of revival is at hand.

God Searches for a Place to Love the Bound and Destroyed Through

A vision appeared, and it took me a little by surprise when I prophesied into it. I ran out ahead for a minute. I saw a countryside full of people, and God was there.

I have said before, and I will say again, that not many have been able to receive much of My compassion and My love. Most of it is manifested to them in My works as I save them, heal them, and comfort them. But not many have been able to receive My love in the form of fellowship, to receive it in fellowship with Me so that I might bring them into the deep seatedness of My love that I have for those who are bound, those who are destroyed, and those who become habitations of devils.

Oh, I search for a place that I can love those people through in the manifestations of My compassion. But not many will fellowship with Me there, even though I have opened the inroads to know Me.

You ask My keys. You ask My keys. The more fullness of My love is the abortion of greed. You ask for keys that will let you into My storehouse and cause you to receive and divide My inheritance to those places that have been devastated and those places that have not had the moves and compassion of My miracles and deliverances. You ask to step behind with Me and receive My inheritance.

What would set you more free from greed than My heart beating inside yours?

Have you not embraced the teaching from this camp? Have you not tried to balance yourself in your ever-ending search for truth as you went here and there? You have taken keys in the fullness of those who preach them in their understanding. You worked this key and that one. You operated many times, only to be disappointed, fall short, and wonder why you are not able to work these things according to what My Word says.

Hear again: where most fall short is that they do not take My life and My love and apply it in every room where what is in that room is stronger than they are.

Is there anything stronger than the manifestation of My love when I provide it in every aspect of your freedom? What makes you think not understanding My love would not be a problem to something I provided for you because of My love?

When you begin to receive it and know Me, you will know that I have not withheld any of those things from you.

I keep coming on you like this because many of you will dare to believe. You will take the grace I am offering and leave years behind. You will come into My presence and find out that these things are so.

The Inroad Is Staying in Private Worship Past the Flesh

When He says to keep coming into My presence, this is me now, it is the inroad we have taught you about private worship: staying in until you get past the flesh enough that you enter into a place in Him. In the teaching tapes, we called it the baptism of your senses.

It would be no use going into all of that now. Get the private worship series. There is enough there to get past your flesh. That is the inroad. He keeps bringing us back to it.

If you want to keep me prophesying like this, if you do not want me to go anywhere else, then keep reaching out with that kind of hunger. You will keep me right on this line. You keep hungering to know Him like you do, and you will keep pulling me in there so He can talk to you.

Little did I know, I thought I was going to enter in and just go. Instead, He makes me a servant to you.

How many remember the prophecy where He said something like, "In your journey, you will find the core, the downfall of cities, and all the mighty moves. Why would you not find it in another capacity of the love that I have for you?"

I want to ask you the same thing I asked you last time. How many of you are going to worship God a little more than you used to?

James Warns That Money Tests the Last Stretch of Ministry

Go to James 5. When we begin, it will be with the first verse.

Father, for these next few moments, I felt You lift off me there. I know I am not going to be prophesying. I thank You for the mind of the Holy Spirit, because He operates out of Your mind, Jesus, for the understanding of Your Word that You said will make us free. Teach us. As Your children, we are careful these next few moments to give You the glory.

He said the love of money is the root of all evil for a reason. When the Word of God says something like that, you can stop your search. Whenever everything else fails to abort a person, the devil will use his craftiness against him. Somebody will climb and climb and get to know God, and if everything else fails, then in the last forty percent the devil will major on this one.

When He said the love of money is the root of all evil, it is the root of all evil. He says men who will be rich err from the truth. The "will" there is strong. It means those whose personal ambition is to be rich, and God help you, whether they are believers or not, if you get in their path. They pierce themselves through with many hurts.

I was telling you about God prophesying so much through me in defense of His character. Through worship, when His compassion began to unfold, I knew Him on outer limbs like I had not known Him before. I used to hang around the trunk of the tree. Now I hear people say things about Him, and I know they are not true because the Word of God explodes in every direction and says, "That is not Me."

But when it comes to money, money is a medium of exchange. The whole world operates on the basis that you can convert what you have to something you can store and then convert it back into goods. Money takes many forms, but it ends up the same thing.

From ten years old to one hundred years old, everybody understands that operation in the world. You get five little children at an airport in India, and they walk along with their hands on your suitcase. When you get to your car, all their little hands go out. Everybody understands it.

So the devil will try to use it in the last forty percent of a ministry. If he cannot get a person to sell out to it, then he will abort his entire ministry and bring reproach on it because of it. If the man cannot get his budget met, many times he will resort to unethical practices, and that will begin to bring reproach. Either way, generally money has something to do with the last forty percent of a ministry's takedown.

Healing Is as Final in Redemption as Any Other Provision

At the cross, when Jesus bore my sicknesses and carried my pains, I was just as healed that day as I am now. There are no ifs and buts. He provided a place in the generation that followed Him and every generation after. I happen to be a couple thousand years later. It makes no difference. That place is omnipotent, all-powerful, omniscient, and it does not change. It is waiting for anybody who wants it because the price has been paid.

I am not supposed to pass off this planet hooked up to a morphine drip, screaming from pain or in a coma. That would scare my great-great-grandchildren. It would not add anything to their walk with God. I am not supposed to leave the planet that way.

Many do, and many men and women of God who did wonderful works for God do. They are received into glory. He embraces them, wipes their tears away, and receives them. But it does not change the fact that in this place He provided, healing is as final as when He bore mine. It is final.

I am supposed to gather my great-grandchildren around me when I am old and say, "I am going home in a week or two. I know that. I just want to tell you there is nothing to be afraid of. I hasten toward it. I know now that I am done, and one day your day will come unless He comes. Do not be afraid. I am sound in mind and sound in body. I am going to leave you a legacy that everything my Lord has said is true, and you can trust Him."

We can take men of God in my generation whose bodies shut down on them in their seventies. They died, and the death they died was not nice. The doctors fought for them. Everybody fought for them. They did wonderful works in the land, and many people were added to salvation because of it.

Even though healing is a place absolutely provided, and I wanted to excuse it too, He said to me on these outer limbs, "You face this, boy, and you honor Me. You honor what I have done. You do not let this slide. You face it. I need you."

I knew and preached with a man who affected the world. He died of disease after several attempts to save him. It took a year or two. Everybody remembers him for the great work he did, and they should. Nobody really said anything about how he left the planet. I am not talking in any form of disrespect or ridicule or taking away from his work. But I will state again: the position He provided for healing is just as final as anything else He provided.

Had the same man been in bankruptcy, owing everybody and bringing reproach on the gospel, that is what he would have been known for: his colossal fall. Why? Because every child from ten to one hundred understands the medium of exchange. The whole world can look at the mess and measure it. They will not excuse that fall, but we excuse the other.

I am set just as free from the second as I am from the first. If I do not face all of it, I will never finish what He has for me to do.

James 5 Addresses Believers Who Let Gain Govern Their Decisions

Everybody thinks this prophecy is about God pouring money on believers for no apparent reason. One day you are standing there full of unbelief, and the next day God comes and pours money on you because He has some sovereign time involved with it. They think the last-day sign is when money transfers from the unbeliever to the believer.

That is what they think James is talking about. That is why he starts in James 5:1:

Go to now, ye rich men, weep and howl for your miseries that shall come upon you.

King James says, "Go to now." What he is saying is, "What you are doing and what you did caused you to go to this place. So go to it."

Back up to James 4:13. He is talking to the same rich man:

Go to now, ye that say, To day or to morrow we will go into such a city, and continue there a year, and buy and sell, and get gain: Whereas ye know not what shall be on the morrow. For what is your life? It is even a vapour, that appeareth for a little time, and then vanisheth away. For that ye ought to say, If the Lord will, we shall live, and do this, or that.

He is saying that you should not take a whole year out of your life and base a decision like that on where gain is. A decision like that should be based on prayer, not on trading and gain. It should be based on the leadership of the Spirit.

So he is not talking about a sinner here. He is talking about a believer.

James 5 continues:

Your riches are corrupted, and your garments are motheaten. Your gold and silver is cankered; and the rust of them shall be a witness against you, and shall eat your flesh as it were fire. Ye have heaped treasure together for the last days.

This is where everybody labels this man a sinner and says he has heaped all this treasure together against the last days, and now we are going to get it. Some preachers preach that the last great day sign will be the transfer of immense funds to the believer.

That is true if believers rise to the occasion, learn what their inheritance is, receive it, and administrate it by the power of the Holy Spirit. But that is not all James is saying.

Withholding Resources From Laborers Makes One a Murderer of Righteous Works

James continues:

Behold, the hire of the labourers who have reaped down your fields, which is of you kept back by fraud, crieth...

Is that not interesting? These people are reaping for this man, and he is holding back the hire of their labor. They are out there reaping. Why does this man have fields? Why not a factory or something? The cries of them go up to the Lord of Sabaoth, and He will respond.

Was James just talking about a rich man employing laborers, stealing from them, and laying money up so that you and I could step in later and have it poured out like rain?

The corruption and lust eating his flesh like fire is the consuming power of money. That is what Paul was talking about when he said those who will be rich pierce themselves through with many hurts. It means, "God help you if you get in my way, because I have been consumed by the lust for money. It is corrupting me and eating me from the inside out."

When James said the man heaped treasures together for the last days, he meant against that man's last days on this planet, not the overall last days.

Who were the laborers working in his fields? They were the ones he, as an entrepreneur and a believer, was supposed to finance. James had already told him not to take a year of his life just to get gain, but to pray and be led by the Lord. He says, "You have heaped this money together against your last days, and it is a witness against you. It has eaten and consumed you like fire. You have held back the money from the laborers, the reason I gave it to you, so they could go out into My harvest and reap souls. You held it back, and their cry is coming up to the Lord of the harvest."

I did not understand the last part:

Ye have condemned and killed the just; and he doth not resist you.

When I took it apart and studied every word, it said that by withholding the hire of the laborers who were reaping the harvest, he became a murderer of righteous works.

Many of these things would not open up without the Holy Spirit, praying, assimilation, and getting to know God with His love.

In James, he had to deal with the rich man in the church. He said the spirit in you lusts to envy. These men held a place with things, particularly money. The Holy Spirit in them was yearning for fellowship, a place in their lives currently held by other things, particularly money. The Holy Ghost was fighting for fellowship with them.

By the time James culminates it, he says the money consumed you until you held back what belonged to the laborers and became a murderer of righteous works.

Revival Requires Adding Love to the Foundation

God, for those You are going to use in this decade, they must add to their foundation those things that You add through Your fellowship with them in the unfathomable depths and nature of the part of their spirit that has the capacity to hold the uncorruptible love of God, or our revival will fall far short.

There may never be a time that you will operate in more grace than when you are operating in His compassion, where grace is poured out. That includes your money.

I have come home to turn a river back, and I am going to have my day in court.

A Citywide Healing Crisis Forces Leaders to Acknowledge God

Let me tell you the last time I slipped into this. Since you heard it already, I can give you the short version.

Remember when I slipped into the room over those tornadoes? I slipped into it again last week. It seemed to be a big office with a big desk, and behind it sat a man of authority. That whole atmosphere was building in me, and I could see it.

All of a sudden, a group of women and some men burst into that place. I heard sounds like a newsroom: "The tornadoes are coming. Seven of them have touched down." Somebody else had said something, and I was picking it all up. That is how it got into that place of authority where people rule.

They came crashing in, wanting something and determined to be heard. Evidently, some of their children had heard about it in time to run to the deaf-mute home or the children's wing in the hospital. Some got there in time, and some made it home. So many were healed that it caused chaos.

The doctors did not know what to do with it. They could not categorize what had happened. They could not just say, "It appears to be an act of God," because that is something you could not prove either way. But when a hundred or two hundred who were deaf were suddenly all going home hearing, this thing caused such chaos that they were trying to figure out what to do with it.

The hospitals did not know. The doctors did not know. A company began to be saved until finally, you know how women are, they took this thing to the mayor's office. They said, "We want you to do something with this."

The tornadoes came. The seven tornadoes sat down. Why would this not come?

That is the way Jesus did things. They had to make decisions from the top about what to do with it. I like that.

You cannot get there without love. I have seen five thousand people's teeth filled in India in one wave of glory. I could not count the blind and the sick. In America, I saw six people come to a meeting on crutches, and six leave without them. He is spilling out all the time.

There is a special reward for harvesting burned-over fields where religion has fallen and left a bad taste in people's mouths. I want it. I want to see hypocrites and every excuse they can dream up with their minds absolutely taken away. I want to see all the reproach brought on the gospel removed and those people come to the foot of the cross and say, "Jesus is Lord."

Can you see those women saying, "We want you to give us this meeting. We want you to give us the keys to the city for this meeting"?

If this seems beyond your reach, then reach. There is no other place for me. Everything has brought me here. I am a dead man. There is no other place for me, no other goal, no other mission.

He will not even give me the next step. He will say things like, "More is at stake than you can even imagine. So stand up, lift your hands, and consecrate yourself with Me."

Tell God you will pray for me as often as He drops me on you. Hold me up, please. This is uncommon ground. It is unexplored territory to me.

Father, I worship You and glorify You and praise You. With all my heart, I thank You and magnify You, exalt You, and exult. First world revival and a sweet sound:

Amazing grace, how sweet the sound, That saved someone like me. I once was lost, but now I am found.

And the town will not escape this prayer.
