1074-01 Private Worship Vol IV - 1

You just have to make your mind up: me and my house, we are serving God. I am not moved by much.

Are you moved by anything? Yes, I am: in the right direction.

What did Jesus say?

I am the vine, you are the branches.

He said, "I am the true vine, and My Father is the husbandman." Is it not interesting that He said true vine? Why would He say true vine? That means there are vines that are not true. There are a lot of them. He is the true vine.

Through that new life we have received from Him, being in the vine and us being the branches, we will bear much fruit. Why? Because we are drawing our life from Him.

It is funny how He concluded what kind of fruit He was talking about. He said that you bear much fruit, that you love one another. Heaven seems to think you are really maturing when you come to the place where you have the capacity to forgive your brother all his trespasses.

What was that second commandment that was likened to the first? The first was to love the Lord your God. The second is like it: love your neighbor as yourself. God seems to think you are really an achiever if you can attain to the real love of God.

Jesus said, "My glory I give unto you." What does this glory work by? What causes it? Is it not funny that when Jesus did the greatest miracles, the thing it said He was moved by was compassion?

I would not fight the love walk if I were you. You can go so far carrying God's anointing, and usually a man will be overturned, destruct, or something will happen. What ensures longevity, the finishing of your course, and the lamp of the righteous burning brighter and brighter, is finding out who He is as your Father and what kind of compassion moved Jesus.

Jesus said, "My glory I give unto you." What did we get when we got His glory? We got the standing He had with the Father. It behooves you to understand what moved Him.

What moved Him? Compassion.

What is compassion? A fruit of agape.

What is agape? A fruit of the Spirit.

So when agape is in manifestation, it shows up or manifests itself in compassion. If you get to the place where you are walking there, then you are going to see some of the greatest things you have ever seen in your whole life.

Go to Psalm 91 for a moment as we get ready to worship for a little bit. Psalm 91:1 says:

He that dwelleth in the secret place of the Most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.

Then verse 2 says:

I will say of the Lord, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in Him will I trust.

Is this not interesting? He that dwells in the secret place of the Most High, where is that? He said it was in the shadow of the Almighty.

What shadow was He talking about? For me to abide in the shadow of the Almighty, what shadow was He talking about? He said the law was the shadow of things to come, but the body is of Christ. In other words, the thing making the shadow is the body of Christ.

For David, he who abides in the secret place of the Most High is abiding in the shadow of the Almighty. But what did He say about you?

When Moses said, "I will see God," God picked him up and put him in a cleft of the rock. That rock, as you come to understand, is Jesus. The rock was a type of Jesus. Moses was put under the shadow.

But when you said, "I will see God," in this dispensation, God did not pick you up and put you in the shadow of the type. He picked you up and put you into Christ Himself. You were born again.

When God passed by Moses, He had to cover him with His hand. He could do everything but step in. Not you. When you said, "I will see God," He put you into Christ and you were born again. God did not pass by you. He stopped and stepped in.

This secret place of the Most High abides in the shadow of the Almighty. What shadow? Even in John 17, that great prayer Jesus prayed for you and me, He said, "I in them, they in Me, and us in You." We are in the Father, He is in us, we are in Christ, and He is in us.

I am not abiding in His shadow anymore. I have stepped in. I am in Him, He is in me, and us in them. I am not under His shadow anymore. I have stepped into Him. So have you.

This gives you what they could not have: the nature like His nature. You have been made a partaker of His divine nature.

Old Testament saints could abide in the shadow in this prophecy about Jesus. They could abide in the shadow, but the one thing they had to pull up just short of was God's fellowship with them. It was limited. It is not that He did not love them. He did. But He had to atone for them with animal blood. He had to restrain them with law. Their fellowship was limited.

Not me. Not you. He changed your nature to be like His. The first big step in the adoption process was made when He gave you a nature like His.

Now you have been restored to the degree that you can have the kind of fellowship with Him that He waited for, for four thousand years, until the man Jesus appeared and talked to the Samaritan woman about a Father who is seeking and looking for people who can give Him the fellowship He waited for, for a long, long time.

All creation, He says, was subjected to corruption by reason of Him who subjected the same in hope. He went on to say that creation will be delivered into the glorious liberty of the sons of God, and not only they, but us also who have the firstfruits, this new nature. We groan in that new nature to be delivered from these bodies at the redemption of our bodies and get a glorified body.

Yet in the first stages you have received a nature that has put you back into fellowship with Him, so that you can indeed give Him what the Old Testament saints could not.

This is the capacity it gives you. It gives you a capacity to love Him and other people with a capacity that passes anything the Old Testament saints could have. Not phileo, but agape, which has the potential and capacity for compassion.

Let me show you a benefit of developing His love. Go down to Psalm 91:14:

Because he hath set his love upon Me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known My name.

Now this is me. "Because he," that is me, "has set his love upon Me," that is God. I am the first "he." Because I have set my love upon God, therefore He will deliver me. He will set me on high because I have known His name.

How do I set my love on Him? These are prophecies about Jesus. How do I set my love on the Father?

In our dispensation, this dispensation of the new nature, I have a capacity to love Him in such a way that He craved this and waited for this, something the Old Testament saints could not quite give Him. The Father had to pull up just short of the unregenerated nature and shed more animal blood, year after year after year, until Abraham's bosom, if you will allow me, was full of accounted righteousness, waiting for the Firstborn to be born from the dead.

How many millions of years could He tuck people away in Abraham's bosom? How long could He atone with animal blood and put them away, waiting for the Firstborn to rise from the dead so our predestination to be conformed to His image could be fulfilled?

Had I been born 2,500 years ago, I would have had to wait 500 years for the Firstborn to be born so I could be conformed to the image I was predestinated to, not only in my spirit but also in my body at that appointed day.

He could not just keep filling Abraham's bosom, the Old Testament type of heaven, millennium after millennium. Someday there had to come a day when He finished the work and brought them home.

You happen to live in a dispensation where you are standing on top of the ground when He finished that work in your spirit. Yet that does not exclude the fact that you have something to give Him that they could not: fellowship on a level they could only dream of. You have a capacity to love Him with a spirit that was literally born of Him.

As the Father had life within Himself, He gave to the Son to have life within Himself, and that same life ended up in you when you were quickened together with Him.

Does it change the fact that you belong to His family, that you are His sons and daughters? There is a capacity for fellowship with Him that He has sought for a long time. Old Testament saints could not give it to Him. Now they are giving it to Him. When we were born again, we could offer Him that.

Even though we have a body given to us by the first Adam that resists most of what we do, it does not have the power to win. I like it when His power is quickened in me. My whole body has to bow down, come under subjection, and like it. I do not leave it any choice.

One day, not only will I glorify Him in my body, but I will have a glorified body. Meanwhile, it is such a privilege to begin to fellowship with Him and give Him what He desires long before I step out of this body and the redemption work is finished.

Because I set my love upon Him, He will deliver me in the time of trouble. I do not wait for trouble. I set my love on Him a long time before trouble shows up.

In fact, I am in a flow of private worship, ministering with Him and to Him, setting my love on Him long before anything goes haywire.

For me and my family, we will serve the Lord. Some of them thought they would not, but they are not the winners here. I happen to believe all things are possible because He is in my corner.

"You do not know what they did to me."

It does not matter. That is just the middle. I do not live with anything but the end. Neither do my emotions. Peace will take you to the end and sustain you while the middle takes place.

Would you give me a suggestion? Yes, I would. I would say that you should set your love on Him, and He will deliver you.

Trust me, times of trouble come from the most unexpected sources. But you have learned to trust Him. You have set your face on Him. What do you think? Do you think a person ought to break into private worship or something?

The other day He said something else to me. I have been talking to men to gather testimonies for the book on worship, not just the great victories, but what they faced entering in, the opposition.

One brother told me, "I reached degrees of peace that I did not think existed anymore through fasting." What fasting does is drive the flesh down below the level where you are operating in your spirit. Because the bounds are taken off, things begin to happen that you have been believing for. You have unsealed your flesh by driving it down through fasting and prayer.

Worship affects your spirit directly through the new nature that is in it. As God begins to magnify Himself in you, the reservoir, the capacity you have to pool and hold God's love, begins to increase. When this happens, all permanency in your walk takes place as a result of your spirit. All permanency.

Brother Coker noticed some things changed. After fifty hours of private worship, Jesus stepped into the room and gave him a big old hug. When it was over, it took him thirty minutes to tell his staff because he kept breaking down and crying.

Does that sound familiar? He was weeping, and when it subsided in the next day or so, he noticed there was a stillness and something had changed.

I said, "Well, God told me to tell you something when I called you. Here it is: sometimes we have problems that can only be taken care of as you go from glory to glory in His presence, being conformed to His image."

There are some problems that operate in our lives that, until your spirit is brought at least to the level of the problem, not much happens in them. Things die. Prayer lines do not seem to work. Hardly anything works.

But I said, "He told me to tell you this: you are going to enter into new realms of health, because some of the problems God just solved for you through worship. Some of the roots and strongholds He just destroyed for you."

You can carry real bad hurts and roots for quite some time. Your body is tough. People abuse it, drug it, mistreat it, and overtax it day after day, and it can still last thirty or forty years.

Unforgiveness, hatreds, malice, and different things can be in a person, way down in them where they hardly ever let them surface. That is why smoking can be so addictive. It is not the physical addiction you have the biggest problem with; that is gone in four or five days. It is because nicotine acts like a drug that sends those kinds of problems back where they came from so you can function.

When my son David would blow up at the worst, tell everybody off, rant, and rave, he would have to smoke almost a whole pack to calm himself down. It is a sedative. It is hard to break, but it is not hard when you start destroying the roots that have to stay sedated.

Your body is tougher than brass. It can take a lot. If you have that cancer of unforgiveness, hatred, malice, and those things in you, they usually start showing up sometime after fifty, with all kinds of health problems. Now, that is not the only reason for sickness. If it were, our children would not be sick. Some sickness is a blatant attack of the devil. There are many, many reasons. I am just naming one of them.

Usually these things show up nearer the end of your life from carrying them and carrying them and carrying them.

There are some things in my body that came online after the worship limb. I did not know what to think of it. I thought, "Well, my faith has kicked in or something." It was not just my faith. It was my fellowship with Him. He reached a place in me receiving His love and compassion. Just the peace and rest of that has an incredible effect on your body.

I said, "Brother Coker, you are going to come to a new state of health."

He said, "You know, I am fifty-something now, and there are just things starting to go wrong. Even my relationship with my wife was messed up. But after He came on me and made an adjustment inside, I noticed I was in intimidation. That was even showing up in my marriage and things. But there is a stillness and a peace, and it is gone now."

He who sets his love, you make the decision to set it on Him, will be delivered in a time of trouble.

It is worth praying in tongues for two or three hours and spending that last one just to get that old soul used to telling Him how much you love Him in a language you can articulate: "I love You." You will not do that very long before you notice He will start coming, staying longer, and filling your room.

I cannot say you are not going to face trouble. Brother Coker said, "I was plotting and scheming against people, how to take revenge, and I would not even like that at all." That was after forty hours of worship. It is not all a picnic.

But one thing is sure: when the devil cannot, no matter what he pulls on you, nor your emotions, nor your strongholds, get you to abort suspending your spirit in fellowship with the Father and adoration, if he cannot get you to stop, he is going to get the other stuff to stop.

Maybe you ought to look down and read that verse again. Psalm 91:14. They dwelled in the secret place of the Most High; they were in His shadow. You are fastening yourself on Him. Not only on Him, but in Him. You have been born again.

Remember the teaching of the prodigal son? It is not really about the younger son; it is actually about the older. Five minutes after the younger son restored relationship, he restored fellowship.

What is relationship? Two relatives in the same ship, not because they necessarily want to be, but because they are related. Two fellows in the same boat, put in there by the same DNA.

I have been born again. I am His son. I am in relationship. We are in the same boat now. But fellowship is when you throw yourself into the same boat because you want to be in the same ship.

The younger son ran out and broke relationship, came back, restored relationship, and five minutes after he restored relationship, he restored fellowship.

The elder brother always had relationship and never broke relationship, but never had fellowship. He was listening to the celebration. The servant came and said, "Your father wants you to come into the celebration."

"No. No."

So the father went out after him.

"You killed a fatted calf for him, and he has been spending money on women and harlots. You never killed a fatted calf for me. I have never transgressed one of your commandments."

He said, "Son, all I have, all I have ever had, has been yours. It has been yours the whole time."

This is what he was saying: "Your problem is that you have always had relationship, but you never had fellowship, son. You did not even know Me well enough to know that all I have ever had is yours every single day, because you based your relationship with Me on your works. Your younger brother based it on the grace that made him My son."

When you base it on the grace that made you His son, you are going to crawl up in His lap, set your love upon Him, and begin to give Him your adoration.

Why? Because you can get something? No. It has all been given to you. It has all been given to you, and you have fallen in love with Him.

When I first started to worship in this private worship limb, I was back there locked up. About a couple of days went into two weeks, and finally He said, "What are you going to do about this?"

I said, "Well, you know you cannot go and worship Him like this so He will give you things." I felt like I was in there worshiping because He would give me things. "Oh God, am I going to get this and get this and get this?"

So I was thinking I was in there worshiping because I knew I was going to get things. Finally my head was going, "What do you do?"

I said, "Shut up. Okay, I know I am going to get things because I am worshiping You, but that is not the reason I am in here. I do not care what I feel like. I am not in here for that reason. So shut up, devil."

That ended it.

You do not know what I went through. I will write a book on it. Another one. I said, "No, I am in here because I love You." The devil, my flesh, and everything else were saying, "You are going to get this, you are going to get that, this is going to come." I said, "Just shut up. The only reason I am in here is because I love Him."

I do not have to be drunk on wine wherein is excess. Do you think I need alcohol to cope? Do you think I need to sedate my senses to make it through a horrible day that has seized upon my poor little person?

I used to be a chain smoker; I just had trouble lighting them.

I do not need alcohol. What did He say? Here is your alternative: be drunk in the Spirit.

What part of an alcoholic does alcohol affect? His senses, definitely. If an alcoholic is really an alcoholic, you do not know him unless he is drunk. If he gets sober, you say, "Who are you? Where did you come from?"

I do not need it. Why? Because I can be drunk in the Spirit, ministering to myself. Ministering to who? I am setting my love on Him. Ministering to who? Myself, in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs. I am making melody in my heart to the Lord, giving thanks for all things to God and the Father in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.

What is going to happen when I keep worshiping Him? What happens to a drunk man? He gets drunk enough, and pretty soon he thinks, "Everything is going to be okay. I am going to make it." And he does.

I have talked to people who had to sedate themselves because of terrible memories. But when you are worshiping God and suspending your spirit in fellowship with Him, it definitely has an effect on your senses. A submergence of your senses by His presence will begin to take place.

How does it affect me? It affects you just the opposite of natural sedation. What looked impossible to you in the natural is now going to be moved on your senses by His Spirit. As the days go by, you will begin thinking, "Why did I think this was so impossible?"

Because before, you had not set your love upon Him, and it was hard for Him to deliver you in a time of trouble.

I was talking to a person whose foundation of life has changed because of the revelation gift. I know a lot of people have tackled worship without the revelation gift. Worship is not a revelation gift. Praying in tongues is a revelation gift.

That is why I have seen people who could cause a presence to come and see a presence, but still believe things about God that were not entirely true. God will almost use anything. He loves people so much that He will use all reserves at hand to do things for them. If He cannot do them, rest assured, it is not because He did not want to.

One day His anointing was riding on a donkey. They had another name for it, but it is not exactly accepted in our culture. Everybody was waving palm branches. The donkey turned around and said, "My, aren't I something? The anointing is really riding on me. Look at them, they are waving those branches at me. I think I will start a church."

You have to remember, when it comes to the supernatural, you really are just the donkey He rode. Without Him, you can do nothing.

Praying in tongues is a revelation gift. My friend was setting his foundation. Lately he has been getting excited over the worship limb. He wants to set his love on God. He said, "How do I do that and maintain? I have really sponged into this, hungry to understand the mysteries of God. I cannot stop praying them out hour after hour in tongues."

So I taught him how to divide it up. When you tackle worship, it can be seasonal. Divide it up. The time you allotted yourself for prayer, divide it up. If it is four hours, do three in tongues and use that last one to learn how to worship Him. Or divide it up half and half for a month, and then you can go back to your four hours of tongues. There is a way to divide these things up because each of them does something incredible in your life.

When you implement the different limbs, they were designed to transform you to His image. They are the fastest path there.

When God slew me under the power and said, "You do not know anything about worship," I thought I did. I thought I was rather expert. It is kind of shocking to have God lay you on the floor and say, "You do not know anything about worship."

It ended up in the instruction to order me in so many hours a day. Now I understand God's logic, because when I hit the fight and the dry spells, I would not quit because I had a word from God. It got so dry that when I breathed, powder puffed. The only reason I survived it is because He ordered me in.

After the first four or five days of dry spells, I said, "I have a sneaky suspicion that this is destroying things of the flesh. It is destroying things of resistance. I just have to stay in. I just have to stay in and do it."

Worship seems to reach very, very deep into your mortification. I understand why God put it on as one of the last limbs for me. I would not have understood it in the light I understand it now had I not had the groundwork leading up to the revelation of private worship. You cannot put a ceiling on until you have the walls up.

I might have written a book like everybody else's: "You are not really in the Spirit, for He seeketh such to worship Him in spirit. If you worshiped Him all day and were dry, you did not break through. You just did not really get in the Spirit." Everybody would have aborted.

But I stayed in and stayed in because I was ordered in until I understood all that. Then I could bring it back to you.

When He taught me how to enter in, He taught me by going in, getting my mind, and bringing it back. If you have one hour to worship, you need to get your mind and keep bringing it back, telling it, "You are not going to wander around while I am in protocol in the presence of my King. You are not going to wander around and not do Him service while I am speaking these words: I love You, I love You."

If you have more time than that, you can keep bringing your mind back. I broke through quicker that way. But even if your mind wanders and you keep saying how much you love Him, even though your mind is solving a problem, do not use your mind wandering as a reason to stop. If you really cannot pull it back, let it wander. You keep saying how much you love Him, and pretty soon you are going to start hearing what you say, and that will begin to pull you in.

I am just telling you what He taught me. I went and got my mind, and even at that, I would look over and notice I had spent thirty minutes wandering around in my mind. But I never stopped telling Him how much I loved Him. Between me bringing it back and wearing it down, it was not long before He began to come, and come longer and longer.

When He was there the strongest, my mind gave me no trouble. It was in the river. It wanted more. In fact, if He started to leave, I would say, "Come back."

You will find that you can be in the midst of chaos, somebody dying, in the hospital room, and their distraction is not enough. It is not a special move of God or a special grace. It is your fellowship with Him. Their distraction is not enough. You can lift your hands, He will come, and He is ready to do anything because you gave Him an avenue to come in on. You are not part of the chaos; you are part of the answer.

Why am I telling you this? I want you to worship Him privately, and publicly but privately.

Do I have to yell? Do I have to whisper? Do I have to talk out loud? Sometimes I whisper and worship to save my vocal cords. I say, "I love Him." I found out, just like praying in tongues under my breath, somehow He heard me.

If I suspect the devil is around, I will talk loud enough for him to hear for sure. "Can you hear me, old crafty one? I said, I love Him. This laugh is for you."

Is it loud? Is it quiet? No. It is intent. You are pouring yourself out in adoration. It is intention. If I am whispering or shouting, it does not matter to me. It is what I am pouring out in His presence.

Are you comfortable? Why do you not stand for a minute, and then sit down any time after I launch you into this. Is anybody having a good time? How many are going to worship God a little more than you used to?

I am not surprised. I dropped the great theological expositions and great exegetical masterpieces to convince Him I loved Him. You may start with elaborate language, but you end up with:

I love You and worship You and magnify You in glory. Hallelujah, hallelujah.

If you hit a vein where He jumps, where you feel Him jump in you and feel His presence come, stay in that vein.

In all this worship, I get some kind of blessing out of thinking it. But people, you have to say it. You have to say it. You cannot just think it. I worship You and glorify You. You have to say it. You can say it quietly, but you have to say it. Your whole man has to form the words and say it.

When He first told me, "I will show you, if you will go home and worship Me all night, what causes Me to come," I settled it in my spirit. I did not know much about worship, but I thought to myself, "I am going to use one phrase: I love You. I love You, Father."

Once in a while: "I love You. I love You. I love You, Father." I was going to empty myself into that one phrase all night long. Vain repetition is what the heathen do. What you do is minister to yourself.

I made my mind up that I was going to use a simple phrase: "I love You." Whatever it took to pull all of myself into that statement, that was the fight I was going to fight.

I love You. I love You. I love You, Father.

Then I would collect myself. You will be staggered with the results. Sometimes He will not particularly come that night. But somebody will say, "Will you pray for me?" and you will step up to pray, and He will shock you with His presence.

Just because payday does not come when you are locked up, believe me, you are going to have a payday.

Do not mind the person next to you. They will not be holding your hand in the day of reward. Neither will your wife or your husband. Rewards are not handed out to family units. Everyone has his own.

Having everything perfect is a plus. It is not a necessity.

The heathen found out that vain repetition would work. It would get them absolutely possessed. It is called chanting: absolute possession. As they give their spirit over to the dark side, chanting and receiving it, submitting their emotions and their senses hour after hour, that is vain repetition.

It was not to discourage you from making statements of adoration over and over. If there is a vain repetition, then you can know also there is a ministering to your own soul and a subjection of your senses and everything about you to the love and adoration of God. There is a truth.

I want you to notice the difference now. You do not have to stop in your worship with Him. If you want to worship in this song, start thanking Him. Give yourself to any kind of worship right now. Watch what happens to the corporate anointing.

For some things belong to Me, and those things in which I say to love the Lord your God with all your heart, your mind, your soul, and your strength. To love Me, to love Me, saith the Lord, is the first and the great commandment.

To be about your Father's business is to deal with anything in you that is opposed. Come, come, come. Learn of Me and take My yoke. My burden is easy. My yoke is easy. It is light. Learn of Me.

Deal with those things, and you will find out that My peace is strong. It is not as this world gives. This peace will sustain you against any attack of the enemy. You can stand in the shoes that are shod with this gospel of peace, stand face to face with your enemy, and know that you have won, saith the Lord.
