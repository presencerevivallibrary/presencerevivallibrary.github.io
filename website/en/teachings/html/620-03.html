620-03 The Glorified Body - 3

Open to Matthew 27.

Is it not something that all of a sudden the resurrection started coming online? I remember riding down the hill from Gatlinburg to Knoxville to catch a flight. Bobby Cornelison, who is with Jesus now, was driving me. We were exploring the resurrection then. Bobby loved to sharpen iron with iron. He loved revelation knowledge, so he liked to take me to the airport from time to time and talk.

The resurrection came up, along with the usual difficult questions. We talked about everything from soul sleep to your spirit, because neither one of us could understand exactly what Jesus was coming back after and why He wanted our body.

There are many variables. It is amazing to me now, because I know I could not have understood the resurrection without The Born Again Trail. It started with a thorough study of the life we received in our spirit, Paul's teaching from the law and Gentile perspectives, and a whole lot of what Jesus said in John. It answered a thousand and one questions.

The day He showed me The Born Again Trail, He showed me that the journey between "all things are possible to him that believeth" and actually doing it was in the verses most people leave out. They leave them out because they do not understand them. They do not understand them because they never received the revelation through the Holy Ghost, praying in tongues, and the activation of the revelation gift.

If a person does not have the Holy Spirit moving on his understanding, his nature, and the feeding of his mind, he is in trouble. Anyone at the mercy of his own intellect is in trouble. You evaluate a verse, and you may be right or you may not. If you are not, usually you do not know it.

That is where whole denominations, wrong teachings, lack of power, neutralization, and false doctrine come from.

The last thing He was trying to show me concerned the resurrection: how I never could see it before, yet how simple it was. It made me understand my carnal mind as never before, the blocks it can put up and how it will hardly let you pass because your thinking is already running down one road.

Mr. Old Coat of Impersonation came and tried to put itself on me. I have been hearing many statements from the realm of righteousness that I have recently become aware of. I love them. They crystallize everything. They give me faith.

One came in Hawaii, when a woman from a church that did not like me was healed of blindness. The devil tried to say, "God healed her to vindicate you." Later I thought about it and realized I did not need vindication. They did not dislike me for anything I had done. They just disliked me because it was me.

The voice said, "See, God healed her to exalt and vindicate you in their eyes." It sounded holy until God spoke up and I found out whose voice that was.

God said, "If you need a reason outside the cross for her to be healed, the devil will give you ten thousand reasons why nobody will be."

That was it. I escaped that one.

These are the kinds of things that take a person off and neutralize God. You sit there thinking, "This is why He did not heal them. This is why." The devil sits there, pretending to be God, and says, "This is the reason why," and it is not the cross.

"He healed them to exalt you."

What if you did not need to be exalted? Did that mean she had to stay blind?

Wake up. There is only one Redeemer who suffered for you. Nobody else is called to suffer for you redemptively. Only one Redeemer is free of sin. He bore your sins. Nobody else is qualified to suffer for you.

If you glorify disease as a teaching tool, you are not qualified to suffer for yourself.

I have told you I was in a fight with impersonation. You can understand how fine-lined the fight has become. I have seen him destroy a few more friends of mine, walking down the street talking to themselves.

More than ever before, I find no reason to be afraid of anything. I would have a right to be afraid if I had stayed where I was, in the land of Do-little. The land of Do-little, especially when you are accountable, is where discarded rewards live.

He comforted me when I was in Tacoma. Somebody had been listening to someone say, "You had better fulfill your mantle, or God will give it to somebody else."

They rolled their eyes because they know God has warehouses full of mantles nobody has even taken yet, much less some tiny discarded mantle running around that you need. You have your own.

If every discarded mantle were picked up and given to someone else because the first person failed, there would have been no dark ages. But there were dark ages.

He started comforting me before the attribute of righteousness crystallized in me: impenetrable, impregnable, forever sovereign, with not even a pinpoint of darkness penetrating His righteousness. Yet He found a way to approach me when I had been put outside of it.

He said, "Son, concerning the dark ages, who do you think lost it?"

The apostles handed it off to the people they trained. They did pretty well. Their generation came up, and they tried to hand it off. After one hundred years, then two hundred, it was getting bad.

He said to me, "My generals lost it."

The foundation the church was standing on was lost. I am not talking about the canon of Scripture being recorded. I am talking about the inability to hand it off and men hang on to it until the foundation was lost.

Unless you think locking yourself up in monasteries and taking vows of silence, when the Word of God says "go ye and preach," is a normal operation of God. Did He say, "Go ye and preach this gospel to every creature," or "Go lock yourself up and take vows not to talk anymore"?

Would I want people lining up to kiss my hand and call me Your Eminence? Do I want a foot-tall hat, long robes, gold lining, and a five-pound gold cross? Do I want to be known as the presence of Christ on the earth and take money for confession?

Something happened. The generals lost it.

So why do you think it is so strange when you find the Holy Spirit as Teacher and He gives you foundational truths?

One difficult thing about the resurrection is this: what if you were blown away by an atomic bomb? What if you were thirty feet from where the bomb went off? I do not think there would be a strand of natural DNA left. The heat is hotter than the center of the sun.

I read an article once that said if you had a pinhead-sized piece of the sun's central heat here, it would kill a man five miles away. Whether that is true or not, there would not be anything left. Every strand and molecule would have separated.

When Jesus comes back, we are changed in a moment, in the twinkling of an eye, and the dead in Christ rise. What is He coming back for?

If I am standing here in my body, that is not difficult. But what about people whose bodies dissolved, deteriorated, or were destroyed? What if there is no body, no strand of DNA anywhere to come back after?

Is it important that it be the same body or something from it? You have to face the fact that some people are gone. There is nothing natural to come back after. Yet they were believers.

So how is He going to accomplish the resurrection?

Thank God that once you start receiving from the Holy Spirit, you recognize the source and the ear of your spirit. The same place He speaks to you from is where revelation knowledge comes from.

Concerning the poison darts of the enemy, once you are hearing clearly from that area, that is the place John was talking about when he said the anointing is truth and cannot lie. If somebody says something wrong, He does not let it pass if He is your Teacher. You may not understand why it is wrong, but you know it is wrong.

What qualifies you for Him to be your Teacher? Get in touch with Him and in fellowship with Him. The quickest and best way to articulate revelation knowledge from your spirit is by praying in the Holy Ghost.

Praying in tongues is like any other form of prayer designed by God to be answered. If you pray the prayer of faith, how can you tell God answered? First by faith, then the mountain moves. When you pray in tongues, you are edified and God is the One you are praying to. If you are being edified, you are benefiting somehow. That means praying in tongues is being answered in the form of edification.

Matthew 27:51-53 says:

And, behold, the veil of the temple was rent in twain from the top to the bottom; and the earth did quake, and the rocks rent; And the graves were opened; and many bodies of the saints which slept arose, And came out of the graves after his resurrection, and went into the holy city, and appeared unto many.

Notice it says bodies. What if you did not have one? What if a shark ate you?

This was associated with the rocks breaking. Many were entombed, especially the old prophets. The veil of the temple was torn from top to bottom, the earth quaked, the rocks split, and many bodies of the saints who slept arose and came out of the graves after His resurrection.

Here we have a resurrection where the graves literally opened and the rocks split. Many of the old saints who slept came out of the graves and went into Jerusalem.

Who were these people? They had to be Abraham's bosom people. That presents another problem. If you do not get your glorified body until He comes back, were these people resurrected with natural bodies? If they were resurrected with natural bodies, did He put them back in the grave and take their spirits home?

Who are these people? Did they come out in glorified bodies or not?

Isaiah 26:19 says:

Thy dead men shall live, together with my dead body shall they arise. Awake and sing, ye that dwell in dust...

That is interesting. Jesus said, "Together with My dead body shall they arise." There was a resurrection on that day. Matthew caught it and recorded it. Thank God.

To look at this resurrection more closely, go to Romans 8. Finally, an old friend of mine has cleared up after many years. That is one reason I never preached on it.

Romans 8:16 says:

The Spirit itself beareth witness with our spirit, that we are the children of God.

This is the first place in Romans 8 where the Holy Spirit is mentioned. His Spirit bears witness with our spirit that we are the children of God, because we have been born again and received His nature.

Then Paul says:

And if children, then heirs; heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ...

If I am a child because the inner witness has made me a child, then I am an heir of God. If I am an heir of God, I am a joint heir with Christ.

As a faith and prosperity person, I used to teach, "A joint heir with Christ? The cattle on a thousand hills belong to Him, therefore they belong to me." That was as far as our joint heirship went.

But Paul says:

If so be that we suffer with him, that we may be also glorified together.

He is talking about the glorified body. When Jesus rose from the dead, He laid one resurrected body onto your account. You are a joint heir with Him, in line to inherit that.

What glorification is He talking about? Not your spirit man. Your spirit has already gone from death to life. He is talking about the end of this lifetime, when, as a joint heir with Him, He comes back and clothes you with a glorified body at the resurrection.

If you are alive when He comes, one moment you will be standing there, and in the twinkling of an eye you will look good. If you waited until then to get healed, you will be healed at that moment.

Romans 8:18 says:

For I reckon that the sufferings of this present time are not worthy to be compared with the glory which shall be revealed in us.

He is talking about your lifetime on this earth. When your joint heirship gives you one resurrected body, a lifetime of telling your flesh to come under subjection to your spirit will be worth it. Everything your flesh did to you, everything it tried to cause, everything connected to the continent you were born on and everything that could happen to you will not compare to that moment.

Just the moment of glorification, even without another ounce of reward, would have been enough.

Romans 8:19 says:

For the earnest expectation of the creature waiteth for the manifestation of the sons of God.

Creation was made subject to vanity, or corruption, through the fall of Adam. The creation itself shall be delivered from the bondage of this corruption into the glorious liberty of the children of God. The whole creation groans and travails in pain together until now.

Creation is pregnant with a new heaven and a new earth, and there will be no beer cans in your rivers. We will get a new heaven and a new earth compatible with the new creation we have become.

Romans 8:23 says:

And not only they, but ourselves also, which have the firstfruits of the Spirit...

We have the firstfruits of the Spirit: born again and filled with the Holy Spirit. What is the second fruit? The glorified body.

Even we ourselves groan within ourselves, waiting for the adoption to be made complete, which is the redemption of our body. That is what verse 19 means by the manifestation of the sons of God. Creation will be delivered, and you and I will get a glorified body.

Romans 8:24 says:

For we are saved by hope: but hope that is seen is not hope...

Paul is not saying we are saved from sin by hope, as if you are waiting for Jesus to come to find out whether you are saved. If that is the case, you are not very saved.

We are saved in hope of the glorification, in hope of receiving a glorified body when the redemption process is made complete at the redemption of our body.

Verse 26 says the Spirit helps our infirmities. Infirmities here means our inability to produce results because of the limitations imposed on us by this flesh. We are saved in hope of a glorified body, but meanwhile someone has come from heaven to help all the weaknesses of the flesh.

The Holy Spirit says, "Excuse Me," and steps in with groanings that cannot be uttered. Your own mind cannot articulate it.

Are you glad that Someone called the earnest of your inheritance has come to help you?

The Holy Spirit is the earnest of your inheritance. God is sincerely set on redeeming you and receiving you: spirit, soul, and glorified body, with a great reward for completing what you did on this earth.

How will He ensure that He can deliver all that up to you? He made a down payment on you against the final possession of the purchased article. The Holy Spirit is His earnest to you that you are going to make it home one day.

Romans 8:27 says:

And he that searcheth the hearts knoweth what is the mind of the Spirit...

The Holy Spirit searches your heart, individually. It does not matter how many billions strong the body is. The Holy Spirit has come to represent you and no other. When He brings you before the throne, you are the only one on His mind. He has God's full attention.

He searches the heart and knows the mind, or plan, of God. That enables Him to make intercession for you according to God's will for your life.

Then verse 28 says:

And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose.

All of a sudden, Paul switches from our dispensation and shows you that God has been working a whole plan since the beginning.

Verse 29 says:

For whom he did foreknow, he also did predestinate to be conformed to the image of his Son...

This is speaking of the plan before the cross. Romans 11:2 says:

God hath not cast away his people which he foreknew.

The people before the cross had to be predestinated because there was no firstborn to be conformed to yet. All Old Testament saints had to wait. They were predestinated to one event: when this Man would rise from the dead and they could rise with Him.

Then Romans 8:30 says:

Moreover whom he did predestinate, them he also called...

Whom He predestinated to be conformed to His image at the cross, He also called to the cross. Whom He called, He justified at the cross. Those Old Testament saints He justified through predestination at the cross, He also glorified.

What happened in Matthew 27?

Undoubtedly, those saints resurrected the same way Jesus did, after His resurrection. One of two things happened. I will not state it as absolute fact because I am still working on this part.

They arose in a natural body like He did. Jesus told Mary, "Do not touch Me yet. I have not yet ascended." If He was not glorified yet, how could they be?

So He resurrected many of the bodies of the saints who slept, and they went into Jerusalem and visited people. He Himself visited five hundred men at once, the apostles, and others, and He walked on the planet for days.

When that resurrection visitation was over and He was glorified, you can rest assured they were caught up and glorified as they went home to be with Him forever.

That also explains Thessalonians like a piece of cake. But we are out of time.

Hallelujah.
