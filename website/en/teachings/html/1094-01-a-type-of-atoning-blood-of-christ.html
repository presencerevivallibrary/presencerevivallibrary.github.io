1094-01 A Type of Atoning Blood of Christ

Occasionally people say, "You need to do more than pray." But where people fall off the wagon is that they have not had enough prayer to do anything else. The real "anything else" never comes without prayer. When you pray enough, it always results in doing the other things.

Do not worry about praying too much and not doing other things. Victory in one produces victory in the other.

When we are talking about pure life, we do not want to get one degree off. A gun can be one degree off an inch from the target and you may not notice. But a mile from the target, that one degree becomes a great distance.

Many men of God were defeated not because they never walked in power, but because once God gave them glory and power, they were accountable for it. You never receive that level before you are able to stand under it. If you merchandise it, sell it, or enter into flesh in handling it, you are responsible for the level of anointing you walk in.

The devil often uses the flesh like a noose, pulling a man in little by little until credibility is lost, the ministry is discredited, or the person dies by Satan's hand because Satan was allowed into his territory.

We do not want to get one degree off.

Last Sunday I kept moving in and out of intercession. It continued through the main service and into the next night. I saw something large come up that needed to be dealt with, and God said, "I need people this time. I will give you people."

That night many people fell into intercession and weeping. Some had never felt anything like it. By Thursday and Friday, that wave came back and left people prostrate into the afternoon and night.

When God can pour that much fullness through a person's spirit, it is not like seeing someone else's child run over in a crosswalk. That is horrible, and you feel remorse. But when it is your own child, your anguish can be heard in heaven.

The world is lost. When Jesus becomes your Intercessor and pours His compassion through you for the lost, that generates tremendous power. It moves things out of the way and moves things into place.

But that kind of intercession cannot be manufactured whenever we want. Among the diversities of tongues, only one operation is available at our will: praying in tongues for personal edification. Deep intercessional groanings operate as He wills because they are based on the compassion of Jesus.

For Him to pour His compassion through you like that has something to do with where you are walking.

Some people once wrote me letters correcting our intercession. I knew their lives and the unresolved flesh issues involved, so I was not going to take counsel from them.

Intercession comes from the heart. Jesus looks for people whose spirits are full of compassion. It comes from a place where your spirit wins over your soul and you really begin to love people.

Real intercession cannot be manufactured for your own exaltation. If you are thinking about how much glory you will get because you are going into intercession to save lost people, you need to clean toilets.

I told them a story about people whose neighbor sprayed something that drifted over and burned their lungs. They sued the neighbor. In doing that, they sold their testimony, because they used their capacity to destroy a sinner.

How can you have intercession out of one side of your mouth and be a destroyer out of the other?

Much of what you do in the simple gift of praying in tongues anytime you want is building you past the flesh into the other operations God has for you.

When you are saved, the Holy Spirit is present to rebirth you. Whether you describe the baptism of the Holy Spirit as Him coming up from within after regeneration or falling upon you from without, the point is that you receive the power and Him.

When I got saved, I felt light and new-natured. But when I was filled with the Holy Spirit, it felt different. However you explain the administration, get people speaking with tongues.

We are on the edge of something glorious. We are getting closer by the moment.

Romans 1 and 2 describe fallen man not wanting to retain God in his knowledge and worshiping creation more than the Creator. God gave them over to vile affections, and they burned in lust toward one another until all flesh went his way.

The early world was destroyed because of corruption, with homosexuality as the epitome of idolatry.

We have been studying salvation before the flood and up to the time of the law. Romans says there is no respect of persons with God. Everything He has done through time has been for the good of every human being ever born. He has no favoritism toward one over another.

Knowing that helps you trust your Father. Everything He does is just and for the good of all, even when we do not understand it at first.

Cain killed Abel and was banished east of Eden to the land of Nod. God followed Cain's bloodline for a short time, then followed Adam's bloodline through Seth all the way to Noah.

There were only 470 years between Adam's death and Noah's birth, because men lived so long. Adam, Seth, Enos, Cainan, Mahalaleel, Jared, Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech, and Noah all stood close enough in time that the witness of God was not absent.

The early world had one main promise to believe: the seed born of woman would bruise the serpent's head and destroy his power over death. They also had the basic commandments that flowed from that promise: no other gods, no worship of created things, and do not take the name of the Lord in vain.

These men preached that truth.

Genesis 6 says:

"And it came to pass,

when men began to multiply on the face of the earth,

and daughters were born unto them,

that the sons of God saw the daughters of men

that they were fair;

and they took them wives

of all which they chose."

I believe this refers to Seth's bloodline mixing with Cain's bloodline, not fallen angels marrying women. Cain had been banished, and his line went its way. Seth's line called upon the Lord. But when the sons of God looked over at Cain's line and found the daughters of men seductive, the bloodlines merged.

With that came idols, flesh, and the unrestrained operation of the devil until all flesh went his way.

God said:

"My spirit shall not always strive with man,

for that he also is flesh:

yet his days shall be an hundred and twenty years."

God would not strive with flesh for nearly a thousand years anymore. Human life would be shortened.

Genesis says there were giants in the earth in those days and also afterward. Some have taught that these giants came from fallen angels mingling with women. That never settled in my spirit.

There were races of giants before and after the flood. Og had an iron bed about eighteen feet long. Goliath was large, though not as large as some. I believe the early human body and genetics had capacities we do not fully understand, especially before the decline in lifespan and conditions after the flood.

I once saw the remains of a woman nearly nine feet tall who had been found mummified in the desert. She was proportioned, not distorted: large joints, long arms, and a skull in proportion. Such people existed. But Genesis 6 is not mainly about where giants came from. It is about corruption filling the earth.

After the flood, Genesis 8:20 says:

"And Noah builded an altar unto the Lord;

and took of every clean beast,

and of every clean fowl,

and offered burnt offerings on the altar."

The first thing Noah did when he landed was build an altar and sacrifice clean animals.

Genesis 9:1-2 says:

"And God blessed Noah and his sons,

and said unto them,

Be fruitful, and multiply,

and replenish the earth.

And the fear of you and the dread of you

shall be upon every beast of the earth."

This was under the shadow, yet God gave them authority over beasts, birds, and fish. If that authority existed under the type, what do you think we have now under the true?

Genesis 9:3-4 says:

"Every moving thing that liveth

shall be meat for you;

even as the green herb

have I given you all things.

But flesh with the life thereof,

which is the blood thereof,

shall ye not eat."

Whether they offered an animal as sacrifice or killed it for food, the blood was not to be eaten. It had to be poured out. If they ate the blood, the life that was supposed to be poured out as atonement was consumed, and the sacrifice ceased to function as a type.

The first blood shed for man's need was when God slaughtered animals to make skins for Adam and Eve. Something innocent died because they were spiritually dead and outside the blessing. God made the promise of the Seed, then met their need through blood.

Genesis 9:5-6 says that if the blood is not honored, God will require the life. Then He gives the command:

"Whoso sheddeth man's blood,

by man shall his blood be shed."

Leviticus 17:11 says:

"For the life of the flesh is in the blood:

and I have given it to you upon the altar

to make an atonement for your souls:

for it is the blood

that maketh an atonement for the soul."

No one in Israel or among the strangers sojourning with them was to eat blood. If a man hunted a beast or bird, he had to pour out the blood and cover it with dust.

That blood was a type of Jesus. He became our curse and suffered our death. The life was in the blood, and in the type, the animal died in our place.

That is why it was such a hard saying when Jesus later said:

"Except ye eat the flesh of the Son of man,

and drink his blood,

ye have no life in you."

The Jews knew the command against drinking blood. They understood enough for it to offend them.

Genesis 9 says Ham saw the nakedness of his father and told his brothers. Shem and Japheth took a garment, walked backward, and covered Noah without looking.

Then Noah awoke and knew what had been done to him. The curse fell on Canaan, Ham's son:

"Cursed be Canaan;

a servant of servants

shall he be unto his brethren."

This was not a small penalty for merely seeing nakedness. Something perverse was done. The severity of the curse shows the nature of the act.

There is power in covering. Your sons may be in terrible sin, but they are in your ark. Ham violated that covering, and the curse manifested through Canaan's line.

Genesis 10 lists the generations of Noah's sons: Shem, Ham, and Japheth. All the Gentile nations came from these lines. In Ham's line, through Canaan, appear Sodom and Gomorrah.

Very few times in history did God come down and destroy a city directly, but He did so with Sodom and Gomorrah. The same spirit that corrupted the early world reappeared.

When Jesus said the days of Noah would be like the days of the coming of the Son of Man, eating, drinking, marrying, and giving in marriage, He was not merely describing ordinary life. He was describing a society given over to rioting, banqueting, perversion, and corrupted marriage.

Judgment begins in the house of God when believers had power and let sinners legislate it away. If it gets to the place where we say, "You will not teach my children that darkness is normal," but we have already yielded the ground, then persecution follows because we surrendered what should have been guarded.

God has given us authority. Under the type there was authority. Under the true, we should be able to go anywhere He sends us, do the work, and refuse to be stopped.
