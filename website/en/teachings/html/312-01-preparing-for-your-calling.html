312-01 Preparing for Your Calling

We are studying the laws of meditation, so we will go to Psalm 1:1. Let us review this briefly.

We are going to continue our study into the kind of meditation that causes this Word to become part of you and causes you to become part of it. It is the kind Jesus described when He said:

If My words abide in you, and you abide in Me, ye shall ask what ye will, and it shall be done unto you.

My Word becomes part of you. You become part of it. You become part of Me. I become part of you. You can ask what you will, and it shall be done.

How many know there is a place in Him? Hallelujah.

Last Friday night I was talking about three of the greatest things that ever happened to me. One was the first time blind eyes opened. The second was when two college professors--one taught language and the other speech--both wanted to talk to me. I thought it was to correct my grammar, but I found out they wanted to know who taught me how to meditate.

The third great time was when Bernard, who was a professor of journalism, wanted to know who taught me how to write and how to capture the thoughts down. Again, I had to say it was the Holy Spirit.

The reason these times were so great to me is because Grandpa always told me I would never amount to anything. When he got mad, I guess he saw my dad in me. He would say, "You are no good. You never will be. You are never going to amount to anything."

That got into me.

Then I was born again in holiness, and the way they preached about God, I thought He was as mad at me as my dad was when he left us. Then I came to find out He is good. He is good. He made me righteous, and I could run to Him with my problems.

At the same time, at the age of thirty, I went full time and discovered praying in the Holy Ghost. I did not know for a full three to six months that praying in tongues had begun to be the source of revelation knowledge that started flowing into my life.

It was during that first year that I lost my mind and picked up the mind of Christ. He began to teach me that I was worth something, that I could learn, that I could amount to something. As I kept transforming, I kept losing my old man and gaining Christ, losing my old mind and gaining Christ.

Seven years from the time I went full time, I walked into Grace Fellowship and preached my first revival to thousands of people. Tape sales in the first six months topped $25,000 in a month. I was seven years into full time. A lot of those events, I thought I had died and gone to heaven.

You see, when you get to know Jesus through praying in the Holy Ghost, when the Holy Spirit begins to reveal Him in you, I will tell you where intense hunger and very intense desire come from. They come when the Holy Ghost is able, because of the freedom you give Him, to empower your soul with that new nature.

When He starts empowering your soul with that new nature, that is where that intense appetite and desire to know Him come from. Whether you are lawyer, preacher, prophet, or Indian chief, it does not make any difference. When the Holy Ghost takes that new nature and rises up and seizes your soul, that is where the intense hunger comes from that begins to drive a person.
