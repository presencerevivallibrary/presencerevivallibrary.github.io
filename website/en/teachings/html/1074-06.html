1074-06 Private Worship Vol IV - 6

Today I think He is going to let me tell something I have been wanting to tell, but to do that I have to go back and catch up a little.

Would you go to Romans 12, a chapter you have been turned to for several months? I am glad there is a way I can work God's plan in my life on purpose, as much as I want to and as often as I want to.

We have referred to this quite a bit: the path to the cross is as sovereign, final, and finished as the cross that provided the things, your righteousness, your healing, your peace. Why would there not be a path to those things that is as immovable and sovereign as the very cross that provided them?

I know what guided me. I found out from John, when I was studying Jesus saying, "I give My glory to you, that you may know the Father loves you with the same love that He loves Me." He said, "I will not leave you alone. I will give you another Comforter. He will guide you into all truth, because He will receive of Mine and show it unto you."

We discovered that praying in tongues is the key to edification you receive from understanding the mysteries of Christ, the mystery of who He is in you, the hope of glory. You cannot even pray an hour without Him having in mind not only your call but your direction. Many times we are working the corner up ahead. When we get there, we have already prayed it through, so we are able to make the turn with no trouble. The devil may try everything, but he does not succeed.

In finding this out, I knew there was a path to everything the cross provided for me that was as sovereign and finished as the cross itself. Then along the way I found out what He wanted: revival. He wants revival.

No use going into the full explanation of the kind of revival we are talking about, but we can get into some of it as we go along.

Father, I thank You for Your strength. With all my heart right now, I thank You for Your strength. This has been quite a time, reaching to go forward. What You are showing me and where You lead me are places I have never been before. Father, I glorify and magnify You, and I take every ounce of strength that anyone is offering up for me in prayer at this moment.

For this I would say to you: what I have for you is hard to understand with your mind. But it is a depth of love that My earnest desire is to bring you to, and to continue to increase it and increase it on the inside of you.

Many things people are looking for, and end up unsatisfied when they are over, are the love that I have for them.

For these years gone by, I have attempted to reveal these mysteries here and reveal them there. But I am able to take these mysteries now a little further than I have been able to take them in most places, as you will see, because they will affect everything I have called you to do, accomplish, or be.

I am never the One who holds back, nor do I pull up short. I am always the One who is there and ready to give you more and bring you as far into Me as you yourself can find the strength to come.

I am here for you, and you will find that out now, or you will find that out later, or you will find that out when you get home. But one day you will find it out.

I hope that is now.

We have been in Romans 12 a lot. Beginning with verse 1:

I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service. And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God.

"Therefore" means that based on what he has already said, he is about to beseech you to do something. Present your bodies a living sacrifice. Be transformed by the renewing of your mind, that you may prove the good, acceptable, and perfect will of God. Thirty, sixty, and hundredfold.

Paul goes on:

For I say, through the grace given unto me, to every man that is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think...

Then jump down to verse 6:

Having then gifts differing according to the grace that is given to us, whether prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of faith.

Paul is speaking out of his apostolic office concerning the grace God gave him. Not only did God give him the office, He gave every man the measure of faith.

There has been a lot of talk about that measure, especially in faith circles. "We have the measure of faith." But what kind of measure is it? How much is it?

It is the measure of faith measured out to Paul to fulfill his apostolic office. All of us have been given the measure of faith to fill the grace He called us to fulfill in the body of Christ. That is why when we stand before Him, we cannot say, "I did not have the faith." Whatever religion, somebody else, or the devil did, you still had a call.

I remember the days when we were trying to find out what the measure was. If you leave it out of context, it is hard. Then it came to me. I was so brilliant I called myself Einstein for a month. Are you ready for the definition of a measure? A measure is part of a whole.

All the measures working together make a devil-stomping, mountain-moving body called the body of Christ.

Paul says, "I am speaking according to the grace given to me. You ought not think of yourself more highly than you should. Did I earn this office? Did I give it to myself? Did I give myself the measure of faith to fill the apostolic office? No. He dealt all of that out. All I had to do was follow Him in the transformation of my mind to prove the thirty, sixty, and hundredfold will of God for my life."

Paul would not say, "Therefore offer your bodies a living sacrifice," unless he had already told you how to find it and how to do it. We do not have to go back further than Romans 8. Chapters 9, 10, and 11 deal with the Jewish question. Not today.

Romans 8:22 says:

For we know that the whole creation groaneth and travaileth in pain together until now. And not only they, but ourselves also, which have the firstfruits of the Spirit...

We have been born again. We ourselves groan within ourselves, waiting for the adoption to be made complete at the redemption of our body. Right now, we are saved in hope. Hope that is seen is not hope.

Then verse 26 says:

Likewise the Spirit also helpeth our infirmities...

In other words, He is going to tell us how to help ourselves during this waiting time. Infirmities are your inability to produce results because of the limitations imposed on you by your flesh. That is what He has come to help.

The Spirit helps our infirmities because we do not know what we should pray for as we ought. The Spirit makes intercession for us with groanings that cannot be uttered. He searches the hearts. He knows what is the mind of the Spirit, because He makes intercession for the saints according to the will of God.

Then we know all things work together for good to them who love God and are called according to His purpose. Whom He foreknew, He predestinated to be conformed to the image of His Son, that He might be the firstborn among many brethren.

He is helping my infirmities imposed on me by wearing a body like this. The Holy Spirit is guiding me into all truth. How? By praying God's purpose and plan out in my life, not just to the thirty, not just to the sixty, but however far I want to follow Him until the hundredfold. He gives you the grace and measure of faith to do it with.

I do not plan on stopping.

I jumped on this not knowing what I was doing. When I first started to pray, the big fight about praying in tongues was whether you could do it any time you wanted. I was ultra-holiness, and I did not know. Some said you could; others said you could not.

I decided for myself that I could, because I quit my job and thought if I spent my hours praying, He would pay me. Then I tried to pray in English and ran out in about twelve minutes. So I switched over to tongues, not because I knew whether it worked or not, but to put the hours in. It turned out I was right. You can pray in tongues any time you turn yourself over to Him.

I remember the first message I memorized and ran out to preach. I was so scared. I had a sixteen-year-old boy on guitar, and he played everything he knew while I was still hiding. I finally came out. I preached about when I was a young Baptist boy on the bed of affliction and God healed my body. I explained how faith worked.

At the end, the silence got longer, so I finished and said, "Is anybody sick?"

A lady came up. I said, "What is wrong, ma'am?"

She said, "I have a deaf ear."

I did not want them to hear that. I prayed for her and nothing happened. I thought I could just look like I prayed. Then I remembered seeing another man do this, so I did it. She said, "Oh my God, I can hear you."

I thought, "You can?"

She said, "Yes, I got healed."

Then boldness came over me. "She had a deaf ear. I prayed for her, and she got healed."

He let me get by with it. I was a baby. I kept praying. The years went by, and I kept growing. I came to Tulsa when I was about thirty-eight or thirty-nine. I kept praying in tongues. I did not know what else to do.

The key of edification was the first door I discovered. I opened that door and ran in. It produced this, produced that, and produced the next thing.

When praying in tongues could not give me what fasting could, He put the fasting key in my hand, gave me the revelation of fasting, and unlocked that door. When reading was not enough, He put the meditation key in my hand, and I unlocked that door and added that part to my life.

Each phase of the way, when a door opened, something else was added to my life.

Finally I got to love. This one was not a little door with a little key. This one was at the end of the hall, past all the little doors and keys, and it was a great big iron safe with a turning handle like a combination. I looked at this key and said, "Oh my goodness, this is talking about the love of God. What are You?"

It cracked open just a little, and bright glory shot out. During that period of time, my life changed drastically. That was synonymous with the worship key. I am talking about private worship. When that door opened, it opened my first little taste of love.

I spent time in private worship just because I knew it was a door He had opened. Otherwise it got too tough. Sometimes I would be worshiping, and it would be so dry I felt like I was drinking powder. "I love You," and it would fall right down on the floor. But worship finally opened that door a crack, and that love shot out.

It showed up in my life and ministry for a while. Even in Brazil, preaching through a translator, the whole crowd began to weep and cry. I had a line. I lost the translator. The meeting was about over because the translator went down sobbing and crying.

That showed up in meetings and camp meetings for a period of time. Then I did not see the door anymore until a month or two ago.

The last time I went to Brazil, I walked out and told you what had happened. I had taken a step forward, and all of a sudden in the spirit I heard an avalanche come down behind me. It shut off going back. There was no way to go back. I went from one realm and put my foot into the next one.

Little did I know that this next realm had everything to do with the love of God. I did not know how much so.

As soon as the avalanche came, I was called away to a meeting in Brazil. I did not understand the avalanche. I did not see the door open right away. I only heard the avalanche come down, and I was in another realm.

In Brazil I looked for any difference, and I did not really see any, just normal miracles, if you want to call them that. Then near the end, I was ten stories up in my room. The meeting was pretty much over except for a Sunday service. I was asleep as usual.

I had seen a vision of the devil one time standing by the exit sign, and devils fanned out over the crowd chanting, "There is something wrong here." He was the spirit of darkness over that realm, and if you start praying too much, you are going to attract attention.

This time I woke up, and to my amazement my room was full of people. They were in little groups of three. One man came up to a group and said, "Would you tell Tim that we need to withdraw a million dollars because there is this big project that has to do with the gospel?"

I heard Allen's voice counseling somebody with wisdom. I watched it for a minute and then fell back asleep, thinking it was probably a dream.

The next night I put my clothes on the chair by the bed. I woke up just a little, moved my hand, and opened my eyes a crack. There was somebody sitting in my chair. He said, "Look, he moved his hand over his heart. Do you think he knows you might kill him with a poisoned heart?"

I knew this was not last night's group.

I sat up in bed. When I sat up, he jumped up and ran over next to the wall. It was dark, but there was a night light, and I could see well enough.

Mohammed had Gabriel appear to him in the 600s A.D. saying, "You are God's prophet. You are His voice on the earth." That was not Gabriel. It was Satan, but Satan had the power to make that appearance.

I thought what I was facing was devils. They were devils, but they were of another class, angelic you would call it.

Five or six gathered around the one by the wall. Another group came in, and five or six stood against the other wall. They began to chant, "I am going to kill you." I thought, "I have heard that before."

Was I afraid? Not for a moment. In Jesus' name, I was not afraid.

Then they secured themselves in a special place, and a woman came out. I had never seen an angelic woman like that. She had a smoking flax, and while they stood guard for her she walked down one wall, fanning the smoke toward me. She would not take her eyes off me. Dead, steely looking eyes. She started back down the other side.

I said, "That is not very nice."

That is how scared I was. They kept on. When she went back and was heading for the other side, I stood up and walked toward them, and they all melted back through the wall. The only thing that did not leave was the chant: "I am going to kill you."

I started telling them stories. I told them, "In the realm we walk in, we love our enemies." They knew what I meant: you are going to hell, I am going to heaven. I told them about a baby whose feet were turned backward. When I laid hands on the baby and prayed, it looked like a mole went down the legs, pushing up under the skin, and behind it the legs were straight. The feet turned.

The mother took the baby home. Her husband started screaming and cussing from the bedroom, asking what they had been doing down there so late. She held the baby up, and he got saved.

Can you imagine how infuriated they were? I stopped that.

They tried one more thing. Another figure came in screaming, "Fire, fire, fire," and ran out. The doors were locked; nothing was open. I checked downstairs and asked if there were any reports of fires in the area. They said no. I went back up and said, "It is over. It is over."

Two meetings before that, a man about six-foot-three or six-foot-four came out of the crowd and stood there until I walked over to him. When they interviewed him later, he said he was going to tear my head off. When I stepped up to him, he hit me three times as hard as he could. It felt like nothing. Allen came over, prayed, "In Jesus' name, come out," and the man hit the floor as if he had been shot. The devil lost that one, because the devil came out of him. The last time we were there, he was sitting on the front row with his wife.

Why am I telling you this? Because I did not know those devils were witchcraft. I found out that kind of witchcraft was prevalent, especially in Brazil. What she was trying to put on me was a death curse. I was supposed to be cursed to die. Not in their best day.

Did I win? The peace has been so good and so sweet. This time I got to peek again at the door, and this is why I get to tell you what I saw about the door that is opening with the love of God.

It does not have any fear. When it manifests tangibly, it is in compassion. When it was manifested by Jesus like that, He healed them all.

I asked, "Is this what has been left out?"

Yes.

He lists all the power gifts and says if you do not have love, you do not really have anything. One reason men of God get messed up, take visitations, and pretty soon think they are Jesus or Elijah to come, is that this is the line they got messed up on.

I was not supposed to get past it. I was supposed to be in sensationalism by now, maybe even Elijah. But we did not take that either. Now we have fought past that door where they were supposed to stop me. They could not kill me in Brazil, and they cannot kill me here.

Last Sunday the door opened again a little bit. It is time. It is not going to stop opening. Every time it opens, there will be more of the love of God, more manifestations, more of His revival, wider and wider, until scientists and doctors will not be able to figure out what is happening in the hospitals, much less other places.

They went through all that trouble, and none of it worked. We got past it. Now the door I thought I was at before was only the taste. The door is free to open a little more and a little more. Every time it does, we get to benefit from whatever He is bringing us.

The law and the prophets were until John. Since that time, the kingdom of God has been preached.

When Jesus came to be baptized by John, John said, "I need to be baptized by You." Jesus said, "No, baptize Me. It is necessary."

What was Jesus going to do? Under the old covenant, a man would grab an animal, kill it in his place, and say, "This is me dying." Jesus was thirty years old and had not yet been doing miracles. But when John pulled Him up out of the water, the Holy Spirit came upon Him in baptism. For the next three to three and a half years, He walked under the power of the Holy Spirit. In that baptism He died in our place, came out, was filled with the Holy Ghost, and ministered in our place.

Why would He say, "The works that I do shall you do also; and greater works than these shall you do, because I go to My Father"? The greater works were because He went to the Father. When He did, He canceled the sin question, and the power was resident here to be born again. Anybody you preach to can receive Jesus and be born again.

The miracles He was talking about were:

These signs shall follow them that believe... they shall lay hands on the sick, and they shall recover.

When He called people to His dinner, He called the maimed, the lame, and the blind.

Religion has gone the other way. I thank God for every church getting somebody born again and babysitting them through. But most of the city will come and hear what you have to say and then go home if your speech is not good enough. Nobody will be able to look the other way when the local children's hospital is empty.

He did very few synagogue miracles. He did them outside.

In Russia, a man came up behind me in perfect English, though he did not speak English, and said, "I am going to kill you." In India, I thought there was nothing but preachers behind me. Perfect English again from a man who did not speak English: "I am going to kill you."

This gets old.

Why does he do this? Because of Him who has come to guide me into all truth. There is a way to get on the path. He will bring me to everything He has done for me on purpose just because I want to go there. I am going. Give me another key; I will do it. I do not care if it is fasting. I will do it.

After the first fasting visit, when he knew I caught on to what he was doing, he said he was going to kill me. I was standing behind my truck, and the same voice from the prayer room came up behind me: "I am going to kill you."

I said, "If you could, I would already be dead."

He said, "Then I am going to kill your family."

That was his threat. I can tell you this much: they are in heaven. If I knew then what I know now...

I am not giving him glory. He failed. He failed. He could not close the door we were not supposed to go through.

What is on this path ahead? Revival. No fear. Love never fails. I am going to finish my course.

No wonder the tongues and interpretation came forward before He let me go ahead. I was not going to tell you about the attack and what was standing at the door to keep the love door from opening all the way until there was enough fruit to substantiate it. It sounds wild. It does not matter what it sounds like. I was not unstable in the past, and I am not now. I have had a visit. I got a good look at the supernatural, and we are on our way to the good side. They know it.

I told them about miracles. "I am going to kill you." I had to take a shower, and after a minute, "I am going to kill you" started over the shower noise. Not in my shower.

Basically after that, the next day I talked with Allen and told him what had happened. I closed it and said, "It is over. That is it. I am not taking this anymore."

All I know now is that we are in the same room with the door that was made out of steel. I believe it is the last one. From the rest of the time, it will just open wider and wider and wider.

Remember in God's attributes, there are ten. Sovereignty, righteousness, justice, immutability, eternal life, omnipotence, omniscience, omnipresence, and truth. Immutability means He cannot change or be changed. There is not any power anywhere that can change Him.

He cannot be changed, and He cannot lie. What did He give you to use? He said, "I will give you My Word." What does He say about disease? He took my sicknesses and carried my pains, and that is the end of it.

What are you? I am healed.

"You do not look like it."

I did not ask you what it looked like. I told you what I was. He is truth.

The whole reason it took four thousand years just to get you is because He could not lie. Do you get it, Adam? "I cannot, I cannot, I cannot." Why did He not just forgive him? He cannot lie. Four thousand years later, He finally fixed it so our place could be taken, His Word fulfilled, and us go free.

He did all that for me, and I think that when I go into His presence to pray, He does not hear me? I think I need some logical reason to approach Him?

You know what I approach Him based on now? I know He loves me. "I know You love me. I know what You have done for me. Why would You not do this?"

We try to give Him reasons why He should do something. You are not going to find one greater than what Jesus has done for you, because He is the One who sent Him. There is nothing greater He could do. So when you approach Him, cash in on the love.

God has a weakness. No, He does not. Yes, He does: it is you.

Mark the date down. This is when we said that the door is not going to quit opening. We are in that realm, on the last stretch to revival, and into enough of God to know the difference between Elijah, Jesus, and some special mouth that thinks it is the only one that can pronounce God's name correctly.

That was Branham, and I would still be waiting for his resurrection if I followed that.

How could a man who shook nations and got so many saved lose count because of the way God used him? What did he not know? I will tell you: this last part. The love of God keeps increasing in you. Where is the fear going to go? Where is the failure going to go? Love is not going to deceive you.

We made it past the snares and traps that belonged on that part. I do not know what kind are here. I do not really care.

When this started getting on me, I booked meetings. I went and did meetings as far away as Brazil and other states to find people and make things right, even if it really was not my fault, but it had not been addressed and continued to hold out against me.

I flew here and there, doing this and that, just to make sure, because I know now that I do not know of anything else that can hinder your prayers worse than holding on to something against a person He loves.

He chose the biggest faith thing we could go for, like the mountain:

Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea.

If you doubt not in your heart, then He stops and deals with the heart for a minute. "By the way, if you have ought, stop the prayer, go fix it, then come back."

Same with giving. You had better be really good at making money if greed is why you are giving. If you discover you have ought, put it back in your pocket, go on, and make it right.

I do not want to swim around on that level when He is turned loose.

Be careful, for this is a day and an hour, a good time, that you begin to pull yourself aside and make it a point. The enemy is not taking this lightly, for he has plans. But My plans are greater than his, and My power is greater than his. I gave you that power over all the power of the enemy.

He will come in many forms. One will be downcast, hopeless, depressed kinds of feelings that get you where you do not want to do anything. That is one of his big ones.

Hear what the Spirit would say: during those times, it is good to continue to pray in My language that I have given to you. No matter what you feel like, you are not affecting the prayer that I am originating on the inside of you. In fact, I am working you for the day that you will be free and receive what I have for you.

You will find out that no matter how downcast you feel, you are still able to pray in My supernatural language. Even if your mind keeps going back to the worry for uncontrolled times, do not stop the edification process, says the Lord.
