2011-11 The Channel Which God Speaks By

We have been studying hearing God's voice. Go back to 1 Corinthians 14:14, where we left off, and let us take this one step further.

It does not matter whether you are Mary Wallpaper, Joe Public, apostle, prophet, teacher, preacher, evangelist, helps, governments, or prayer ministries of diversities of tongues. Every child of God has access directly to the Father. Every child of God has a right to hear and function in the voice of God.

Under the Law, every man taught his neighbor through ordinances, precepts, holy days, and Sabbaths to know God. But in this covenant, every one of you, because you are born again and have received His nature, can know Him from the least to the greatest.

His voice is not reserved for people like William Branham moving profoundly under the word of knowledge, telling people's addresses and names. That is a tremendous gift in operation, but His voice is not reserved for a few preachers who call people out and minister by word of knowledge.

His voice is available to everyone, from Mary Wallpaper to the mighty apostle, in business, everyday affairs, and walking with Him.

We have been studying hearing God's voice and what we need to develop if we do not hear His voice. We found out that He communicates with us in four basic ways.

The first way He communicates is by the inner witness.

You either get an inner witness, a peace inside that it is all right, or you get what we call a check in the spirit. You receive peace to go ahead, or a check not to go ahead.

This is the most common way He communicates with His children. It generally guides you: yes, no, do it, do not do it. You are about to do something you should not do, or you are not doing something you should do.

The second way He communicates is by revelation. This is His most effective way of communication.

Revelation is like capsuling an entire revelation onto a computer chip. He pours a whole revelation of God into that chip, sends it from the Holy Spirit across to your spirit, and releases the program inside all at once.

A moment before, you were like a little computer sitting there with no program. Suddenly this chip releases an entire program on the inside, and you say, "My God, I know everything. I understand the mystery of healing. I understand why they are not getting healed. I understand why I am not getting healed."

Because He loves to communicate with your human spirit, which was created in His class, He can pour an entire revelation into you in a millisecond. Many times when I received an entire revelation, I received it in less than a second, and it took me three months to preach out what I got.

He did not reserve that kind of thing for preachers. If you own a business, He can straighten your entire business out in a millisecond and give you what you need to do to turn a million dollars over in a year.

The question is, can He communicate like that with you? Do you hear Him?

The third basic way He communicates is in English, or whatever language you think in.

If you speak Spanish more than English, He will communicate with you in Spanish. He communicates with you in the language you think in.

For the longest time, when God would prophesy through me or speak to me, He always talked to me in perfect King James. Had He not done that, I would not have believed it was Him.

In those days, I did not know He could communicate by revelation, so when He spoke English to me, He had to do it in King James lest I believe it was not Him.

These days He prophesies through me by revelation. He alerts me that He wants to prophesy, then drops a whole revelation, and lets me choose my own words to explain the revelation.

The day He broke me from needing King James was when He asked me, "Son, how would a Japanese person prophesy in King James?"

We know He will communicate with you in your language, the language you think in.

The fourth way He communicates is by vision.

For a while, I mixed up visions. I thought discerning of spirits was vision because one day here at the prayer center I saw devils mixed in the crowd. I thought I was having a vision. The problem was, you people were walking around talking to each other, and I thought, "What kind of vision is this?"

Then I learned that was discerning of spirits, not a vision. Through discerning of spirits, He opened my eyes so I could see into the world of the spirit. I saw what was going on in here that would cause a service to fall dead at your feet.

I saw a ruler of darkness, a spirit of religion, over by the exit door. He was commanding a host of unclean spirits, little devils, and they were setting up a yammering atmosphere and killing the service.

An open vision is when everything disappears in front of you and is replaced by a vision. I have had three or four of those.

The most common vision is the one He paints in your spirit. He will materialize a picture in your spirit. When you get used to Him doing that and know how He communicates with you, you learn the channel He comes through.

He will literally materialize a picture in your spirit. It is not in your mind. It is deeper than your mind. It is actually in your spirit. Most of the time I am under an anointing, but I am fully conscious and looking ahead at everything. Suddenly He materializes it.

He uses that often when I minister to people by word of knowledge. I may be standing there looking at someone, and suddenly a heart materializes with a dark valve in it. Then I know God wants to do a miracle in that person's heart, specifically in the valve.

Sometimes a doctor's report will materialize in front of me, and parts of it will highlight. I know what the doctor said.

When you are not under an anointing, it can be hard to tell the difference between pictures your mind produces and a vision materialized deeper inside. It has taken a lot of prayer to understand the difference.

Sometimes I would be witnessing to people, and suddenly a picture would materialize in me that had nothing to do with what I was saying. One time I was trying to get a woman saved, and I saw empty cupboards and a mattress with no blanket or sheet. When I switched over and ministered to her condition, that did it. Then I was able to help her.

Sometimes God surprises me and shows me practical things. One time I lost my wallet. It had slipped out of my pants when I unloaded my suitcase and had fallen into the suitcase, which I threw in the closet. I looked and looked and looked.

Finally I stopped and prayed. Immediately I had a vision of the suitcase partly open, looked inside it, and saw my wallet lying there. I ran over, got the suitcase, opened it, and wished I had prayed three hours earlier.

Another time my son David lost the keys. I prayed and had a vision of them lying on top of the Coke machine. I said, "They are down the hall on top of the Coke machine." He had laid them there to get a Coke. He went back, and there they were.

God communicates through visions that materialize in your spirit. Once you learn how the Holy Spirit communicates with you, you can develop yourself in listening for the ear of the Spirit.

People ask me all the time, "What is the most qualifying thing you need in your life to hear His voice?"

The peace of God.

If you do not develop peace, you will not be able to hear the inner witness. You will be troubled all the time. How is He going to trouble you when you are already troubled? How is He going to give you an inner witness of peace to go a certain direction if you are in turmoil all the time?

One of the most powerful things that fosters hearing God's voice is developing the ground of peace.

Isaiah 28 says:

With stammering lips and another tongue will he speak to this people.

To whom he said, This is the rest wherewith ye may cause the weary to rest; and this is the refreshing.

When you pray much in tongues, the rest you received when you were born again and ceased from your sin is brought forward. When that rest dominates your whole soul, He will be able to communicate with you on such a level that you will do nothing but conquer the rest of your life.

First Corinthians 14:14 says:

For if I pray in an unknown tongue, my spirit prayeth, but my understanding is unfruitful.

If I, Dave Roberson, pray in an unknown tongue, my human spirit is doing the praying. In order for my human spirit to be the one praying, the Holy Spirit has to transfer that supernatural language from Himself to me.

He does that by creating the supernatural language on the inside of my spirit.

Why does He transfer it to my spirit? It is a transference of authority. In order for it to be your authority praying, it could not be the Holy Ghost alone praying. He had to transfer the language to you. Then it is you praying in your authority, and the other members of the Godhead answer.

One member of the Godhead creates the prayer perfectly out of the mind of Christ, and the other two answer it. God is praying for you, and God is answering it. How can you fail?

Most of what is in your way is you. We want Him to change everything else and leave us alone. But He transfers the language because you are the one with authority. He needs your authority to operate in this earth, and you need His power.

Recently God said to me, "Son, you must understand: you are the one who has the authority here, but I am the One who has the power. I need you, but you certainly need Me."

When we trade off correctly, you watch what He can do.

Where does He create the language in your spirit?

When people say, "God spoke to me," I ask, "Where did He talk to you?" They say, "Deep in my spirit." But where?

Over twenty years ago, in the prayer closet, He cleared this up for me. I saw the outer man, the inner man, the darker figure representing the flesh, the soul as another shade, and the spirit absolutely full of light. The baptism of the Holy Spirit was represented in the spiritual mind, which is housed in the natural mind.

The place He created the language was inside the spiritual mind.

That is why, if you do not let the tongues come out of your mouth, they bypass your mouth and drift up to your thoughts. You can hear the tongues in your mind. They feel like they come up from deep inside your spirit, but they come from the recesses of your spiritual mind, which lies within your physical mind.

As long as you let the language come out of your mouth, your mind is free to think about other things, such as reading the Word. I have developed the ability to read the Word while praying in tongues.

People ask, "Can I just pray in tongues in my mind?"

Not really effectively.

You can do it, but if you do not let the prayer finish its channel and come out of your mouth, eventually your mind will shift to something else and you will find yourself not praying.

I believe your mouth is the doorway that releases the prayer from the spirit into this world. When that authority is released, your prayers bring things to pass in this world.

Paul said:

If there be no interpreter, let him keep silence in the church; and let him speak to himself, and to God.

To speak to yourself means in a tone nobody around you can hear. You are edifying yourself because you are speaking to yourself and to God, and it is coming out of your mouth.

If you stop your mouth, the language bypasses your tongue and drifts up to your mind. You can hear the tongues come up to your mind, but eventually your mind switches to something else and dismisses them. If you let the language come out of your mouth, the flow continues, and your mind can think on other things.

When you pray in tongues for a while, it opens the channel to your spirit, the part of your spirit through which God communicates with you.

The tongues He creates in your spirit are actually connected to the ear of the spirit, the channel He comes through to your mind if He is going to speak in any other language.

If you pray in tongues for a minute or two and then stop, those tongues will keep coming up to your mind briefly. If God were talking to you in English, the English would come up through the same channel the tongues came through.

This is why God often instigates tongues for interpretation. The tongues open the channel to the spirit. Now that the channel is open, the interpretation can come through that channel. What follows is known to be of God because it is coming through the spirit.

I do not really need that process the same way anymore. I pray in tongues, but God talks to me all the time. I know how to open that channel. I know how to shut down in the middle of chaos and monitor that part of my spirit where it comes from. I can listen for English, receive a vision, an inner witness, a check in the spirit, or whatever I need.

The only reason I can teach these things in detail is because He taught them to me in detail. He could not have taught them to me in detail unless a channel between me and God had opened up.

The more you pray in tongues and consistently give yourself to prayer, the stronger, louder, and more definite that channel becomes. The more rest you live in, the more you are able to shut down, monitor, and listen to the voice of God.

Let us do a little experiment.

Pray in tongues until I tell you to stop. Then stop and cleave your tongue to the roof of your mouth. Do not let it move anymore. When you do not let it move, those tongues will float up to your mind. You will hear them in your mind.

What I want you to realize is that where they are coming from is the same channel He speaks English through, gives inner witnesses through, gives visions through, and communicates by revelation through.

The more you pray in tongues, the more you will develop the ability to listen to that ear.

When you pray in tongues, you put yourself in the spirit because you open the channel to another world. You open the channel to your human spirit and to the Holy Spirit. That is why Paul says:

Howbeit in the spirit he speaketh mysteries.

When you hit a crisis, you need to put yourself in the spirit. Open the channel. Identify the voice and the channel of God.

At the moment your mind switches anywhere else, it cuts the flow off. You have to give yourself back over, and the channel opens again.

When you go down that channel, you will find the Holy Ghost living there. He says, "Did you come to visit Me? Are you going to unlock Me or what?"

When you were born again and received a regenerated spirit, an absolute phenomenon took place. That new nature separates you from the Old Testament saints.

What they did not have, and would have loved to have, was a nature that actually had the capacity to understand spiritual things.

The carnal mind is enmity against God. The carnal mind is the mind they had under the Law. The things of God are spiritually discerned, meaning they cannot be understood outside spiritual discernment.

When you were born again and received the nature of God, the most important thing you received was a capacity to understand spiritual things. That is why heaven sent you the Teacher of all teachers to make sure you come forward in discernment, wisdom, and the understanding of everything God is.

The more mysteries you pray in tongues, the more you open yourself to the mystery hidden in God on the other side of spiritual death, now made available to the Church: the mystery of Christ in you, to you, and through you, the hope of glory.

The mind of Christ prayed through tongues includes the understanding of the mysteries of the Word of God, but it also includes your personal ministry and calling. You cannot spend an hour praying in tongues without the Holy Ghost working on the personal mind of God for you.

Jesus said concerning John the Baptist:

Among them that are born of women there hath not risen a greater than John the Baptist.

How could that be? John did no miracles. Elisha raised the dead, moved in power, and saw armies stopped by angels. But Jesus said John was greater.

Why? Because of all the old prophets, none had more knowledge of Christ than John. God chose him to be the forerunner, to preach that One was coming whose sandal latch he was not worthy to unbuckle, One who would baptize in the Holy Ghost and fire.

Yet Jesus said:

He that is least in the kingdom of heaven is greater than he.

Mary Wallpaper and Joe Public are greater than the greatest prophet of the Old Covenant because they are born again, have access to the nature of God, the baptism of the Holy Spirit, and unlimited access to God.

You are a tongue-talking, praying child of God. You have a Teacher. Your channel is open. He is going to teach you how to go all the way in.

As He brings the new nature, the real you, forward, He bypasses the trash in the soul and flesh and deposits Himself in your spirit, in the power plant.

The Holy Ghost says, "I am going to teach this man. I am going to work on that new nature. I am going to teach him his authority."

As the authority of that new man comes forward, it will make the soul realm look like it was run over if it gets in the way. It will make the flesh wish it had died in mortification.

By the time He is done, everything that usually stops men will be in the wake behind you. He will bring the real you forward with all the authority you have.

He has the power. You have the authority. When He is done teaching you, blind eyes will see.

Paul probably received revelation by this channel more than any man after Jesus. Look at who wrote most of the New Testament.

He said:

I thank my God, I speak with tongues more than ye all.

If you lined up every Corinthian and asked how much each prayed, Paul could still boast, "I thank my God I pray more in the supernatural language that encompasses the understanding of the mysteries of Christ than any of you." God answered those prayers, and Paul wrote them down.

So what are you praying when you pray in tongues? You are picking up what Paul wrote down.

First Corinthians 14:16 says:

Else when thou shalt bless with the spirit, how shall he that occupieth the room of the unlearned say Amen at thy giving of thanks, seeing he understandeth not what thou sayest?

Paul says that the person giving thanks in tongues gives thanks well, but the unlearned is not edified unless it is interpreted.

Then Paul switches to his own personal prayer life:

I thank my God, I speak with tongues more than ye all:

Yet in the church I had rather speak five words with my understanding, that by my voice I might teach others also, than ten thousand words in an unknown tongue.

The key word is "also." Paul says, "In my private life, I pray more than all of you in this tongue you have misused so badly. But in church, I would rather speak five words in my own understanding so I might teach others also."

That means Paul had received edification first. If others were going to receive edification also, he had to give them the revelation in a language they understood.

I asked God, "Why did You say ten thousand words in an unknown tongue?"

He said, "Time it."

So I did. I prayed at a normal speed and counted enough to multiply it out. Ten thousand words is roughly between an hour and a half and an hour and forty-five minutes.

Paul was saying, "It would benefit you more in a public service for me to speak five words in a language you understand than to speak tongues for the length of the whole service, because the tongues edify me while everyone else stands by."

In private prayer, Paul received something from praying the mysteries. But if the church was going to be blessed also, he could not stand in front of them and speak ten thousand words in tongues for an hour and a half. He had to give them the revelation that came from those tongues.

That is why public prayer meetings differ from public services. If we come together specifically to pray in tongues all day, that is what we came for. But in a public service with the unlearned and the lost, revelation must be communicated in understanding.

Open that channel. The first strong teaching the Holy Spirit will give you personally is revelation. He will begin bringing great revelation across that channel.

Some of your old teachers may not seem as good as you thought they were after a year. It is not that they changed. You did.

Let us stand up and worship God awhile.
