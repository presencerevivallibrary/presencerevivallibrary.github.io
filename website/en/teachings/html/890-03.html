890-03 The Called Must Be Formed Into the Chosen

Do you really want it?

You know He will come and get you and glorify you. You will go to heaven. You will be there, probably somewhere in the Millennium. There will be a lot of things that need to be done. It will be a supernatural and natural government together.

Do you want to hear something shocking? At the end of the thousand years, Satan is going to be turned loose for a little season. During that time, people will have lived and never died. They will have had children the whole time, and none of them will have died. The lamb will lie down with the lion. Children will play at the hole of an asp.

Once again, there will be a regeneration process going on. Satan will have been bound. These people will have grown up in a glorified government with the Lamb sitting on His throne, all the apostles there in their glorified bodies, and many glorified saints reigning throughout that government.

Do you want to misjudge an enemy so deceptive that he will turn those nations against the Lamb again, even though they were subjected to a supernatural government?

How could he do that?

What do you think he has done to religion?

Do you think you have to wait to forsake all? That begins right where the rich man was. Jesus said to him, "If you want to be perfect..."

None of us are in any way, shape, or form even approaching perfection, but one thing we can do is obey what Jesus said. In another teaching, Jesus said that anyone who comes to Him and does not forsake houses, lands, and his own life also cannot be His follower.

What do you think? That your old man will just lie down and die with no trouble? That one day Jesus will ride in on a chariot with a mighty anointing in His hand and smite you with it?

No. It begins with you. "I choose to take this space I am in right now and begin to serve Him."

He called it forsaking all.

I cannot imagine how the Millennium will be. I cannot imagine the glory you are resurrected in. I cannot imagine the status He gives you. I cannot imagine, when the Lamb sends you to different quarters of the earth, that what He clothes you in will speak something of your rank. I imagine it will demand the respect that you learned to give the Lamb a long time ago.

I imagine it will be a joy and a glory you would have to die to appreciate.

I just want it.

Worship Him.

Hallelujah, Jesus. I glorify You, praise You, adore You, and worship You. I love You, Jesus. Hallelujah.

He is saying this:

In understanding that many are called but few are chosen, I have called many to enter into this room to fellowship with Me. But most could not see to the end of their life, much less to the life to come.

So many, in the lifetime of the cares of this world, had their calling taken away from them in the part where they would qualify to be chosen by Me, says the Spirit of the Lord.

Even though many are called, few are chosen. Because so few have been chosen, only a few understand the things I require for a man to enter in and fellowship with Me on the level where I would entrust him with lands and multitudes of brothers and sisters and children for My sake and the gospel's.

Hear what the Spirit would say. I have desired to pull those aside and train them that they might understand how to enter into this call. But most of them stop their training at the level where they themselves become exhausted.

Hear what the Spirit would say: many are called, but few are chosen.

If you will allow Me, I see enough qualification to begin to unfold the revelation it will take for you to understand what it will cost you to walk with Me.

For in that day, great shall be your reward.

I am hearing, "In the end, in the end."

I do not know how He is doing it, but He is working and calling everybody aside that He can. I do not know how He is doing it at this point. I will understand it before it is over. But He is attempting to get over to men the answers to entering into these kinds of things.

Now I know what He is saying here. More is opening up.

How many desire to be chosen?

When I start hearing Him talk to me on that level, I get so far out of it that I am not good for much else. The mind of what He wants to say is in the formation stage, way down in the well, in the treasure of my spirit. I can feel it, and I can almost understand it, but I cannot say it. It will finish forming, and I will be able to say it.

Right now, the mind of Christ is endeavoring to form something. It will be in me many days. We will be in this many days. You will understand thoroughly what He wants you to know by the time it is done. You, my friend, will have the privilege, as He said, of being among those who have forsaken all.

That sounds worse than it is. It means that no matter what He has asked you to do, you have followed Him wherever He went.

Now I understand the reward He has contracted with me for, a penny a day. This is not only a hundredfold in this lifetime of money to lay up treasure in heaven, but also eternal life when He calls me in and pays me the penny for my day.

He will say, "Son, 7,276,000 were born again. Will you reign with Me?"

I am going to get the report right next to Peter and Paul. "How am I doing? Your chair was not available, but if it had been, you can bet I would have gone for it."

It is not as bad as you think. Do not wait until somebody prophesies you to India.

There is no man who comes to Him and forsakes houses and lands--start coming to the place where you can do what the rich man could not. Trade your wealth on this earth to lay treasures up in heaven, because that is where your true wealth on this earth begins. If you can lay it up in heaven, then you have tapped into a source no man can turn off.

Your children will eat when Tulsa starves. You will not have to join the militia and get a gun. You will not have to join special groups forming around some super churches, where they meet, give you an Uzi, and tell you to store bread in barrels under your house. Then you end up shooting little children when they want a handful of the meal you stored up.

Where will it end? Who are you going to kill? Your neighbor? How many people will you kill protecting your supplies?

You do that kind of thing because you are afraid.

It begins here. This is where it begins. The rich young ruler was not lost. He traded his wealth for a reign in the Millennium, and possibly a seat.

I am prophesying now. With me, this is not going to stop. I will just go write it down. If you do not want to listen to me anymore, my paper will listen to me.

I do not know what else to do but go ahead and minister this grace to you. This is not necessarily about you running up and throwing a big offering in. That is not what we are teaching on. We are teaching on being able to do what the rich young ruler could not.

"Who can be saved?"

Peter, it cost Me everything for you to say that. You do not know what I left behind to become what you are.

With God, all things are possible. I am going to change your nature. I am going to give you an inheritance in heaven. Follow Me.

We were taking an offering.

Bring the buckets up and put them on the steps. Give them an envelope.

Folks, I may have to go to the back right after this, because you may ask me something and I will tell you the truth. When I am in this, the distance from my mouth to your answer is just this far. Sometimes I say what I think. It is not that it is wrong. It is just that I have blurted things out and wished I had not handled it that way.

"Boy, I do miss you. I am glad it is over tonight. I have been wanting to come down and pray like you have been teaching, but I am awful busy."

No, you are busy because you want to be.

"No, I mean I would really come if I..."

No, you are not coming because you do not want to. You do whatever else you want.

Tim, you had better take me out of here.

What you will see in the days to come, you will hardly recognize. For those to whom I have given this have been so few and far between that those who picked up the mantle had no teachers and forgot how it was when those who walked with Me had the limits taken off.

I desire to come in all My glory and all My power. I desire to administer the fullness of grace in everything that Christ is to every person who will receive Him.

I have redeemed you in every area, and I desire to give the ministry of the simplicity of giving to those who are able to receive it.

Hallelujah.

Something about answered prayer:

Many have stood in My presence and prayed noble prayers, yet they stood not in My presence absent of those things that I need in order to move on their behalf.

Understand this, saith the Spirit of grace. Many have stood in My presence and prayed noble prayers. I know the secrets of your heart, and I know your prayers even before you pray.

Hear what the Spirit of the Lord would say. For those who stand in My presence and desire many things from Me, the decisions have already been made because of the price paid and established on your account through your covenant.

When you reach the place where you qualify, the blessings have already been given. They do not enter into you. You enter into them.

But I cannot prematurely give them to you while things still exist inside you that will not allow them to be received, because the enemy would come and destroy you because of your weaknesses.

Hear what the Spirit would say. Enter into My presence and fellowship with Me, for I desire to purge you and make you ready to receive My glory.

If I were afraid to miss a series, it would be this one.

Stand up, please.

Tell them about the trap.

It has been handed down from apostle to prophet, to teacher, to pastor, to evangelist. This teaching has been handed down to those who are called by Me to be good stewards, to cease not to offer up prayers day and night, and to give themselves continually to My Word and prayer.

Tell them the rest of it.

Even though Satan could not remove those who received My call too far from these principles, he has removed those I have called to be chosen in these areas from their principles.

Many have fallen short of the illumination and revelation I desired to feed into their spirits because Satan cut them short on the teaching.

They could not see into the Spirit far enough to understand that they needed to follow Me as the apostle followed Me, go into My presence, and take this realm away from the enemy.

I desire to bring you in as I desired to bring My apostles into their anointings. But because deception has come in, they seem not to need it and only gave themselves over to the making of money, and the money finally made them.

Hear what the Spirit would say: I have called you to seek Me out and know Me as much as I have called them. Do not be tripped up, for the things you need to see are in the fullness of My presence.

Come and walk with Me, saith the Spirit of grace, and I will make you one of My chosen, for you already are one of My called.

Tell them that Satan took that part away. They do not look at themselves the same way.

An apostle looks at himself and knows he must give himself to the Word of God day and night. In the same way, entrepreneurs have been defeated from the revelation they needed to take this realm of glory away from the enemy.

Why do you reason within yourself and say this? Those reasonings, those strongholds, have been given to you by the enemy.

Those who deliver My power in the hour and a half, two hours, or three hours they spend in front of you have spent many hours with Me.

Those who operate in that kind of wisdom know that it takes only a few hours a day to make the kind of decisions I flow through, decisions that cause the strong man to come down.

The rest of the time they spend nurturing their spirit in My presence.

Just as My men of God minister My anointing and spend the rest of their time with Me, so My entrepreneurs minister My anointing, make those decisions, cut with the sword, pull strongholds down, and fight with My wisdom.

The rest of the time they spend in prayer.

Do not say in your heart that this man has all the time he needs to seek Me, for the same wisdom available to him is available to you. That is a trap. Do not use it again.

By the time you are handling billions, you will only make decisions for a few hours a day. Just like the man of God delivers and spends the rest of the time with Him, you are going to have to be where He is.

You have been deceived. He is not letting you stomp. All you will do is pay, pay, pay, to drive hell backwards.

You will have whipped it with your wisdom before you took the best gift you know and employed them to preach people into the kingdom for you.

It is a lot better when He does it.
