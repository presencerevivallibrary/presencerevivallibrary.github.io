As you continue on in My prayer, times will come and you will have seasons where your faith will be strong in the realm you are walking in. Then the enemy comes with great cares of this world, persecutions, and tries to take this away from you by force before you advance further into higher places in My Spirit.

There is one beginning now, and this is one of great excitement and answered prayer. In this one you will learn to minister to yourself, both in the assimilation of My Word and edification, but also to minister to yourself in My fellowship and in coming to know Me.

This is an area, saith the Lord, where you will see a very, very powerful and visible difference. Excitement will come. Many have made it on and finished their course who made it this far, and many of you are entering into this time.

So be strong and know that in the midst of adversity, great victories, miracles, and moves of My Spirit lie just ahead. Be faithful in your edification, assimilation, and your times with Me, for only then will you understand the importance of this when I begin to move through you powerfully in revival, saith the Lord.

For it is your foundation now.

First we enter into great times of harvest. You will marvel and wonder in amazement as you see Me move in the pureness of My Spirit and My power. Many of you will say, "I have been deceived. If I had known it was this way, I would have begun to change long ago."

What do you think the words "times of refreshing from My presence" mean? In all opposition and all resistance, I will put it down, for you will be refreshed, saith the Lord. You will be refreshed by My presence.

Settle it within yourselves: it is what I have done, not what you have done. Not by your works, lest any man should boast. It is My gift. I created you unto good works; the good works did not create you. There is no area that My grace covers that you can cover.

So stand up and be bold in yourselves. Rejoice and know that the first journey you will make is the journey of the heart: the journey of peace, soundness of mind, sanity, great joy, and lack of torment and fear.

There is no circumstance that can challenge you, for I am the Lord your God, who has shed My grace abundantly upon you through My Son. Therefore receive, that I may work My plans through you in these last days, saith the Lord.

Have I not said, "Is anything too hard for Me?" For I am the God of the impossible.

Even in all this, know this, saith the Lord: when I begin to do notable miracles for you, always remember it is your change as you grow closer to Me that I rejoice in. The miracles are nothing to Me; your fellowship is everything.

For the endless eternities He will fellowship with you. This is His desire. This is His desire. This is His desire. So enter into the fellowship now. Why do you wait all day for what I desire for the eternities? I desire it now.

As you move forward in My anointings and the supernatural, those which are taken from the fullness of My grace, know this: when your faith releases Me to cross over those lines, you know it is not by your works but by the manifestation of My love toward you.

There is none of you who could be good enough within your own selves. It is the standing I give you. So do not condemn yourself for your faults. Just do not stay where you are. Continue, line upon line and from glory to glory in My presence.

You working your future standing by going from glory to glory releases Me with My grace to come and do for you what I can do now.

Therefore, rejoice not in your own selves but in the strength I have given you. Look for the perfection that comes from My Son through the grace I have given you, not your own strength. Yield yourself to Me with no condemnation, and let Me work that which I work in you, saith the Lord.

This presence that you enjoy corporately can be enjoyed individually. There is a place in Me that you can go.

The rest of it was that we get so refreshed from meeting together and worshiping. Could you imagine how refreshed you could walk if you learned how to stay in that presence? The message was something like that.

At the end, He was saying that so many saints have traded off His presence for a form of godliness and denied the power of the form. Your fears and worries can become great anticipation. Your fears can become great confidence. He that seeks Me shall find Me.

This is like a frontier that has yet to be conquered by many whom I have called into the harvest of the precious fruit of this earth in the last days. This is a frontier that must be conquered, that I might shed My abundance liberally on those who do My will.

You who are in tune can see the seasons changing. The season is here. Many of you will experience things you have not before. You will drink deep from the rivers of living water and operate freer than many of you have before.

Be faithful and be strong, for there is no building that has ever been built whose foundation was not eventually tried. Be strong. Be strong. Be strong.

In what He was showing, many of you were running out and bringing parts of the harvest back, and your joy and your operation were contagious. Many were strengthened and encouraged and established themselves even stronger.

If what I desired for Tulsa could have been done, it would already have been done. Therefore, something must be done that has not been done. It is My desire to have an outpouring here that sweeps in the masses.

So be strong and rejoice in your path, for it is the path I have laid for you. I am able to do what I could not do before when I find what I need to do it with. So be faithful, saith the Lord. Great days.

You are looking at Me, and I am looking at you, speaking in His stead. I am looking at you, and you are looking at Me, for I am speaking of things, saith the Lord, that are heavenly. I am speaking of things that are in My mind, things I have for you.

When I can find someone who will utter the things which I say, do you think I will not stop and speak these things to you that you might be free? It is My desire to set you on this kind of path of fellowship with Me, for I am meek and lowly, and you will find rest for your souls.

There is no one who has done anything for Me, saith the Lord, whom Satan did not come against with persecution, affliction, and care, using all the instruments at his disposal to stop him. There is no one who has gone to where My creative presence lives, to where the outpourings of My Spirit affect entire cities, whom he did not try to stop with everything he does.

There is nobody who does these kinds of things for Me who did not have to get past his worst. You will see what I am talking about when these things come to pass. You will see.

Many will come from many places to look at what is happening here, and they will be so convinced by Me, because the limits have been taken off, that they will go back with aspirations. They will go back knowing that they can accomplish everything they have seen accomplished here.

Therefore know this: it has been a while since I have gotten to visit with this kind of visitation. So know that the enemy is not just trying to stop you here; he must stop you. Once this begins to manifest itself, it will be much too hard for him to turn back.

If He were to take all of us and whisk us out and take us on a journey all over the world to everywhere that He is doing something, you would not find one place that is in the fullness of where He is bringing us. In this generation, He just has nothing to work with.

So there is a whole lot more banking on you, and I think other places, than you realize. He is not just trying to stop us; he is trying to stop something from getting out of control.

Just now He said that this is some of the most invaluable training, absolute bar none, that you will get from Him: getting to know Him in the places He will send some of you, if He can get you to them. He prefers that you take this kind of training from Him so He can send you into the impossible places that people do not come back from if they have not had the training.
