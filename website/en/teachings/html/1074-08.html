1074-08 Private Worship Vol IV - 8

I wanted to precede this with tongues, but when I did not do it right away, He started in with it. It went something like this:

Do not be surprised with the things you are going to begin to see in the not too far future. Many things you have been waiting for are about to arrive at the door. So be strong and know that you have stood with some of the best, because Satan held not back in some areas of testing.

Rejoice and be glad that you have won in an area that will begin to come forward. You will see what it results in as this year goes by. Some who thought they would never be caught off guard will be caught off guard. Some who heard and left because of time spans, unbelief, and the many things the devil uses to destroy the Word will be surprised. But I will be able to move in their midst also, confirm, and make glad.

The major thing you will find coming forward is two things that I am: I am truth, and I am love. These two things you will see come forward very, very powerfully, says the Lord.

Hallelujah. I praise You and worship Your name and give You glory and honor. I exalt You and lift You up. With all my heart, I am glad.

In forty years of ministry, or thirty-nine and a half, and testimony close to fifty years, when you receive something new after this long, you should get excited. After a season of ministering on a foundation for forty-something years and knowing Him close to fifty, to be shown something new is quite an occasion.

We have been contending for the faith once delivered to the saints. As you know, we prayed enough in the Holy Ghost to receive the mysteries, to know what has been made available to the church, and to know what God can do in a town like this. We were motivated by different visitations from time to time to contend for this faith.

Years ago, I remember the shower I jumped into. I was getting ready to go do a meeting, and I was showering and worshiping God a little. All of a sudden I saw a belt around the earth. It was a vision. People were trying to get through to God, but they could not get past that belt.

In our case, way back then, He was letting me know He wanted revival. He said, "I want one."

I thought, "That is the principality of the air and the powers that are stopping us." I would see one person pray and pray, and pretty soon he would get his hand through. He would come back, and God would use his hand to heal people. Some stuck their mind through it and came back as teachers.

I said, "That is the realm of principalities and powers of the air that they are getting past in the vision."

He said, "No, it is not. That is your flesh."

Of course the devil uses it.

Then somebody broke through. I did not know what they broke through with, but when they broke through, they glowed. They came back with the glory that was on the other side of the dark side. Their whole person glowed with it.

I thought, "What is this fullness that we can come back with?" When they came back, they had a revival and the city caught on fire. Flames would shoot over to another city and kindle it there. Then another city would explode, and it spread all over.

Sometimes the things that keep me going are the many visions He has given me. I have used them enough, but I do not care. That is why He gave them to me.

This little child represented all little children with his condition: twisted up, sometimes maybe blind, unable to talk or walk, too little to blame them for their faith.

Look through Scripture. You have to justify this: Jesus was moved with compassion and healed all of them. In the first churches, the apostles were trained by Jesus Himself, so they all got healed when they came in.

You cannot blame it on the child. You cannot blame it on the Bible; the Bible says it is done. How do you explain to the little child, "I am sorry. God loves you. It just happens"?

Am I putting you under condemnation? No. You would have to take condemnation. I am not giving it. But how do you tell a little child, "It just happens"?

Let me try to find a place where I could bring him where Jesus is ministering. Where? I could get a plane or something. Where could I take this child that I know I can bring him home whole?

I do not live under condemnation. But if I found somebody like that, where you bring children to his meetings and they all leave whole, maybe he emptied a children's hospital the other day or something, I tell you what: if it were my child, I do not care what it cost. I would sell my truck and my house. I would find that person and bring my child.

Do you remember the testimony I gave two or three weeks ago? Since I saw you last, I took another hit. I am not going to talk about it today, except to say that his intent was to kill me again.

Coming up to this door, while in Brazil, I had a visitation on the tenth floor of a hotel room by about fifteen devils. Before that, I had heard devils speak out loud. They had been singing, trying to get me into the sensationalism move of gold dust and that kind of thing. I beat that, but I had never seen a fallen angel.

This time they came into the room. How would you like to wake up to someone sitting in a chair staring at you, about a foot taller than you are? My first response was to move my hand. I opened my eyes and saw him, and when I moved my hand, he said to somebody else, "Look, he moved his hand over his heart. Do you think he knows that you are going to kill him with a poisoned dart?"

That is not a very happy alarm clock.

By the time it was over, they had tried to put a curse on me and do many things like that, but we ran them off. They tried to hang around and chant, "I am going to kill you."

After I got back, I found out why they attacked at that particular time. We had just made it through one realm, praying in tongues and the mysteries. When praying in tongues could not give you what fasting does, praying in tongues put the key in your hand to the fasting door and unlocked it. Then fasting and praying in tongues gave you what you needed at that point. When praying in tongues could not give you what you needed to understand how to offer your soul on the altar of God's Word, it opened the meditation door. Down the line, each piece of equipment came under the foundation because we are believing for revival.

Finally I got to the door of love, where enough love poured out on me to make me dangerous. If He had left that level of love on me when I did not have the mortification I needed, I would have gone wrong with it. So He pulled back on that, and I finished that realm.

When I walked out the other side, those devils were supposed to stop me from looking through the next door for the next part of the revival we are heading for. But they failed. The door opened, and I looked down a long road. At the other end was a great master bank safe, open about a foot and a half, and light was spreading out everywhere.

I said, "What is this?"

I knew. He said, "That is what you are looking for. That is the fullness of My love."

I already knew some things about love from the last round. Perfect love casts out fear. Love never fails. If it is the love of God, your love for God will love your brother too. If you do not love people, you do not love Him.

Is this revival? Is this what brings a revival that has duration?

Yes, when the other mysteries, understandings, and foundations have also been put under you.

God is love. Love is one of the ten attributes that make up His one essence. He is love. When that love begins to manifest where you can feel it, what form does it take? Compassion.

What was Jesus moved by, and what did He do when He was moved by compassion? He healed them all.

I love it when Jesus said:

The works that I do shall ye do also; and greater works than these shall ye do; because I go unto My Father.

The greater works are because He went to the Father. What was lost in the garden could now be restored. You have the power to minister a new nature to somebody, something He could not do before He went to the Father.

What would be the best way to get about a billion people to ask for that new nature? Jesus was moved by compassion and healed all.

When that second door opened, I was returned for one minute to the place where it is done. Have you ever seen a movie twice and known what was going to happen? If you have seen Walker, Texas Ranger, you know the bad guys are going down. You have no doubt because you know.

I was Walker, and I was walking to a children's hospital. You get it? I did not have to wonder what was going to happen. I knew.

I wanted to know how the fullness of His power worked. What gives you a will of confidence like that? Where does it come from? What joins itself to the Word and to you so that you leave a legacy behind in the Word?

It happened in the early church, and He healed them all. They brought them to Peter and the others. Are we different? Are we worth as much?

What if you did not have a Bible and you were looking for the letter from Paul that was read three months ago? You would go to the neighboring place and preach what you know and what you prayed out in the mysteries of the Spirit. It would start coming on you. You could say you were prophesying, or you were translating a mystery you had prayed into existence. But it would be the Word.

The devil cannot afford a plague of that to break loose in the church. You praying in tongues, everybody getting out of the foolishness, understanding mysteries, and beginning to walk in faith.

Gary had a vision of the second man Adam and God preparing Him. Mine was something like that, only different. I saw a chromosome connected with the atoms and things that made up a child. I wondered, "Is this Jesus?" Not exactly, but it was like the program for the girl or the boy being a boy.

There was one chromosome that glowed. I wondered, "What is that chromosome?" It would not be glowing if He did not want me to mark it, so I did.

He said, "That chromosome is the love of God that is born into your nature when you are born again. Just like you wonder where your glorified body is coming from, it is in the spiritual DNA of your new man. That is why it is a transformation."

It glowed. It was part of the new nature, all the attributes that He is. We have His nature. But I looked at it, and it belonged to you. God has His love, and He gave it to us. It is programmed into our new nature as part of His nature, but it belongs to us.

As this person grew, the capacity for love got bigger in some than in others. God would come and visit, and when He did, He would love them with the love He had and the love they had. Yet it was still separate love that could hold the love of God.

He was getting this over to me: if He only programmed you to say, "I love You," then it would not be fellowship. What makes it fellowship is that it was your choice to raise your hands, your choice to love Him.

I saw God's love come in and start filling that love. The love we had was filled up with His love. Then He turned around and used the faith we have in that love, which had been programmed by His love, and wiped the hospital out. Why? Because it was our faith. It was coming from us. He was using it, but it was in the fullness of His love because He came in. We let Him mature us in His love and walk in it. He took where we were and turned around on the disease, was moved with compassion, and started healing.

I am here to explain the interpretation. You cannot continue to pray in tongues steadily without provoking an attack against you, for the worst thing the enemy fears is your understanding of the Word of God. He fears that as much as the love that would bring this forward.

This is why Jude says to earnestly contend for the faith once delivered to the saints. There is no use saying that if you are not going to tell them how to contend, which he does:

But ye, beloved, building up yourselves on your most holy faith, praying in the Holy Ghost, Keep yourselves in the love of God...

You do not have to be like those who separate themselves, sensual, having not the Spirit. Build yourself up on your most holy faith above that mess, keeping yourself in the love of God.

It took me a lifetime to find out that I was praying myself into position for revival. But to understand a sustaining revival, love certainly had to be mixed in there. To understand a revival that does not destroy you, I know the part love plays.

For these many interesting things that you will begin to see, stand fast and hold on because they are within reach.

Once again, when I walked up to the impossible, which may hold the children of the state if it is a specialty hospital, you are reaching pretty far. But I know what is available.

Can you imagine the chaos in the hospital, with the mayors, mothers, fathers, and scientists? Can you imagine what they would do? Science could not disprove it. Medicine could not deny it. Has that happened? Yes. Are we reaching beyond a little here? Yes. But do not worry about it. We are on the trail.

I have waited for years to find out what I am getting to know now. Again, Jesus was moved by compassion and healed them all, and went on to give His life for us.

God so loved the world, the world being us, that He gave His only begotten Son, that whosoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life. You already have that. There is no human being who ever came into existence who is not somewhere right now.

One more time: I saw the love separated, not from the new nature, but for identification purposes, so I would know it is in there. It is a love that came from His nature, but it belongs to me as much as my new nature does, which I received from Him.

When I saw Him pouring in, and my love expanding to receive His, it reached the place where the love I walked in here with and the authority released Him to be moved by compassion and empty that hospital.

I cannot wait to continue in this. The last hit I took may have been the closest to death I came. We will talk about it someday. The only thing we need to know now is that the enemy is more nervous than ever before, and we are closer.

Studying history again, I am starting to understand some of the things that happened to those men. The devil does not sit there while you go by him. He likes to be close to fire.

What did Jesus say? The sower sows the Word, and Satan comes immediately to steal the Word. You are not going to go out and sow the Word in the midst of miracles and go unnoticed.

You may say I sound like I have been through scary things. I am less afraid of anything than I have ever been in my whole life. Who cares about an experience when you do not have fear?

Satan comes immediately to steal the Word. He has to be nervous about the fullness of this. If you do not have any fear, we need a lot of theology on the next part. He said love never fails. No fear, no failure.

What does love do to your faith? You would think love was the power gift.
