Take these things to heart, for times are changing and seasons are changing. Know that many things are going to come. These things are going to come whether many people do what they are supposed to or not. So hear what the Lord would say and move forward. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready. You will be ready.

Keep in mind, Paul could take great pleasure in the presence of God and worship, whether he was in the ocean, getting a beating, imprisoned in a sewer, or having his feet broken. It must be a powerful key that switches you over to grace. It activates what He has done about it.

The road the enemy finds most interesting into you is to stop you from doing the daily things that free Me to move in My plan for you day after day. Anyone who stays in My presence for lengths of time and abides in Me is freeing Me to get closer to them, closer to the fullness of their call and heart, and do things I cannot do on a normal basis.

Walking in more of what I have for you does not necessarily depend on your title. It depends on where you want to walk and what you want to receive that I have for you. Did I not say to all that these signs would follow them that believe? I did not name certain persons. I said, "Them that believe."

My fellowship with you is in great delight when you come to Me walking in My truth and I fellowship with you around that truth. I am able to do many things I desire to do, and you will receive many things you desired to receive. When I fellowship with you and it fills you with joy, I rejoice with you, says the Lord.

These places you are moving into, I have prepared. I prepared them by the power of My Spirit. To say long waits were on purpose--long waits were because it was necessary. If I have My way, says the Lord, you will move into a place that has been guaranteed. You will see all that I told you. Just stay in. Stay in the Spirit. Pray in the Holy Spirit and worship Me. Worship Me.

Something is getting closer. I did not give you that word endurance for nothing. So be strong. You will see much in these last days, says the Lord.

Do not be alarmed, but rather rejoice when change seems to be coming, because this change that is coming is good. You will see that not only will much good come out of it, but things that have not been understood before will be understood.

Rejoice and know that in My joy and in My presence everything will be increasing. As this love continues to increase, you will understand things you have not been able to understand in the past. You will know why this could have happened, and this could have happened, because of the love of God that was attained to by these different seasons.

So be strong. Hold Me up and know that love never fails.

The reason it has been so hard to bring you to this place is because so few have been there to paint any kind of picture you can see. Therefore trust Me when I say that beyond anything you have believed yet, I am waiting for you with all I have promised for the days to come.

How many of you will understand what your worship does to your soul? When I know you are coming before Me to draw closer to Me, it pleases Me, says the Lord. For I seek such to worship Me.

As you begin to flow in all that I have, My Spirit, even wisdom, you will notice there is a closer point between the natural and the spiritual than most walk in. From My fullness, you can bring My power over to that side and bring that side into My power.

Every time My church during their dispensation would get serious with Me, serious about prayer and fasting, My love and that walk in it would spring up time after time, because this is who and what I am, says the Lord. They will not travel down the path I have laid for them supernaturally without running into My love and My desire to bring them forward into the fullness of it.

In that there is no fear, and in that you will never fail.

I said, "Covet earnestly the best gifts, yet I show unto you a more excellent way." Though I speak with tongues of men and angels and have not love--what He was saying is this: the more excellent way is to go after the love of God with the same tenacity that you had to go after the workings of miracles that would have exalted you in the body of Christ.

If you go after the love of God with the same tenacity that we would go after that kind of power, that is the more excellent way. When you go after the love of God with tenacity and refuse to back down, then everything you need to minister to and to help, eventually you will be equipped with.

I have much to teach you about the ways of entering into My rest. You will be amazed how it will strengthen you, strengthen you, strengthen you. This rest is based on My fellowship with you and the Word of God alive in you.

Not only can I give you a job; I can see to it that you receive one you love, not just for the money. You are wondering how I would do that. If it took Me a season, I can do it because I can change this and that and let it head in your direction.

The thing is, do you spend time with Me? What do you want? All things are possible to him that believes that I am what I said, and that you are what I said you are.

In fellowship with Me, you will find it grows richer and richer as you approach Me walking in My love and My manifestations, because this is what I am. When you see the revivals come into manifestation in these last days, you will understand why they were not accessed before, and that the one ingredient that is a power gift more than anybody knows is the manifestation of My love when it is flowing in its fullness, says the Lord.

I can tell you over and over how much I love you, but when you begin to practice returning that love, you will get the power that is in it. By returning that love, I mean your heart in fellowship with Mine. He loves such that worship Him in spirit and in truth.

He waited four thousand years and waited the dispensations out for the first Man to appear after Adam who had a nature like His and could give Him the fellowship He wanted. That was Jesus, to worship Him in spirit and in truth. This is what He loves.

When you worship and pray in the Spirit, you are setting yourself a direction. You cannot worship and pray in the Spirit without setting yourself a direction. When you do these things, you welcome Me to come in My strength and set your direction.

When you worship Me, says the Lord, as My sons and My daughters, you put yourself in a place with Me. You recognize Me as your Father, and I recognize you as My sons and daughters.

Do you think I do not take great pleasure in answering your prayers and giving you the things I have to give you? It is the enemy who is accusing you. I have always been here, and I have always enjoyed giving you the things you love in the relationship and fellowship I have with you.

You are My sons and daughters. I do not hold back from you, nor do I turn My back on any need you have. I am here. So do not be programmed by the enemy. Be programmed by Me, by My Word, and by what I have promised you.
