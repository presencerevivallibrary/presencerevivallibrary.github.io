625-02 The Fullness That Makes Judgment and Miracles Present Again

Acts, the fifth chapter, and then we will probably go back or forward to 1 Corinthians, the third chapter.

Thank You, Jesus.

I have probably seven pages written already on Ananias and Sapphira. That was not what it was really about. It was really about the early church, but it has turned out to be about them.

Then I have four pages, maybe seven now, on the love of God, "In My Father's house are many mansions," and all about the two creations we can know. So I am doing two letters at a time. Sometimes I will do three, plus meditate.

You know how you work. Sometimes you get stuck somewhere, and you have to totally shift all your thinking for a minute to clear it. You are on the wrong path. The way you can do that is if you have an alternative letter going. Or any kind of teaching. You get stuck on something, then you get onto the other teaching, straighten something up on it, and then it clears you. You go back to the other one, and it comes online.

I used to, if I was stuck on a letter, right after I preached I would grab my letter and read it. A lot of times while I was still under the anointing, I could get past where I got stuck.

"Do you have writer's block?"

No. I have typing block.

We got on this, and I was asking God, "I would like to go on with this while I have a chance."

It seems people get stuck on Ananias and Sapphira. If you are a verse-by-verse teacher, it is a nightmare. If you are a subject teacher, it is a subject to be avoided.

If you are a greedy preacher, usually that verse is only used when you are jumping out thinking you are some kind of divine appointment on this earth and can get by with things because you are special.

Ananias and Sapphira were not preachers. Good land. When judgment falls on a poor little lamb like that, what hope do we have?

Preachers begin to think they are a divine appointment, that God put them on this earth for you to give to so you can get rich too. A lot of them seem to think that way. At times they use this verse, since they believe they have an exemption from it, to tell a bunch of little lambs somewhere that the only time God ever killed anybody with a new nature was over an offering.

Of course, they do not go much deeper than that, and you cannot blame them.

But the problem with this verse is that the circumstances had to be different than most of our church age. If God respected no person and went down the line judging equally, they would need trucks to haul the Ananiases and Sapphiras away, not just young men.

So I knew from praying mysteries and understanding the mystery in this book and the one in that book that complicated mysteries start coming together to give you complicated answers. You are not going to assimilate book after book without them beginning to answer what is in each other.

We have to go back and make another pass on meditation, imagery, and delivery, and meditation and assimilation. I think we need to take about three days just to catch you up with all the new stuff we are employing.

Hallelujah, Jesus.

Have you ever wondered why you went to bed at night with a problem, not marriage counseling or anything, but you were stuck not understanding something, and the next morning the moment you woke up, you said, "Oh, I know what I will do with that"?

Why? Because when you sleep, a lot of times your spirit will prioritize. Your spirit will prioritize everything.

Where were you when Kennedy was assassinated? I was on a destroyer in Yokosuka, Japan. Where were you? Sixth grade? All right, a more mature group over here.

How come you can remember that and cannot remember what you were doing three weeks ago on Thursday at five, six, or seven? Something organized it and prioritized it.

This is the kind of rest you run into when He says:

This is the rest, this is the refreshing...

Of course, that was a prophecy fulfilled on the day of Pentecost:

For with stammering lips and another tongue will He speak to this people...

He was talking about the new nature we would receive and entering into His rest. You cannot pray in tongues without Him continually doing the same thing. He will develop you and mature you toward that rest, where the same things begin to happen. There will be such peace.

When you go to sleep, during that time of sleep, your spirit prioritizes. Let us say you have been reading 1 John about a hundred times. After a while, your heart starts figuring out that this is the best thing for you, or else you would not be giving so much attention to it. So it will go in and start prioritizing and getting rid of everything in you that is against it.

And you know what 1 John is about: the love of God.

How would you like it if, while you sleep, it worked on your ugly problems?

There is a lot to it.

These mysteries you get into, every time something else opens up, it agrees with three or four more until finally you start understanding mysteries like Ananias and Sapphira, and as much as possible, what happened there that day.

Let us read the account of their death:

But a certain man named Ananias, with Sapphira his wife, sold a possession,

And kept back part of the price, his wife also being privy to it, and brought a certain part, and laid it at the apostles' feet.

But Peter said, Ananias, why hath Satan filled thine heart to lie to the Holy Ghost,

and to keep back part of the price of the land?

Whiles it remained, was it not thine own?

and after it was sold, was it not in thine own power?

why hast thou conceived this thing in thine heart?

thou hast not lied unto men, but unto God.

Good thing you are not under law anymore with tithing and stuff. Some of you missed two or three pennies on your tithe. You rounded it out, and you could die over that.

And Ananias hearing these words fell down, and gave up the ghost:

and great fear came on all them that heard these things.

I cannot say I could blame them if I had been in church that day.

The young men arose, wound him up, wrapped him in some kind of sheet or blanket, carried him out, and buried him. About three hours later, his wife, not knowing what was done, came in.

Peter answered her, "Tell me whether you sold the land for so much."

She said, "Yea, for so much."

Then Peter said:

How is it that ye have agreed together to tempt the Spirit of the Lord?

behold, the feet of them which have buried thy husband are at the door,

and shall carry thee out.

Then she fell down straightway at his feet and yielded up the ghost. The young men came in, found her dead, carried her forth, and buried her with her husband.

And great fear came upon all the church, and upon as many as heard these things.

Then:

And by the hands of the apostles were many signs and wonders wrought among the people;

and they were all with one accord in Solomon's porch.

And of the rest durst no man join himself to them:

but the people magnified them.

This core group had people walking on the outside who got into fear. They did not want to join themselves to that group. Obviously, this was the group selling off their possessions, spending day and night in the temple as much as possible, sitting at the feet of the twelve apostles, ministering, breaking bread from door to door.

Multitudes were being added to the church, yet there was a group that would not add themselves to them, though they magnified them. They knew what was happening was of God.

Then the early church reached a place where the power, I do not know if it has ever been paralleled again. If it has, I have not really heard.

But our intention is to see these things:

And believers were the more added to the Lord, multitudes both of men and women.

Insomuch that they brought forth the sick into the streets, and laid them on beds and couches,

that at the least the shadow of Peter passing by might overshadow some of them.

If you are wondering who these were, they were laying along the road where Peter came in. I made a comment before that I think I understand this. Peter probably would not have wanted to stop and lay hands on some of them, being as they were probably Gentiles, at least some of them.

Even on the day of Pentecost, the three thousand filled with the Holy Spirit were devout Jews who came from every nation under heaven. This was pretty much a Jewish population he was ministering to.

If the revival reached far enough and they put people on the street, at least Peter would not have to touch them. His shadow could overshadow them. Of course, you know what God would have done if He got the chance.

But it did not stop there:

There came also a multitude out of the cities round about unto Jerusalem,

bringing sick folks, and them which were vexed with unclean spirits:

and they were healed every one.

How many? Come on, you know.

Oklahoma City comes in, all the sick from Oklahoma City and Houston and Dallas. Now we are including all the demon possessed and all the sick. Can you imagine what was in the sick out there? People with missing parts, arms, legs, blind eyes.

And the possessed? I bet they had to have some of them chained while they were waiting. Gadarenes. I bet they had them chained.

Here you are walking down the street, and it clears the street. Then they start bringing the multitudes in. It says they were healed, every single one.

If you want to play "no respecter of persons," then why do we not believe that hell was hotter then? Was He favoring those people because hell was hotter, so He decided to get in and salvage them, but in our generation hell is not as hot, so He will just let us go to hell with no signs and wonders to look at?

Why did He do that? Do you not know that when you find these keys, it is the first big, fat step. Are you doing them?

You have to admit there were unusual circumstances going on that day.

They had already been in two separate great big outpourings. One was on the day of Pentecost, when devout men from the cities round about were there, and Peter preached that noble message right in their midst. Three thousand Jews were saved and filled with the Holy Spirit.

In that revival, nobody told them to do anything. It was such an outpouring, such a time, that they took it on themselves to go and give themselves totally to God.

Gary has been etching away at those messages on provision, and this is provision and this is His. He does not teach it like a tithe. In the tithe, if you are going to give God a million dollars, look how much you have to make just to give Him that. You get this much; poor God gets this much.

The way Gary broke it down for us is that you figure out how much you need to live on. That is your provision. Then everything else that comes in, you give to God.

I heard him in action. Tim asked me to go over to Gary's house so he could de-illiterate me on computers, at least give me an idea of what they are capable of. I watched him turn it on, so I knew I could do that.

Gary had talked about a vision of a stick of gum, and he was mailing it to people. This was years ago. On that stick of gum, they can hold the memory of an entire computer. What he did not know was that it looked like a stick of gum, but he was mailing those out and sending the Word all over the nation. He saw that before they came out.

If you live on seventy or one hundred thousand a year, and then three million comes in over that, guess who gets the three million? We are talking profit, what your business makes and all that.

"What do you do about things outside of that? What if you need a new car and your budget does not cover it?"

Gary says, "You go to the Father and ask Him, and He gives it to you."

If you need an increase, you bring that before God and pray, and then you increase whatever amount you think you need to live on the next year. That is how he lives.

I was there when they got a phone call because somebody wanted to buy something real bad. They were on that line of being able to understand everything, and Gary said, "No, we cannot get that right now." He explained, "That is not our money. We cannot spend somebody else's money."

I remember when God gave him his car. It probably was not in your budget, was it, Sue? I have heard that laugh before. It was not in your budget, and you got a big long car with a big engine, did you not? I thought so. I rode in it.

Hallelujah.

Nobody told those people in Acts to do this. God had poured out in the twelve apostles. They sold all their possessions and had everything in common. God was using the whole overall bankroll to feed the people and do the things necessary so they could stay at the apostles' feet.

The first thing they did even before the outpouring, because Judas was gone, was cast lots for Matthias. They had the twelve who slept and ate with Jesus and heard every parable. That was critical.

Now we have these three thousand people, and not long after that, five thousand more added after the lame man healed from birth, over forty years of age. He confirmed the same thing again: everything in common.

It was not until Ananias and Sapphira died that fear gripped a certain group. They thought, Oh boy. But they did magnify and glorify what they were doing.

These people sold out. They were the ones that, when Paul was breathing threats and slaughter and going into their houses and hailing them to prison, dispersed to every city while only the twelve stayed. Do not tell me they had not heard every parable, seen every miracle, and been in every move. They had already sold out. Nothing belonged to them. They were released to the nations round about just like that.

Why did that happen to Ananias and Sapphira? You have to admit, with these miracles He is talking about here, we have not seen anything even remotely like it.

We have seen enough miracles to know they exist. Some people in the land have really strong miracle ministries, but the hosts of the sick are generally not touched much. If you take percentages, almost no percentage of the sick who turn out end up getting healed.

We thank God for what we get, because it is not staying the same. This city is ours, and we are going to take possession. We have to find this stuff out.

Gary gets a piece. I get a piece. Tim gets a piece. We keep bringing it together. We are walking down this path, and we will not walk down another one.

The early church established under the twelve, especially those established under Jesus, came to an incredible fullness. The only reason they came to a fullness like that is that they were taught how to, and they did it.

Those men established the church and set a precedent. That is not the only generation He wants to do that. It is not the only time He wanted to use a tool to save cities and nations.

Most people will attribute it to the special favor the Jews have. You hear preachers going to the Scriptures now to pray for the peace of Jerusalem and to be on Israel's side. Israel is like a big clock in prophecy. You can watch her and find out about where we are at.

But if you think for one minute that He loves a Jew more than He loves you, you have made a mistake. He loves people.

We are going to see all that again. You can join in, fight, or go somewhere and watch us. You can dare not add yourself to our group if you do not want to.

Hallelujah.

I hate to keep asking, "Is anybody having as good a time as I am?" because, as amazing as it may seem, some of you do not look like you are.

You do not know my problems, and you do not know how big He is.

I found out recently God did not like the way I thought. I thought in millions. I do not think that way now. Why would I think millions when the earth has got more than that? If it only took a dollar to get everybody saved, I would still need billions. Millions will not even work for breakfast.

The point I have been working on is that we have not really seen a time after this like this one. But when it is restored, two things will come back that were here.

One is judgments that take place presently. Two is miracles that take place presently.

Is instantaneous judgment going to take place? Who will He use like that? How about apostles who are not after your money?

The primary call of the apostle is doctrine. It is truth. When the church goes astray, this is why they get beat up so regularly. The whole church is going down a highway of false teaching, and one old apostle jumps out on the road and says, "No, you do not." They just trample him.

Unfortunately, to the full mantle of that anointing belongs the place where instantaneous judgment takes place.

If there had been one in town, that doctrine that went from Tulsa and is beginning to spread now would not have made it that far. But because there are no mantles in town that He can use like that, in fact He can hardly use any anywhere I know of at this point.

The one time He tried to teach me a little bit on it, it scared me. I learned some of the qualifications that go with judgment. One is that mercy rejoices against judgment.

Any mighty apostles I have seen trying to judge people, it was more personal: "I will teach you." I have seen deacon wars where they cursed each other. Finally they would add more to the end: "I curse you in the name of Jesus." "Oh yeah, I curse you in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ." "Oh yeah, I curse you in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit."

I called them the deacon wars.

But the one time I knew He did it, I was not really operating under it. He came and got me and took me for a little ride. I was with Him when He did it, and I could tell He wanted something from me. All He wanted from me was to tell them the truth. When I told them the truth, they acted directly against God in the truth I told them, and the judgment took place after that.

It is not that I did not know it was going to. I did to a certain degree. But I also knew I was only along for the ride as a mouthpiece for truth, not to speak words of judgment per se.

The two people He had me bring a message to are with Him now. I cannot tell you one of them because it is too close to home, but I can tell you about the other one. He is pretty well known.

A long time ago after a meeting, I went to a somewhat-to-do house among them for fellowship. I had gone on a long fast and came off it. I found out how to overcome the flesh a little and the role flesh played.

The Lord only spoke one thing to me. He was trying to salvage this guy. He said, "Go tell him how to overcome the flesh now through fasting."

I did not know he was having an affair with an associate's wife. I did not know that.

I was over at this house, and I said, "God, make a way, because right now there is nowhere. They are all waiting for their turn to make a speech."

I went in to get my coat, and the guy was in the coat room. My chance.

I said, "Hey, I just got off a longer fast, and He taught me some things about the flesh. Did you know you came up in it, and He wanted me to tell you what He taught me?"

He jumped back like I branded him with a Montana branding iron.

"There is nothing wrong. There is nothing wrong."

I did not know he was flying out almost every weekend to rendezvous.

"There is nothing wrong. You want to help me? I owe eighty thousand by Monday."

It always comes around to money.

I shut up and stepped out backwards. When I left, he told everybody in there I was operating in witchcraft. He had to cover himself.

Four or five years later, somebody asked me if I would do a meeting under his flag. They were high up in the organization. I said, "You had better clear it."

She tried and said, "He will not let you."

I said, "I told you that. But I will take it a step further. I will go see what I can do."

I went. I had a witness with me. I was thinking about joining his organization. I was standing there, and the Lord said, "Nothing has changed."

I said, "I cannot join in."

Then He said to me, "Besides that, there is death working in his body."

I witnessed it with the person with me. I said, "There is death in his body."

I said, "God, do You want me to tell him?"

He said, "He will not listen to you anymore. He did years ago."

A couple of years later, the cancer showed up. He died not long after that. It was not because he said I was in witchcraft. I was not the only highway he was leaving behind that was destroying people.

Would you rather see that happen for his sake and theirs?

All we have to do is not stop what God is establishing here in these last days.
