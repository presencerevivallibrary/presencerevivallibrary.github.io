620-02 The Glorified Body - 2

Hallelujah. Is it not something? After thirty years, the lamp of the righteous burns brighter and brighter. After thirty years, it gets better and better.

"My whole world is falling apart."

Not the one He gave you. If you are holding on to that one too tightly, you may go down with it.

"Everybody is forsaking me."

Not Him.

"You just do not know the problem nobody can solve."

I beg to differ with you. I know Someone in whom nothing is impossible. Nothing.

In fact, there is only one person who has the power to defeat you. It is not the devil. It is not your flesh. It is not God. It is you. You are the only one who can defeat yourself. Nothing else has the power.

"God is stronger than me."

He is stronger than you if you are in agreement with Him. But if you and your heart and your actions decide not to believe Him, you render Him absolutely powerless.

Thank God there is only one person who can stop me. That is me.

I do not mind telling you that about the last two months, revelation on the righteousness of God started crystallizing enough that I began receiving from that realm. Many statements in our prayer and power conference came from that realm.

When I talk about His righteousness, I do not mean yours. I mean His, the kind that has always been. I still do not know that much about it, but I know enough to talk about it.

Those statements that came from another realm answered many questions, the kind that keep taking you farther down the born-again trail to the place where He says, "Nothing shall be impossible to you."

This righteousness I glimpsed, the only kind of glimpse God can give you, you do not know how long it lasts. A millisecond, it does not matter. Once the glimpse comes, understanding begins.

The things I have already said out of that realm, mostly just statements, have done so much for my receiving and my faith that I can hardly stand it.

Tim made a statement: "There is one thing God cannot do. He cannot override the will of a person who does not want to be overridden."

I said, "That is right. If He could, there would not be anybody in hell."

Do you not love little statements like that?

To understand that righteous place in God's attributes, I had to understand other facets of His attributes. I had to understand something of His love, which I started experiencing a couple of years ago. Then that love let me have a look at my Father, and I started experiencing my Father like I never had.

Then came the statements: "Do not accuse Me. Think it not strange concerning the fiery trials which are to try you. Do not accuse Me through these things."

Several things came online, and when I understood them, then came this glimpse of His righteousness. For some reason it did more for my receiving than any single thing in a long time.

I do not know why exactly, except that I saw His righteousness sovereignly go into the future. The immutability of His person means He cannot change, nor can He be changed, because nothing has the power to change Him. In His sovereignty, He is sovereignly omnipotent. There is no say-so, no operation, no power higher than what He has said. The way it is, is how He said it is, and He is sovereign.

I found out He is sovereignly immutable and immutably sovereign. Just to look at His love stunned me. He is sovereignly love, and He loves sovereignly. He is sovereignly righteous and righteously sovereign. He is sovereignly love, and He loves sovereignly. He loves righteously and is righteously loved.

All His attributes are each other. Each facet of Him explains the part that can never change.

James says there is no shadow of turning in Him. The sundial marks time, but in Him there is no shadow of time's turning. Billions of days from now, He will have the same righteousness. He cannot change.

In that righteousness, He is impregnable. In Him is no darkness at all. No matter what you do, you cannot put the slightest indentation in His righteousness, not even a little impression or the beginning of disturbance or entry, because He cannot change.

Yet when you were cut off, He found a way to come after you.

He found a way to come after you. It took four thousand years for the cross to be erected. Up until that time He had to find a way to talk to you.

"God, I need to talk to You."

"I cannot talk to you. I am impregnable. There is no darkness in Me at all. Wait a minute. I will take your death penalty, lay it on this animal, and kill him in your place. It will charge your death to the cross. Now, what is it that you want?"

What kind of God, who created the molecular structure that holds together the chair you are sitting on, would slaughter animals by the thousands just to talk to you?

He has the blueprint for the atom. There is even finer matter than atoms, something so small it can pass through the earth and not touch anything because matter is like giant caverns to it. He blueprinted it, created it, and created all the forces that hold it together. He is so organized that if He gave you an ounce of wisdom, you could make billions from it.

What kind of God, who can create all that, would kill animals by the thousands just to talk to you? Is that disorganization? What kind of bloody mess is that?

Even worse, the salvation of the entire world lay in a cradle in an obscure village called Bethlehem. Every nation was populated, and the salvation of the whole world hinged on one baby.

What kind of business plan is that? I would have put one in every home. Who would plan something that difficult, with that kind of handicap?

I can tell you who and why. There was no Plan B. Ten billion equations later, in the righteousness that He is, there was no other way to come after you.

Could you help Him out a little by getting somebody saved? He overcame all that difficulty just to walk up to you and say, "Hi." He overcame all of that to say, "You are My son. You are My daughter."

He could not salvage the angel population, but He salvaged you.

Just in case you are interested in not getting caught up in religion, unbelief, and insults against Him, He has made all that He is available to you. He did not entrust you to one of the mighty archangels, Gabriel or Michael. They are sent with their armies as ministering spirits for those who shall be heirs of salvation.

But you are family, and you need to be taught. The devil is a destroyer and a murderer. His seat was with Hitler, Stalin, the king of Tyre, and the king of Babylon. He tried to destroy the world.

That is all right, because the third Person of the Godhead came to live in you and with you, in case you happen to stumble on, "All things are possible to him that believeth."

"I am illiterate. I can barely pray. My education is only in the sixth grade. What can I possibly do?"

Everything.

"I do not know how to pray."

That is all right. The Holy Spirit says, "Step aside, boy. How about Me writing the prayer for you so it can be answered?"

Besides this, I have Somebody inside me who prays hour after hour in tongues to make sure I understand what is in here, the purpose of my life, and what my calling is.

We are talking about God's right standing. We are talking about His righteousness. We are talking about a plan of salvation with no Plan B, because it could be done no other way. But we are also talking about a Father who has sworn to you that all things are possible to him who believes.

For that little lesson on righteousness, we need to jump up, lift our hands, and thank Him for the miracles.

Jesus. Jesus. Jesus.

Tulsa is My house, and I want it.

Programs are supposed to keep up with the move of the Spirit, not replace it.

Worship. Fellowship.

Yes, for surely things shall be seen that have not been seen in many, many centuries. So hear. See. Believe. And for you who have ears to hear, hear.

For it shall be line upon line as My foundation is in place and I build thereupon. He that has ears to hear, indeed even more shall be given.

As these times come and go, the manifestation of My glory shall begin small here and begin there, in many isolated places. But each of these shall burn as fire until soon the whole earth shall be raging in the knowledge and the glory of your Lord, so much so as the water covers the sea.

In the last days I shall raise a standard, and the whole world shall see and know that I am Lord. In that day I will return, and many of you shall see Me face to face.

So be strong and of good courage. Have no fear, nor take the cares of this life. Be bold and move forward, for what is too hard for him who believes? Is there anything too hard for Me?

Even in your own city you shall be amazed by what you see. Continue on the path you are on, and there will be a strong fire raging in this place that will set the precedence for many places.

So be faithful, for things are about to come that you have not seen before, saith the Lord.

Boy, can I believe that. Can I believe that.

Turn to Genesis 1. Let me pass in review and work on one point to clear it up.

Father, we thank You for these next few moments in Your Word. You said we can know the truth, and truth will make us free. That whole thing started when You gave us a new nature. As You open truth after truth, You were right when You said faith comes by hearing, and hearing by Your Word. I have never had so much confidence in You. I thank You for making me Your son. I thank You for laying the whole world out before us. With all my heart, I thank You and glorify You for the Holy Spirit as Teacher, and for the supernatural way He releases You to move on our understanding. For this, we give You praise, honor, and glory.

The thing that got me thinking about how God teaches me is when I pray a mystery out in tongues. Sometimes the mystery is something everybody else is running down a wide path about, and you are swept up into believing it like everybody else because that is how almost everybody teaches it.

A theologian asked me, "When you get something that goes against the tide, how does God snatch you up and turn you?"

I said, "Usually He drops a revelation in my spirit that goes every direction at once and gives me an understanding of it. If it is a hard thing to understand, sometimes He materializes a vision in my spirit."

Once you become accustomed to it, you can tell the difference. It is not a vision out there. It materializes in your spirit. I can be looking at you and looking through that vision at the same time. It also activates sometimes in words of knowledge. I will see something wrong in somebody's body, and it will materialize for a moment like that.

But He also uses it in revelation knowledge. That is what happened in Genesis when I saw the creation of Adam and understood that the whole human race, including Eve, came through one single strand of DNA.

When Genesis says God formed man's body of the dust of the earth, what words would they use? To form something is to form something out of what already exists. Our body was made out of the dust of the earth.

I used to preach that God took mud, patted it into the shape of a body, and blew His Spirit into it. I had no other way to understand it until a couple of months ago, when God arrested me in the middle of meditation.

We were studying whether children are born spiritually alive. As I came to understand the creation of all of it, I was going down the same broad path everybody goes down. Suddenly the vision came.

I saw the kingdom of God, the realm of the Spirit, and the kingdom of the natural. Everything natural, all the animals, whales, giraffes, comes from a single strand of DNA. So do our bodies.

Then I saw the difference. From the kingdom of God came a strand of DNA. The natural strand of DNA came, and suddenly they merged and wrapped around each other. The kingdom of God united itself with us, the natural world, in one strand of DNA.

So here we are: the God-man. We belong to the realm of the natural, yet we have a spirit that came from a strand of spiritual DNA that wrapped around the natural strand and merged. Adam was the result.

Genesis 1:27 says:

So God created man in his own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them.

Genesis 2:7 says:

And the Lord God formed man of the dust of the ground...

It was only in the last few decades that we understood DNA, the building blocks of life, and how something is formed. Every cell, even hair and saliva, carries the blueprint for the whole body. Every cell carries the information for the next layer and the next until the whole body is created.

When Scripture says God formed man, did we know He was using a strand of natural and spiritual DNA, feeding everything into it, so it itself formed the human?

Genesis 2:7 says He formed man of the dust of the ground, then breathed into his nostrils the breath of life, and the man became alive and aware, conscious of his surroundings.

At the end of Genesis 2, God took Eve out of Adam. I wonder how long Adam slept. Genesis 2:21-22 says:

And the Lord God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept... And the rib, which the Lord God had taken from man, made he a woman...

The real word there is builded, much like formed. When I heard people try to break these verses down, one friend preached that when God first created Adam, He also created the woman's spirit, and her spirit occupied Adam's spirit. So God took the rib to form the body and put her spirit into it.

But in reality, Eve existed in Adam's DNA. The reason God needed a rib from the man was to get the DNA sequence to make the woman. He did not create her out of nothing. He built her from something that already existed.

There is only one chromosome difference between a man and a woman. The Latin word for chromosome is rib. One chromosome different. One rib different.

All my life I wondered why men have breasts. They are not good for anything, and they are in the way. Yet our bodies are that close. They can fill a woman with male hormones and she starts growing a beard. Our bodies are that close.

God caused a deep sleep to fall on Adam and took his rib. He took the sequence of DNA, the chromosome, the one variation between man and woman. When I heard that the Latin word is rib, I thought, "We are cooking."

The whole human race came through one strand of DNA.

Go to Luke 1. Let us read about this birth.

Luke 1:30-35 says:

And the angel said unto her, Fear not, Mary: for thou hast found favour with God. And, behold, thou shalt conceive in thy womb, and bring forth a son, and shalt call his name JESUS. He shall be great, and shall be called the Son of the Highest... Then said Mary unto the angel, How shall this be, seeing I know not a man? And the angel answered and said unto her, The Holy Ghost shall come upon thee, and the power of the Highest shall overshadow thee: Therefore also that holy thing which shall be born of thee shall be called the Son of God.

What happened here?

Hebrews 2:14 says:

Forasmuch then as the children are partakers of flesh and blood, he also himself likewise took part of the same...

Underline the word part. He took part of the same so that through death He might destroy him who had the power of death, that is, the devil, and deliver those who through fear of death were all their lifetime subject to bondage.

Verse 16 says:

For verily he took not on him the nature of angels; but he took on him the seed of Abraham.

Then verse 17 says He was made like His brethren in all things, so He could be a merciful and faithful High Priest, and verse 18 says:

For in that he himself hath suffered being tempted, he is able to succour them that are tempted.

That holy thing born of Mary was conceived of God. What part of Him came from us? He did not take on the nature of angels, but the seed of Abraham.

He was tempted in all points like us. If He was conceived of the Holy Spirit, what part of Him was like us? Did He get His body from God, or did He get it from Mary? Who did He get His spirit from?

This is interesting to meditate on until we get there.

Meanwhile, you can think about those people who jumped out of the grave too. Did you have a good time?
