1071 Bonus Worship Prophecies 1 Private Worship Bonus Prophecies 1

When you are through a season of fasting, major much on worship and praying in tongues. For indeed I want to lay My plan out in every one of your personal lives.

The thing I am bringing, you will call a new thing. It will not be a new thing to Me; it will be a new thing to you. Worship much. Come to know Me in My presence. Find out what My plan is. For My plan is shifting as major as the next layer of foundation coming upon the temple that I am building.

So be strong and know it will be a little different than what you are used to. But glorify Me, for things do not change. How will there be a return and a revival?

Be exceedingly glad, for many of you will learn the true operation of the deepest kinds of intercession in this year. Remember these words: as My outpourings begin, many of you will be moved into that place that allows Me to come forward and pour out Myself in ways you have not seen. Many of your loved ones will be part of this harvest. So rejoice and be exceedingly glad.

Still others will find a place in faith they have never walked in before, a place where you will not take no for an answer. In that place there is no disease, no manifestation, and nothing I have prepared in glory for you that can be withheld. For many will find in the secret place of the Most High that there is a faith that will not and cannot be denied.

So continue in your fellowship with Me, for that is where these things are found.

I heard what He said in my spirit, but I do not know how to explain it. It is like last year in that prophecy about the love of God, when God said, "I cannot love Myself in you for you."

In other words, the love He has for you, and the love you have developing in your new nature, has to develop in you and be part of you in order for you to give it back. "I cannot love Myself in you for you, for the love you give back to Me must come from you."

You might as well know that worship pulls on reservoirs of that kind of love, the kind you turn around and hand back to Him.

But I heard something about truth there. It seemed like He wanted to slip something in before I began to teach on it. What He slipped in had something to do with all things being possible. In other words, all limits are taken off what you can believe for. That is what He said.

To worship Him in spirit and in truth has to do with developing the capacity in your nature to receive and operate in truth. To worship Him in spirit and in truth is the ability to give that part of you that can discern and understand truth over to Him so thoroughly that your worship of Him in that would cause all things to become possible to you. You would go to that place where you worship Him in the truth He represents, in the truth He is, in the truth of what He said, and in the truth of what He will do.

For speaking of this realm, says the Lord, I have yet many things to say, even as I have already said many things. As My grace begins to come and impart many of you, because it will be such uncommon ground, many of you will begin to walk in this only to find out that the more you fellowship with Me, the more of My presence and My wisdom I make available to you through the divine flow of this fellowship.

For many of you, this year shall bring you to a place where you consecrate your heart and dedicate yourself to make this journey into your inheritance and find out what I have for you. I will not withhold the blessing, says the Lord.

These things are necessary, for the enemies always overturn My principles and My truth in those whom they have not become part of. My grace is here, and My strength, and the keys of worship, prayers, and fastings. All of these things establish a foundation.

Many are always looking for a new truth, a new thing, always addressing the same old problem with some new approach. I am the answer, says the Lord. I am your strength. When My grace is personified in you, My standing comes forward, and I am all that you need.

It is in My presence that all I am is made available to you. Even in My giftings, in the gifts and flows of My Spirit, I add it all together. I reach down even past where many of you are walking. Even though you are walking there, you are still reaching for Me, and I will reach down, pull you up, give you things you have not been able to receive until now, and set you free.

There is no group of people who come together to practice My presence that I will not show up and make them professional in their practice.

There are many things I have to say to you. For many of you who have sought My face, stood, and asked Me for certain things, indeed, for you who have ears to hear and can understand the things the Spirit is saying to the church, there is a time of understanding and teaching that it is My intention to reveal to you.

You can enter into My presence and fellowship with Me, and long-standing emotional strongholds and many such things will give way to My healing power. My joy shall become your strength, and My peace will order a whole new day for you. You shall end up full of years and full of days.

What is it that can trouble you? What is it that you will walk in fear over? What is it that can pull you down when you are successful in receiving the peace I have for you that is not of this world?

Hear what the Spirit would say: My love casts out fear. He that has fear has torment and has not been perfected in My love. There is a state waiting for you who will partake of My Spirit.

The part of you I will magnify is the standing you receive from Me. The part in you I will bring forward as a reigning conqueror will be the nature I have given you. It will overtake every dark part you are walking in, illuminate you, and fill you with light. You will see that what I have given you is enough.

So take what I have, develop it, and walk in it. For I said, you are free.

Understand this first, son and daughter: when you begin to pour yourself out in My presence, when you suspend yourself and hold yourself in My presence, you are also holding yourself in My love. The enemy will come and try to shake your foundation. He will try to badger, pressure, and teach you out of it. But you who stay shall reap the reward.

The enemy will come and tell you all things of the flesh. He will empower your emotions. Everything he can possibly do, he will do to try to get you to abort. He has seen people like you come this way before. When they broke free of him, they rose above the flesh, for they found out that through My love there was nothing they could not do.

The enemy has seen those who broke away from both him and their flesh and nurtured themselves in My love. They rose to great walks of joy and power, and they enjoyed in their generation My finest hours. I came and changed things from the way they were to a closer walk with Myself.

I am looking for those who will suspend themselves in My presence. When you choose this path and I find you faithful in little, I will move mountains out of your way to give you much, so you can be faithful in much.

First, My joy will begin to replace your weaknesses with strength. My joy shall become your strength, and you will become stronger and stronger. Things that used to stop you will stop you no more. In fact, you will approach things that once turned you back, and you will laugh, scoff, mimic, and mock the evil one, wondering in great confidence how he ever stopped you.

Know this: when you know in your nature that you are who you are, then you have the deepest kind of heart faith, and the mountains shall not stand before you, says the Lord.

The times and gatherings committed to Me in the nation are far and few between, says the Lord, when I get to come and speak My mind in the order it needs to be spoken, when I get to come and reveal the mysteries hidden since before the foundations of the world but now made available to the born-again church through the power of the Holy Spirit and the born-again nature you received.

It is a great day and a pleasurable time for Me when I come to a service and get to manifest My mind for that hour, says the Lord. It is then that I can equip you for what is coming. I can prepare you for what now is.

Truth is truth. When a man finds it, he has found something inalterable. Truth stands as truth by merit of truth alone. All Word preached in accuracy is truth, and it will add to your life. There is logos, and there is rhema. When I come with My spoken Word, I am speaking out of My mind and equipping and preparing you for the hour that now is.

In My mind, says the Lord, I have a plan. My plan is not the same for this hour as it was for the generation that preceded you. They walked in another level of darkness, but when they conquered, won, and laid a foundation, My mind came to lay upon that foundation.

As I build line upon line and precept upon precept, the things I say in maturity will be different than the things I was able to say to them. But stand fast in the liberty wherewith I have made you free, and know I have many things yet to say to this generation and to this decade.

In My mind I have formulated a plan. I have a plan for every person, and I have a plan and a mind for every city. In every city I have a small cell of people I deal with continually, that they might rise up and hear what the Spirit is saying, even as I have here.

Know this: I delight in the voice you have given Me in this place. I delight in the pulling out of the mysteries of all that pertains to redemption and showing it to you. I delight in the giftings that have fastened themselves steadfast as anchors upon the foundation and allowed Me to put in pillars and lay large building blocks that cannot be moved, because of the anchors you are affording as pillars in the church.

The things I give are sometimes hard to say because they do not flow with the flesh, such as the teachers I am laying hold of who are hearing My voice and My words and are beginning to stand up among you to stand tall.

I am saying all these things to you so you will know they are necessary to be heard and necessary to be developed against My return. So be steadfast. Continue in your praying and add this dimension of worship you are learning. It is My desire for you to be able to stand in the peace and in the strength of your joy, which will cause you to stand in the days of persecution that are visiting the land.

Be strong. Come to this place of fellowship with Me, and know that I am the Lord God who strengthens and heals.

In times past, when those who walked where they walked laid My foundation, I winked at those times when they found out they could stand on My Word regardless of what their emotions and circumstances said, because My Word was not based on what they were going through or what their emotions said.

I even winked, says the Lord, when they found out My blessings were theirs and played on the edge of fire, went after them, and used My faith to heap up blessing after blessing upon themselves.

But hear what the Spirit of the Lord would say: even now, the time to lay upon this foundation is the personal fellowship you have with Me. That fellowship releases Me to move within your nature, bring it forward, and develop it against these days that are coming, when My joy must be your strength.

One preached this, and I took what he said and laid upon that. Another preached this, and it released Me to lay upon his foundation. But now it is necessary to lay upon all foundations. What they walked in and the enemies they attacked, you must take a step further. Nurture yourself in My presence. My joy and My peace must become your strength for what I have for you and for what is to come.

Even as I have called these meetings, I have called you together to speak to you and for you. This is mandatory if you are to have the best I have in this walk with Me. For you who begin to spend little time, be faithful in the little, and I will do the miracles that will enable you to see and to have much.

Perfect love casts fear out. He that is perfected in love has no fear. Know you not that in the administration of the Spirit, when I was able to give you My nature and make you a partaker of My divine nature, that which was lacking under the old covenant is present in this one? In this nature is My capacity for love.

He that has ears to hear and can tune in to spiritual things knows that what you are looking for can only be found in My presence. Many of you are looking for strength you do not have and peace to endure, and many times you end up looking in the wrong places. Many have come and left these principles completely out, leaning to the law of My Word and not to the very transformation to it.

There are things waiting for you that can only be found in My presence. Take heed to the signposts along the way, for all the Word that is knowledge of Me points to the right way, and the right way is fellowship with Me.

In the eternities to come, I will gather you to Myself as My family. You will look back on the days of redemption, the days of My Word, and the days of law, and rejoice that all of them spoke of these days when you would spend eternity with Me as My family.

Therefore enter in now, for there is much benefit that transcends all realms and belongs to you. Fellowship with Me is one of those things. Enter in. This is up to no other man. Look not to your left nor to your right, for no one else is responsible for your walk with Me. Nobody can hold you out. Nobody can keep you from entering in. Nor do you need another man to lead you in. All that I ever have been and am is made available to you.

Come and take heed to the Word. On this foundation, those who preceded you have laid such an excellent foundation of the truth of My Word and My righteousness. Know this: the foundation holding this up has the strength to hold and sustain you in the fellowship I am calling you to enter into.

Already your enemy trembles at what you can become. His intention is to stop you, but he does not have enough power. For behold, I have given you power over all the power of the enemy. Therefore exercise your rights.

The more you learn about fellowshipping with Me and spending time in My presence, the less trouble you will have with negative, depressive emotions. When My love is developed in your nature, the emotions that used to be your enemy will come under subjection and serve you. They will serve you well.

My joy is your strength. I have peace based in the new nature that is not of this world. He who walks in these things is strong, and they become a strong place for people who have no answers.

Therefore be strong. Just because there is turmoil, cares, and people's hearts failing them for the things coming upon the world does not mean you cannot secure yourself in Me in a place of peace and be full of joy and strength in the hours to come, says the Lord.

Where you are and what you are is what I am fellowshipping with. When you spend time in My presence, I work change. Yes, I do. I work change. I work change. I work change. When you are here, I fellowship with you here. Then when you are there, I fellowship with you there. From then on, My walk is with you there.

You may have whole seasons when you have nothing you can do but minister My name, My presence, and My Spirit every waking hour, but I am with you because I fellowship with you where you are walking with Me. Then you come further, and I fellowship with you there.

It is not a walk of condemnation, but a walk of fellowship. If you called My name out to crowds twenty-four hours a day and harvested, you would increase in My presence, and I would walk with you in each new place. When you stop, pull Myself into you and yourself into Me, and attain new places, I walk with you in those new places. There is nothing I would not do for you and in you.

Therefore delight yourself in Me, and I will grant you the desires of your heart. It is in this manner that you win over the one thing. It is in this manner that you win over all. The only thing that varies is the strength of the keys you use that deliver you to My grace.

Is there not praying in the Holy Spirit? Is there not communion in worship? Is there not fasting? All these are designed for the same thing: to magnify, amplify, and establish My standing in you, which I have given to you by My grace.

Therefore consider yourself strong when you are weak. Let the weak say, "I am strong." Consider yourself tall in the Spirit when you come up short. When you establish yourself in the laws of the Spirit, maintain them, and are strong in your fellowship with Me, all other things will be brought under subjection to My grace. Your problem will be solved, no matter how trivial or how large it may be to you.

Many fall short because they do not endure. Many fall short because they pull back just short of the change. The enemy is faithful to watch for these signs, and he is the one who enacts tribulation. He is the one able to cause people to come against you with persecution.

Be strong. Lift your hands and worship Me. Know that My joy is a sustaining strength through these things. There is no disease, no trial, no onslaught of the evil one that can come against you and wear you out before you operate in the laws of My Spirit, says the Lord.

In the dispensation of time, I operated with people according to My covenant and My promises. Yet the reward I offer all the saints through all the ages was according to when and where they walked. Those who walked with Me under spiritual death and under the law, I judged according to what they walked in and how they walked, and then rewarded them accordingly.

The saints of all times have the chance to be rewarded as you and take their place for all time in My rewards, to reign and rule with Me.

Therefore know this: were not all men born of Me through My son Adam and My Son Jesus, says the Lord? Did I not bring you forth from that which is born of Me? Did I not call you My family? I called you My family, that you might be My sons and daughters and be in fellowship with Me for all time.

Where is your wisdom? Where is that part in you that you walk according to, that I identify with? What is it in you that I desire to identify with? What is it in you that I said you were born for? Why did I induct you into My family? Is it not to spend the eternities of time with Me in fellowship?

Should you not know by My wisdom that fellowship with Me will get you the same thing now? When you enter into fellowship with Me, I will lift you up into those places of grace that are in My Son and set you free.

Why would I leave you the same, when the whole reason you are My family is for fellowship? Do you not know I want you to come into My presence now and fellowship with Me while you walk this walk with Adam's body? Soon you will have your glorified body, and it will not even be the same realm.

So fellowship with Me here and now, while tribulations are present, so I can show you My glory.

For many of you, this is a year when what was impossible to you will begin to be believed with your heart. This is a year for many of your transitions from knowing what I have said into believing it.

Did I not say that if you believe with all your heart, you will have whatsoever you say? Many of you know the truth already, and still many are daring to believe that a path into Me is as direct as the one I have plotted for you. Indeed, it is.

Most of what those who are hungry are looking for is what can only be found in My presence. When I lift you up into Myself and My Spirit sheds abroad My love in your heart, know this: to this man all things are possible, for he believes.

Did I not say that the journey you are taking is the journey I have taken? Most of what you are looking for is in what those who teach you leave out. Most glorify the end of the matter, exalt the answers, and talk of the mountains in great faith, but very few can plot for the multitudes whom I love a direct course into everything I have.

This is a year for many of you when your dreams will be realized, and they will be realized in the truest sense.

Many will be stopped by persecution, affliction, the cares of this world, and the absence of many programs that subsidized their lack. When these vanish away, many will cry in the midst of affliction and persecution, and the cares of this life shall overshadow them. But no one will say that day, "Why aren't You moving?" Those who have been taught, heard what the Spirit said, and became accountable for what they understood will not say, "Why aren't You moving?"

They will know this is a time when I will move as strongly as possible to convince you to come over to this side of the Word, where it comes alive. Come into My presence and know Me. I have authored what I have said. I am what I have said, and what I have said is what I am. I am the finished answered prayer, says the Lord.

So come to Me.

He said to me, "Do you remember when you were lying on the floor and I said to you, 'You do not know anything about worship'?"

I said, "I am on a fast."

He said, "Fasting cannot give you what worship can. But neither can private worship give you what fasting can."

He said, "I gave all the keys for a reason. When it comes time for them, they are the only things that can add the dimension to your life that you are lacking in that area."

He said a lot of people will put all their major emphasis on one key. Unfortunately, there are many things He cannot do for them because they do not let Him finish.

I felt a little bad for the moment, because my major key was praying in tongues. He knew what I was thinking, so He answered before I could finish the thought.

He said, "Fortunately, you gave yourself to a key that was a revelation gift. With a revelation gift, when it came time for all the other keys--fasting, private worship, and inheritance--I was able to add them to your walk."

The love of God, I thought, was a limb. I thought worship was a limb. But they all were part of the revelation of the love of the Father.

In tongues, the interpretation was this: you are going to cost yourself time if you dismiss the worship limb lightly. It is going to cost you time.

I say that in light of your hearts laying hold of God, because what most of you are laying hold of Him for comes through those keys. If you are biding your time, waiting for the worship to pass and not taking any of it home with you to heart, He said there is really only one route into what you are looking for: take that key and add it to the rest of your walk.

For I return continually to these things of your freedom, particularly the worship that constitutes My presence and your personal fellowship with Me. It is necessary for this decade to come and for the things that are coming. It is necessary that you enter into a new degree, a place to walk with Me where neither your emotions nor your thoughts are under the control of the evil one.

Surrender yourself to Me, that your emotions may be brought under subjection to the life that is in you. Then you are able not only to stand but to receive direction that comes to you in the inner witness and the walk you have with Me from walking in the peace I give you.

When you receive this peace from fellowshipping with Me, then I can lead and guide you. In this last day, leading and guiding will be one of the most important things you walk in. So you who have ears to hear, hear what the Spirit would say. Do not only hear; take heed and walk in them. It is necessary that you obey for what is to come.

It is My desire to pour My Spirit out in this place. Not only in this place; it is My desire to pour My Spirit out on this city. He who has ears to hear, hear what the Spirit would say. I am attempting to prepare a people who are ready for this outpouring. In it you will be prepared. You will say what I say. You will move the way I move. You will teach them the basics of walking with Me.

After the outpourings of the great revivals scheduled to come, the enemy is going to revisit. He is going to try one more severe move before I return, to neutralize. So be strong. It is My desire that you teach them the first principles of the oracles of God and take them on in your generation into the deeper things, that I may return in the building of My building, says the Lord.

He who walks down this path will soon understand that fellowship with Me is the drink of water that quenches the thirst that men cannot quench, nor can men give you. Therefore walk down this path. Minister to yourself. Pay no attention to the emotions that come your way.

He just said there will always be an increase. In fact, the very thing you are building yourself above will seem like it comes alive and fights for a while. Especially give no heed when you are doing the things that are right and they rise up and get worse. During that time, pay special attention, nurture yourself, and continue to build yourself up.

You will not get too far down this path before I establish Myself in you in such a way, says the Lord, that you will never turn back. Even the victory that precedes the victory, the fellowship with Me, will be too sweet.

He that nurtures his own spirit builds a strong city. Even the weaknesses in you I can overcome. When the Holy Ghost nurtures your nature, when He takes what you have become and magnifies it, then the love of God is shed abroad in your heart, and this gospel and you are made not ashamed.

Therefore be strong in those things that change things, such as fellowshipping with Me. Why spend the day at the gate, wondering whether you should enter in? Enter in. Taste and see.

For many of you, your journey is really just beginning, a journey with both sides of the trail laced with victory. You will stop along the path, for there are many streams and many wells. You will drink deep. There will be many celebrations, for there will be much victory along the way. When you release the Holy Spirit to take everything you are in Me, then all things are possible to this person who believes.

Have ears that hear. The enemy of the Holy Spirit has for a long time taken what is in his mind and tried to establish it as truth between truth. In the transition from things believed that are not true to things to be believed that are true, the enemy always stands at the gate and uses all he has to fight a good fight against you, that you might stay where you are and not enter into what I have for you.

The devil has upped his forces. He senses something is about to take place. He has been monitoring the activities of angels. He has been monitoring what saints are giving themselves to, what is being taught, who is hearing, and who is walking in what He has inspired.

But this is a time when I am close enough to you and to what you have been taught. My greatest desire is to establish a strong foundation that I can build from. Indeed, I am coming to take this city. I must have a foundation to retain and to build a building upon, so that what is poured out will not go away, but rather will spread like revival fires from one place to another until other revival fires are met and the flames unite and burn and burn.

Indeed, the wars, earthquakes in diverse places, flooding, wars, and rumors of wars that have taken place--are they not signs for the end time? My revival fires will burn in this place and in that place. But I am finding enough here. I am finding enough. I am close enough to this great foundation, and many are obeying Me. Steps are being taken into My presence that will be heralded in heaven: "Look, He is about to pour Himself out."

These things have been waited for since the foundations of the world.

Know this, you who are believing for blessing, believing to be able to finance, support, and give like you have never given before to these great moves of Mine: even since the days of Noah, did I not say that as long as the world would stand, there would be seedtime and harvest? There is a standard I always use to meet the needs of the world, and that is the harvest of My crops and the life that is in seeds.

Great flooding will take place in these last days. In the floodings, things that worked before will not work quite as well, because there will be shortages here and shortages there. But look up, look up, and rejoice. Not a hair on your head needs to be damaged. Look up and rejoice, because the time of My return is drawing close.

The precious harvest of the planet is coming very close. I am ready to stick in the sickle and harvest. Do you not know that you prayed this day would take place? It is taking place.

So be strong and know that I am close enough for you to touch Me. Take My Word and speak against the strongholds of lack. Declare an abundance. You will release Me, because the membrane between Me and you is getting very thin. I will be able to come and pour out on your circumstances and on you, because that is how close I am.

Speak the victory. Pull the strongholds down. Rejoice. Take extra time to spend worshiping Me and praising Me, because I am close and the outpouring is close. It will be preceded by Me pouring out on many of you to prepare the way, get things ready, take every media, and pull every stronghold down, that I might establish My foundation.

So rejoice and be glad. You who have ears to hear are going into the land of plenty. I need you as a testimony of what is over there. Be strong. Bring it back to those who ride the fence and say, "Get in. He loves you. Get in. He loves you. This is what a foundation will produce for you." So come, come, come.

Where many people engage in testings and trials is in the financial realm, because of all places in this realm there is much fear there. The things that affect the daily walk of your life, the place you walk in at that moment, are the things that generate the most fear and are least dealt with.

If you knew the end of the matter, you would back up to the beginning of the matter and, no matter what the circumstances said, apply yourself to what I said, because I am your answer.

In the areas of disease, fear also lives. Too many have been indoctrinated by the things they see. Too many have called upon My name and named My name, yet suffered the same condemnation and defeats as the world. Even though they had been delivered from them, they still are condemned and die of the same things the world dies of.

In My provision for the same things the world fears, My people also fear. They do not really believe that I am their provision. They look to the west, east, south, and north, and evaluate everything. But there is no fear that accompanies any knowledge that may come your way when I am the basis for all the truth you walk in.

What is it that you would fear? What is stronger than I? What circumstance are you in that I cannot turn at any point and give you the victory? If you go from where you are to where I have provided for you, standing in Me, you will be amazed. Your testimony will change. Your confidence and your health will change.

When you walk in the kind of peace that says, "I am the Lord your God who goes before you, and I even order your provision," you will walk in great peace.

Therefore, leaving those things that are behind, press toward the prize of the mark of the high calling. He that has torment has fear, for there has not been a perfecting of My love. Did I not say that fellowship with Me causes the outpouring of My love to begin to affect you, spirit, soul, and body, as My presence permeates every part of your being and your walk?

Come and fellowship with Me. You will find in My presence a surety where there will be no torment and fear. I am your health. I am your provision. I am He who waits for you at the gates of eternity. Is there anything too hard for Me?

Trust Me not only for your salvation when you cross the line; trust Me now. I am a God of performance, and I will perform for you, says the Lord.

There will be much weeping in the land, for many will find they had not made My stand. When provision runs out, all those who used faith to serve themselves and their greed will find out it was not Me behind the scenes moving My hand on their behalf. When storehouses and provisions begin to fail, all the hay, wood, and stubble will fail with it.

Therefore be strong. I am the first and foremost One you need to get to know in the lines of defense. I am the standard you need to learn how to hold up. I am the answer before the answer is received. Walk in it.

When you lose your fear, My operation in you will be escalated. I put up with fear in babies, for they do not yet know Me. Many of you have received many calls to come and know Me. First it will be sacrifice. But when My peace begins to dominate a day that used to wear you down and wear your spiritual defenses down, with My peace a day like that is taken in stride.

Hear what the Spirit would say: I am your strength, but you must let Me be who I am.

I will speak with the Spirit and with the understanding. Come on. Lift Me up. Lift Me up. Glorify Me. When you lift Me up, I can lift you up.

The first and foremost commandment is to love the Lord your God with all your heart, your mind, your soul, and your strength. With purpose and heart, pursue Me. Love Me with all your strength, heart, mind, and purpose. In doing so, you begin to love your neighbor as yourself.

What many are looking for is what has been lost. Yet while looking for something lost, they do not know what they are looking for. So they search in wrong places, not knowing that what they are really looking for, the thirst they have, can only be quenched and satisfied with what I have to give, says the Lord.

So come on and lift Me up.

It is a powerless generation that seeks My power in formulas. My power is generated in the same place inheritance is received. It is not in your works, nor in programs that replace the movings of My Spirit. Those who seek to move by My Spirit, and seek My grace, glory, and power, must come visit My place and receive it.

There are those who wait, but they wait for what man cannot do. Do you think you will find those things in any other place than in fellowship with Me? What many of you seek and hunger for can only be found in My presence.

When people become so programmed in a certain direction, it is hard for Me to break in with instruction and correction. But hear what the Spirit would say to you who have ears to hear: some will be breaking through those barriers. Some will not be denied.

The answer is in what we were born for in the first place. The answer is in why He came back to get us when we were separated by the void of spiritual death. The answer is in what He told the twelve when they came back so excited that devils were subject to them, and sickness and disease were subject to them in His name.

He said yes, but He switched the emphasis: "Rejoice rather that your names are written in heaven." You are going to spend eternity with Me. But if you do not mind, I would kind of like to get to know you now.

What My children fail to realize is that the more they yield themselves to My grace, the more they establish My standing in their lives. When you do those things that promote My grace, you are promoting Me.

Many of you will come within range of something I have for you, and your hunger will intensify. Your reach will become more powerful. Because of your hunger, thirsting, and reaching, I am able to employ My grace in its highest form. That is the form it takes: unearnable, unmeritable, in a free flow.

The highest kind of grace I love to give is when you are reaching for Me, reaching for fellowship and a place in My Spirit where you will walk with Me where you have not walked before. That will cause joy, rejoicing, laughter, and fullness in you that you can have no other way.

It is My pleasure for you who are reaching in this manner to move through this place at this time and give you some things you have not been able to receive before.

The enemy's charge is to get you to turn against yourself, to take all that I am and all that I said and discredit it, to turn you against believing in or even understanding what I finished for you and what I have done. If he can get you to turn away, that is the only way he can defeat you.

Calls are being answered. Calls are being received. Calls are being equipped. Gifts are being given. Many of you shall go forth. You will lay hands on the sick, and they will recover. I will use you in ways not commonly seen in the last century.

For many of you shall go, and it will be nothing to you whether you are in a service, a house, or a hospital. From the things you learn from the presence you are receiving, from the things I pour out and teach you, you will carry this to many places.

I will come on you and use you where disease is prevalent. I will come on you and use your mouth when people are distressed, in situations, and looking for answers. I will send you to trouble spots and places of darkness. You will carry the torch. You will carry light.

It will not be on the order you are familiar with. Did I not love the whole world and give My only begotten Son? It is a time when light will go where darkness is, instead of trying to coax darkness into where light is. I will send you into the darkness and meet them at the cross, where I healed them, saved them, gave them peace, and cast devils out.

Rejoice, for it is a great day. You will drink deep of My Spirit, and you shall be full, says the Lord.

It is a pleasing thing to Me, says the Lord. It is a pleasing thing to Me that I am being positioned in your lives to take you across certain barriers that I have not been able to get many across. You will not only stand in My presence concerning My power, but you will have the right things to say to those who come to you hungry to know about Me.

He is pleased. I feel His pleasure. It is beyond pleasure, beyond pleased. I cannot get a word for it for you, but that is what He has toward you as you enter into this and let yourself be equipped in these areas He has seldom been able to get past before.

There must be a word in the English language. When I got the blame off God and quit assassinating His character, I felt His pleasure more than I had felt anything for a long time, so I called it His pleasure, or pleasing Him. But when people are supposed to be feeling His pleasure, and He wants you to know He is pleased, it is not exactly what I was feeling so I could name it His pleasure. He is pleased with you.

I am trying to find that word that comes to me in prophecy, and I cannot put it in its place. He has pleasure. This is bringing Him great pleasure, that so many people are lining up and being lined up to enter into this kind of fellowship with Him.

For the times of refreshing from My presence, I have held this place in store, and the desire of My heart was the outpouring. I take pleasure and delight in this level of fellowshipping with the saints. It is on this level that I corral darkness with light. It is on this level that great alternatives to the darkness people are walking in are given to them through manifestations of signs and wonders.

Even still, the peace will take another strength for those who exercise themselves thereby. There are places I have for many of you, and there is no going there without the manifestation of My peace. There is no going there.

Therefore prepare yourself. As a waiter would load a golden platter with fruit and wait on those who have accepted who they have become as My sons and daughters, so with golden fruit My grace will be prevalent for those who have accepted. You will eat of My goodness and sing My praises.

In a time of trouble such as the world has not seen, look up. Know Me. Know Me. Your redemption is drawing close.

Many have said, "Where is it? These things have been spoken and prophesied about. Where is it?"

Hear what the Spirit would say: many have come to the door, the door that opens for revival in the burnt-over fields. Many have come to this door, only to find out what opens the door and leads you in.

He told me to stop right here and exhort a minute. He said to tell you that when I began to find it out, I thought He was teaching me private worship so I could teach it to you. He was teaching it to me to change my life, so I would step over and get to know the Father.

The love He touched me with that day I touched Him is what many people abort and go back from. When they start getting close to a walk like that, it costs them something long before they get a good enough look at Him. Not the look that only spells, "Price, price, price," but the look that says, "You have been wanting a long, cool drink of My water. You substituted many things for it, only to find they did not quench your thirst."

I say to you, for many turn back: this last part is contingent on walking in My love, My power, and My Word. It is My attempt to avoid at all costs those who put Me on display and then fall in front of them, bringing reproach.

But all those who will let My love work in them, it will bring mortification to levels they never dreamed possible. When I say levels, I mean that as I let My love work in them, the change is not something dreaded. The change becomes something embraced, as the deep wells quench the thirst that can only be quenched by knowing Me and the power of My Spirit.

Like those who crossed over Jordan and said there were giants, but also brought back the fruit, there will be more than you know and more than you thought possible. Go and taste the fruit, and come back and say, "Come on. It is not as hard as you think. Come through the door and taste this fruit and see that it is good."

Things indeed can be expedited in these last days, for I pour out as much as I can at all times on as many as can receive. Most of the limitations put on Me are put on Me by the people themselves, by wrong teaching, by attitudes, by people's walk in the flesh.

I desire to do all and to give all that many ask, but not many walk with Me, even in the fellowship they have been taught. Not many walk with Me close enough to get past the hard places. Most of the reason I cannot give people what they ask is because they are not walking in the love it takes to support what they have asked for.

I desire to pour out the fullness of My gifting and power, the fullness of My workings of miracles, the fullness of My gifts, the fullness of healings, and the fullness of My compassion. It is My desire.

But most have measured their life against the flesh and decided to walk a certain path, balancing the Spirit with the flesh, not wanting to give up too much of the flesh to have more of the Spirit, settling in a comfortable place of the Spirit while not having to give up the flesh.

It is still My desire. Many have said many things of Me that are not so. Know that My desire is to pour out all that I am, at all times, every time.

Where grace will abound, many will go in ahead of time and be saturated in My love. They will have eaten the fruit of their own vineyard and will come back and say, "It is all true." When I am able to use them to minister My grace and My love, many of the strongholds you were holding on to will vanish away, and I will get to pour more and more of what I am upon you.

Therefore the whole process into grace shall escalate. It shall even be the great outpourings before My return, says the Lord.

For the purpose of worship, as you identify yourself by that nature and fellowship with Me, know you not that you will get to know Me? You have been made a partaker of My nature, and you will get to know Me.

When you get to know Me, you will know My character. Because you know My character, you will know what I will do and what I will not do. If you can receive it and come to the place where you know Me, then you will also understand how I will move, what I will do, and what I will not do.

Many miss the mark by misunderstanding how I operated on the outer limbs of the tree. Many aborted on the last stretch of their harvest. But those who know Me know whether I will do these things or not. They know whether My character has been outlined correctly or not. They know whether the things said about Me are true.

It is you who know Me who will have the final touches to finish your course, says the Lord.

Did I not say that prophecy was for exhortation, edification, and comfort? The things I exhort, the things I give you from the Spirit, I give from My mind, the mind of Christ. Listen to the exhortation, for the exhortation is based on present truth. It is based on those revelations, those foundational truths you stand on, those that have made you free.

Did I not say prophecy is for exhortation and comfort? When exhortation comes forward based on the Word, it will instruct you by the Spirit, bringing together the current times you live in, My mind, and where you are walking with Me.

When I exhort based on the Word, I will not speak beyond you or below you, but I will give you that which edifies you.

So hear what the Spirit would say: when I say through exhortation that there is a direct route you can chart into those things you thirst for, through the fellowship you desire, into the walk of power that all who desire to walk in My power dream of, beyond all of that, to know Me by My nature in such a way that you will never be barren or fruitless, nor will you fall--this is My promise to all who know Me, says the Lord.
