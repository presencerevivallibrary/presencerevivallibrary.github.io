The Presence Revival

Many were drawn to Pastor Dave Roberson's ministry because of what they experienced in his meetings. During seasons remembered by attendees as the "crying days," people testified that when Pastor Dave entered the room, they could immediately sense a change in the atmosphere. The presence and love of God became so overwhelming that many would begin weeping, often before any preaching or ministry had taken place. Testimonies of divine healing and miracles were common throughout his ministry. Through Pastor Dave's life and ministry, God gave many a glimpse of the revival that would precede the return of Jesus Christ—a revival that would come to be known as the Presence Revival.

The central message of these teachings is how believers can prepare for and participate in the Presence Revival. Throughout these teachings, Pastor Dave explains the transforming work of the Holy Spirit in the life of a believer.

Pastor Dave Roberson taught that this transformation does not happen automatically. God has provided practical means through which believers can deliberately cooperate with the Holy Spirit and yield themselves to His transforming work:

  • Praying in Tongues — Learn to pray continuously throughout daily life. This edifies the inner man, mortifies the outward man, and helps reveal God's will and purpose for your life.
  • Assimilation, Confession, and Meditation of God's Word — Learn to renew the mind with the truth of God's Word. Assimilate God's Word by repeatedly reading entire books of the Bible to equip yourself for day and night meditation. When combined with praying in tongues, this helps unlock revelation knowledge of God's Word. Repeated confession and declaration of the truth of God's Word renew the mind and break strongholds of unbelief.
  • Fasting — Learn to use fasting to bring the flesh into subjection to the new nature and break through spiritual strongholds and unbelief.
  • Private Worship — Learn to dwell in God's presence through simple words of love and adoration until God's presence becomes a tangible reality in daily life. This also allows God to deal with your emotions.
  • Hearing and Obeying the Holy Spirit — Learn to hear God through the inner witness, revelation, hearing His voice in the language you understand, and visions. Then follow His guidance in every area of your life.

These teachings grew out of Pastor Dave Roberson's own pursuit of God. Through years of praying in tongues, meditation in God's Word, worship, fasting, and fellowship with the Holy Spirit, he received revelation knowledge and taught these same principles to help other believers experience the transforming work of God for themselves.

At the heart of these teachings is the reality of Christ in you, the hope of glory:

The goal is to be changed from glory to glory into the image of Christ, becoming a vessel through whom the Father can freely accomplish His purpose in the earth by the power of the Holy Spirit.

These transcripts are provided to preserve and make accessible the teachings of Pastor Dave Roberson of Family Prayer Center, Tulsa, Oklahoma, enabling them to be studied, searched, shared, and translated into other languages. It is our hope that believers around the world will benefit from these teachings, grow in fellowship with the Holy Spirit, and be prepared for the Presence Revival.

About the Transcripts

These transcripts were produced from the original audio recordings of Pastor Dave Roberson's teachings using automated speech recognition and AI-assisted transcription and editing tools.

The goal of this project is to make these teachings more accessible for reading, study, search and translation.

Two transcript versions are provided on this website:

  • A transcript-only version without section headings.
  • A version that includes AI-generated section headings to improve readability and navigation.

The section headings are editorial aids generated by AI and were not part of the original teachings. While they are intended to reflect the flow of the teaching, readers may prefer the transcript-only version if they wish to read the message without added headings.

Because this archive contains hundreds of teachings, the transcripts have not been manually proofread in their entirety. While every effort has been made to improve accuracy and readability, occasional transcription errors, formatting issues, misidentified words, punctuation errors, or other inaccuracies may remain.

Readers should regard the original audio recordings as the authoritative source whenever precise wording is important.

If you discover a transcription error, correction suggestions are welcome and appreciated.

The original audio recordings and related ministry resources remain available through www.daveroberson.org.
